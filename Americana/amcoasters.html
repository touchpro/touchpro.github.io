Americana is no Cedar Point; it doesn’t have a dozen roller coasters. The major one it does have, however, looks like it was great: the Screechin' Eagle, a big wooden one with numerous hills and turns.




The other coaster is one of those contained kind that look like you could haul them with a truck. Still, those can be fun. Too bad they didn’t leave the key in the roller coasters.
