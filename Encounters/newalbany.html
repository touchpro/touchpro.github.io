A Haunting in New Albany, Indiana


An interesting story from out of state, e-mailed to me.

This is about my grand mother Helen ragsdale it started when she passed in 1980 i was 8 yrs old. my grandfather owned his own business in steel fabrication they owned a modest house in town when my grandmother passed her room was in the rear of the house when ever i had to go to the bathroom it would face her room and i would get this feeling that my grand mother was sitting on the bed this went on for as long as he owned the house! my grandfather remarried in 1981 he had all the locks changed do to a robbery in his house no one had any keys. my grand father kept a picture of my grandmother in the top drawer he had left for work and when he came home that evening the picture was on the top of the dresser no one was home he just placed it back in the drawer and went about his business well a few days later this happened again he repeated the same thing he just placed it back in the drawer well it happened one more time and the picture has never been seen again i think my grandmother was telling my grandfather not to forget her. and a few other things went on like when i was in the rear of the house a music box would play and when i left the room it would stop! i always felt her in that house she was a great lady and i miss her very much and the last thing she reached out to me in a dream i was sitting in a white room she was sitting across from me talking and smiling but i could not hear what she was saying but it was when I was 25 when the dream happened!

Extra note my grandfather moved out of the house in the mid 90s and me and my mother returned to the house after they had moved and my grandmother was no longer there she had chosen to move on I guess which is fine with me she was at peace I hope!

Creepy stuff. According to the sender, his grandmother died in 1978.
