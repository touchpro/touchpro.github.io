Ghosts abound at Ohio's state parks and nature preserves. The land was usually given to the state, and the guy who owned the land was always an eccentric crazy who killed people/got killed/killed himself, and so on. Very cool stories at a few of them; my personal favorite is probably the Mushroom Lady at Beaver Creek.
