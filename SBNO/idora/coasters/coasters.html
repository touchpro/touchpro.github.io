Idora Park
** Both Coasters have been destroyed. **

Idora Park was home to three roller coasters at the time it burnt. One coaster was a kiddy coaster for children. I wasn't able to get any pictures of it before it was destroyed. The other two coasters were the WildCat, and the Jack Rabbit (Back Wabbit). The Wild Cat burnt in 1984 which was ultimately the end for Idora. The Jack Rabbit stood strong for years to come until it, as well as the partial remains of the WildCat, were demolished.

Below are links to the pictures of the coasters that I was able to get photos of before they were destroyed.
