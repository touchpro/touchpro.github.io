CLIPPINGS
These clippings are clippings from the Youngstown Vindicator from around the time when Idora Park burnt in 1984. They are photographs that I cut out for reasons unknown. I apologize for the quality of the cutting as I was only 8 years old at the time. I don't remember why I cut these out and until recently I didn't even know I had them. I found them in an old binder when I was looking for a place to store a few things. I couldn't thumbnail these pictures because the quality was poor once I shrunk them down. They are saved very large and may take a while to load. I plan on shrinking them in the future but for now, enjoy...
