Idora Park
** Most buildings are no longer stnading. **


During the time that Idora operated there were many buildings on the grounds. The Fire that ultimately took the park out of business also destroyed many of the buildings. Shortly after the first fire, a second fire destroyed a few more buildings. Over time most of the remaining buildings rotted away, were removed, or burnt in smaller fires. The most recent loss was the burning of the ballroom.
