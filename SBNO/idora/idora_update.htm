Idora Park 2005 Final Update

This will be the last update to the Idora section. My most recent visit to the park was dissappointing, yet at the same time expected. As it ends up, the park is entirely gone. There are a few foundations and stair ways remaining, but the rides, buildings, etc are otherwise all gone. Its difficult to even tell where some of the buildings once stood. I grabbed a bunch of pictures of what remains, but they really aren't in any specific order since they're mostly just pictures of grass and similar nonesense.

After the final fire that destroyed the ballroom, the remains on the grounds were bulldozed. I'm assuming this was done to avoid another fire and other similar hazzards.
