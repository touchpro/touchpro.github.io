Heritage USA - Heritage Grand Tower - Interior
   
I was finally able to get in to the tower late one evening when things were pretty dead around Heritage. I gained access without being forceful, so I considered entery a success. The tower was every bit as amazing as I expected although my photos mostly suck. The views were amazing, and being alone in there at night was unreal.
