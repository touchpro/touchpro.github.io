Heritage USA - Grand Hotel
   
I was able to pop in during a service and walk around a little in order to take some pictures. This is a courtyard attached to the main lobby. It was in extremely poor condition. It was weird that there were people inside the other parts of the building yet this section was still as abandoned as ever.
