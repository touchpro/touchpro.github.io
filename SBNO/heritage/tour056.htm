Heritage USA - The Barn
   
On my third visit, the roof had been removed. The curtains and flooring had also been removed and there were drying fans around the building attempting to dry it out. This is the first time the barn had been touched in quite some time. My understanding has always been that the Hurricans in 1989 were what damaged the building. If accurate, the building was long overdue for some help.
