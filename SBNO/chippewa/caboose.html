Caboose

Sitting deep in the woods near the Wild Mouse is an old Grand Trunk Western Rail Road Caboose. There really isn't much to say about an old Caboose. I'm not sure how it go there. I assume it was added after 1963 since there is a 1963 inspection on it. Its still in pretty decent shape, but probably pretty tough to get out of there.
