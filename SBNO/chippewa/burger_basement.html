Burger Factory

The basement of the Burger Factory, stand A, was used as storage for various park items. When I was able to get in there, it was mostly lake items, including the floatie rope thing that goes around the swimming area, inner tubes, etc. There were also plenty of those famouse 1978 cups that people are selling on ebay from time to time. I believe on my first visit there were literally boxes of them... they've since been stolen of course. Anyway, here are a few pictures of some of the things inside the basement.
