Outdoor Theater Stage

 

Outdoor Theater

The outdoor stage originally stood on the upper grounds near the ballroom. Eventually that stage was torn down, and the current theater was built on a area that was formerly an athletic field. As of this writing in 2008, the stage still stands, adorned with graffiti, and rotting in to the landscape. The above picture is none other than Bob Segar playing on the stage. Many acts performed on the outdoor theater over the years.
