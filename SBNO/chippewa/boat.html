The Boat

 

Tom Sawyer

I really don't know much about the old Tom Sawyer. It was one of many boats that Chippewa Lake Park had over the years. It was a (fake) steam engine that took passengers around the lake. As of this writing, it was sitting on the shore near the beach rusting away. The Tom Sawyer lettering can no longer be seen.
