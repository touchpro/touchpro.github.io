The Midway
(pictures current june 2000)

Not much remains of the Old Chippewa Lake Park Midway area. I had somehow neglected covering this area on this site for quite some time. On my last visit it was very overgrown. I did my best to get some pictures of what remains, but it really doesn't give a good idea of how the midway was set up.


Bumper Cars building (i think). Both remaining buildings looking down midway. Inside bumper car building.
Bumper car building with a little lean to it. Between the remaining buildings. Fun House.
Fun House (look at the exit). Fun House. I'm guessing someone sat in this booth. Burnt Part of the Fun House
Pole with lights in center of midway. Two rusted Trucks in the midway. Notice the Rail Road Tracks. Just plain midway.
Looking down the midway. Video Game parts from the burnt down Arcade.
