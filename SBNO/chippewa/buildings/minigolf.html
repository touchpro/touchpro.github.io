PEE WEE GOLF
(pics current june 2000)

There isn't much to see in these pictures as this area is very overgrown, but this is where the pee wee golf course once stood. During my fall trip it appeared that most of the course still stands under all of the leaves and weeds. Most of what you see here is the entrance and the little building. There are lots of flowers that are still growing after 22 years. I'm sure if one tried to grow these flowers they would die in a year. I guess that's just the way the world works.
