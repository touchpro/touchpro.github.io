Rocket Cars
(pics current june 2000)

Every park had them, and Chippewa wasn't an exception. The Rocket Cars are a timeless ride. Upon my last visit to Six Flags over Ohio, I rode their Rocket Cars. They're always on top of a building of some sort, and that is all that is left of the Chippewa Lake Park Rocket Cars which once swung out over the lake. You can tell by the shape of this building and the remains of the ramps that once took riders to the top of the building, that this is what the building used to be.
