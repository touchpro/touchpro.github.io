Pier

There isn't really a lot you can say about a pier. Built in the 1920's, this pier replaced the old wooden dock at the lake. It was used for the typical Pier like activities, such as docking, launching, and loading boats. As of this writing, the Pier is still standing, in fact, I've actually seen boats docked to it on occasion.
