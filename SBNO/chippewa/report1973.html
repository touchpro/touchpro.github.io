1973 CBE Annual Report


This is an annual report from CBE or Continental Business Enterprises INC from 1973. The pages are very large and may take a while to load. Keeping them large was the only way I could keep the text clear enough to read. There are some intersting things in here. The most interesting thing in my opinion is the planned park that is feature on the cover and on the second page. The back page talks about the new rides and additions for the 1974 season. There is mention of the park on most of the pages, however, if you are using a slow connection, I suggest the front, second page zoomed, and the final page for viewing. Enjoy....
