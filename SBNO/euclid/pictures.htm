Euclid Beach Park

Euclid, Ohio

 

On the coldest day ever, I managed to snap a few pictures of the apartment complex and lakeside area that was once Euclid Beach Park. The grounds are now mostly used for ederly people homes, although there are plenty of signs of the park along the lake. There appeared to be some homeless camps in the woods, so I didn't venture too far off the main path, but I did get pictures of the walking area that was once the old time cars, the midway path, steps leading to the beach, a fountain, a pier, and a few other things. Enjoy!
