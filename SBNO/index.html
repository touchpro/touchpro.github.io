about the site...

Welcome to standing but not operating. A site dedicated to bringing you abandoned amusement parks, their history, and mostly, pictures of them standing, but not in operation. The site gets updated as frequently as possible, but quite honestly, no where neary as often as I'd like. The most recent revamp of the site was in late 2008.

So anyway, come on inside and check out the pictures. There are several parks featured, some with a small number of pictures, and others with hundreds of pictures taken over a long period of time. Some pictures are blurry, some are just poorly thought out, and others are great, but only by chance. I'm not a photographer, I'm just a guy who takes pictures, so don't set your expectations high.

Lastly, feel free to send me an email. I try my best to respond, but often times I get behind on email and eventually have to just give up and start fresh. Please don't be offended if I don't respond.
