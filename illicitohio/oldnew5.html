Fort Hayes Barraks - 2006

 

Fort Hayes was originally an Arsenal, later becoming a intake and training facility, and later yet becoming Fort Hayes, named after Rutherford B. The base was mostly closed by the greatest year ever to be born, 1976, and is now used mostly by Columbus Public Schools. Much of if sat abandoned for years, some of which is found on the main page or by clicking here. You can find out more about the recent renovations HERE. (offsite)
