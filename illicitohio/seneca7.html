Roof

The roof was the first place we visited on this trip. We had the feeling we were being watched the entire time by people in the near by skyscrapers. Otherwise, the view was great and it was nice and windy up there. We were wearing construction clothes so we hoped people would think we belonged. Fellow explorers note, this construction outfit thing seems to work.


We could see ver far off in the distance from all sides. We could even see the great Blind School building on the corner of parsons and main with no problem. If you look deep in to the right top of this picture you can see it too. Unfortunately its a little too blury to tell that's what it is.
