Bomb Shelter

This is the second station that we visited. I think this is the one that had been turned in to a bomb shelter in the 1950s. Some of the block walls put in for the shelter had been removed. The layout was difficult to grasp, but interesting to see. There used to be a tunnel in between this one and the last, but it is bricked in. There are a lot of pictures here and not much I can say so just enjoy the view.
