In case you're just joining me, check page one for more info. The following are all pictures from my trips through the now abandoned former Mike Tyson home.

PICTURES

Pool Area

The pool area was the first thing I saw... I came in through a pool service room door that was open. I could smell chlorine in the air, and it almost felt warmer for some reason. I had no idea what I was in for, and as I rounded the corner, I saw the below room... Very neat!

 
This was pretty much my first view upon walking in. This pic is actually from day 2 when I had a tripod.
This is looking from the dive toward the waterfall that is next to the hot tub.
A fountain in the pool. There was another at the other end. I'd have loved to have seen this place when it was operational.
The hot tub. BIG!
As you can see, the water was just slightly green and just about to freeze completely. It wasn't as frozen as the pictures might imply.
A better view of the waterfall. The hot tub is nestled behind it. Yes... nestled. Notice the plants seem to be real and not quite dead?
There was a sundae bar next to the pool. This place is almost too good to be true.
A view of the water. As you can see, not as bad as you might think. That is a phone on the bottom of the pool.
Looking from the hottub, over the fountain and waterfall, toward the dives... yes two of them.
Looking across the pool. You can see some of the only vandalism in the entire place. Some idiot decided to break some glass.

 

Pool Bathroom

Next to the pool is the changing/shower/bathroom. It was really really dark and I was actually too much of a wimp to go all the way in.

 
I actually kind of dig the color of the sink. As you can tell, there is nice stuff in the place. Not always my taste, but certainly not budget by any means.
More of the changing area next to the pool.
A shower door in the changing area.
Right around the corner were the controls for the pool room and entertainment room.
