The worthington Inn has been a fixture on High Street in Worthington since the 1830s. The Inn was originally called the Union Hotel, then the Central House, and is now referred to as the Worthington Inn. The building originally did not have the roof you see in these pictures, as that roof was added after a fire damaged the old roof, and the owner decided to rebuild with a ballroom on the top floor. Recently the Inn started offering Condos. Check out their site HERE for more history.
