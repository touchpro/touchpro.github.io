Idora Ballroom Stage
Idora Ballroom Stage - 1970s
Idora Ballroom Stage - 2000

 

The stage inside of the Ballroom at Idora park remained almost untouched until the park's final major fire in 2001. Aside from some minor changes in the background, you can see the obvious similarities. Today the ballroom is nothing more than a grassy hill side off of the edge of Mill Creek Park in Youngstown.
