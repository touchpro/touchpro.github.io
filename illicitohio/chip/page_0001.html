Chippewa Lake, Ohio



Chippewa Lake, Ohio This section is a brief look at one of the parks I cover extensively on my other website at http://sbno.illicitohio.com. This is an old amusement park near Seville Ohio that has been abandoned since 1978. Some of the things pictured below have since been burnt, stolen, knocked over, absorbed by mother earth.

It has been quite some time since I've visited the park. Most of these pictures are from early visits. The ballroom, one of the best I've seen, is now sadly gone. The park just gets worse and worse every year. As much as I hate to say it, it is surprising that the company that ownes the land hasn't gone in and knocked it all down. I'm just waiting for the day I get that email.

Anyway, Chippewa is a place I found on line and decided to visit. I've had some good times there. I visited the park with a couple of guys from down south in a band called Squint. They were grabbing pictures for an album they were working on. One of sbno's (my other site) pictures of Idora ended up on the cover which was cool. A couple of pictures from my site also ended up on the cover of an industry magazine that I plan on scanning someday. During the Squint visit, one of the guys fell through the tumble bug platform making for an inappropriate laugh from me. It wasn't funny, but it was... just like any fall.

The park opened as a trolly park and was owned by the same family for decades. It was sold to a business who planned on turning the park in to a resort. There are plans for the resort on my other site as well as more pictures. Those plans never materialized and the park slowly faded away, finally closing in the late 70s... after more than 100 years of operation.

Anyway, enjoy the pictures. There are countless more including old pictures at http://sbno.illicitohio.com.
