This is the barn Church at PTL Heritage USA in South Carolina. This was the amusement/resort/village that James and Tammy Faye Bakker built. Herigate is amazing to see in real life. The park basically has been stuck in time since the day James Bakker was taken away. James is now out of jail, Tammy is or was married to one of the people who managed construction at the park if I'm not mistaken, and Heritiage is being partially revived by another church after sitting empty for years. The barn was the site of many televised events. It stands pretty much as it did the day they walked away, with the exception of the large holes in the roof. The PA system is still intact, chairs still stored in the main room, etc. It is an amazing site to see. You can see many many more pictures of the park on my other site at http://sbno.illicitohio.com/
