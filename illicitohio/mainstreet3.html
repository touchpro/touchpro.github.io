The pictures in the first row show the weird thing... I don't believe in weird things, but that was a little freaky. Basically the room was void of 99% of light. The digital camera has a LCD display, so I snap the picture in to total darkness, look down at the display, and there is some crazy white see through thing on the picture. I take another one just to light the place up, and I see it again. I take a third... it's not there. I got the hell out of there just in case. I went home and spit soup everywhere while my head spun around... then slept it off.
