Upper Floors

We only visited the 8th and 10th floors extensivly on this trip. There were lots of dead birds throughout the tenth floor. We found some toothpaste in a cabnet on the 8th floor. There were a few bathrooms, but from what we could tell the rooms were set up a suites, with one bathroom for more than one room. There were also "shut off the light killowatt" stickers above every light switch. I'm guessing these are from the days the Seneca spent as the EPA building.
