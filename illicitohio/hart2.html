The Basement(2)

We found this giant breaker panel in the basement. I'm assuming there is no electric going to the building, but strangely enough I later noticed an electric meter with a dim amber light glowing inside. Maybe we should have turned these on and got this place jumping!


There were various other similar but smaller panels around the building. I'm guessing from the hip paneling behind this one that it came slightly after the one pictured above. I wonder who their designer was?


We found this long hallway on what seemed to be the East Side of the building. We thought we were really on to something. We walked through about 2 inches of water and came out in a mysterious spot about 10 damn feet from where we started. Yet again I get wet feet!



Here is one more elevator. This one had doors on both ends. The picture on the left is the outside and the one on the left is the inside of the shaft. We tried to get these doors open, but to no avail. I took the picture on the right by holding my camera around the door that we pried as far as possible.



This bathroom was still pretty clean. Its probably cleaner than my own in fact. I like what they've done with the lighting here too! The picture on the right is the hallway that led away from the elevators pictured above.
