Illicit Ohio


Columbus, like most cities, has an abundance of abandoned buildings and other various ruins. I have toured a few and have many more on my agenda. Through the years various warehouses, schools, hotels, and even a prison have come my way. I have passed up opportunities that I now regret. These include the now remodeled Smith Brothers office building, and the Columbus Auto Parts site. I was however, fortunate enough to make it into the prison, and various other places. My journeys will be feature here for you to see. These include not only buildings, but drainage systems, and more. Please be patient as time is a rarity. Updates come slowly these days. I have a lot of old stuff I haven't put up. Someday I hope to have it here... in the mean time, Enjoy!
