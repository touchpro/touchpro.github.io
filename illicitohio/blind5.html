The Columbus School for the Blind

The Writing's on the Wall

The writing was on the wall! The very upper rooms, right before the roof, held all kinds of great graphiti from as far back as the 1890s. I don't mean great as in good pictures, but great because of their age, I mean c'mon, let's remember where we are here!


The writing to the left is a message from the people who are remodeling the bottom floors as we speak (or actually, as you read). The message states that the upper floors will remain untouched. This made me happy to think that all of this would stay for years to come! YEAAAAH!



I do believe that the writing is as old as it suggests. The walls were just plaster, and hadn't been painted. The names all sounded old, and there were dates ranging from the 1890s to now. It all looked authentic, so if you did it about 1 week before we went in, good work! These two pictures are hard to see, but the writing was old looking. Kids were dirty little bastards back then.


The new stuff


You are reading that picture to the right correctly. That says, "Public Safety Hall of Fame". That would be an interesting one to see. Unfortunately all of the pictures were taken down. This is obvoiusly from the Highway Patroll days. This hallway was right inside the main entrance.


This is a light in one of the offices in the new section. I'm guessing this office belonged to someone important because it was much nicer than the others that we came across. The windows in these rooms were left open for some reason. It didn't really smell bad. Maybe they're removing asbestos.


Check out those floors and fireplace. I'm proud to say that I helped pay for that with all of those speeding tickets that I've gotten. I'm glad to see it goes to something good like hardwood floors instead of say ROADS! Oh well, it was cool for us to see in the end.


The Basement


This is a picture from the crazy basement. The basement had all of these strange pathways leading all over it. The first time we were there we saw doorways at each end. The second time, somone had kicked one of them in. It wasn't us for the record. The last time I road by the School there was a sign that said "KEEP OUT, This means you!" on the door. I guess they know we were there. Well, that's it for now. Stay tuned for more!
