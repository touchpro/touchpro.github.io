The Maumee Bay Brewing Company

The building which houses Toledo's Maumee Bay Brewing Company was once called Oliver House. When it was built in 1859, Oliver House was a big deal in Toledo. Legend has it that Abraham Lincoln slept here. Later, injured soldiers from the Spanish-American War were tended to in the upstairs rooms.

One room in particular at the Oliver House is said to be haunted: the Pool Room. A ghost called "The Captain" has been seen here in full uniform. He also appears in a certain hallway. During renovation the ghosts were particularly active. And it turns out Oliver House was built on top of that old favorite, an Indian burial ground.

The Maumee Bay Brewing Company is located at 27 Broadway in Toledo, (419) 241-1253.
