Convoy Heller House



The house that occupies the corner of Convoy Heller and German Church Roads near Middlebury features a curious reversal of the usual family-murder scenario. Here a woman supposedly killed her husband, and it's his angry ghost which haunts the building. A light upstairs is notorious for switching on and off in alternating periods of five minutes. Harmless enough--but the ghost inside has also been reported to fling knives at trespassers.
