Under Columbus

An interesting specter is said to haunt the elaborate system of tunnels and drains under downtown Columbus. It appears dressed all in black, wearing a hood, and has reportedly spooked employees at Columbus businesses whose basements open on the tunnels. The Char Bar is one such business, although gaining access this way would require permission from the owner.
