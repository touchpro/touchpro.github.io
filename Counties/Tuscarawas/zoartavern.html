The Zoar Tavern and Inn



Yet another haunted house in historic Zoar, Ohio, is the Zoar Tavern and Inn. This extremely nice restaurant was built as house number 23, the residence of the original village doctor, Clement Breil. Today you can get a meal there and you can stay in one of their quaint guest rooms. Ghostly occurrences are regularly reported in all parts of the building.
