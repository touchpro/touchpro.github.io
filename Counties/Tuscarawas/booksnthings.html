Books 'n Things



Zoar bookstore Books 'n Things is haunted by the smell of coffee even when no coffee is brewing. The ghost, said to be that of a tinsmith who once lived in the building, tucks the owners into bed at night, and will cause rocking chairs to move back and forth in the bookstore. Not a bad ghost to have, really. Maybe they can train him to clean the bathroom and give massages.
