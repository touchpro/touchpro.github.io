The building which now houses the Wood County Historical Museum in Bowling Green was the County Poorhouse and Lunatic Asylum from 1900-1937. It is haunted by a lost old lady in pajamas, as well as the inmates from the asylum, who claw at barred windows and steel doors. Ghosts are frequently sighted by employees and patrons alike.
