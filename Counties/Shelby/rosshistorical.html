The William A. Ross Historical Center



The Shelby County Historical Society is housed in this former funeral home. Like many former funeral homes, a wide variety of ghosts haunt it. Mysterious footsteps are heard, and things tend to fall off the walls for no good reason.

Some of this might be exaggerated, however, because the Historical Society is fond of their ghost stories and likes to play them up. Some have called it overkill, though who am I to level such a charge? I'd still like to tour the place.
