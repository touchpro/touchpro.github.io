Circleville Firehouse



The 115-year-old firehouse on Franklin Street in Circleville is haunted by ghostly footsteps and a shadowy figure. They might belong to Chief J. M. Baer, the Circleville fire chief who dropped dead of a heart attack on duty in 1926.

I've received word that the Franklin Street firehouse was demolished in early 2001. Maybe the site it stood on is still haunted.
