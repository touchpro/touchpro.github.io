Harrod Cemetery

To understand why Harrod Cemetery is haunted, you have to know the story of Logan County's evil Hatchet Man, so click away and read his complete story. He murdered two wives and two children in just a few years, and now he stalks Township Road 56.

His burial spot, however, is at Harrod Cemetery, where the gravestone he shares with his murdered wife and kids is said to glow with a ghastly light on certain nights.
