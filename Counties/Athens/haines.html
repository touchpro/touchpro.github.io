Haines Cemetery



Haines Cemetery in New Marshfield is haunted by the ghost of an insane Civil War soldier who killed his family with an axe. His homicidal spirit stalks the cemetery and anybody unlucky enough to stop there at night. The cemetery is located on Luhrig Road.
