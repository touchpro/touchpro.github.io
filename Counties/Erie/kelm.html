The Kelm House



The abandoned and heavily deteriorated Kelm House, located on Risden Road off Cherry Road outside Vermillion, is said by locals to be haunted. The ghost story is rather vague--the house is said to be cursed for some reason--but it's been enough for local teenagers to spraypaint the place with satanic symbols.

As it turns out, the Kelm House has been demolished. There's a shiny new place where it once stood. Maybe the new house is haunted too.
