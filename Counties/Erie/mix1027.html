Mix 102.7 FM



Sandusky's radio stations WLEC, WCPZ, and WMJK are all housed in an old Quonset hut near the Cedar Point causeway. The building was originally an Army barracks, and today the employees are plagued by sightings of ghosts and poltergeist activity--lights going on and off, odd sounds, etc. Near the place where a piano used to sit, a certain ghost still shuffles cards. Tune in sometime and see if you hear anything in the static.
