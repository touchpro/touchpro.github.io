Hercules Engine Factory



Part of this Canton plant is extremely old and was used for a time as a military training facility. A certain stairwell which leads to the old training ground is haunted by a strange haze and moving orbs of light. The rest of the building is a scary place as well; cold spots, disembodied voices, and misty apparitions pop up from time to time. People often feel a cold hand on their shoulder but turn to find no one there.
