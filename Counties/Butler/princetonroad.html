Princeton Road



Princeton Road near Hamilton is a very haunted stretch of road. A woman stands on the side of the road on certain nights, warning motorists of danger at the site where she wrecked and died. If you stop for her, she will hitch a ride.

Also, at Maud-Hughes Road, there is the Screaming Bridge, named for the ghostly screams of the people who have died there. Balls of light have been seen on the Screaming Bridge, along with a phantom caboose.
