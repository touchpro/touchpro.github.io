Ohio's Creepiest Cemeteries


We say that the hour of death cannot be forecast, but when we say this we imagine that hour as placed in an obscure and distant future. It never occurs to us that it has any connection with the day already begun or that death could arrive this same afternoon, this afternoon which is so certain and which has every hour filled in advance.
--Marcel Proust



A look at some of Ohio's scariest final resting places--the cemeteries which dot the entire state. There aren't many places where you can so readily find a link to the distant past. Many of the oldest cemeteries in Ohio are still around. And more than a few are haunted, as well.
