A Haunted House in Lorain County


First-person account of a haunting in Lorain County, Ohio; submitted by Dan F.

We are changing my brother's room around and it seems that the ghost in our house doesn't like change.Something keeps knocking on his door from the inside of his room while we are in the living room watching tv.My brother gets up to check it out and it stops knocking.Something similar happened when we took off posters in his room.We heard someone making weird sounds before,like a mentally ill teenager. We've seen dolls in our Mom's china cabinet looking at us.I've seen a green light travel through our living room.My brother's speakers have been thrown across the room.Doors have opened and closed by themselves.

I help my aunt clean a church somewhere in lorain county(where i live).It's definetly haunted.We've heard whistling and loud thumping noises.I was at the top floor of the church while my aunt was on the elevater and I heard sucking noises near my ears.

Your site is great.I really liked the story on gore orphaneage.I live about 20 minutes away from it.My friends Mom has a friend that lives down the street from it.

I think it would be great that you entered my story into your site. Even though I am fascinated with ghosts, the one in our house has scared us. We even have a cousin that comes & stays with us from time to time, and he has even heard and seen things. For the longest of time, we could hear heavy breathing in our house, real close to us. That scared us. My Mom even went to a fortune teller (which was supposed to be just for fun). It turns out that when my Mom went to her, she told my Mom that our house was haunted and that my little sister (who was 3 years old at the time) was very scared of the spirits here and that we had to burn sandalwood incense all through our house. My Mom just laughed about it, until one day my sister told my Mom that she saw 3 things come up the stairs. She thought they were people in black hoods and you couldn't see their faces, and they carried candles up the stairs and disappeared. We all went around burning sandalwood after that. We still hear things every once in a while, but we don't see or hear anything too scary like that anymore.
