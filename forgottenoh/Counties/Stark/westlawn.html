West Lawn Cemetery



This very creepy cemetery is said to contain a stone with the actual name FRANKENSTEIN. The story goes that a man was buried alive in this grave, and that you can still hear him on still nights, clawing and screaming to be let out.

It also happens to contain the earthly remains of our nation's third assasinated Chief Executive, William McKinley, but despite his sudden and violent death he appears to be at peace.
