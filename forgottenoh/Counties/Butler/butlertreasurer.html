Butler County Treasurer's Office



The ghost of a former night watchman has made appearances at the Butler County Treasurer's Office, where he was killed in a robbery in the 1860's. The robbers hung his body from a rafter to make it look like suicide but had actually killed him with ether. In 1893 he appeared to a county employee by making the clock chime 21 times and materializing on the face. Maybe he still hangs around.
