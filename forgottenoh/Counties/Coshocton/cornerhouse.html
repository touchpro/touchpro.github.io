The Corner House

In Coshocton County, an abandoned house at the intersection of State Routes 541 and 79 is supposedly haunted by the ghost of a man who came home, found that his wife had not made him dinner, and cut off her head and put it in the oven. Who can blame him, really?

Today "hot drafts" are felt in the vicinity of the house. Screams are heard, and you can sometimes smell burning flesh.
