Secrest Auditorium



Zanesville's Secrest Auditorium is haunted by a ghost who may be Mr. Secrest himself. He's often seen in the balcony area, sometimes walking up the stairs, and occasionally carrying a ladder. There is some doubt about the ghost's true identity, however, because Mr. Secrest died in 1993, and the auditorium was said to be haunted before then.
