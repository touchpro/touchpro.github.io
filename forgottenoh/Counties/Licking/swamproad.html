Swamp Road



A mystery girl in white crosses the road at a particular spot in Licking County, near Pataskala. She is called Swamp Road Sally by locals, though no one knows whether her name is really Sally or something else. What they do know is that she appears quite often, crossing Swamp Road, usually around midnight, in a rural place where she may have died in a car accident, though her origins and motives remain unknown.
