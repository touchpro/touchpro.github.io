Horseback Knob



Ross County's Black Run Valley is a mysterious area with plenty of local legends. One involves the sounds of a ghostly infant which can be heard in the woods of the region known as Horseback Knob. Supposedly, a pioneer family buried their dead baby here while travelling through, and its spirit has been restless ever since. The baby's solitary gravestone stands in the middle of the woods nearby.
