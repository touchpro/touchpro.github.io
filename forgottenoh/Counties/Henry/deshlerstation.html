Deshler Station

The very old Deshler Station bar and restaurant in tiny Deshler, Ohio, is plagued by ghostly occurrences. Built at the turn of the twentieth century, the building was used as a hotel under various names until 1976. These days patrons and employees report the same unexplainable things: cold spots, odd drafts of air, footsteps on the vacant second floor. The ghosts of two old men are sometimes seen seated at the bar after hours.
