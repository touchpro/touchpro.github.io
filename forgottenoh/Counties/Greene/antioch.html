Antioch College

Antioch is a small liberal arts college located in northern Greene County, near Springfield. It has been around since 1853, which means it's had a century and a half to accumulate ghost stories. The ones I know anything about are listed below.




G. Stanley Hall
A former college building which is now boarded up, this 1860s mansion is supposed to be haunted by weird shapes and optical distortions which float through its halls.

Glen Helen Nature Preserve
Indians and the original Helen after whom the preserve was named haunt this "living laboratory" located in the woods near campus.
