Joy Cemetery

Joy Cemetery (ironic name, huh?) in Ottawa Hills is supposed to be haunted by a lake-boat captain who stumps around looking for his missing wooden leg. If you do see him he shouldn't be too hard to run away from.

I tried to go visit the captain, but couldn't find Joy Cemetery in Ottawa Hills. There is a huge cemetery there, but Joy isn't it's name. Since then I've gotten directions and hope to head back up there sometime.
