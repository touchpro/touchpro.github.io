A never-complete compendium of haunted locales in the state of Ohio. When you click on a county you'll go to that county's page, and a little red ball---- next to the name of a place indicates that I have visited it. Clicking on it will take you to a full investigation page. Otherwise, there will just be a description. This list is highly dependent on Chris Woodyard's Haunted Ohio series, which is a great thing for our state to have, as well as numerous helpful contributors. I have omitted most private residences from the list, since there are thousands of supposedly haunted houses in the state and I think it's only fair to offer places which you stand a chance of being able to visit. Click on a county to get started. And remember to e-mail me if you do visit any of these sites, or if you know of any I don't have on the list.
