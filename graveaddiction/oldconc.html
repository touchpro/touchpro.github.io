Old Concord Cemetery, also known as Concord United Methodist Cemetery, is located near the intersection of Hoover Road and London-Groveport Road in Grove City, Ohio (Jackson Township, Franklin County). It is located east of Concord United Methodist Church.

Old Concord Cemetery is no longer in use...however, the East Concord and West Concord Cemeteries located just to the south are still in use today. The cemetery is very well taken care of, but there are many broken gravestones. It does appear that many of the gravestones have recently been repaired/restored.
