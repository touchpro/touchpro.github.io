Ban a Spirit?

April 6, 2008



On April 6, 2008 I received an e-mail from Janine about an interesting ouija board experience:


We just started using the Ouija. We read through all the tips on your site, and took all the necessary precautions to do it the right way.

We have a spirit bothering us, Guss. The first time we spoke to him, he told us to die. The second time he just jumped on without being summoned and told us that the Dark Lord wants us, and that we must stay on till 3 AM. The third time we used it, he just jumped on and started counting from 1 to 5. By then I realized what was happening and said goodbye, and the planchette moved to goodbye too.

The other spirit we talk to, Ryan (who is very friendly), tells us he is a dork and pretends to be evil. Ryan also told us that Guss does it because of hate. I asked Ryan if Guss is trying to hurt us, and Ryan replied no. I asked if we should fear him, and Ryan said no.

As much as I like talking to Ryan, I do not trust him yet, and I don't know whether he is telling the truth or not. Guss is really worrying us, and I would like to know if you have any advice, or if there is any way that we can ban him from using the board.

Would really appreciate any help.


If you have any advice for Janine, please send me an e-mail and I'll forward your advice on to her.
