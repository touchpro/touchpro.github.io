Funny Story

August 6, 2007



On August 6, 2007 I received an e-mail from Becki F. about an interesting ouija board experience:


One time my friend Heather and I were in my room, at midnight, with candles lit and a glowing ouija board. It was storming outside, so we were all thinking it's the perfect scenery! :D We were running out of questions, so I decided to ask it about my future.

"Oh great Ouija Board! What college will I go to?" The Ouija board didn't move, so I said, "Say no if you don't know."

It moved to No. I glanced at my friend, and she shrugged, then decided to ask it the same question. She did, and it moved to spell out one of the in-state colleges. Heather grinned at me, and I got mad at the ouija board for answering her question and not mine. So I asked it again.

"Oh great Ouija Board! What college will I go to?"

It didn't move, so I glared at it, "Say no if you don't know."

It moved to No again.

By now I was getting fed up, "Oh great Ouija Board, do you know *anything*?"

It moved to Yes.

"Then tell me what college I'll be going to!" I ordered.

My friend snickered as it slowly started to move towards a letter. It spelled out F-U-C-K Y-O-U. By now my friend was cracking up, and I was forced to beg forgiveness to the Ouija Board before it would answer any of our questions. We taught it to say "Up Yours" instead of what it told me before, for sake of decency. So now, whenever it's Heather and I using the Ouija Board, it always says "Up Yours" if it gets mad at something we say. It's hilarious. :)
