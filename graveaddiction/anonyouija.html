Cursing Board

January 15, 2006



On January 15, 2006 I received an e-mail from an anonymous website visitor about an interesting ouija board experience:


"On Christmas of 2005 I got an ouija board and began to play with it. My mother played with me, and it was quite fun. But the next couple of days after we used it, I played again. First it cursed to my mom, spelling out her name...then the word! After that event, my buddy and I went into a different room to play and cleanse the board. We asked if the spirit guide was in the room with us and it replied "yes". So I took my camera and went crazy! In a picture of me opening the board on Christmas, millions of orbs surrounded me! Then about five days later, I played with my friend. When I took a picture of the board, there was a huge orange thing, kind of misty, in the right lower hand corner of the board. I just thought that it was the most amazing thing ever!!"
