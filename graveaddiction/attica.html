The Attica-Venice Township Cemetery is located on Lemmon Street, east of OH-4, in Attica, Ohio (Venice Township, Seneca County). The coordinates are 41° 4' 2.71" N, 82° 52' 52.71" W.

The Attica-Venice Township Cemetery is large, and it is still active. It is adjacent to Saints Peter and Paul Cemetery. There are many gravestones, including an unusually high number of white bronze monuments. These monuments are made of zinc, and were sold by salesmen. There must have been a very good salesman in this area! In addition to all of the white bronze, there are also several marble and even a few sandstone monuments with interesting carvings. The cemetery is very well maintained.
