Saint Johns Cemetery is located beside the Chief Blackhoof Memorial Park near the intersection of US-33 and OH-65 in Saint Johns, Ohio (Union Township, Auglaize County). The coordinates are 40° 33' 26.34" N, 84° 5' 1.93" W.

Saint Johns Cemetery is filled with old gravestones, and the majority are in great condition. However, there are a few that have been damaged. And although several of the damaged stones have been repaired, some of the repair jobs were a little messy. The cemetery grounds are very well maintained.
