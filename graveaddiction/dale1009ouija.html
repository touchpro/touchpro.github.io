My First Experience

October 30, 2009



On October 30, 2009 I received an e-mail from Dale about an interesting ouija board experience:


So I bought my ouija board earlier in the week, probably Tuesday, but I didn't get to try it the first night. I waited until the next day to go over to a friends house and try it for the first time, but with no results. Then yesterday which was 29 Oct 2009, we got movement and a response. Here's what we got:

Me: Are there any spirits who wish to speak with us?
Board: Yes

Me: Are you a good spirit?
Board: N/A

Me: Can you tell us your name?
Board: Yes

Me: Will you spell it out for us?
Board: just went to random letters

Me: Can you tell me my name?
Board: couldn't spell my name right

Me: Can you spell the person's name sitting in the front left of me?
Board: Kristian (his real name is spelled Christian)

Me: Can you tell me how old I am?
Board: 20 (which is right)

Christian: Can you tell me my age too?
Board: 23 (he turns 23 in a week)

Me: Do you like me more then Christian?
Board: yes

Me: Does Christian bother you?
Board: no

Me: Does Wade bother you? He has been kind of offensive.
Board: no

Me: Can you tell us when you died?
Board: 2006

Me: Are there any other spirits here with you?
Board: yes

Me: How many?
Board: 7

Me: Are any of them bad?
Board: Yes

Me: How many of them are bad?
Board: 3

Me: Do they want to harm us?
Board: No

There was a little more to it; I just left some of it out, but it was a good experience with only my 2nd attempt with the board. Still sorta unsure of how it happened or if it was just the other person moving it, but it was defenitely a neat experience.
