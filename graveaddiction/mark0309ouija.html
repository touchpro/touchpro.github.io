String of Predictions

March 23, 2009



On March 23, 2009 I received an e-mail from Mark H. about an interesting ouija board experience:


I did a ouija board session alone back in 1986. I have had no experience of nasty goings-on, but had a string of predictions into the future, all of which became true.

The board told me someone called Jonathan was going to die, and a few days later, someone at school called that was run over and killed. The board told me I would marry a foreigner. I am married to an American (I'm English).

In 1994, I had constant nightmares of a girl being murdered at my former senior school. They lasted for a week, until the murder actually happened.

But what concerns me is that, after a major operation in 2004, I found I can sometimes predict the future correctly. I sit and think of a question and the answer comes to me. Was I possessed back in 1986?


If you have any advice/comments for Mark, please send me an e-mail and I will send it on to him.
