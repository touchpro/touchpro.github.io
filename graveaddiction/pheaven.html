The Pet Heaven Cemetery is located on Main Street (US-40), just a few miles east of Reynoldsburg.

This was my first trip to a pet cemetery. I was amazed at how many gravestones there were...we always buried our deceased pets in my parents' backyard! When my little sister and I were little, we would make them little cross headstones out of twigs and even hold memorial services for them :) Anyway, some of the headstones in this pet cemetery were bigger and more ornate than some headstones I've seen for people...which is a little sad to me. I really enjoyed reading the inscriptions though, it was obvious that people put a lot of thought into them!
