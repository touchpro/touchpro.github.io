Talking with a Spirit Named John

October 14, 2004



On October 14, 2004 I received an e-mail from Kelly Hutchinson from Manchester, Kentucky. She shared a few of her interesting ouija board experiences with me to post on the site. This story is about a discussion she and her friends Ricky and Nathen had with a spirit named John:


Kelly: Is there anyone here that wishes to talk to us?
Board: Yes

Ricky: Have we talked to you before?
Board: No

Nathen: Who do you wish to talk to?
Board: Girl

Kelly: Me? What do you wish to tell me?
Board: Careful. Evil

Ricky: She is evil?
Board: No

Kelly: Something evil will happen to me?
Board: Yes. Careful

Nathen: Are there evil spirits here?
Board: Yes

Kelly: Is it them that want to harm me?
Board: No. Heart (spelled out)

Ricky: Her heart will hurt?
Board: Yes. Safe (stoped after that)

Nathen: She is safe?
Board: No. Safe Haven

Kelly: We need to find a safe haven?
Board: You

Kelly: I need to find a safe haven?
Board: Yes. Hurt

Kelly: Why are you telling me this?
Board: Nice...Safe haven

Nathen: You think she is nice?
Board:between yes and no No hurt

Ricky: You are telling her because you've been hurt before and you dont want someone to feel that pain as well?
Board: Yes

Kelly: Who will hurt me?
Board: It then spelled out the name of the guy whom I like, which no one else knew about

Nathen: Do you know who that is?
Kelly: Yes...When will he hurt me?
Board: Soon

Kelly: He will break my heart?
Board: Yes

Ricky: Is there anything else you wish to tell us?
Board: circled then went to end
