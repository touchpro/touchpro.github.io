Eisenhour Cemetery is located on the east side of Bradner Road, not far from the dead end of Eisenhour Road, outside Pemberville, Ohio (Freedom Township, Wood County). The coordinates are 41° 23' 13.79" N, 83° 26' 6.71" W.

Eisenhour Cemetery was established in 1841, and it's still active today. There are many rows of old gravestones, and very few are broken. The cemetery is very well taken care of by the township.

Many thanks to Mike McMaster for taking me to this cemetery!
