Saint Joseph's Catholic Cemetery is located on North Court Street, about one mile north of US-22, in Circleville, Ohio (Circleville Township, Pickaway County). The coordinates are 39° 37' 4.94" N, 82° 56' 38.06" W.

Saint Joseph's Catholic Cemetery is still active. It is located next to Forest Cemetery. There are many old gravestones, and almost all of them are in great condition; very few have been damaged. The oldest stones are located at the back. The church does an excellent job of maintaining the cemetery.

Many thanks to Joe Szablewski for telling me about this cemetery!
