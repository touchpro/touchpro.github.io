I Love You

April 5, 2007



On April 5, 2007 I received an e-mail from Travis V. about his interesting ouija board experience:


One night my mom's friend Julie came over to see my new Pathfinder ouija board. She wanted to try it, so we did. By the way, it worked. So we said a certin prayer that was listed on the instruction list: "Let the power of the white light protect us." We asked if her daughter was there because we lost her when she was two. It moved to yes. We asked if she was with Papa (grandpa). She moved to yes. So she's happy, and she said I miss you. We sighed, then she said hi personally to me and said, "I remember you." I said I know, I used to play with you when I was four. I wanted to sing her favourite song: Barney's "I Love You," but she said she had to go and we said rest in peace, Goodbye.
