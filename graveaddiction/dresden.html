Dresden Cemetery is located off Main Street in Dresden, Ohio (Jefferson Township, Muskingum County). The coordinates are 40° 7' 3.46" N, 82° 0' 25.84" W.

Dresden Cemetery is a rather large cemetery that is still in use today. There is a very nice Veterans' Memorial at the front of the cemetery. There is also a small memorial in the cemetery dedicated to all of the people whose gravestones or names have been lost through time. I've never seen a memorial like this, and I thought it was a fantastic idea!

Dresden Cemetery is extremely well taken care of. There are a few broken/fallen gravestones, but not many. It's definitely a very interesting cemetery to visit!

For more information about Dresden Cemetery, I'd recommend visiting the Unofficial Dresden Cemetery website.
