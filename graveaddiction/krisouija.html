Big Black Shadow

April 13, 2005



On April 13, 2005 I received an e-mail from fourteen year old Kris Trexler in Philadelphia, Pennsylvania about her extremely creepy ouija board experience:


"Ok, it was about 1AM and my best friend Kim was spending the night. We were bored so I said want to play with a Ouija board and she said ok. So we went downstairs, sat on the sofa, and lit 10 candles around the table. We started playing.....

Kris: Anyone with us tonight?
Board: Yes.

Kris: Who are you?
Board: Robert

Kris: Ok. Robert when did you die?
Board: 1971 Basement

Kim: Ok, How did you die?
Board: Wrists

Kim: You slit your wrists? Why?
Board: Lonely

Kris: Oh ok! Are you good or evil
Board: Both

Kris: Ok why are you here?
Board: To help you kris

Kris: To help me why?
Board: to guide you the right way

Kris: What about Kim?
Board: No MOVEMENT....5 min later
Board: He's comming (spelled very fast)

Kris: Who's coming
Board: Satan (spelled very fast)

Kim: Are you sure?
Board: Very

Kim: Are you still here?
Board: Get out he's coming

**Ran into dinning room**

A few seconds later we saw a big black shadow by the board and it went towards the door!!! Two candles blew out!!

We didnt touch the game until the next morning"
