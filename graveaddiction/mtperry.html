The Mount Perry Cemetery Association is located at the end of Church Street, just northwest of OH-204, in Mount Perry, Ohio (Madison Township, Perry County). The coordinates are 39° 52' 53.25" N, 82° 13' 14.56" W.

The Mount Perry Cemetery Association is still active. It sits between the United Presbyterian Church and the Methodist Church. There are many old gravestones, and most are in great condition. Only a few gravestones have been damaged. The cemetery is very well maintained.
