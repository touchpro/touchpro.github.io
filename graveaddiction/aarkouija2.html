Moving Off The Table

April 24, 2005



On April 24, 2005 I received another e-mail from Aaron about another interesting ouija board experience he and his friends had:


This conversation was done today with Kristopher and I. Our younger brother Ryan recorded but also filled in for the last two questions. We get gibberish from spirits a lot as this conversation shows...

Aaron: Is there anyone here?
Spirit: *yes*

Aaron: What is your name?
Spirit: c g foc
(It moved off the board and we had forgotten that means *end of answer*. We thought it had left.)

Aaron: Is c g foc there?
Spirit: *no*

Aaron: Who is this?
Spirit: nocod1d *yes*
(At this point, we remembered that when it moves off the board, the Spirit is done answering)

Aaron: Is there anyone there who would like to talk to us?
Spirit: *yes*

Aaron: What is your name?
Spirit: nocod1d

Aaron: Is nocod1d there?
Spirit: *yes*

Aaron: Where are you from?
Spirit: frfo nc

Kristopher: Are you from North Carolina?
Spirit: *yes*

Aaron: When were you born?
Spirit: 1634 (Note that not even the United States existed in 1634.)

Aaron: Are you male or female?
Spirit: mbocqabci

Aaron: Are you good or evil?
Spirit: *yes*

Aaron: Point to the sun if you're good and the moon if you're evil.
Spirit: *sun*

Kristopher: Do you speak English?
Spirit: *yes*

Aaron: If there's anything important you'd like to tell us, please tell us now.
Spirit: ciahbjll

Aaron: What language was that in?
Spirit: (moves off the table)

Aaron: Are you still living?
Spirit: *yes*

Aaron: Is your body still living?
Spirit: *yes*

Aaron: How so?
Spirit: endvencuncin(i/j/*space*--we couldn't tell which)

Aaron: Do you speak any other languages?
Spirit: (At this point, it moved off the board and across the table and headed toward the Ouija board box. We kept moving the box away until the box wasn't on the table anymore and the planchette moved off the table.)

Aaron: What religion are you?
Spirit: agnovstc (It probably meant agnostic.)

Aaron: Why do you keep moving off the table?
Spirit: cofo(a/space--We couldn't tell which it meant)

Aaron: What is cofoa?
Spirit: ahaboa

Aaron: What is ahaboa?
Spirit: clav *yes* *picture of the woman* (There is a picture of a man and a woman playing on both the lower left and right corners.)

Aaron: Is our conversation over?
Spirit: *no*
Aaron: What else would you like to tell us?
Spirit: l *goodbye*
