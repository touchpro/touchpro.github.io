Spring Hill Cemetery is located on the south side of OH-119 in Fort Recovery, Ohio (Recovery Township, Mercer County). The coordinates are 40° 24' 49.5" N, 84° 47' 51.33" W.

Spring Hill Cemetery is large, and it is still active. The oldest gravestones are located on the east side of the cemetery, and most are in excellent condition. The grounds are very well maintained.
