Philipps Cemetery is located on the west side of Phillips Road, just north of Cambria Road, outside Granville, Ohio (Granville Township, Licking County). The coordinates are 40° 6' 42.57" N, 82° 29' 9.47" W.

Philipps Cemetery is small, and it is no longer active. There are many old gravestones, and many have beautiful, detailed carvings. Although some gravestones have been damaged, several have recently been restored. The township does a fantastic job of maintaining the cemetery grounds.
