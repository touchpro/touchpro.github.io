Stelvideo Cemetery is located at the intersection of OH-121 and Arcanum-Bearsmill Road outside Stelvideo, Ohio (Richland Township, Darke County). The coordinates are 40° 9' 55.51" N, 84° 33' 5.36" W.

Stelvideo Cemetery, also known as the New Coppess Cemetery, is still active. The oldest gravestones are located closest to OH-121, and most are in good condition. There are also several interesting homemade markers. The grounds are very well maintained.
