May 16, 2003 (inside)

Beth, Christian, John, Karen, and Ryan



On May 16, 2003 Christian, John, Karen, Ryan, and myself used the Ouija board at my great grandparents'/great uncle's old abandoned house. Don, Jim, Katy, and Nancy watched. The session was ended a little abruptly after 21 minutes and 59 seconds, read on and you'll find out why...

Beth: Are there any spirits in the room with us?
Board: Yes

Beth: Can you tell us who it is?
Board: Brigit

John: Do we know who you are?
Board: No

Ryan: Do you know anyone who we know?
Board: Harold

Beth: What year were you here?
Board: Never

Karen: Brigit, are you a spirit guide?
Board: No (At this point Jim told us that he read some letters from a lady named Brigit to Harold when he was helping clean out the house)

Karen: Brigit, are you from Long Island New York?
Board: Yes

Karen: Brigit, did you know Harold?
Board: Yes

Beth: Were you his friend?
Board: Yes

Beth: Is that why you're visiting tonight, because you're friends with Harold?
Board: Yes

John: Is there something you want to tell us Brigit?
Board: No

Christian: Thank you Brigit.
Board: Voice

Christian: Who's voice?
Board: Voice

Karen: Brigit, did you want to talk to me?
Board: No

Christian: Brigit, who are you trying to speak with?
Board: Christian

Christian: What do you want to tell me Brigit?
Board: Strong

John: Do you think he has a stong voice?
Board: Yes

Christian: Brigit, is Alexander there with you?
Board: Yes

Christian: Does Alexander want to tell me anything?
Board: Yes

Christian: What does Alexander want to tell me?
Board: GA (planchette starts to circle)

Beth: Do you want to try spelling it again?
Board: FPNG

Christian: Do I know what that is Brigit?
Board: No

Christian: Can you give me another clue?
Board: DRF

Christian: Brigit, are those initials or are you trying to spell a word?
Board: Trying

Christian: You're trying to spell something for me, correct?
Board: Yes

Christian: Brigit, is this message for me?
Board: Yes

Christian: Is this message from you or Alexander? If it's from you it would be yes.
Board: No

Ryan: Is it an important message?
Board: Yes

Beth: Want to try one more time?
Board: Job

Christian: Is this something to happen?
Board: Need One

John: Does Christian need a job?
Board: Yes

Christian: But Brigit, why are you telling me that? Is it really from you or from Alexander?
Board: Me

Christian: Is there another message for me?
Board: No

Beth: Is Harold here?
Board: Yes

Beth: Harold, where did the tombstone come from? Did you find it or was it already here?
Board: Here

Beth: Was it originally here or did someone bring it here?
Board: Yes

Beth: Is the body buried here?
Board: No

Beth: Can you tell us where it's buried, you know, maybe a city?
Board: Thurston

Beth: Does the grave have a new marker? A new marker where the body is?
Board: No

Beth: Why is it here, is it family?
Board: Yes

Beth: Can you give us a name?
Board: Akt (we weren't sure if it was a "k" or a "l")

Beth: I'm sorry, could you try spelling it one more time?
Board: Alt

John: Do you know the first name?
Board: No

Christian: Was it a baby when it died?
Board: Yes

Beth: I can only see months and days on the tombstone. Should there be a year in front of it?
Board: Yes

Beth: Can you tell me how old the person was?
Board: 1

John: One year old?
Board: Yes

John: Is there anything else you want to tell us?
Board: No

Beth: Is there anyone else who wants to talk to us?
Board: Yes

Beth: Who?
Board: Maude

Beth: Will you tell us what you wanted to say?
Board: Leave

John: Are we in trouble?
Board: No

John: Can you tell us why we should leave?
Board: Not safe

Beth: What's not safe?
John: Is it the building?
Board: Yes

Beth: Is it the structure?
Board: No

John: Is it spirits?
Board: Yes

With that said (or I guess I should say spelled!), everyone ran outside!!
