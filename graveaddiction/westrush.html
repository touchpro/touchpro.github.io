West Rushville Cemetery is located on the south side of Rushville Road in West Rushville, Ohio (Richland Township, Fairfield County). The coordinates are 39° 45' 51.61" N, 82° 26' 34.2" W.

West Rushville Cemetery is large, and it is still active. The oldest gravestones are located on the west side, and most are in excellent condition. Many contain beautiful carvings by John Strickler, a local stonecarver. The cemetery is very well maintained.
