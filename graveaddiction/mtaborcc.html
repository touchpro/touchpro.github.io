Mount Tabor Cemetery is located on the east side of OH-245, just south of OH-507, outside West Liberty, Ohio (Salem Township, Champaign County). The coordinates are 40° 9' 43.07" N, 83° 43' 37.91" W.

Mount Tabor Cemetery is located on both sides and behind the old Mount Tabor Church. There are many old gravestones, and almost all of them are in excellent condition. In addition to the large zinc monuments mentioned on the Ohio historical marker, there are also two interesting cast iron sarcophagus tombs near the back. The cemetery is very well maintained by the township.

Many thanks to Kristine Cook for telling me about this cemetery, and for giving me terrific directions to it.
