The Davis Farm Cemetery is located on the west side of US-35, south of Cain Road, outside Jackson, Ohio (Jackson Township, Jackson County). The coordinates are 39° 9' 51.62" N, 82° 44' 12.85" W.

The Davis Farm Cemetery is a small family plot that is no longer active. Even though most of the seventeen gravestones are older, all are in great condition. I'm not sure who maintains the cemetery, but they do a terrific job!
