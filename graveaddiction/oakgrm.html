Oak Grove Cemetery is located on the west side of Ludlow Road, between Kanagy Road and Sibley Road, outside Kingscreek, Ohio (Salem Township, Champaign County). The coordinates are 40° 12' 0.68" N, 83° 41' 55.16" W.

Oak Grove Cemetery, also known as Hooley Cemetery or Mennonite Cemetery, was established in 1856 and is still active. There are many old gravestones and most are in good condition. The cemetery is very well maintained.
