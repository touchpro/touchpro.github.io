Weird Name

June 8, 2008



On June 8, 2008 I received an e-mail from Antonio about an interesting ouija board experience:


One night my mom went out. I was only eleven, and I wanted a ouija board so bad. I asked my mom, "Hey, can I get a glow in the dark ouija board?" She said that she had played one before, and she had asked who her sister was going to marry. It said TRUCK DRIVER. Low and behold, ten years later she married one. So anyway my mom agreed, and like two hours later I got a ouija board.

The next day we played for an hour or so. I met a spirt named badakada...weird name, right? Well, he was my brother in the year 141, or even maybe 1414...I could not understand him. I said goodbye...Oh yeah, this was with my mother. He said NO, and I asked if he liked us. He said NO, so I said goodbye, flipped it over, and put it away. Now I might go back and ask why he did not like us, but right now I'm too scared.

THIS IS A TRUE STORY!
