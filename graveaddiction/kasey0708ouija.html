Something Grabbed my Ankle

July 28, 2008



On July 28, 2008 I received an e-mail from Kasey Dixon about an interesting ouija board experience:


A few months ago at my slumber party, two of my friends stayed the night. Two weeks before my sleepover, my mom bought me a ouija board. I had been looking them up on the web, and I thought it looked fun. So it was about 1:00 in the morning,and my two friends and I decided to play the ouija board. We started out by asking if any spirits were there. The board replied yes, and we began asking questions. Then one of my friends asked a question that seemed to upset the spirit. We heard scratching on my bedroom walls, and my friends kept begging me to turn the lights on. I agreed, so I got up off the bed, and I tripped over some birthday presents. I was about to get up when something grabbed my ankle. I was screaming, then I finally got up and ran to turn on the light. We all were freaking out, that night was creepy! So be careful and watch what you ask it.
