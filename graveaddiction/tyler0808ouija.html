Loaded with Spirits

August 5, 2008



On August 5, 2008 I received an e-mail from Tyler K. about an interesting ouija board experience:


Me and a couple of my buds made an ouija board and had NO problem with it working because his house is loaded with spirits...five in every room we were in. It was good for a while, then things turned bad. Here's our story, and if anyone knows how to get rid of this please let me know!

We said a prayer and lit four white candles and burned some incense...we also layed down silver.

Us: Are there any spirits present in this room?
Board: slowly goes to yes

Us: Do you want to talk to us?
Board: Yes

Us: What is your name?
Board: Bruce

Us: Are you from the sun or moon?
Board: goes to sun

Us: Do you mind us asking how you died?
Board: Fire

Us: You were burned in a fire?
Board: Yes

Us: Where was the fire?
Board: Wisconsin

Us: So you lived in Wisconsin?
Board: Yes.

Us: What year did you die?
Board: 1856

Us: Thank you for talking to us, Bruce. Is Nick's spirit guide here with us?
Board: Yes

Us: Hello, is this Nick's spirit guide?
Board: Yes

Us: What is your name?
Board: L.T.

Us: Are you from the sun or moon?
Board: goes to sun

Us: Are those initials?
Board: Ya

Us: Thank you L.T. Is Dustin's spirit guide here with us too?
Board: Yes

Us: Hello, is this Dustin's spirit guide?
Board: Yup

Us: Are you from the sun or moon?
Board: goes to moon

Us: What is your name?
Board: David

Us: Where are you from, David?
Board: South

Us: Where in the south?
Board: Somewhere ha

Us: What year did you die, David?
Board: 1903

Us: How did you die, David?
Board: Kittens

Us(laughing): What color were they?
Board: Purple

Us: David, do you like to be funny?
David: Indeed

Us: Thank you, David. Is Tyler's spirit guide here?
Board: Yes

Us: Is this now Tyler's spirit guide?
Board: Yes

Us: What is your name?
Board: Wang

Us: Your name is Wang??
Board: Yes

Us: Where are you from?
Board: Korea

Us: Will you tell us when you died?
Board: No

Us: Okay, we are sorry.

Us: How did you die?
Board: Grudge

Us: You had a grudge with someone?
Board: Wife

Us: What happened with your wife?
Board: Nothing

Us: Do you like our board?
Board: Yes

Us: Do you like the letters?
Board: No, stupid

Us: You think the letters are stupid?
Board: Yes

Us: Is the person who made them stupid?
Board: Gay

Us: Is there anyone else who wants to talk to us?
Board: Vance

Us: Has Vance ever contacted Katie Conrad?
Board: Bitch

Us: Is this still Wang?
Board: Yes

Me: What is my house number?
Board: 173 (correct)

Us: Good job, Wang.

Us: Is there anyone else who wants to talk to us?
Board: Yes

Us: Is this the new spirit?
Board: Yes

Us: Are you from the sun or moon?
Board: moves quickly to moon

Us: What is your name?
Board: Bad

Us: We do not want to talk to you. Goodbye.
Board: Never

We then said goodbye and moved the planchette to goodbye. We also flipped the board and planchette.

About twenty minutes later we went into a different room and used the board again.

Us: Are there any spirits from the sun who wish to talk to us?
Board: No, Goodbye

We tried again in about ten minutes.

Us: Do the animals in this room bother you?
Board: No

Us: Are you from the sun or moon?
Board: goes quickly to moon

Us: We don't want to talk to you, goodbye.
Board: No

Us: What is your name then?
Board: Bad

Us: Do you like Jesus and God?
Board: No savior

Us: You do not like God?
Board: No

Us: What year did you die?
Board: 0

Us: Do you not want to tell us?
Board: No

Us: Okay, we respect that.
Planchette begins to make a figure 8, we immediately say goodbye and flip the board and planchette and turn on the lights.

Two of my friends go in to try to get this bad spirit to go away.

Them: Is this the spirit we were just taking to?
Board: Yes

Them: Are you mad?
Board: Very

Them: Please tell us your name.
Board: Vance

Them: Vance, you are from the moon?
Board: Yes

Them: Vance, may we please speak to David?
Board: For a bit

Them: Is this David?
Board: Yes

Them: How are you, David?
Board: Splendid

Them: That's good.
Board: Yes

Them: So tell us about the kittens.
Board: Claws

Them: They clawed you?
Board: Maybe

Darin, being stupid: Do you fight demons?
Board: Yes, Kill

Darin: Oh, so you fight demons?
Dustin: Wait, this isn't right...it's not supposed to spell kill. Is this still David?
Board: No

Them : Goodbye (as they flipped it over)

We then tried again and got Vance again first.

Us: Is this Vance?
Board: Yes

Us: Why do you keep talking to us?
Board: Fun

Us: It's fun talking to us?
Board: Yes

Us: Why are you a bad spirit?
Board: Sorrow

Us: We're sorry...can we please talk to Darin's spirit guide?
Board: Just a bit

Us: Is this Darin's spirit guide?
Board: Yes

Us: What is your name?
Board: Baron

Us: How did you die, Baron?
Board: Water

Us: You drowned?
Board: Yes

Us: Was it an accident?
Board: Beer

Us: You were drunk?
Board: Yes

Us: Do the other spirits in here like Vance?
Board: No

Us: Why?
Board: Goodbye

We decided to then just close the board for the night, and we all went to sleep. I forgot to mention that Vance said the silver on the board bothered him. Nick, just to be safe, wore a silver necklace. He woke up and said he was sweating all night and felt things he's never felt before that was making his back arch and his body twitch. We're thinking that it was Vance trying to take advantage of him, but the silver protected him. We're thinking that Vance has taken over the board...How can we get rid of him?


If you have any advice or comments for Tyler, please send me an e-mail and I'll pass them on to him.
