Linnville Cemetery is located at the intersection of US-40 and Linnville Road in Linnville, Ohio (Franklin Township, Licking County). The coordinates are 39° 57' 23.9" N, 82° 20' 52.49" W.

Linnville Cemetery is small, and it is no longer active. Many of the gravestones have been damaged over the years, but the cemetery grounds are very well maintained.
