His Name was Russel

June 17, 2008



On June 17, 2008 I received an e-mail from Emily about an interesting ouija board experience:


This was me and my friend Peyton's first ouija board experience. We had never played before. We started out by spinning the planchette around four times, while saying "ouija" four times as well.

Peyton: Is anyone there?
Board: Yes

Peyton: What is your name?
Board: Rus

Peyton: So, your name is Rus?
Board: No
Board: Russel

Peyton: Are you a good spirit or bad?
Board: Bad

Me: When did you die?
Board: 79

Peyton: So you died in 1979?
Board: No

Peyton: What year did you die?
Board: 79 min

Peyton: Can we take a break?
Board: Yes

-----------break---------

Came back.

Peyton: Is Russel still there?
Board: Yes

Peyton: Do you like Emily?
Board: No

Me: Do you like Peyton?
Board: That is up to her

Peyton: Is it alright if we say goodbye?
didn't answer after trying several times
Board: 79. Good bye.

We don't know what "79" means... if you have any thoughts, please let us know.


If you have any advice or information for Emily, please send me an e-mail and I'll forward it on to her.
