The Baltimore Community museum is located off State Route 256 (Market Street) in the small town of Baltimore, Ohio.

Even though I grew up in Baltimore, my visit on August 10, 2003 was only the second trip I had made to this museum. The first time was when I was in elementary school, and it has changed a lot since the late 1980's!

There are several exhibits in the museum, which include a lot of information about the Ohio-Erie Canal, covered bridges, farm equipment, town landmarks, and lots more.

The museum is opened on Sundays, Mother's Day through Labor Day, from 1PM - 5PM.
