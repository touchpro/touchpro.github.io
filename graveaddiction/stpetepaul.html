Saints Peter and Paul Cemetery is located on Lemmon Street, east of OH-4, in Attica, Ohio (Venice Township, Seneca County). The coordinates are 41° 4' 4.57" N, 82° 52' 48.3" W.

Saints Peter and Paul Cemetery is adjacent to the Attica-Venice Township Cemetery. It is still active. There are many gravestones, including an unusually high number of white bronze monuments. Most are in great condition. The cemetery is very well maintained.
