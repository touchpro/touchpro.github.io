Homemade Board

June 2, 2006



On June 2, 2006 I received another e-mail from Alicia about an interesting ouija board experience she had:


We couldn't find our Ouija board, so we decided to try and make one.

We used an old chess board upside down, and a CD for the planchette. It didn't work as well, but it still worked. However, I think it's cursed or something.

Session 5: Hell {figure 8}

Alicia and Carly at the board, Main speaker: Carly

Does anyone want to speak with us?
Board: HELL {It did a few figure eights around L}

At this point we said goodbye and turned the board over. I think I read somewhere that figure eights meant that evil was controlling the board.

We tried again, but it didn't seem to understand our questions and kept saying "NO" to everything we said, even if it wasn't a question. I thought making an Ouija board would be a good idea, but at this rate I might as well look harder for the manufactured one.
