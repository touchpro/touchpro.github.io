New Baby

June 24, 2007



On June 24, 2007 I received an e-mail from Rachael about an interesting ouija board experience:


My name's Rachael, and I'm 13 years old. I'm e-mailing today to tell you about a story that my mum told me about the Ouija, which happened when she was little.

My mother, 12 at the time, her best friend, and a couple of other friends were playing with an ouija board one night. This was my mum's first and last time - she dislikes them, and says they are very dangerous from the stories she has heard. So, unfortunately, I can never use one without going behind her back, although I am dying to. She said it was one of the most scariest experiences of her life. Luckily nothing bad happened though.

Anyways, my mum and her friends spoke to a man - she can't remember the name or age, or how he died, but they did ask. At the time my Auntie was pregnant with my older cousin. My mum was curious, so she asked if the baby would be a boy or girl. The board said that the baby would be a little girl. But it turned out to be a boy, and this just prooves that you should not trust EVERYTHING an ouija board says, because sometimes they can be wrong.
