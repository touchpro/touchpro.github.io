The New Carlisle Cemetery is located at the intersection of OH-235 and Musselman Road in New Carlisle, Ohio (Bethel Township, Clark County). The coordinates are 39° 55' 25.34" N, 84° 1' 43.4" W.

The New Carlisle Cemetery, which now includes the old Honey Creek Presbyterian Cemetery, is still active. There are several old gravestones, and while some are in good condition, many of the oldest monuments have been damaged. Also, some of the older sections were a little overgrown when we visited in July 2008. Overall though, the cemetery is well maintained.

Many thanks to Tammy Ankrom for visiting this cemetery with me!
