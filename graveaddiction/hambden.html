Hambden Township Cemetery is located on the east side of OH-608, just south of US-6, in Hambden, Ohio (Hambden Township, Geauga County). The coordinates are 41° 36' 12.58" N, 81° 8' 37.81" W.

Hambden Township Cemetery was established in 1817, and it is still active. The oldest gravestones are located closest to the entrance. Most of the gravestones are in great condition, but several have been damaged. Many of the damaged gravestones have been repaired though. The township does a great job of maintaining the cemetery grounds.
