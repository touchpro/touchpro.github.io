Sleepy Hollow Cemetery is located at 540 North Broadway in Sleepy Hollow, New York (Westchester County). The coordinates are 41° 5' 49" N, 73° 51' 35" W.

Sleepy Hollow Cemetery is large, and it is still active. It is adjacent to the Old Dutch Burying Ground, which was the setting for Washington Irving's story The Legend of Sleepy Hollow. Washington Irving is buried in Sleepy Hollow Cemetery, on a hill overlooking the Old Dutch Burying Ground. There are many beautiful monuments and mausoleums in the cemetery, and although some have been damaged, most are in excellent condition. The cemetery is very well maintained.

For more information about Sleepy Hollow Cemetery, please visit the official website.
