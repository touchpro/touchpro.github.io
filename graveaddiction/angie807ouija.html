An Experience to Remember

August 24, 2007



On August 24, 2007 I received an e-mail from Angie about an interesting ouija board experience:


My friends and I were playing with the Ouija Board on the night of August 23/24 of 2007.

We met a spirit by the name of Dug.

We asked him if he could blow out a candle for us after talking for awhile. He blew it out at least three times. He only blew it out if the candle was on the board with:

Pearls
Hair from each of us
Onyx with silver
A dice on side 5

Do these mean anything specific?

Also, when this was happening we had our mouths and noses covered, just to make sure no wind would occur. Even though this was happening, we still felt wind on our hands and our arms. There were no windows or vents around for a draft to hit us. We also noticed that when we would 'chant' Dug's name in our heads, the candle would go out faster. Then, when we would chant it out loud, still having our air passages closed, the candle went out within ten seconds. Does any of this mean anything?

Is there anything else we could possibly do to be amazed any further?


If you have any information for Angie, please send me an e-mail and I'll pass it on to her.
