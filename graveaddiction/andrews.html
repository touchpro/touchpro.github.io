Andrews Cemetery is located near the intersection of OH-60 and Hackney Road outside Beverly, Ohio (Center Township, Morgan County). The coordinates are 39° 35' 44.29" N, 81° 41' 33.32" W.

Andrews Cemetery is no longer active. It is located across the Muskingum River from the AEP Power Plant. Most of the gravestones are in great condition, although a few of the older stones have been damaged. The cemetery is very well maintained.
