Newcomers Cemetery is located on the south side of East Home Road, at the intersection with Mechanicsburg Road (OH-4), in Springfield, Ohio (Springfield Township, Clark County). The coordinates are 39° 56' 59.33" N, 83° 46' 0.7" W.

Newcomers Cemetery is large, and it is still active. The oldest gravestones are located on the north side. Although some of the older gravestones are in great condition, many have been damaged over the years. There are several interesting homemade markers too. The grounds are very well maintained.
