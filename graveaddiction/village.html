The Village Cemetery is located at the intersection of NH-102 and NH-121 in Chester, New Hampshire (Rockingham County). The coordinates are 42° 57' 28" N, 71° 15' 20" W.

The Village Cemetery was established in 1732, and it is still active. There are many old gravestones with interesting carvings, including many slate stones with winged skulls and soul effigies. The cemetery is very well maintained.
