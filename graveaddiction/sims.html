Sims Cemetery is located on the south side of E. University Avenue, between E. 36th Court and E. 37th Street, in Des Moines, Iowa (Lee Township, Polk County). The coordinates are 41° 36' 2" N, 93° 32' 39" W.

Sims Cemetery is also known as the Harris Cemetery and Agency Cemetery. There are many old gravestones, and several have detailed, interesting carvings. Although a few of the gravestones are still standing and in good condition, many of the older ones have been broken and damaged. The cemetery grounds are very well maintained.
