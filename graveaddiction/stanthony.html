Saint Anthony Cemetery is located at the intersection of Edison Drive and Berlin Street in Milan, Ohio (Milan Township, Erie County). The coordinates are 41° 17' 40.46" N, 82° 35' 47.6" W.

Saint Anthony Cemetery was established in 1866. It is adjacent to the Milan Cemetery. There are many old gravestones, and although a few have been damaged, most are in good condition. The cemetery is very well maintained.
