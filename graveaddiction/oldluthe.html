The Old Lutheran Cemetery is located on Concord Street in Adelphi, Ohio (Colerain Township, Ross County).

The Old Lutheran Cemetery is VERY old indeed, and there are many interesting gravestones to look at! There are at least six Revolutionary War veterans buried at this cemetery, as well as several Civil War and War of 1812 veterans. The cemetery is very well taken care of, but like all old cemeteries there are a few broken gravestones.
