Bad Ouija Board

November 24, 2007



On November 24, 2007 I received an e-mail from Kasey D. about an interesting ouija board experience:


Hi, I am Kasey. One day I decided to make a homemade ouija board. It took me two hours!! When it was done, I tried it by asking, "Are there any spirits here that would like to talk?" I waited 35 minutes, but it didn't answer! I said this is fake!!! I threw it at my wall. My mom said to put it in my closet, and I responded NO! She said go now! So I did...I put it in my closet. I was tired so I decided to take a nap. I got into my bed. Like two minutes later, I felt the need to kick my closet door, so I did. I got back into my bed and I slowly fell asleep. I had the worst dream ever! It was about when I went up north, and when I came back a little creature was playing my board...it had red eyes and blue horns! I was so scared that I woke up and I heard knocking everywhere! I screamed so loud that my mom came in. She took the board with her. Always remember play with CAUTION!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!
