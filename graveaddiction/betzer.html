Betzer Cemetery is located on Pickerington Road, about 1.3 miles north of Lithopolis Road, outside Carroll, Ohio (Bloom Township, Fairfield County). The coordinates are 39° 47' 27.72" N, 82° 44' 22.73" W.

Betzer Cemetery is known for it's fantastic view from the top of the hill...when standing at the top of the hill you can see for miles! While I found the view at the top of the hill spectacular, my favorite part of this cemetery was the section on the other side of the road. There are many old gravestones, including many carved by J.W. Jungkurth and John Strickler, who were local stonecarvers. Although some of the gravestones have been damaged, most are in excellent condition. The cemetery is very well maintained by the Betzer Cemetery Association, Inc.

I also recommend checking out the Bloom Township Genealogy page, which contains a lot of great information about Betzer Cemetery.
