Very Weird Encounters

October 5, 2009



On October 5, 2009 I received an e-mail from Soccer about an interesting ouija board experience:


Last Saturday night my friend, Irish, and I, Soccer, were playing the Ouija board, and we had some very weird encounters. This is what happened that night.

Me: Is there anyone that would like to speak to us?
Board: Yes

Me: What is your name?
Board: Zeply Goodbye.

Now that started to happen throughout the whole night. I would ask their name, and they would say it and then say goodbye. But what happened later was pretty creepy!

Me: Is there anyone there?
Board: Yes

Me: What is your name?
Board: Taz

Me: Okay-(I get cut off)
Board: MOM

Me: Awww, do you miss your mom?
Board: no

Now Irish and I were looking at each other saying we got a real weirdo here.

Me: What's wrong?
Board: your mom

Me: Whose mom are you?
Board: U

Me: Are you Irish's mom?
Board: No

Me: Whose mom are you?
Board: (Starts spelling out my whole name! Even my middle name, which my friend did not know!)

Me: Okay, how old are you?
Board: 61

Me: How old was I when I died?
Board: 9

Me: Aww, thats sad. How did I die?
Board: (spells out Zach)

Right when I was going to ask who that was, Irish said goodbye and moved the pointer to goodbye. When I went to look at him, he had a real creeped out look on his face. I know that I was definitely creeped out too, but I still wanted to know more. So later on I decided to play again with Irish.

Me: Is there anyone there?
Board: Taz Goodbye!

I looked over at Irish and I knew that he felt that she was angry too. This was a fun and totally creepy night!
