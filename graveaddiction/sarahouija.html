Opening a Door

March 6, 2005



On March 6, 2005 I received an e-mail from Sarah Lemette in Sutter, California about her experience with the ouija board:


"So I was at a party and they started talking about using an Ouija board. I didn't know what it was so I was like sure, whatever. We never did get very far in it, we just got one question because later I found out there were two spirits back there so it was moving back and forth. The spirits haven't left me alone and its been over a year since I did one. They talk to me and they never leave me alone. I don't talk back because I'm afraid what they'll do, so I would strongly advise you people never to use them because when you use it it can posess your body because it has been done to me before for two months. It's like opening a door for yourself to get hurt because i have scars from what these things did to me!"
