Photos taken by Jill and Robert Jones

In October 2005 I received an e-mail from Jill and Robert Jones with some great ghost photos from Lakin Hospital. They also sent me an explanation of everything they saw in the photos:

"We have been to Lakin twice. The first time we did not take pictures, but the second I took our digital pics and they were interesting. Below is the number of the pics and where to look. You'll need to enlarge some.

Pictures and what we saw.

Pic 461- Look in top in window block, a skeleton head
Pic 467- Window by door at corner
Pic 502- Orbs all over as we approach the building
Pic 513- 1st window at the bottom
Pic 526- At right corner of door, at the top glass 2 faces at top of bush. Enlarge that spot."
