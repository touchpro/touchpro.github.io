Rock Springs Cemetery is located on the north side of OH-7, just west of the intersection with US-33, outside Pomeroy, Ohio (Salisbury Township, Meigs County). The coordinates are 39° 3' 31.39" N, 82° 1' 10.67" W.

Rock Springs Cemetery is still active. There are many old sandstone and marble gravestones, and most are in very good condition. Several of the gravestones have beautiful, detailed carvings. My favorite gravestones are the two that have the carvings of a basket of flowers with a butterfly flying out toward Heaven. The cemetery is very well maintained by the township.
