The Old Basil Cemetery is located on the south side of OH-256, about a ½ mile west of OH-158, in Baltimore, Ohio (Liberty Township, Fairfield County). The coordinates are 39° 50' 42.31" N, 82° 36' 46.54" W.

The Old Basil Cemetery is large, and it is no longer active. More recent graves can be found in the Basil Memorial Cemetery. There are many old gravestones, including several with beautiful carvings. Most of the gravestones are in excellent condition. The cemetery is very well maintained by the township.
