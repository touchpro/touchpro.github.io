Saint Louis Cemetery is located at the intersection of North Star-Fort Laramie Road and Greenville-St. Marys Road outside North Star, Ohio (Wabash Township, Darke County). The coordinates are 40° 19' 28.13" N, 84° 32' 7.52" W.

Saint Louis Cemetery is still an active Catholic cemetery. Most of the gravestones are in good condition; very few have been damaged. The grounds are very well maintained.
