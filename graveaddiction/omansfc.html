The Old Mansfield Center Cemetery is located on the east side of CT-195, at the intersection with Cemetery Road, in Mansfield, Connecticut (Tolland County). The coordinates are 41° 45' 44" N, 72° 11' 45" W.

The Old Mansfield Center Cemetery was founded in 1693, and it is no longer active. More recent gravestones can be found in the nearby New Mansfield Center Cemetery. There are many interesting, old gravestones in this cemetery, including several with detailed carvings. Most of the gravestones are in excellent condition. The grounds are very well maintained.
