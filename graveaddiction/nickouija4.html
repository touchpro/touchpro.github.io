Using The Board Alone

April 17, 2006



On April 17, 2006 I received an e-mail from Nick about another interesting ouija board experience he had:


I just turned 12 and got an oujia board for my brithday. I decided to use it alone (which I never do). It was about 11:00 at night (lights on and tv on) when I decided to use it. A boy named Burn, who was 13 years old, contacted me. He died in 2004 when a murderer came into his house and killed his family. Burn was very mad and wanted revenge. I said I was sorry that happened to him. He wanted me to murder the one who killed his family, and I said no. He said if I did not murder him, he (Burn) would hurt me. Right then I said goodbye; it was 12:00 then so I went to bed.

I woke up at 3:00 AM when I heard a sound like banging in my basement. I ignored it and went back to sleep. Fifteen minutes later I woke up again and it was FREEZING in my room. I went to check the temperature and it was 73 degrees. I thought I was going crazy...it was so cold. I go back to bed, then I hear a banging in my closet. Then a black figure (about as tall as me, maybe a little bit taller) crashes out of the closet, jumping on my bed atacking me. I punched it in the face...it scratched me on my arm. I then get my metal bat and slam it in the head. I just kept hitting the figure with my bat, then it faded away. There was blood on my carpet. The next day I tell my parents, and they tell me to throw away my board. I listened to them. Then one evening I find my board in the basement!! So I used it and Burn talks to me again. He said IT ISN'T OVER, so I ripped up the board and threw it in the dumpster. It returned again in my basement, so I ripped it up again. It never came back.
