West Carlisle Cemetery is located on the east side of County Road 80, just north of County Road 39, in West Carlisle, Ohio (Pike Township, Coshocton County). The coordinates are 40° 13' 51.85" N, 82° 7' 2.37" W.

West Carlisle Cemetery is large, and it is still active. The oldest gravestones are located on the south side of the cemetery, and most are in excellent condition. The grounds are very well maintained by the township.

Many thanks to Laura Mumford for telling me about this cemetery!
