The McKendree Methodist Episcopal Cemetery is located behind the church at 3330 Cleveland Avenue in Columbus, Ohio (Clinton Township, Franklin County). The coordinates are 40° 2' 6" N, 82° 57' 45" W.

The McKendree Methodist Episcopal Cemetery was established in 1859 (although the first burial was in 1831), and it is no longer active. There are many old gravestones, but unfortunately the majority have been damaged. In fact, part of the cemetery may have been paved over for the church's parking lot many years ago. The cemetery is well maintained by the church.
