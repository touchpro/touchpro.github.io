Buxton Cemetery is located on the south side of Hinton Mill Road, west of Myers Road, outside Marysville, Ohio (Dover Township, Union County). The coordinates are 40° 13' 40.58" N, 83° 18' 0.76" W.

Buxton Cemetery is small, and it appears that it is no longer active. There are many old gravestones, and the majority are in good condition. Many of the stones that were damaged have now been repaired. The township does a great job of maintaining the cemetery.
