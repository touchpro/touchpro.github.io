Bell Cemetery is located back a lane north of Bell Church Road, about 3/4 mile east of Clell Road, outside Utica, Ohio (Morgan Township, Knox County). The coordinates are 40° 14' 31.75" N, 82° 22' 56.77" W.

Bell Cemetery was established in 1834, and it is still active. There are many old marble and sandstone monuments, and several have beautiful carvings. There is also a fantastic statue of a young boy. Most of the gravestones are in good condition, but some of the older monuments have been damaged. The cemetery is very well maintained.
