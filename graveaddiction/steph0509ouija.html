My Future

May 5, 2009



On May 5, 2009 I received an e-mail from Stephanie H. about an interesting ouija board experience:


My name is Stephanie H.

My brother, Ryan, and I, Stephanie, played the ouija board tonight, so here is our story.

Me: Is there anybody who would like to talk to us? If so, please move to yes.
Board: (very slowly) Yes

Me: What is your name?
Board: (after a few tries) Sims

Me: May we please ask you a few questions about our future?
Board: Yes

Me: Ok. Well am I gonna be asked to this year's senior prom? (I am a freshman)
Board: Yes

Me: By who?
Board: Dylan

Me: As in Dylan J***** (who was rumored to ask me)
Board: Yes

Me: When?
Board: Wed.

Me: So I'll be attending?
Board: No

Me: Oh, why?
Board: Homework

Me: Can I change that by doing all my homework?
Board: Yes

Me: Ok. Well, will I ever fulfill my dream of acting in Hollywood?
Board: Yes

Me: What age will I get discovered?
Board: 18 (I am 15 now)

Me: Ok! What age will I get married?
Board: 20

Me: To who?
Board: Nick J

Me: As in Nick Jonas?
Board: Yes

Me: How will we meet?
Board: Movie

Me: We'll do a movie together?
Board: Yes

Me: Ok, how many kids will we have together?
Board: 5

Me: And you are telling the truth, right?
Board: Yes. I wouldn't lie

Me: Ok. Well thank you for the answers.
Board: No problem.

Me: Bye
Board: Good Bye

Well that was a great experience, and got a lot of questions answered. I just hope it is telling the truth. The problem is that I heard if you ask questions about your future, it can change it. So we'll see if he's telling the truth Wednesday, and see if Dylan asks me. And if someone could answer the question about the whole changing the future part that would help a lot. Thank You :D


If you have any comments/advice/answers for Stephanie, please send me an e-mail and I will forward it on to her.
