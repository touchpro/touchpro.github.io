Saint John's Lutheran Churchyard is located at 5 Clifford Street, at the intersection with Archdale Street, in Charleston, South Carolina (Charleston County).

Saint John's Lutheran Churchyard is filled with old gravestones, and the majority are in excellent shape. When I visited in April 2006 the front gate was locked, however, the side gate within the Unitarian Churchyard next door was unlocked. The church does an excellent job of maintaining this cemetery.

For more information about the church and the graveyard, please visit their site: Saint John's Lutheran Church.
