Saint Michael's Cemetery is located on Bickel Church Road, a ½ mile west of Basil Road, outside Baltimore, Ohio (Liberty Township, Fairfield County). The coordinates are 39° 52' 22.67" N, 82° 37' 59.08" W.

Saint Michael's Cemetery is also known as the Bickel Cemetery. It is still active, and the oldest gravestones are located on the east side. The gate has a date of 1914, but there are a lot of gravestones that are older than that. Many of the old gravestones have interesting carvings and verses, and most are in good condition. However, some of the older monuments have been damaged over the years. The grounds are very well maintained.
