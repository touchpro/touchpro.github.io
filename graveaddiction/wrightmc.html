Wright Cemetery is located on the east side of Glade Run Road outside West Jefferson, Ohio (Deer Creek Township, Madison County). The coordinates are 39° 56' 2.53" N, 83° 21' 41.65" W.

Wright Cemetery is small, and it is no longer active. There are many interesting gravestones, including some with cause of death epitaphs. Most of the gravestones are in good condition, but there are many that have been damaged. The township does an excellent job of maintaining the cemetery grounds.
