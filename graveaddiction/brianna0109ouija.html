Haunted Graveyard

January 10, 2009



On January 10, 2009 I received an e-mail from Brianna C. about an interesting ouija board experience:


One early Saturday morning, at three in the morning, my friends and I decided to perform a seance in a supposedly haunted graveyard. We started at exactly three, to have it be just right. We used a ouija board for the base, surrounded by candles, incense, and bread for an offering. There were twelve of us surrounding the board, holding hands (all of us are 16-17). Our "speaker," Dylan, researched online what to say, English and Spanish, to be done just correctly.

After about a half hour, we decided to call it quits since nothing was happening. After we correctly unheld hands and blew out the candles, Dylan passed out onto the Ouija board and all of the candles. We decided to quickly pack up and carry Dylan back to the car. As I was walking back with the Ouija board in my hand (I didn't even want it, ughh), I tripped and the Ouija board flew from my hands and hit the ground. At the exact same time, Dylan was slammed into the ground and began coughing up blood.

He doesn't remember a thing after falling forward.
