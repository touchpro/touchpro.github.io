Find Me

February 11, 2008



On February 11, 2008 I received an e-mail from Kevin about an interesting ouija board experience:


We have been using the board a little bit over the past few months. My ex-roommate and I started trying to find real haunted places around Halloween time. We get random ideas all of the time, and usually follow through. It all started as a joke, that we'd talk to the dead and all that. Well his girlfriend Becca said she has used it, and it scares her, so we decided to try it. On the first try it told me to die, and my roommate Dustin was told the same thing. As we said goodbye, Dustin stood up, turned his ankle, spilt the red candle, and to this day there is wax on the carpet in the shape of a pitchfork.
