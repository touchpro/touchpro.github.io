Darby Township Cemetery is located on the east side of OH-142 (Chillicothe Street) in Plain City, Ohio (Darby Township, Madison County). The coordinates are 40° 5' 54.15" N, 83° 15' 44.41" W.

Darby Township Cemetery is located on the west bank of Big Darby Creek. There is a beautiful view of the creek from the back of the cemetery. It is quite large, but no longer active. Most of the gravestones are still in great shape, but there are several that have been damaged over the years. Many of the broken gravestones are stacked at the back by the fence. The cemetery grounds are very well maintained by the township.
