The Whately Center Cemetery is located at 215 Chestnut Plain Road in Whately, Massachusetts (Franklin County). The coordinates are 42° 26' 35" N, 72° 38' 9" W.

The Whately Center Cemetery is still active. The oldest gravestones are located in the middle of the cemetery, and most are in good condition. However, like any older graveyard, some of the stones have been damaged. There are many slate gravestones with detailed carvings. The grounds are very well maintained.
