The Old Centerville Cemetery is located at the intersection of Spruce Street and North Street in Centerville, Indiana (Wayne County).

The Old Centerville Cemetery is no longer active. There are many rows of old gravestones, including several sandstone monuments with detailed carvings. Although some of the gravestones are in good condition, many have been damaged. The cemetery grounds are well maintained.

I'm not sure if "Old Centerville" is the correct name for this cemetery. I wasn't able to find much information online. If you know the correct name for this cemetery or have any historical information, please send me an e-mail and I'll update this page.
