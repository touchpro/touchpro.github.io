Zion State Line Cemetery is located at the intersection of Pine Road and State Line Road outside Wabash, Ohio (Washington Township, Mercer County). The coordinates are 40° 31' 39.58" N, 84° 48' 7.83" W.

Zion State Line Cemetery is still active. It sits right on the Ohio-Indiana border. There are many old gravestones, and several have beautiful, unique carvings. Although some of the older gravestones are still standing and in good condition, many have been damaged and are stacked throughout the cemetery. The grounds are very well maintained by the township.
