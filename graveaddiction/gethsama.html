Gethsamane Catholic Cemetery is located at 2001 Spencerville Road (OH-117) in Lima, Ohio (Shawnee Township, Allen County). The coordinates are 40° 43' 41" N, 84° 8' 9" W.

Gethsamane Catholic Cemetery is very large (24.12 acres), and it is still active. There are many interesting gravestones, including many with beautiful statues. The cemetery grounds are extremely well maintained by the cemetery association.
