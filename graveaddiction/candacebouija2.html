NJS

June 9, 2007



On June 9, 2007 I received an e-mail from Candace B. about an interesting ouija board experience:


This is a continuation of my previous ouija experience, called Murder at Camp. I was over at my friend Kelsey's again, and we talked to the same girl.

Kelsey: Are there any spirits here who wish to speak?
Board: Yes

Kelsey: Hello, what is your name?
Board: No

Kelsey: You mean you won't tell us your name?
Board: Guess. NJS

Candace (me): Is this...could this be Erin?
Board: Hi Candace hi Kelsey

Me and Kelsey: Hi Erin!
Board: Be careful

Me: Careful? Of NJS?v Board: Yes

Me: What's he gonna do?
Board: No

Kelsey: Is NJS dead?
Baord: Yes

Me: Is he gonna talk to us?
Board: Maybe

Me: Is this still Erin?
Board: Yes

Me (talking to Kelsey): Oh thank god...I thought it would be the weird freak who killed her!
Kelsey: Yeah so did I...

Me (back to the board): Can you keep him away?
Board: I can only try

Me: Do you have enough energy?
Board: No weak

Kelsey: You're weak?
Board: Yes but storm makes me stronger (it was currently raining heavily outside, and it was supposed to thunderstorm later on)

Me: Do you also get your energy from us?
Board: Yes weird

Me: Weird?
Board: Energy

Kelsey: You get like, what, weird energy from us?
Board: Sort of

Me: Then you probably get a lot of energy from us! We're just about some of the weirdest people on the planet!
Board: I know thats why u guys

Me: That's why we what?
Board: Hello girls

Kelsey: Who is this?
Board: No movement

Me: Who the hell is this?!
Board: NJS

Kelsey: Oh shit! *moves the pointer to goodbye*

That was the last time Kelsey and I used the board, but a few of my other friends are real interested in it, so we may wind up using the board again. And also, on a side note, this was a homemade board. We used a piece of non-lined paper, a permanent marker to write the numbers and letters, and a CD as the pointer. It worked just fine, like any other ouija board.
