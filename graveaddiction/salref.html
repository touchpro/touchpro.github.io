Salem Reformed Church Cemetery is located behind an old church on the south side of OH-180 outside Hallsville, Ohio (Green Township, Ross County). It is also known as the White Church Cemetery and the Lutheran Cemetery.

Salem Reformed Church Cemetery is still an active cemetery. It is rather large, with lots of old gravestones. There are at least two Revolutionary War veterans buried here, as well as many veterans of other wars. I also noticed several old gravestones with German epitaphs. The cemetery is very well taken care of, there aren't many vandalized gravestones and most are still legible.
