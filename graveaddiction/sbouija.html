Evil Spirit

May 14, 2005



On May 14, 2005 I received an e-mail from Scott Bodey about a creepy ouija board experience:


"I can't give an exact date, but sometime in December of 2004 my fiance and I were playing around on the ouija. We have contacted few spirits before, and I will hopefully post them soon. I know now that most spirits are liars, but for now this is one that scared me a little. We have had the board for about a week. This is the conversation:

Me: Is there a spirit here?
Board: Yes

Me: What is your name?
Board: 39

I thought that maybe the spirit had misunderstood me so I asked again

Me: Your name is 39?
Spirit: Yes

The spirit then went to 39 again, then again. Then it went to the number 6, then the number 6 again, and it started to come down again. I basically knew where it was going from here so I threw the pointer to "goodbye". Just knowing that there was an evil spirit in the same room and it didn't even try to hide scared me. It was a very strong spirit. Curiousity got the best of me. It wasn't the last time I used it, but I did put the board away for a short while."
