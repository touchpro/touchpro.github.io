Erik left a comment to last night's post and he asked for the spiced shortbread recipe. So Erik, here you go:

Ingredients:


  • 1 1/4 cup flour
  • 1/4 cup brown sugar
  • Approx. 1/8 teaspoon cloves (I used a bit more)
  • Approx. 1/4 teaspoon ground ginger
  • Approx. 1/2 teaspoon cinnamon (I used a bit more)
  • Approx. 1/4 teaspoon cardamon (optional - this was just something I added)
  • 1/2 cup butter (I suppose margarine would work too)

Sift the dry ingredients together. Next cut in the butter with a fork until the mixture is clumpy. Then knead the dough until smooth. You can roll it to 1/2 inch thin and use a cookie cutter to cut it into rounds, or you can cut it into strips. Or you can do what I did - the dough it really thick, so I just rolled it into a log shape, like those Pillsbury cookies you can by in the refrigerator section of any grocery store. Then I sliced it into 1/2 inch thick slices...it worked just fine! Then bake them for about 20 minutes at 320 degrees on ungreased cookie sheets. And that's it! Pretty simple, really.
