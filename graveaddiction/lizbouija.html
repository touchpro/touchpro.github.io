Missing Boy

April 21, 2007



On April 21, 2007 I received an e-mail from Liz B. about an interesting ouija board experience:


About a year ago, I think early 2006, there was a missing boy named Ricky. He was missing from a town in Michigan - there were signs posted all around town with his picture. My girls and I decided to use the board one night after work. Well, the spirit started to tell us that its name was Ricky, and it told us who killed him, what he was wearing, what happened, and where his body was located. We called the police with the information, and they found him a few days later...right where he said he was going to be. Freaky, I don't know what to think of this!!!
