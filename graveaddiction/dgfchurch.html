Diane G. sent me the following e-mail about the Old First Church and Cemetery:

This was a great trip to take; such a beautiful area all around, especially in the fall. Unfortunately, on this visit I didn't get to go into the church, but the first chance I get I will and send the photos. The Bennington Museum is practically on the same property, right next door to each other. Walk through the cemetery at the church entrance to the back, and there will be a very small path through the woods to the back of the museum. There will also be another section of the cemetery. You can start at the museum and make your way the same way to the church. The people are very friendly and helpful there.
