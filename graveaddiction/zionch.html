Zion Church Cemetery is located on the south side of TR-71, just west of TR-42, outside Stoutsville, Ohio (Washington Township, Pickaway County). The coordinates are 39° 36' 44.08" N, 82° 52' 7.31" W.

Zion Church Cemetery is small, and it is no longer active. There are many old, interesting gravestones and most are in very good condition. The old Zion Church used to sit beside the cemetery; there is a now a monument marking its location. The cemetery is very well maintained by the township.
