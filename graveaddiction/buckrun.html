Buck Run Cemetery is located on the north side of OH-245, between Buck Run Road and Allen Center Road, outside Milford Center, Ohio (Allen Township, Union County). The coordinates are 40° 13' 27.65" N, 83° 27' 37.38" W.

Buck Run Cemetery is small, and it is no longer active. Many of the gravestones have been damaged, and there are several stacked together throughout the cemetery. The township does a great job of maintaining the cemetery grounds.
