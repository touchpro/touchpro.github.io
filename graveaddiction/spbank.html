Springbank Cemetery is located on the east side of OH-104 outside Yellowbud, Ohio (Union Township, Ross County). The coordinates are 39° 28' 56.53" N, 83° 0' 56.56" W.

Springbank Cemetery is located across the road from the church. There are many old gravestones, and most are in good condition. The oldest are located on the northeast side. The cemetery is very well maintained by the township.
