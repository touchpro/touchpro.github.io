Hill Cemetery is located at the intersection of Elm Street and Prospect Street in Hatfield, Massachusetts (Hampshire County).

Hill Cemetery was established in 1668, and it is no longer active. There are many old gravestones with interesting carvings, and most are in good condition. Several contain interesting epitaphs. The cemetery is very well maintained.

Many thanks to John M., Bill B., and Ta Mara C. for visiting this cemetery with me!
