Friendly Ouija Conversation

September 2, 2007



On September 2, 2007 I received an e-mail from Mike K. about an interesting ouija board experience:


Me and two other friends decided to try using the ouija board in our friend's garage. We didn't think it would work because it was so early and daytime, but we were pleasantly surprised.

Mike: Are there any spirits who would like to contact us through the Ouija board?
Board: Yes

Mike: Can you please tell us your name?
Board: Brad

Mike: How old were you when you died, Brad?
Brad: 23

Mike: How did you die?
Brad: Shot

Mike: Were you murdered?
Brad: Yes

Mike: What year did you die?
Brad: 1923

Then we asked it some test questions about us...

Mike: I’m thinking of a band right now, can you please tell us the letter that this band's name starts with?
Brad: W (I was thinking of Wolfmother)

Mike: Are you the spirit that we talked to last night?
Brad: No

Onto the questions...

Clayton: Was there a person who died in my basement who starved themselves to death?
Brad: Yes

Clayton: Does he haunt my house?
Brad: No

Clayton: So he moved on?
Brad: No

Mike: How many spirits haunt Clayton's house?
Brad: 16

Mike: Brad, we heard a loud bang on the garage door when we were here trying this Friday, was it a spirit that we were talking to?
Brad: A mad 1

Mike: Was he mad that we blew out the candle?
Brad: Yes

We asked him if our friend was a square, we often call him this because of how uptight he is. Here's what happened...

Chris: Is Charlie a square?
Brad: A big one.

(Laughter)

Chris: Brad, were you a comedian?
Brad: Yes

Chris: Were you famous?
Brad: No

Chris: Does John's mom hate me?
Brad: No

Chris: Dislike me?
Brad: Yes

Clayton: Do I love Chris?
Brad: Yes

Mike: Do you like us?
Brad: Yes

Mike: We like you, you're funny.
Brad: Yes

Clayton: I've been having dreams lately, do you know anything about them?
Brad: Me

Mike: Were you causing the dreams?
Brad: (Moves between yes and no)

Mike: Maybe?
Brad: Yes

Brad: I'll stop.
Clayton: Thank you.
Brad: Yes

Mike: Would it be a good idea to put "You're welcome" on the Ouija board?
Brad: Don't

Mike: Would it be unsafe?
Brad: Yes

(We hear clatter at the garage door, Clayton checks it out)

Mike: Was that you?
Brad: Yes

Chris: Did you do that for your own amusement at seeing us scared?
Brad: Yes

Chris: You’re funny.
Brad: LOL (we all laughed that he actually said lol)

Brad: cj
Mike: Are you talking about Clayton? (Those are his initials)

Brad: Yes
Chris: Do you want to ask him a question?

Brad: Yes
Mike: Ask away, Brad.

Brad: Why does bj (his brother) hurt you?
Clayton: Because he's a jock.

Brad: ok

Mike: Did you live in this house?
Brad: No

Mike: This general area? (Our city?)
Brad: Yes

Mike: Were you killed in Clayton's house?
Brad: Yes

Mike: Do you stick around this house with the other spirits?
Brad: Yes

Chris: Is the afterlife good?
Brad: Yes

Chris: Is it kind of like our world, but with other people who have died?
Brad: Yes

Chris: We're going to end this session now, we appreciate talking to you, Brad.
(Board moves to goodbye)

When we left his garage, an apple fell from the top of his garage...there is no apple tree from were it fell, we figured that this was Brad fooling with us again.
