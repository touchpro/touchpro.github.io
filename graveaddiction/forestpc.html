Forest Cemetery is located at the intersection of North Court Street and Haywood Avenue in Circleville, Ohio (Circleville Township, Pickaway County). The coordinates are 39° 36' 54.28" N, 82° 56' 34.69" W.

Forest Cemetery is very large, and it is still active. It is located next to Saint Joseph's Catholic Cemetery. There are many old gravestones, and almost all of them are in great condition; very few have been damaged. The Forest Cemetery Association does an excellent job of maintaining the cemetery grounds.

Many thanks to Rocky Saxour and Francie Chaffin for telling me about this cemetery!
