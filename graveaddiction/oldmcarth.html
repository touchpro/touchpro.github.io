The Old McArthur Cemetery is located on the south side of US-50, east of South Walnut Street, in McArthur, Ohio (Elk Township, Vinton County). The coordinates are 39° 14' 44.32" N, 82° 28' 33.18" W.

The Old McArthur Cemetery is no longer active. There are many rows of old gravestones, and most are in great condition. However, there are several gravestones that have been damaged over the years. The cemetery grounds are very well maintained by the township.
