On July 27, 2007 I received an e-mail from Johnny L. about an interesting ouija board experience:


My mom and I just bought the glow in the dark ouija board today. We did have other experiences, but not too much for me. But anyways...

Me: Is there anyone there?
Board: Yes

Me: What is your name?
Board: Fred

Me: What is your last name?
Board: Dugan

Me: How old are you?
Board: 31

Mom: Did you die from a sickness or murder?
Board: Cancer

Mom: Do you have a wife?
Board: Yes

Mom: Do you have any kids?
Board: Yes

Board: Goodbye

It was fun and we are going to try to learn more about him. I recommend this to anybody that wants to have some good clean fun.
