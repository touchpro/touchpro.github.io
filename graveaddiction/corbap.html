Baptist Corners Cemetery is located at the intersection of Coonpath Road and Lake Road outside Lancaster, Ohio (Pleasant Township, Fairfield County). The coordinates are 39° 46' 24.08" N, 82° 30' 21.58" W.

Baptist Corners Cemetery is also known as Pleasant Run Cemetery, Primitive Baptist Cemetery, or simply Baptist Cemetery. It is no longer active. There are many rows of old gravestones, and most are in excellent condition. Several of the gravestones have interesting carvings, including many carved by John Strickler, a local stonecutter. The cemetery is very well maintained.
