The Genesee County Home Cemetery is located on the Death Row Trail within the Genesee County Park and Forest in East Bethany, New York (Genesee County). The park is located off Bethany Center Road.

There are many graves belonging to people who died at the old Genesee County Home (now called Rolling Hills), but only six are marked. These six gravestones dating from the 1800's were found in the basement of Rolling Hills, and were donated to the park. There is also a nice memorial in front of the cemetery.

Many people have experienced paranormal activity in the park. This isn't surprising since there are so many unmarked graves.
