Munsell Cemetery is located on the north side of Munsell Street in Belchertown, Massachusetts (Hampshire County). The coordinates are 42° 20' 15" N, 72° 24' 47" W.

Munsell Cemetery is small, and it is no longer active. There are many old gravestones, but the majority are either very weathered or not carved at all. There are are also several slate gravestones, including one with a very nice sun carving. The cemetery grounds are well maintained.
