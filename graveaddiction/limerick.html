The Limerick Cemetery is located on the east side of Savageville Road, about a ¼ mile north of County Road 25, in Limerick, Ohio (Jackson Township, Jackson County). The coordinates are 39° 7' 35.6" N, 82° 44' 49.84" W.

The Limerick Cemetery is small, but it is still active. There are several old gravestones, and while most are in good condition, a few of the older monuments have been damaged. There are also a few interesting homemade markers. The cemetery is very well maintained.
