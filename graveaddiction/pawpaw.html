Pawpaw Cemetery is located at the intersection of OH-37 and Miles Road, about a ½ mile north of OH-309, outside Marseilles, Ohio (Grand Township, Marion County). The coordinates are 40° 39' 2.91" N, 83° 22' 53.03" W.

Pawpaw Cemetery is no longer active. There are many rows of old gravestones, and most are in great condition. Most of the gravestones that have been damaged are now repaired. The township does a great job of maintaining the cemetery.
