The City of Dublin Cemetery is located on the south side of US-33 in Dublin, Ohio (Washington Township, Franklin County). The coordinates are 40° 5' 52.37" N, 83° 7' 1.91" W.

The City of Dublin Cemetery was established in 1816. The worst part about driving through this cemetery is that some of the gravestones are very close to the road and it looks like you're going to hit them! The newest sections of the cemetery seem to be located in the back. The older sections are in the front and center parts of the cemetery.
