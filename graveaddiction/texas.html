Texas Cemetery is located off the east side of CR-4A, about a ½ mile north of US-24, outside Liberty Center, Ohio (Washington Township, Henry County). The coordinates are 41° 25' 42.49" N, 83° 57' 14.77" W.

Texas Cemetery is no longer active. It sits pretty far from the road, you can access it through one of the driveways by the new houses. Several of the gravestones have been damaged over the years, but the cemetery is very well maintained.

A big "thank you" to Mike McMaster for taking me to this cemetery!!
