The Old Dutch Burying Ground is located adjacent to Sleepy Hollow Cemetery in Sleepy Hollow, New York (Westchester County).

The Old Dutch Burying Ground is no longer active. It sits next to the Old Dutch Church. The church and cemetery are part of Washington Irving's story The Legend of Sleepy Hollow. There are many interesting gravestones, including many old sandstone monuments with detailed soul effigy carvings. The cemetery is very well maintained.
