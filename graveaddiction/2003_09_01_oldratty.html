Ahhh, yes, I know it has been a while again. Well, I am a busy girl, I am not here just to entertain the masses you know!
I love fall. Cooler weather and open windows. I can run from one end of the house to the next, leaping into and out of the window sills, startling people and mutant creatures alike, charging between their feet on my quest for a better window seat and feeling the pleasure that only making someone trip can provide. Ahhh yes, fall is here.
The "boys" or those debris gathering, long-haired dust mops of the world, have been getting to go outside in the other world for long evening walks. They group together by the door and whine, then take off running, snarling and biting, putting their noses to the ground all in the guise of being interested in the great outdoors. I know the truth, those shit-eating creatures are looking for a fresh morsel of poop provided by the inferior cats or perhaps they are just looking for some other poop to roll in, doesn't matter, they are pretending to be mighty hunters of the groundhog or rabbit when really all they want is something that stinks to eat or roll in. Gross creatures, why do the people in this house love them so? I hear the family say the "doggers" make them laugh, that is their job...hell, I really work, I catch mice, well three in the nine years I have been here and there would be more but the mouse community has heard of my hunting prowess and stays away (the inferior cats think it is their doing because they catch lots of moles, voles, outside mice, birds and the occassional squirrel and they think that is why the house remains pest free but they are dumbasses, it is my doing alone). I am a mighty hunter, no one is better, certainly not those rodents that call themselves dogs. I am the only animal here that earns her keep.
Since I am a mighty hunter mommy thinks I can withstand torture and periodically she puts this to the test. The other day I was holding my ear funny and shaking my head because, well because I wanted to do that. She thinks I have ear mites so she grabs me and holds me in this vice-like grip and her mommy sticks a tube in my ear and puts this wet stuff in. It tickles and trickles down my throat, it makes me want to run and hide, and the treatment definitely makes me growl and grumble. Why does mommy do this to me and why do I think she will do it again. For retaliation I have decided to keep her awake. One morning I purred and cuddled around her head starting a 4 A.M. and last night I decided to start around 2 A.M. So much fun, jumping on and off the bed, stomping all over the bed, purring or yowling in someone's ear. She will regret messing with my ears. She does it again and she will wake up with cat butt right in her face. I know she really dislikes closeups of my butt first thing in the morning. Perhaps a small fart would add just the right amount of payback.....will let you know.

Hello loyal subjects, I have neglected you for far too long. But last week was just fab-u-lous!!!! Only mommy and I were home. The butt-sniffing mutuants were boarded at the vets and mommy's mommy and daddy went away on vacation for a few days. The house was quiet, no one chased me, mommy was scared so sit and petted me lots and had all the lights on in the house....life was perfect. It is amazing how good "food for mature" cats tastes when not covered in dog drool. My water bowl was clean, no slimey dog spit in my bowl. Heaven cannot be any better than last week.
The people were talking, seems when they delivered the "boys" to the vet that Oscar threw a fit and thrashed in the arms of the caretaker and snapped at him. Ha Ha.....didn't even faze the guy and he just plunked Oscar the Ogre right into a cage...a cage with a cement floor. This was done to the crown prince of dogdom, like I said, last week was my glimpse into heaven. I heard the other two disgusting creatures were more cooperative and just accepted their fates and were placed into cages as well. Bet they were miserable. You see, there are other cats there, inferior to me of course but one that has my coloring really things she is queen and I do not like her at all. I also heard there are kittens there looking for a home...no way! This is a one cat household and will remain that way, I am the ONE!
Mommy's mommy was sad Sunday, she said she really is looking forward to retirement and vacation just makes it so much harder to go back and face a new week. Not sure what "work" really is but I know people seem to dread it. Must be worse than being boarded at the vet's.
Glorious September has arrived and some cooler weather. The air conditioning is turned off and the windows are open. I love having all the windows open. First of all, I am a full figured lady and the sills are not quite large enough for me to be totally comfortable and secondly, I can smell the outside smells and hear the sounds more clearly. I love to dart from window to window chasing those stupid birds. Someday I might have the opportunity to catch one. Not sure why I want to do that, just know that it would fulfill a need. Just like when the occassional mouse invades the house and I get to kill it. It just makes me feel so invincible. I am the mighty one, I AM RATTY. Yes, open windows make me feel all powerful. People should have that opportunity but sadly I think it is just for us cats.
Know this post is not all that exciting but I have just been so mellow that it is difficult for me to be my usual sarcastic, viscious self. Never fear, the real me will return soon....BEWARE!
