Pine Hill Cemetery is located at the intersection of Cemetery Road and Bunker Hill Road outside Sugar Grove, Ohio (Good Hope Township, Hocking County). The coordinates are 39° 36' 13" N, 82° 36' 12.23" W.

Pine Hill Cemetery, also known as Our Lady of Good Hope Cemetery or the Old Catholic Cemetery, was established in 1853. It is no longer active. The Our Lady of Good Hope Catholic Church used to be located by the cemetery, but it is no longer standing. There is an altar placed in the cemetery as a memorial to the church's founding members. The cemetery is very well maintained.
