The Zion Reformed Lutheran Cemetery is located on the south side of High Point Road, east of OH-13, outside Thornville, Ohio (Thorn Township, Perry County). The coordinates are 39° 51' 19.49" N, 82° 23' 35.69" W.

The Zion Reformed Lutheran Cemetery, also known as Zion Ridge Cemetery, is one of the oldest in Perry County, and it is still active. The oldest gravestones are located on the east side, and most are in great condition. The church does an excellent job of maintaining the cemetery grounds.
