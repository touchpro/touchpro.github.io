Oak Dale Cemetery is located at 319 Patrick Avenue in Urbana, Ohio (Urbana Township, Champaign County). The coordinates are 40° 6' 7.28" N, 83° 43' 54.52" W.

Oak Dale Cemetery, located across the road from Grandview Cemetery, was dedicated in 1856. It is large, and it is still active. There are many old gravestones, including several with beautiful statues. It is also the final resting place of Simon Kenton, a Revolutionary War soldier and scout under George Rogers Clark, and John Quincy Adams Ward, a famous American sculptor. Although a few of the older gravestones have been damaged, most of the gravestones are in fantastic condition. The cemetery is very well maintained.
