Jack Loves Me

October 23, 2006



On October 23, 2006 I received an e-mail from Rachel about her interesting ouija board experience:


My name is Rachel, and I have had an experience with an ouija board (on August 23, 2002) that I will always remember!

One night myself (Rachel), my two sisters (Kristy and Chelsea), and my 3 friends (Trisha, Sara, and Vanessa) all stayed the night. Trisha brought over the ouija board. None of us had played the ouija board before, and we all wanted to try. We got the same number of candles to match how many people were there that night. We all sat in the middle of my living room floor and started to play. Kristy and Trisha were the ones with their hands on the planchette.

Trisha: Is there anyone here with us?
Board: Yes

Trisha: What's your name?
Board: Jack

Trisha: How did you die?
Board: Gun

Trisha: When did you die?
Board: 1954

Trisha: Did someone kill you?
Board: No

Trisha: Did you kill yourself?
Board: Yes

Trisha: Why?
Board: azbycx

Trisha: What's that mean?
Board: Love

Trisha: You love someone?
Board: Yes

Trisha: Who?
Board: Rachel

At this point we thought that Kristy and Trisha were moving the planchette, but they claimed they weren't. We couldn't be sure so we decided to blindfold them. Then I (Rachel) began to ask questions...

Rachel: Why do you love me?
Board: I always have

Rachel: Do I know you?
Board: No

Rachel: Then how can you love me?
Board: watching you

Rachel: You watch me?
Board: Yes

Since we were getting freaked out, we decided to ask questions that no one could possibly know about us...not even each other.

Rachel: Where is my birthmark?
Board: Head

Rachel: What kind?
Board: Strawberry

Rachel: How did you know I have a strawberry birthmark?
Board: I know

Rachel: You know I have a boyfriend, right?
Board: Yes

At that point the planchette was flying so fast around the board that it slipped out from under Kristy and Trisha's fingers...we all decided that we wanted to know who was going to die first out of all of us.

Rachel: Do you know who is going to die first out of us?
Board: Sara

Rachel: How?
Board: Car accident

Rachel: With another person?
Board: Yes

Rachel: Who?
Board: Katie

Rachel: Her sister??
Board: Yes

We all started freaking out, and Aara wanted to know when...keep in mind that Trisha and Kristy had blindfolds on...

Rachel: Sara wants to know when.
Board: 22

Rachel: When she's 22?
Board: Yes

Rachel: Why is her sister going to kill her?
Board: Jealous

Sara's sister is very jealous of her, and we all knew that so we got scared...none of us wanted to know who was next to die so we dropped the subject. The planchette started flying all over the board and slipped out from under their fingers again. We all freaked out!

Board: No phone

Board: No phone

As the board kept spelling that out, the phone rang...and it was my (Rachel's) boyfriend Rick...

Board: Kill kill him

Vanessa: Kill who?
Board: Rick

Vanessa: How do you know him?
Board: Rachel

Vanessa: What about her?
Board: Rachel loves Rick

Vanessa: Yea, so?
Board: He must die

Vanessa: Is Rachel going to marry Rick?
Board: No

Vanessa: Who is?
Board: Sara

Vanessa: What?
Board: Sara (as the planchette flew off the board)

Vanessa: Are you a mean spirit?
board: No

Vanessa: Then why are you being violent?
Board: I love her

Board: No phone I said no phone

So I threw the phone...we didn't know how he knew or how to explain it.

Board: Blood

Vanessa: Blood?
Board: Yes 3

Vanessa: 3 what?
Board: People

Vanessa: Us?
Board: Yes

Vanessa: We are going to bleed?
Board: Yes

We decided to take a bathroom break and come back...we just left the board. Vanessa, me, and Sara all went to the bathroom, and to our suprise we all three got our periods. We were freaking out, and I started to cry.

Rachel: How did you know that?
Board: I'm sorry

Rachel: About what?
Board: Crying

Rachel: You're scaring me
Board: I'm sorry

Rachel: If you're sorry stop scaring me!
Board: Don't cry I love you

Rachel: I know...
Board: Don't cry

Rachel: Are you going to haunt me?
Board: No

Rachel: Promise?
Board: Yes

I had stopped crying and the phone rang again, but we picked up. It was Rick, and we told him what happened. He didn't believe us so we told him to come over. He came over and this is what happened...

Rachel: Rick's here.
Board: Fuck Fuck Fuck

Rachel: Fuck what?
Board: Him

Rachel: What do you mean?
Board: Rick LEAVE

Rachel: But I love him. Why does he have to leave?
Board: I'll hurt him

Rachel: Don't hurt him!
Board: Yes

Rachel: He's not leaving.
Board: azbycx

Rachel: What's that mean?
Board: I hate

Rachel: Who?
Board: Rick

Rachel: Why?
Board: He loves you

Rachel: He does?
Board: Yes

Rachel: How do you know?
Board: I know

Rachel: Okay, well are you still going to hurt him?
Board: Yes

Rachel: Why, he is good to me.
Board: I know

Rachel: Then why do you want to hurt him if he is good to me?
Board: Because

Rachel: Because why?
Board: I said so

Rachel: Well don't...if he loves me and you love me then you have something in common, correct?
Board: Yes

Rachel: Then don't hurt him.
Board: I won't

Rachel: Thank you.
Board: I love you

We decided to switch people on the planchette, and it was now Vanessa and Chelsea. Trisha was talking now.

Trisha: Is anything going to happen to us tonight?
Board: Yes

Trisha: What is going to happen?
Board: Death

Trisha: Who's going to die?
Board: Everyone

Trisha: All of us?
Board: Except my love

Trisha: Except Rachel?
Board: Yes

Trisha: Why do we have to die?
Board: I hate you

Trisha: We are going to say goodbye.
Board: Wait...

Trisha: What???
Board: I love you Rachel

Trisha: Goodbye
Board: Goodbye

We have used the board since then, and Jack is still on there. He is haunting the board. And every time he says I love you...and says he is watching me...I always feel like someone really is watching me now.
