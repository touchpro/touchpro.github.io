Four Corners

January 6, 2009



On January 6, 2009 I received an e-mail from Rebecca about an interesting ouija board experience:


Sunday afternoon at around 1:30 PM, my friends Brandi, Karen, and myself decided to try the Ouija Board for our first time. We turned off all the lights and shut the basement door. Now, Karen is slightly afraid of the dark, so when we started actually getting a message she flipped out at random times.

Anyways...4 spirits came through.

Me: Is there a spirit here?
Board: Yes

Me: Are you a good spirit?
Board: Yes

Me: Are you a boy?
Board: No

Me: How old are you?
Board: 6

Brandi: When did you die?
Board: 1496

Me: What is your name?
Board: Eve

Me: Why are you here?
Board: Guarding

Me: Who are you guarding?
Board: Rebecca

That's my name, and I wasn't really shocked. I wanted to keep talking to her, but my friends wanted to know who was guarding them. So we switched. Brandi's guardian was Destiny, and Karen's was Lynn. Destiny was 5 years old when he died, we didn't ask how. Lynn was 6 years old. After we were done there, we switched back to Eve.

Me: Why do I need to be protected?
Board: Evil spirit

(Karen ran out of the room, and decided to sit on the basement steps)

Brandi: Can we speak to this evil spirit?
Board: Yes

Brandi: Is this the evil spirit?
Board: Yes

Me: What is your name?
Board: Daimien (We call him Damien for short)

Me: Are you trying to hurt Eve?
Board: No

Brandi: Then why did she say you were evil?
Board: Broke

Me: What did you break?
Board: I broke her doll

Brandi: Are you evil?
Board: Yes

Me: Are you playing with us?
Board: Maybe

Anyways, we talked to him for a good 15 minutes. We found out that he was her older brother. Not blood related. He was 18 years old. Eve was very upset with him, so I offered to give her my stuffed doll as a replacement. But it got stranger and stranger once we continued our conversation.

Me: Damien, who are you guarding?
Board: Four corners

(Now, four corners is a spot in the woods that me, Brandi, Karen, and Nick made up. Like a club. And also, we were given like elements: Water, Fire, Earth, and Air. Relating to Wicca.)

Brandi: What's my element?
Board: Earth

Brandi: What are Rebecca's and Karen's?
Board: Fire and Air

The fact that he knew so much about us freak me out. And I'm extremely paranoid. I don't know if this was real. I don't know if he could have been lying. And he said that he would send us a sign. Nothing bad has happened yet, but I hope it doesn't.
