Directions to the Louiza Catharine Fox Murder Site



  • From I-70, take the OH-800 exit (I believe it's 202) towards the Egypt Valley Wildlife Area (there are lots of signs for the wildlife area). If you're coming from the west, you'll want to turn left on OH-800. If you're coming from the east, you'll want to turn right on OH-800.
  • Turn right on C-108
  • Turn right on C-100. It's a gravel road, so drive slow!
  • Turn left on TR-546, Starkey Road (yes, there's actually a sign!)
  • You'll eventually see a dirt driveway going up a small hill on the right side of the road...this is where the marker is located.
