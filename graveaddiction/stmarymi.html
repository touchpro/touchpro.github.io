Saint Mary Catholic Cemetery is located in a triangle formed by 12 Mile Road, North Main Street, and Rochester Road in Royal Oak, Michigan (Royal Oak Township, Oakland County). The coordinates are 42° 30' 13" N, 83° 8' 39" W.

Saint Mary Catholic Cemetery was established in 1875. There are many rows of old gravestones, and most are in terrific shape. Most of the stones that had been damaged are now repaired. There is a lot of information about the history of the cemetery located near the middle, where it joins with Royal Oak Cemetery. It is very well maintained by the city.
