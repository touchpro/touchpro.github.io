Kinda Scared

August 12, 2008



On August 12, 2008 I received an e-mail from DJ about an interesting ouija board experience:


This is the transcript, as I remember it.

Me: Is there anyone there who would like to talk to us?
Board: *No movement*

Me: Jake, maybe you should say something since it's your house.
Jake: Alright (he was a little uneasy about doing this because of all the stories he had been reading)

Jake: Is anyone there?
Board: Yes

Me: What is your name?
Board: Irene

Me: How old are you?
Board: 44

Then Jake's mom walked down and the board stopped. But the weird thing was Jake's mom saw the board and she got kinda scared. She asked us if we had been getting anything, so we said no, just because we didn't know how she would take it. I asked if she had ever had a board and she said, "Yeah, but I threw it away." Then I asked if it ever moved, and she said it did, but she thought her sister was the one who was always moving it. Then she left and the board never started moving again.
