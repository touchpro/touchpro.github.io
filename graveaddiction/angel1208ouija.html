Lali

December 27, 2008



On December 27, 2008 I received an e-mail from Angel W. about an interesting ouija board experience:


My friends and I (Adriene, Chey, Ashley, Kayla, and I) were messing with an ouija board one night. Ashley was recording the conversation:

Kayla: Is there anyone here who wishes to talk to us?
Board: Yes.

We took turns asking questions.

Adriene: What's your name?
Board: Lali.

Me: Are you evil, Lali?
Board: No.

Chey: May we ask how you died?
Board: Yes.

Ashley asked questions, too.

Ashley: How did you die?
Board: Stairs.

Kayla: Did you fall?
Board: Yes.

Board: Watch...

Adriene: What do you mean? Watch what?

Adriene's freaked out at this point.

Board: Watch...yourself.

Adriene: Why?
Board: Hurt. Adriene hurt.

We put the board away. The next night we took it out again. ******************************************************************************************

We're listening to the CD player before we take out the board. We shut it off and take out the board.

Me: Is Lali here?
Board: Yes.

Board: I don't want to talk tonight, if you don't mind.

Chey: May we ask one more question?
Board: ONE.

Ashley: How does Adriene get hurt?
Board: Goodbye.

Three weeks later, Devin breaks up.
