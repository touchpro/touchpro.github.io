Gulick Cemetery is located on the south side of Gulick Road, about one mile west of Darbyville-Columbus Road, outside Darbyville, Ohio (Muhlenberg Township, Pickaway County). The coordinates are 39° 43' 38.15" N, 83° 7' 20.48" W.

Gulick Cemetery is very small, and it is no longer active. Although many gravestones are still standing, some have fallen and most are very weathered. However, the cemetery grounds are very well maintained.
