Deep Copper

June 20, 2007



On June 20, 2007 I received an e-mail from Candace B. about an interesting ouija board experience:


I was over at my friend Jamie's house this time, and we were using another homemade ouija board.

Candace: What is your name?
Board: Danielle

Candace: Do you mind me asking your last name?
Board: No. Pigan

Candace: Pigan
Board: Yes

Candace: Hello!
Board: Hi

Candace: What year were you born?
Board: 1767

Jamie: Dang thats a long time! What day and month?
Board: 3 23

Candace: March 23?
Board: Yes

Candace: What year did you die?
Board: 1779

Candace: What day and month?
Board: July 30

Candace: So you were only 12?
Board: Yes

Jamie: (to me) She was so young...
Candace: Yeah, she was...

Candace: Anyway, on a different subject...what is your favorite color?
Board: Deep copper

Candace: Oh thats cool. What's your hair color?
Board: Red

Candace: Eye color?
Board: Green

Candace: How tall were you?
Board: 5' 4"

Candace: That's cool! But...oh wait it's moving.
Board: Goodbye
