The Lutheran Cemetery is located at the intersection of Pearl Road and Biddulph Road in Cleveland, Ohio (Cuyahoga County). The coordinates are 41° 25' 49.81" N, 81° 43' 3.52" W.

The Lutheran Cemetery is large, and it is still active. There are many interesting gravestones, including a few with statues and several homemade monuments. Although a few gravestones have been damaged, most are in very good condition. The cemetery is very well maintained.
