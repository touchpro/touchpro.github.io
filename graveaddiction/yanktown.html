Yankeetown Cemetery is located on the north side of Cook-Yankeetown Road, just west of the Pickaway County line, outside Mount Sterling, Ohio (Madison Township, Fayette County). The coordinates are 39° 39' 31.03" N, 83° 15' 21.84" W.

Yankeetown Cemetery is no longer active. The cemetery sits at the top of a large hill near Dear Creek; there is a beautiful view. There are many old gravestones at the cemetery, and most are in good condition. However, like any old cemetery, many of the stones have been broken/damaged over the years. The township does an excellent job of maintaining the cemetery.
