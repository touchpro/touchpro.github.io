Harrod, Ohio

Ridge Cemetery is located at the intersection of Lawrence Road and Clum Road outside Harrod, Ohio (Auglaize Township, Allen County). The coordinates are 40° 43' 4" N, 83° 54' 0" W.

Ridge Cemetery is small, and it is no longer active. There are many old gravestones, and most are in great condition; only a few have been damaged. Several of the gravestones have interesting carvings. The cemetery is very well maintained.
