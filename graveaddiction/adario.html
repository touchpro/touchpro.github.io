The Adario Cemetery is located at the intersection of Adario Road and Olivesburg-Fitchville Road in Adario, Ohio (Butler Township, Richland County). The coordinates are 40° 55' 51.42" N, 82° 26' 49.87" W.

The Adario Cemetery is still active. The oldest gravestones are located closest to the church, and most are in good condition. I did notice that some of the old marble gravestones had been painted white though; I'm not sure how the paint will react with the stone over time. The cemetery grounds are very well maintained.
