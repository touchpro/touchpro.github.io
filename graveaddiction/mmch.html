Marty sent me the following information about this house:

"This is the Corner House, where it is said a man once came home to his wife to find she hadn't fixed breakfast. As a result, he promptly chopped off her head and stuck it in the oven. You can supposidly hear the screams and smell burnt flesh here at the house. However, my friend and I didn't see or experience anything out of the ordinary when we went to check it out."
