Killed

February 21, 2009



On February 21, 2009 I received an e-mail from Tony and Richie about an interesting ouija board experience:


Our names are Tony and Richie (we have both have changed our names). The other day (2/20-21/09) Richie had Tony over and they were playing with a Ouija Board. Here's what happened:

Us: Is anyone here that would like to speak?
Ouija: Yes

Us: What is yor name?
Ouija: T

Us: Does that stand for Tyler?
Ouija: No

Us: Is your name Timmy?
Ouija: no

Us: Spell it for us.
Ouija: T-i-m

Us: Do you not like the name Timmy?
Ouija: Yes

Us: How did you die?
Ouija: (No answer)

Us: Are you in this room with us?
Ouija: Yes

Us: Who are you closer too: Richie or Tony?
Ouija: R

Us: Do you dislike us?
Ouija: Yes

Us: Are you from the moon or the sun?(Moon=Bad and Sun=Good)
Ouija: (Moves to moon)

Us: Goodbye
Ouija: (Moves to Goodbye)

-----We come back later-----

Us: Tim, are you still there?
Ouija: Yes

Us: Do you still hate us?
Ouija: Yes

Us: Well, how did you die?
Ouija: K (And holds it there)

Us: Were you knifed?
Ouija: No

Us: Were you killed?
Ouija: No

Us: Were you a KKK member?
Ouija: No

Us: Were you killed by a KKK member?
Ouija: Yes

Us: Were you burned?
Ouija: Yes

Us: Are you African-American? (No racism intended)
Ouija: Yes

Us: Do you hate white people?
Ouija: Yes

Us: Did anybody else you know die?
Ouija: Yes

Us: Was it your family?
Ouija: No

Us: Was it friends?
Ouija: Yes

Us: Were they burned?
Ouija: No

Us: Were they hanged?
Ouija: Yes

Us: Do you want to kill us?
Ouija: Yes

Us: What is your last name?
Ouija: P

----After that we decided to leave, but we will probably come back for more.----
