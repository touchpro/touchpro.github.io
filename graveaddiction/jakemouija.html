Life on Other Planets

June 20, 2007



On June 20, 2007 I received an e-mail from Jake M. about an interesting ouija board experience:


Blake and I were sitting there playing the board. We were bored and we had no idea what to ask. So I asked a random question. The question was "Is there life on other planets?" The board went to yes. Right after that, Blake looked into the sky (it was around 11:15 PM) and noticed two lights that seemed to be stars, but they were following each other at the exact same speed, distance, everything. We thought it might be something long with something attached to its front, or maybe two planes following each other. But we thought if it was two planes, they wouldn't be that close together. As we watched it fly over us, we just couldn't believe what we were seeing! But after a few minutes both lights faded away at the same time. So there's no way it could have been two planes, because the first light would have disappeared first, then the second light.
