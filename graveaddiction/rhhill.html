Reynoldsburg, Ohio

The Hill Road Methodist Cemetery is located off Waggoner Road in Reynoldsburg, Ohio near the intersection with Main Street. It is located on a small hill to the north of the VFW Hall.

The Hill Road Methodist Cemetery is also known as the Reynoldsburg Cemetery and the Methodist Hill Cemetery. The cemetery isn't in very good condition, in fact, it has been classified as an abandoned cemetery. The majority of the headstones are broken, there are stacks on broken pieces throughout the entire cemetery. There are three fenced sections of the cemetery, and there are lots of trees. Even though the condition is far from good, this cemetery is awesome! All of the trees and broken headstones make it look really creepy!
