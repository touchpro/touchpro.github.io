Spirit of '76

Marblehead, Massachusetts





The "Spirit of '76" painting is located in the Reading Room at Abbot Hall in Marblehead, Massachusetts. It was painted by Archibald M. Willard, an artist from Cleveland, Ohio. After it was displayed in Philadelphia in 1876, General John Devereux bought the painting and gave it to the town of Marblehead, his hometown.
