Harmony Cemetery is located on the south side of Township Road 233 outside Maxville, Ohio (Monday Creek Township, Perry County). The coordinates are 39° 37' 59" N, 82° 22' 34.5" W.

Harmony Cemetery is small, but it is still active. The Harmony United Brethren Church used to stand next to the cemetery. There are many old gravestones, and the oldest are located on the southwest side. The township does an excellent job of maintaining the cemetery.
