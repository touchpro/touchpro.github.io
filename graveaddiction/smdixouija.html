I'm Now A Spirit

February 24, 2006



On February 24, 2006 I received an e-mail from Brianne about their interesting ouija board experience:


"My cousin, her boyfriend, her friend, and I played the ouija board on February 20, 2006. We ended up getting in touch with Dug Hole. It was a ton of fun talking to him! But when my cousin's boyfriend left, something strange happened. I told Dug to wait a minute because the others had left to say bye to my cousin's boyfriend. When they came back, I said, "Dug, did you know that you told us a few lies tonight?" The board answered, "No. What." I said, "You told us that Jacob's (my cousin's boyfriend) mom was going to be late and that Lindsey (my cousin) and Jacob were going to kiss tonight. None of that happened." The board replied, "I'm not Doug." I said, "Who are you?" It told us that it was my cousin's sister. I told it that my little cousin was not a spirit, and it told us that she was now a spirit. We ended the session and called Lindsey's mom and sister. They were okay."
