Asbury Cemetery is located on the east side of Sells Road, just south of Marietta Road, in Lancaster, Ohio (Berne Township, Fairfield County). The coordinates are 39° 43' 18.76" N, 82° 33' 24.1" W.

Asbury Cemetery is small, and it is no longer active. It is located behind Sanderson Elementary School. Although many gravestones are still standing, just as many have been damaged. The grounds are very well maintained by the township.
