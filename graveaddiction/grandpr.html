Grand Prairie Cemetery is located at the junction of OH-423, CR-67, and OH-231 outside Morral, Ohio (Grand Prairie Township, Marion County). It is also known as Brush Ridge Cemetery, and it is technically in the unincorporated community of Brush Ridge. The coordinates are 40° 41' 25.3" N, 83° 9' 13.1" W.

Grand Prairie Cemetery is still active. There are quite a few old, interesting gravestones. When I visited at the end of September 2004, it appeared that there were some restoration efforts underway. The Grand Prairie Cemetery Association takes terrific care of the cemetery; there are very few broken gravestones and the grounds look fantastic!
