Chad Agin sent me the following information about his visit to Royer's Chapel Cemetery:

My friends and I, Jason Hummel and Kyle Hamilton, went to find Mary Stockum's grave. We where told to go from SR-83 to CR-280 and go left at the first y in the road go to the second intersection and take a right then a left on the first road and that should be it. Park and walk for a while. Well we didn't make very far, we didn't even make it to the first intersection before we blew a tire. So we back tracked to SR-83, and where CR-280 meets SR-83 there is Royer Chapel and Cemetery. It was still kind of light out, so we checked out the graveyard. Please tell me what you think of the photos. Take a real close look at DSCN0233 - there is a small headstone just behind and to the left of the largest one in the middle of the pic. It looks like an orb, and on the small headstone when you zoom in it looks like a skull orb. The one with the smoke - I had just exhaled a hit from a cig, but it still got some orb I think. I am new at this, so I keep checking the lens of the camera for spots and there are not any. Oh yeah, to beat all we sat at Royer for four hours waiting on someone to bring us a tire because we didn't have a spare. We felt like we where being watched the whole time. Some really crazy stuff has happened every time we go to find her grave.
