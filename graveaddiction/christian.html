The Christian Cemetery is located on the west side of Water Street, north of US-22, in Williamsport, Ohio (Deer Creek Township, Pickaway County). The coordinates are 39° 35' 12.62" N, 83° 7' 11.69" W.

The Christian Cemetery was used from 1803-1886. It is no longer active. There are many rows of old gravestones, and most are in excellent condition. Most of the gravestones that had previously been broken are now repaired. There are many war veterans buried in the cemetery. The grounds are very well maintained.
