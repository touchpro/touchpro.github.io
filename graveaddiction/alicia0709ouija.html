A Warning from Hell

July 16, 2009



On July 16, 2009 I received an e-mail from Alicia about an interesting ouija board experience:


My friend Bonnie and I (Alicia) were on a ouija board on a Thursday night, 7/16/2009. We talked to a spirit from hell.

Bon: Are there any spirits in the house?
Board: yes

Bon: What is your name?
Board: Hamish

Bon: Hamish?
Board: yes

Me: How old were you when you died?
Hamish: 32

Bon: What year did you die?
Hamish: 1940

Me: How did you die?
The cup circled around the board about 3 times before we asked another question.

Me: Murder?
Hamish: kind of

Bon: wtf what does that mean?
Me: I don't know!

Me: Did a human kill you?
Hamish: yes

Bon: Who?
Hamish: offisd

Bon: Offisd?
Hamish: no

Me: Who killed you?
Hamish: guards

Me: Oh, did you mean officers?
Hamish: yes

Me: Officers of what?
Hamish: jail

Me: Were you in jail?
Hamish: yes

Bon: Sentenced to death?
Hamish: yes

Bon: What for?
Hamish: murder

Me: Are you in hell?
Hamish: yes

Bon: Are you a bad spirit?
Hamish: no

Me: Is there anything you want to tell us?
Hamish: yes

Me: Do you want to tell Alicia or Bonnie?
Hamish: both

Bon: What is it?
Hamish: stop

Me: Stop what?
Hamish: sinning

Me: What will happen if we don't?
Hamish: hell hell hell

Bon: What is hell like?
Hamish: hell

Me: Yes, what is it like?
The cup circled the board again

Bon: Is hell forever?
Hamish: yes

Me: How many people go to hell?
Hamish: most

Me: What is worst about hell?
Hamish: satan satan satan

Bon: What does he do to you?
Hamish: torture

Me: How?
Hamish: Burn

Me: Any other ways?
Hamish: yes

Bon: Can you tell our future?
Hamish: yes

Me: Do any of us go to hell?
The cup moved slightly towards yes, but then hesitated and spun around the board quickly. It then moved to goodbye.
