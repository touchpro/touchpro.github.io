Using the Ouija at a Bed and Breakfast

November 29, 2004



On November 29, 2004 I received an e-mail from Richard Broadhead about a cool ouija experience he had while staying at a Bed and Breakfast:


"We were bored so we decided to make a board and play it...only after I read your stories and loved them!

We made the board out of and old set of cards that had letters instead of the original and made a yes or no.

John: Are there any spirits in the house?
Board: yes

John: Can you spell your name?
Board: FI

(Board went dead so we put the cards away. We then started placing the cards out face down and turning them over 1 by 1. The cards had 3 of every vowel and 2 of every other letter except w and x only 1 of those. We don't know how it happened. I've edited it to just show the messages)

John: What was your name?
Board: fiona

John: How did you die?
Board: fiona murdered in this house

john and me: Were you murdered or was it suicide?
Board: killed by man

John: what room did you die in?
Board: i died in room..(we got freaked out i was in 9 he was in 10) TEN

(we kept trying but it was a while before the messages started again)

John: where did you live?
Board: huddersfield (where i lived)

John: how did you die?
Board: strangled for sex (strangled spelt backwards)

John: how old were you?
Board: i was eighteen

John: what year did you die?
Board: two thousand

John: do you want to tell us anything?
Board: enough, said goodbye

That was the end but we still can't explain it. Like I said, I edited all the words out leaving just the messages. But if that isn't wierd I dunno what is!"

On January 4, 2005 Richard sent me an update to his story:

"After re-reading an email I checked up and guess what...there WAS a Fiona who stayed in the coach house! She WAS strangled for sex, but it wasn't until I learned the exact date. The land lady I know now took over in 2000. I was suspicious enough to check it out. Wanna hear the scariest bit? She died on August 22nd...MY BIRTHDAY!! How freaky is that?! I couldn't belive it! I found this out a couple of weeks ago and thought you may wanna know that it is true. I used to be an atheist...trust me when I say I'm not anymore!"
