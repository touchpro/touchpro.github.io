Byhalia Cemetery is located on both sides of Lingrell Road, just west of OH-31, in Byhalia, Ohio (Washington Township, Union County). The coordinates are 40° 27' 48" N, 83° 28' 2" W.

Byhalia Cemetery is large, and it is still active. The oldest gravestones are located on the south side of the road, and most are in good condition. The township does an excellent job of maintaining the cemetery.
