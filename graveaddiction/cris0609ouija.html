First Time

June 11, 2009



On June 11, 2009 I received an e-mail from Cris about an interesting ouija board experience:


My name is Cris, and I used the ouija board for the first time on June 2nd. I have visited your website for awhile, and wanted to try using the ouija board. I made my own out of paper, and I used a shot glass for a planchette.

Me: Is there anyone out there who would like to speak to me?
Board: Yes

Me: Welcome, my name is Cris.
Board: I know

Me: How do you know?
Board: Follow

Me: You followed me?
Board: Yes

Me: From where did you follow me?
Board: Friend

Me: Was it one of my friends?
Board: Yes

Me: Can you tell me which one?
Board: Yes Ale

Me: Do you mean Alejandra E?
Board: Yes

Me: Where you following her?
Board: Yes

Me: Are you related to her?
Board: No

Me: What are you to her?
Board: Friend

Me: Does she know you?
Board: No I just followed her

Me: Oh, so you want to be her friend?
Board: Yes

Me: Can I take a 5 minute break?
Board: Yes goodbye

After that, I went back on and asked if it was still there.

Me: Is there anybody here that would like to speak to me?
Board: Yes

Me: Is this the same person I was talking to earlier?
Board: Yes

Me: Can I have your name?
Board: Yes Cristina

Me: Oh, so you are a female spirit?
Board: Yes

Me: How old are you?
Board: 14

Me: Me too. How did you die?
Board: Gun

Me: When did this happen?
Board: 1972

Me: I'm sorry.
Board: It's okay

Me: So why did you follow me?
Board: Cute

Me You think I'm cute?
Board: Yes very

Me: Oh, that's nice to hear.
Board: You be my friend

Me: Yes, I will be your friend.
Board: Yes

Me: Did you want to talk to Alejandra when you followed her?
Board: Yes

Me: Are you mad at her for not talking to you?
Board: No

Me: Well, are you good or bad?
Board: Good

Me: That's good to hear.
Board: Tired

Me: Ok, then I will let you go to Goodbye, Cristina.
Board: Friend Goodbye (it was moving slow)

This is my ouija board story.
