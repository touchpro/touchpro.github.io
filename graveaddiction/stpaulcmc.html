Saint Pauls Catholic Cemetery is located at the intersection of Watkins Road and Meiring Road in Sharpsburg, Ohio (Gibson Township, Mercer County). The coordinates are 40° 22' 0.56" N, 84° 42' 43.95" W.

Saint Pauls Catholic Cemetery is still active. It sits behind the church. There are many old gravestones, and several have beautiful carvings. Most are in very good condition, and many of the gravestones that had been damaged are now repaired. Many are set on long concrete bases. The cemetery is very well maintained by the church.
