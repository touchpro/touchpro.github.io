The Fort Street Cemetery is located on the west side of Fort Street, north of West Franklin Street, in Nelsonville, Ohio (York Township, Athens County). The coordinates are 39° 27' 46" N, 82° 13' 58" W.

The Fort Street Cemetery is no longer active. There are many old gravestones, and although most are in good condition, several have been damaged. Daniel Nelson, the founder of Nelsonville, is buried here. The cemetery is very well maintained.
