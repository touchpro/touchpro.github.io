Green Lawn Cemetery Ghost Photo

Columbus, Ohio



Photo taken by Gary W. Royer



Gary W. Royer took this photo at Green Lawn Cemetery in Columbus, OH. Specifically, the photo was taken at the Soldiers' Memorial (the one with cannons) on June 15, 2005 at about 11:30 AM.

If you look closely, you can see a ghostly soldier in the picture. He is in the tree behind the cannon, about half way up. The first thing that stands out is his rolled-up sleeve. He is standing with his back against the tree trunk.
