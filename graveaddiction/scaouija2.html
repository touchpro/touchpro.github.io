Spirit In Love

May 30, 2006



On May 30, 2006 I received an e-mail from Alicia about an interesting ouija board experience she had with her friends:


We used it last night and this is our recording.

May 29th, 2006

Session 1 - 13 year old at the board

Alicia and Carly on the board, Main speaker: Carly

Is there anyone out there who would like to speak with us tonight?
Board: YES

What is your name?
Board: {letters don't make sense}

Are you happy?
Board: YES

When were you born?
Board: 1976

When did you die?
Board: 1989

Are there any other spirits in the house?
Board: YES

How many?
Board: 4

Are there any other spirits that visit here?
Board: 9

Do you ever talk to them?
Board: YES

Do you like them?
Board: YES

Do you like all of them?
Board: MOST

Do you want to talk to our mom?
Board: NO

Is it because of the age difference?
Board: YES

Are you tired of talking to us?
Board: YES

Goodbye.

Session 2: I LUV U

Alicia and Carly on the board, Main speaker: Alicia

Is there anybody who would like to speak with us?
Board: YES

What do you want to say?
Board: I LOVE YOU

My sister{Carly} or myself{Alicia}?
Board: YOU

I'm flattered, but.....what's your name?
Board: FRED

Fred, I like you.......so far....as well.....
Board: ARIGATOU

When were you born?
Board: 1989

When did you die?
Board: 1998

You don't ever follow me around, do you?
Board: I TRY NOT TO

Do you ever get jealous?
Board: YES

Of who?
Board: EVERE1 {everyone}

When did you fall in love with me?
Board: 2005

How long have you been here?
Board: 5

Years?
Board: YES

Did you know me before I moved here?
Board: NO

Did you ever think you would contact me?
Board: NO, I LUV THIS

Do you ever talk with the other spirits here?
Board: NO

Why?
Board: I DONT CARE FOR

You mean you don't care for them?
Board: YES, I LUV U

I think we've already established that. It's getting pretty late....can we leave?

Board: NO
Ummm........it's late....is it alright if we close this session?

Board: YES
We'll talk to you later...maybe tomorrow.

Board: OH GOOD...I LUV U
Do you want to say goodbye?

Board: *slides over goodbye*

That was last night........it was kind of creepy. Can spirits fall in love with you? I don't think so.


UPDATE

In August 2006 I received an e-mail from Michael Williams regarding this story:

I was reading the Ouija stories, and I read the one titled "Spirit in Love." I don't know if the submitter knew it, but the spirit talked in Japanese:

Fred, I like you.......so far....as well.....
Board: ARIGATOU

That means "Thank You" in Japanese, so they could have been talking to a Japanese spirit. Just thought it was some good trivia for your site or something the person should know.
