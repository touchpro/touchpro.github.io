Pedophile

November 22, 2006



On November 22, 2006 I received an e-mail from Cece about her interesting ouija board experience:


Well, my friend got an Ouija board and brought it over to surprise me. She knew I was kinda scared of paranormal stuff, but she wanted to force me to use it.

So when we pulled it out, we turned the light off and put our hands on the planchette. My hand got really cold, like someone put ice water on it and then dried it off with cold air.

Well, we asked our spirit his name, and he said it was "Shiv." We knew it was an odd name, but we kind of forgot about it and went on to the next question.

Me: How old were you when you died?
Shiv: 23

Me: How did you die?
Shiv: Gun

Me: Where did you die?
Shiv: House

Me: Where did you live?
Shiv: Nebraska

Me: What is your last name?
Shiv: Denman

My friend wanted to get off the topic of Shiv and ask a funny question...see, I have an online friend named Barry, and she thinks he's a pedophile. So she asked "Is Barry a pedophile?", and he said "Yes."

My friend was kind of confused about the name Shiv, so she asked, "Is Shiv your real name?" He said no, and she asked, "What is your real name?" He said, "Barry."

We asked him if he was talking about himself back when we asked if Barry a pedophile, and he said yes.

So we uh...talked to a dead pedophile.
