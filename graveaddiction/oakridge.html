Oak Ridge Cemetery is located at 1441 Monument Avenue in Springfield, Illinois (Sangamon County). The coordinates are 39° 49' 29" N, 89° 39' 33" W.

Oak Ridge Cemetery was established in 1860, and it is still active. It is the second most visited cemetery in the United States, behind Arlington National Cemetery. Why? Because Oak Ridge Cemetery is the final resting place of President Abraham Lincoln. His tomb is a very popular destination for visitors. There are many other notable people buried there, and there are many interesting monuments and statues. The cemetery is very well maintained.
