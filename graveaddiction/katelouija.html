Talking with an Evil Spirit

February 13, 2005



On February 13, 2005 I received an e-mail from Katelyn Anderson about the time she and a friend contacted a scary spirit:


"One night, about 12:00, me and a friend were using the ouija board. Some strange things happened...

Katelyn: Is anyone there?
Board: Yes

Addy: What's your name?

Addy: How old are you?
Board: 4

Katelyn: Are you in this room?

Addy: If you're in this room make the candle go out..
Board: No response

...candle blows out and we are in pitch dark..addy turns on the light....we light 2 more candles

katelyn: Peter are you still there?
Board: Yes

Katelyn's dog opens door and comes in and starts to bark at nothing and all candles blow out....ouija board is glow in the dark and starts to scatter everywhere....after about 10 seconds it slams itself against the wall..after a few screams come out...

we play again

Katelyn: Is anyone out there
Board: Cody

Addy: Cody, are you a good or bad spirt, go to the sun if your good and the moon if you are evil
Board speeds to the moon

Katelyn: Why are you evil
Board: no repsonse

Addy: ANSWER!
Board: Don't remember

Katelyn: Okay goodbye
Board: NO

Addy: Leave us alone
Board: hell no

Board: Look behind you
Addy: KATELYN IM SCARED

Katelyn: Nothing is there
Board: yes

Board: just watch
Katelyn: no

things scatter off dresser"
