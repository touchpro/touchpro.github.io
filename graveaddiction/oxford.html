Oxford Cemetery is located on the west side of Oxford Road (US-27), about ¾ mile south of Oxford, Ohio (Oxford Township, Butler County). The coordinates are 39° 29' 46.45" N, 84° 43' 47.04" W.

Oxford Cemetery was established in 1855, and it is still active. It's a large cemetery, with plenty of old gravestones to look at. Many faculty members and students from nearby Miami University are buried here as well. The cemetery is very well maintained by the Oxford Cemetery Association.
