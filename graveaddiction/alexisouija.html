Zaza and Nero

December 1, 2005



On December 1, 2005 I received an e-mail from Alexis about her interesting ouija board experience:


My friend, Nicole, and I have also encountered evil spirits. One who goes by Zaza, and the other called Nero. After the first time we spoke to Zaza, Nicole had stayed the night. When we woke up, I had scratches on my chest, shoulders and face. I was so freaked out! We now have a spirit named Nero who won't leave our board, and when we speak to him he just keeps going to "Ouija, Ouija 666," or sometimes he says 616 instead. He claims he is the devil. He is threatening to kill us, so we made a homemade board and we have a new spirit named Nestin. He keeps warning us to get out of this house because Nero is here, and Nero is going to kill us.
