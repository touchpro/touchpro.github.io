Bacher-Baugher Cemetery is located on the west side of Brook Road, just north of Ginder Road, outside Carroll, Ohio (Greenfield Township, Fairfield County). The coordinates are 39° 47' 1.83" N, 82° 38' 10" W.

Bacher-Baugher Cemetery is the final resting place of four Revolutionary War veterans. There are many old gravestones, and most are in good condition. There is a pile of broken gravestones at the very back of the cemetery, and there are a few broken gravestones throughout the cemetery. That's not uncommon for such an old cemetery though. Overall the cemetery is in fantastic condition, the township does an excellent job of maintaining it. Saint Paul German and English Evangelical Lutheran Church was located beside the cemetery until it was destroyed in the 1930's.
