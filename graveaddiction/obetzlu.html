Obetz Cemetery is located on the south side of Groveport Road, about a ½ mile north of I-270, in Obetz, Ohio (Hamilton Township, Franklin County). The coordinates are 39° 52' 33.56" N, 82° 56' 53.77" W.

Obetz Cemetery, also known as Zion's Lutheran Cemetery, was established in 1868. It is very large, and it is still active. There are many old gravestones throughout the cemetery, and most are in great condition. Like any old cemetery though, there are a few that have been damaged/broken. The village does a great job of maintaining the cemetery.
