Central Cemetery is located on the west side of Main Street, just north of Villa Road, in New Ipswich, New Hampshire (Hillsborough County).

Central Cemetery is large, and it is still active. There are many beautiful carvings, including many slate stones with winged skulls and soul effigies. There are also many gravestones with interesting epitaphs. The cemetery is very well maintained.
