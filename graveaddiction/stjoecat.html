Saint Joseph Cemetery is located on the east side of US-23, north of Rowe Road, in Lockbourne, Ohio (Hamilton Township, Franklin County). The coordinates are 39° 49' 27.25" N, 82° 59' 52.78" W.

Saint Joseph Cemetery, also referred to as Saint Joseph's Catholic Cemetery, was formally established in 1912. It is very large, and it is still active. The oldest gravestones are located towards the front of the cemetery, and all of them are in excellent shape. There are also many unique, beautiful statues. The cemetery is extremely well maintained by The Catholic Diocese of Columbus.
