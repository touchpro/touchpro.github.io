Liberty Center, Ohio

Wright Cemetery is located on the south side of Cherry Street, almost a ½ mile west of OH-109, in Liberty Center, Ohio (Liberty Township, Henry County). I wasn't able to find coordinates for this cemetery.

Wright Cemetery was established in 1849, and although it's a small cemetery, it still appears to be active. The oldest gravestones are located towards the back. Many have been broken and damaged over the years, but the grounds are still very well taken care of.

Many thanks to Mike McMaster for taking me to this cemetery!
