Ebenezer Methodist Episcopal Cemetery is located on the south side of Johnson Road, just west of Norton Road, outside Galloway, Ohio (Pleasant Township, Franklin County). The coordinates are 39° 53' 28.06" N, 83° 9' 42.36" W.

Ebenezer M.E. Cemetery is a beautiful little cemetery that sits west of Hellbranch Run. It is surrounded by an iron fence. The old church used to be across the street from the cemetery. It has now been renovated and is used as a house.

The cemetery is in great condition, it is very well taken care of by the Pleasant Township Trustees.
