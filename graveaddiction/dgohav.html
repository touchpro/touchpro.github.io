Diane G. sent me the following e-mail about the Congregation Ohav Sholom Cemetery:

Congregation Ohav Sholom is located at 113 New Krumkill Road in Albany, New York. It is very small, and it is located on a hill. I couldn't find any information on this cemetery, other than the story of the young man who died on October 7, 2007 at the Pentagon. He was 200 yards from the finish line of an Army 10 mile race. So tragic, God bless his friends and family.
