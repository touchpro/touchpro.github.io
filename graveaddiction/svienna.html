South Vienna Cemetery is located on the north side of US-40, about a half mile west of OH-54, outside South Vienna Ohio (Harmony Township, Clark County). You can see the cemetery to the south when driving on I-70.

South Vienna cemetery is a beautiful cemetery that is very well taken care of. There is a great view of the surrounding farms from the cemetery. It looked like this is still an active cemetery. There are many interesting gravestones to look at, and there are two gorgeous angel statues.
