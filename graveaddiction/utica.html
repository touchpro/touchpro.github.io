Utica Cemetery is located on the west side of OH-13, right at the county line, in Utica, Ohio (Washington Township, Licking County). The coordinates are 40° 14' 36.76" N, 82° 27' 9.68" W.

Utica cemetery is no longer active. The oldest gravestones are located towards the back of the cemetery, and although several have been damaged, there are still many that are in good condition. The cemetery grounds are very well maintained by the township.
