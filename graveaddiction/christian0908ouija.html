Stu the Sniper

September 3, 2008



On September 3, 2008 I received an e-mail from Christian B. about an interesting ouija board experience:


I just got my ouija board last month, and it is already starting to scare me.

When I finally managed to summon a ghost, he said his name was Stu. I asked him if he was a good spirit. Nope. But I didn't care. I was just happy to talk to him, to finally have a spirit to converse with. Apparently, he lived in the 1400's, died at 25 during a fight (he lost), and had 4 children. I asked to speak to one, but I was denied access. So much for that. It was hard to get rid of him, but I promised to talk to him later.

I did talk with him later, but I avoided all questions that I figured would make him mad. I later stopped talking to him completely. Every time I got him on the board, I'd kindly ask him to leave. After a few times, he must have deemed this rude, because he wouldn't let anyone else talk to me. So I started talking to him again. Stupidly, I asked if I could take a picture of him. He agreed, but nothing I could detect showed up in the picture. However, a few days later I began seeing figures late at night. I thought maybe I was just tired, but Stu insisted it was him. I stopped using my board, but he kept showing up.

I recently used it again, but instead of the planchette moving, I heard a grown man's voice. I was the only one in the house, all TVs off. Like a moron, I was too scared to understand what he said. A day after, I was typing some stuff up on my computer, midafternoon. I looked at my hand and saw a red dot from something like a laser pointer. I freaked out, thinking I was going to get shot. I have not spoken to Stu since, because I have a hunch he did it. Hey, he had every reason to.

I owe him a an apology, I guess. Hehehe...Sorry, Stu!
