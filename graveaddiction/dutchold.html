Dutch Hollow Cemetery (Old) is located on the south side of US-22, just west of Sixteenth Road, outside Amanda, Ohio (Clear Creek Township, Fairfield County). The coordinates are 39° 38' 46.98" N, 82° 47' 27.12" W. Dutch Hollow Cemetery (New) is located directly across the road. This cemetery is also known as the Israel Lutheran Cemetery.

Dutch Hollow Cemetery (Old) is very well taken care of. There are several gravestones with German epitaphs, and even though they're old, they are still very legible. Like all older cemeteries, there are several broken gravestones.
