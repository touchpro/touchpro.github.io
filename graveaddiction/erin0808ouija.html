Raid

August 15, 2008



On August 15, 2008 I received an e-mail from Erin S. about an interesting ouija board experience:


A friend (Jessica) and I (Erin) were sitting in my bedroom, where we have had a certain spirit talk to us. Her name is Emily, and she is 15. We have had trouble getting her to communicate with us. So we are going to try again. Anything said, or moved, will be recorded.

Started: 8:38
End: 10:00


Erin: Is there a spirit here who wants to talk to us? We have movement.
Spirit: Yes.

Erin: What is your name?
Spirit: (no movement)

Erin: Could you please tell me what your name is?
Spirit: Jen.

Jessica: This is not the spirit we talked to before.

Erin: How old are you Jen?
Spirit: 16

Erin: In what year did you die?
Spirit: 1812

Erin: Did you see the war that happened in 1812? (Talking to Jessica) In history, the War of 1812. Don't you remember?
Spirit: Yes.
Jessica: No

Erin: Yes to you were in the war?
Spirit: Yes.

Erin: What did you do to help out in the war?
Spirit: Nurse

Erin: How did you die?
Spirit: Raid. (Somewhat confused, because it was between D and E)

Erin: Did you spell out raid?
Spirit: Yes.

Erin: Did the raid happen at the nursing quarters you were serving at?
Spirit: No.

Erin: Where did this happen?
Spirit: House.

Erin: So this happened at your home?
Spirit: Yes.

Erin: Jessica, can you take over for a second?
Jessica: Sure.

Jessica: Hello
Spirit: (Small movements)

Erin: It stopped for like a second there.
Spirit: (Movement) hi

Jessica: Is this still Jen?
Spirit: Yes

Jessica: Where is your home?
Spirit: (Creaking coming from board) Resffsesf

Erin: Jen, could you please give us and actual place?
Spirit: Qcrerdpqc

Jessica: Are you trying to spell an actual word or not?
Spirit: Yes

Jessica: Are you a bad speller?
Spirit: No

Jessica: Are you trying to spell something in another language?
Spirit: Yes

Jessica: What language?
Spirit: Fiviv

Erin: I am going to ask you, could you please spell clearly?
Spirit: No

Erin: Why won't you speak clearly for us?
Spirit: Xhvil

Erin: Do you have a problem with Jessica?
Spirit: No

Jessica: Do you just have bad English skills?
Spirit: No

Jessica: Please answer me so I can understand.
Spirit: Xkwjvhug (Board was moving faster) twxfvrdqcpboan

Jessica: What, are you 16 or 6?
(This happened for a while, I have ceased recording because she would not stop)

Jessica: Is this Jen?
Spirit: No

Jessica: It's not Jen, and she's doing it again.
(Multiple letters happened again)

Jessica: Erin? Do you want to try this?

Erin: Well, I guess I'm going to have to. I don't know what she's doing.
(Jessica and Erin switched places)

Erin: Jen, are you here?
Spirit: Yes.

Erin: Who was talking to Jessica before?
Spirit: Sister.

Erin: What's your little sister's name?
Spirit: Jess.

Jessica: That's f***ing creepy. Did she die in the same raid as you?
Spirit: No

Erin: When did Jess die? Wow we have movement.
Spirit: 1810

Jessica: What did Jess die of?
Spirit: Sick.

Erin: What did she die of?
Spirit: don't (Planchette went to no)

Erin: Did you mean to say don't know?
Spirit: Yes

Erin: So you don't know the name of the sickness she died of?
Spirit: Yes.

Jessica: Were you with her when she died?
Spirit: No

Jessica: Where were you?
Spirit: home

Erin: Where was your sister?
Spirit: (no movement)

Erin: Jen, would you mind telling me where you lived during the war?
Spirit: Here

Erin: So you lived in Green Bay?
Spirit: Yes

Erin: Was it called Green Bay at the time?
Spirit: No

Jessica: What was it called?
Spirit: (no movement)

Erin: Jen, are you still here?
Spirit: Yes

Jessica: Jen, what was it called when you lived here?
Spirit: Bay

Jessica: Is there any more to it?
Spirit: No

Erin: Do you know where the war was held?
Spirit: No

Jessica: How could you have been a nurse in the war if you didn't know where it was?
Spirit: I was taken.

Jessica: Blindfolded?
Spirit: No

Jessica: Didn't anybody tell you?
Spirit: No

Erin: Who were you taken by?
Spirit: Men in suits

Jessica: Army suits or real suits?
Spirit: Army

Jessica: How old was your sister when she died?
Spirit: 6

Jessica: Why did she keep following the letters on the board? Would you know?
Spirit: Illiterate

Erin: Why did these men take you away?
Spirit: Help

Jessica: Help healing people?
Spirit: Yes

Erin: Why did they need to take you?
Spirit: Mama wouldn't let them

Jessica: Your mama?
Spirit: Yes

Erin: Why wouldn't she let you?
Spirit: Death

Erin: Was she afraid you would die?
Spirit: Yes

Erin: And they took you anyway?
Spirit: Yes

Erin: What did they do to your mother?
Spirit: Killed her.

Erin: That's terrible, and then they took you?
Spirit: Yes

Erin: Did you watch them kill her?
Spirit: Yes

Erin: What did they do to her?
Spirit: Shot her

Erin: It's moving back and forth.

Jessica: Were you killed at the same time as your mother, or a different time?
Spirit: No

Erin: Did they let you go back home?
Spirit: Yes

Erin: Did they not need your services anymore?
Spirit: No

Jessica: Ok, so what was with the raid...did they come back to get you?
Spirit: No

Jessica: Ok, then what happened?
Spirit: Baby

Jessica: Your baby?
Spirit: Yes

Erin: Were you pregnant when you went off to war?
Spirit: Yes

Erin: Did they send you home because of the baby?
Spirit: Yes

Jessica: What happened to the baby?
Spirit: Alive

Erin: Did she live?
Spirit: Yes

Jessica: So the raid was about kidnapping the baby?
Spirit: Yes

Erin: Why did they want the baby?
Spirit: War baby

Jessica: Did they want to raise the baby up to be in the military?
Spirit: Yes

Jessica: Was it a boy?
Spirit: Yes

Erin: Did they take the baby?
Spirit: No

Jessica: Then what happened to the baby?
Spirit: Grandmother

Erin: Did she take the baby to save it?
Spirit: Yes

Jessica: How did you die though?
Spirit: Beaten

Erin: Why?
Spirit: I tried to stop them.

Jessica: Tried to stop them from what? Going after the baby?
Spirit: Yes

Jessica: Was it the father or the army?
Spirit: Army

Erin: Where was the father?
Spirit: Gone

Jessica: Gone where?
Spirit: Home

Erin: How old was the father of your baby?
Spirit: 18

Erin: Why did he leave?
Spirit: He didn't know what to do.

Erin: Spirit, do you mind if we say goodbye?
Spirit: Goodbye

We ended because we were both tired. We are going to try again later tonight. We will also record that one and send it in.
