Round Bottom Cemetery is located on the east side of OH-60 outside Coal Run, Ohio (Waterford Township, Washington County). The coordinates are 39° 33' 16.66" N, 81° 35' 11.15" W.

Round Bottom Cemetery sits next to the Round Bottom Schoolhouse, and it is no longer active. There are many old gravestones, and most are in excellent condition; there are only a few that have been damaged. The cemetery is very well maintained.
