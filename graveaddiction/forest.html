Forest Cemetery is located at 1704 Mulberry Street in Toledo, Ohio (Lucas County). The coordinates are 41° 40' 14" N, 83° 31' 27" W.

Forest Cemetery was established in 1832, and it is still active. The oldest sections are located closest to the main entrance. There are many interesting gravestones to look at, and most are in great condition. However, like any old cemetery, quite a few have been damaged over the years. When we visited in July 2006, the cemetery appeared to be well maintained, but earlier this year there were complaints about the grass not being mowed. It is maintained by the Forest Cemetery Association.
