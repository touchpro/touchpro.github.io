Spirit Writing

March 6, 2010



On March 6, 2010 I received an e-mail from Hayley about an interesting ouija board experience:


Spirit writing is pretty much the same thing as a Ouija board, only with paper and pencil. You have to hold a pencil in this weird way and let the spirit guide it across the paper.

I was with my 11 year old sister, Molly, and her best friend, Hope. We weren't getting any good results on the Ouija board that day, so we decided to do this instead.

You can only do it with one person, so this is basically how Hope's conversation went.

Hope: (draws three circles) Are there any spirits here?
Paper: (Starts drawing random squiggles)

Hope: Draw a heart if that's a yes.
Paper: (draws a heart)

Hope: What's your name?
Paper: Tiffiny

Hope: How old are you?
Paper: 23

Hope: How did you die?
Paper: Car

Hope: Did you die in a car accident?
Paper: Yes

Hope: What year did you die?
Paper: 2010

Hope: Did you die in February?
Paper: Yes

Hope: What day?
Paper: 19

Hope: Do you have anything to tell us?
Paper: Yes

Hope: Go ahead.
Paper: I know you guys

Hope: Do you know our names?
Paper: yes

Hope: What's my name?
Paper: Hope

Hope: What's the girl's name in the red sweater? (that was me)
Paper: Hayley (That is my name. I then started laughing hysterically.)

Hope: What's the girl's name in the gray sweater? (that was Molly)
Paper: Molly

Hope: Do you follow us around?
Paper: yes

Hope: Do you know the names of our schools?
Paper: Yes

Hope: What's the name of mine and Molly's school?
Paper: **** ******* (she wrote the name of Molly's and Hope's elementary school)

Hope: What's the name of Hayley's school?
Paper: ******* (she wrote the name of my middle school)

Hope: What's my mom's boyfriend's name?
Paper: Brian (her mom's boyfriend's name was indeed Brian)

Hope: Do you have anything else to tell us?
Paper: I don't blame you for hating your mom, Hope. (Hope does hate her mom)

Hope: Why?
Paper: Doesn't pay any attention to you.

Hope: Do you know any of our friends?
Paper: Yes

Hope: Do you know any of Hayley's friends?
Paper: Brittany (I do have a friend named Brittany)

Hope: What's a name of one of our friends?
Paper: Hannah. (they do have a friend named Hannah)

Hope: I have to go do my homework now. Bye.
Paper: Bye

Sorry this is long, but right after that, I had a conversation with the same girl. Here goes...

Me: Are there any spirits here?
Paper: Yes

Me: Are you Tiffiny?
Paper: Yes

Me: Do you want to talk to me?
Paper: No

Me: Why not?
Paper: You hate me

Me: No, I don't. I was just surprised that you knew me.
Paper: Oh

Me: Do you want to talk to me now?
Paper: Yes

Me: Do you really know me?
Paper: Yes

Me: How?
Paper: IDK

Me: What class do I have second period?
Paper: Math (she was right)

Me: Do you know any of my other friends?
Paper: Lexzie (I also have a friend named Lexzie)

Me: Are you sitting right here? (I pointed to the space next to me)
Paper: Yes

Me: Are you touching me?
Paper: Yes

Me: Where?
Paper: Neck (I kinda started freaking out then)

Me: Are you going to hurt me?
Paper: No

Me: Do you like drawing?
Paper: Yes

Me: Can you draw something please?
Paper: (draws a heart)

Me: Can you draw anything else?
Paper: (Draws a peace sign)

Me: Were you with me and Brittany when we were doing spirit writing at her house?
Paper: Yes

Me: Did you try to talk to me?
Paper: Maybe (pencil then goes off the paper. I scream.)

Me: Please don't go off the paper, ok?
Paper: Ok

Me: Do you have anything to tell me?
Paper: You like my butt.

Me: I like your butt?
Paper: Yes

Me: How can I like it if I can't see it?
Paper: (Random squiggles)

Me: Do you know my middle name?
Paper: No

Me: Ok, I have to go now. Bye.
Paper: (writing slowly) Bye.

That's the shortest story I have...I think. All my other Ouija Board stories are too long.

THE END
