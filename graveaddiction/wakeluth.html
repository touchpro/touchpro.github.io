Wakefield Lutheran Cemetery is located on the east side of Greenville-Celina Road, west of US-127, outside Greenville, Ohio (Greenville Township, Darke County). The coordinates are 40° 8' 27.73" N, 84° 36' 47.79" W.

Wakefield Lutheran Cemetery is small, and it is no longer active. There are many old gravestones. Most of the monuments are in good condition, but several have been damaged. The grounds are very well maintained.
