First Time

April 14, 2008



On April 14, 2008 I received an e-mail from Stacey S. about an interesting ouija board experience:


My boss and I have been talking about the Ouija Board a lot over the last few weeks. She has done it many times before, but I never had.

Today, we print and cut out the letters of the alphabet, and the words yes and no, and placed them in a circle. We used a glass face down as the planchette. It took about five times of us asking if anyone was there to finally get a response of yes.

We asked the spirit its name and if it had a message a few times, but it kept spelling out random messages that we did not understand.

I don't know if the spirit got angry or frustratned, but it moved the glass and pushed all the letters off the table.

We then asked it if it wanted us to leave, and it quickly said yes.
