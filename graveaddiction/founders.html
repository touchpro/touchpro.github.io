The Founders Cemetery is located at the corner of Southgate Parkway and Turner Avenue in Cambridge, Ohio (Cambridge Township, Guernsey County). The coordinates are 40° 1' 25" N, 81° 35' 22.76" W.

The Founders Cemetery is no longer active. It is the resting place of many of the first settlers in Cambridge. Many of the gravestones are damaged, but some have been repaired. There are also some replacement markers. The cemetery grounds are very well maintained.
