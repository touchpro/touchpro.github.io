Blood Killer

June 15, 2007



On June 15, 2007 I received an e-mail from Nicole about an interesting ouija board experience:


One night myself and four of my friends, Krisitin, Matt, Brittany, and Janelle, were talking to the board. This is our story.

Me: Are there any spirits in the room?
Board: Yes

Kristin: What is your name?
Board: maw

Kristin: Are those your initials?
Board: Yes

Kristin: What is your real name?
Board: Fag u ha (for those who don't know, fags are cigarettes)

Kristin: Me?
Board: Yes

Kristin: Why are you laughing at me?
Board: Because

Kristin: What's your first name?
Board: U

Kristin: Me?
Board: Matt

Matt then, at this point, starts talking.

Matt: Who are you?
Board: Maz

Kristin: Is that your nickname?
Board: Yes

Matt: Do you know me?
Board: Yes

Matt: Who are you?
Board: BK (which stands for blood killer)

Matt is a crip, so it makes him happy to see that...

Me: Are you in a gang?
Board: Yes

Me: Are you a crip?
Board: No

Me: Are you a blood?/
Board: Yes

Me: Then why do you say BK?
Board: Because Matt

Me: Is something going to happen to Matt?
Board: Yes

Kristin: How old are you?
Board: 23

Then Kristin asked how Maz died, and the board didn't say anything back. So we just assumed they didn't want to talk any more. It was getting late and I had to be home.

Kristin: We have to go, I'm sorry.
Board: No

Kristin: You still want to talk?
Board: Yes

Kristin: I'm sorry, we have to get Nicole home.
Board: Goodbye

Obviously something's going to happen to my friend Matt, and us being dumb didn't even ask what. But I'm telling you - I'm scared for him now...and we still haven't figured out who Maz is.
