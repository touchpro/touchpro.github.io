Plains Cemetery is located on the west side of VT-5 in Hartland, Vermont (Windsor County). The coordinates are 43° 33' 37" N, 72° 23' 10" W.

Plains Cemetery is small, and it is no longer active. There are many old slate gravestones, and several have very detailed, interesting carvings. Although the cemetery was a little overgrown when we visited in June 2008, it is obvious that it is still maintained.

Many thanks to Ta Mara C. and Sue T. for visiting this cemetery with me!
