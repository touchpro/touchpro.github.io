Failure of Possession

July 25, 2008



On July 25, 2008 I received an e-mail from Daniel about an interesting ouija board experience:


A few nights ago, some friends and I bought a Ouija board. Hayley, Lauren, and Britney. This story is kinda long, but believe me: it's all true and we all believe in spirits now. This story takes place over three different nights.

Night 1:

Daniel: Is there anyone who wants to speak?
Board: No

Daniel, Hayley, and Lauren: Goodbye

Daniel: Are there any good spirts that want to talk?
Board: STOP

so we all said goodbye and put the board away

Night 2:

Daniel: Are there any good spirts that wish to speak?
Board: Yes

Daniel: Ok, let's start by getting your name.
Board: S.A.L.

Daniel: Are those initials?
Board: Yes

Daniel: What do they stand for?
Board: Steven A. Low

Daniel: How old are you?
Board: 47

Daniel: Are we bothering you in any way?
Board: No

Daniel: Do you want us to leave you alone?
Board: STAY

Daniel: Have you ever encountered an evil spirit?
Board: No

Daniel: What about good spirits?
Board: Yes

Daniel: Do you wish to tell anyone anything?
Board: HS

Daniel: You want to tell Hayley something?
Board: Yes

Lauren: Do you like Hayley?
Board: Yes

Lauren: As more than a friend?
Board: No

Daniel: Is there anyone here that you don't like?
Board? No

so we proceed in asking basic questions, ranging from how he died to what all he could do as a ghost

Night 3:

Daniel: May we speak to the good spirit of Steven Low?
Board: Yes

Daniel: Is this him?
Board: Yes

Daniel: Are we bothering you now?
Board: No

Daniel: Do you know if there is a ghost in our house?
Board: Yes

Daniel: Is it a good ghost?
Board: Yes

Hayley: Is it my grandma?
Board: Yes

Britney: Are you in our circle right now?
Board: No

Daniel: Are you sitting on my truck?
Board: No

Daniel: Do you want to sit on my truck?
Board: Yes

so we talk to him about more random things, when all of a sudden...

Hayley: Ouch...something just hit my arm (she looked scared and as if she were about to cry)

Daniel: What do you mean something hit your arm?

Hayley: I don't know...I felt something hit my arm. I'm not playing anymore...I'll just watch.

Daniel: Did you hit Hayley?
Board: Yes

Daniel: Did you mean to hurt her?
Board: No

Daniel: Then why did you do it?
Board: LOVE

Daniel: Do you like Hayley as more than a friend?
Board: Yes

then he started talking all weird and stuff

Britney: Is there an evil spirit present?
Board: Yes

Daniel: Can you make it leave?
Board: Yes

Daniel: Is it gone?
Board: Yes

Daniel: How did you make it leave?
Board: LEAVE

Daniel: So can people make ghosts leave like that too?
Board: No

Daniel: Did the evil spirt try to harm Hayley?
Board: Yes

then out of nowhere, the planchette starts moving without us asking anything

Board: DZW

Britney: Derrick? (her brother who wasnt even present)
Board: Yes

Britney: Is he in trouble?
Board: H...

Britney: Is he high?
Board: no response

Daniel: Are you still there?
Board: no response

after about five minutes or more

Daniel: Is the good spirit of Steven Low still with us?
Board: No

ALL: GOODBYE

We flipped the board over in the box and put it away. Later on, when Lauren and Britney left, I could tell that something was bothering Hayley.

Daniel: You know what I think? What if the bad spirit was trying to possess you and Steven did something to make it stop, and you felt it?

Hayley: I don't know...Daniel, I'm not trying to say anything about Steven, and I didn't tell anyone this, but after I felt that, I said a prayer to God asking him to please make all evil spirits leave...and right after that Steven stopped talking...

Hayley never has been a spiritual person. In fact, she knew about the Bible and everything that has to do with Jesus and God, but doubted a lot of it, like me...after tonight we believe in it all. We decided to never play with the ouija board again. I believe some of the stories I read on this site, and I think we are all really lucky that something worse didn't happen. OUIJA BOARDS ARE REAL, AND IT IS NOT A GAME!!!
