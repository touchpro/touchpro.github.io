Oakdale Cemetery is located at 1290 West Fifth Street in Marysville, Ohio (Paris Township, Union County). The coordinates are 40° 14' 35.33" N, 83° 23' 35.71" W.

Oakdale Cemetery was established in 1880 and it is still active. It is located across the railroad tracks from Our Lady of Lourdes Catholic Cemetery. There are many old gravestones located throughout the cemetery, and most are in excellent shape. The grounds are also very well maintained.
