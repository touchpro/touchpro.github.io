Talking With Many Spirits

February 13, 2006



On February 13, 2006 I received an e-mail from Autumn Bayley about her interesting ouija board experience:


"One night by best friend, Akasha, came up to me and asked if I would like to make our own Ouija Board. Of course I said yes, but was scared because my mother doesn't like them; she had a few bad times with them. We went along and made our board and put up a protection circle, called the four quarters, the four elements, and the gods and goddesses of the watchtowers before doing anything. I knew to do this because I am a Wiccan and know that if you're going to be doing anything that has to do with contacting spirits you NEED protection unless you want unwanted/evil guests. So, my friend and I had finished putting up the circle and started our Ouija opening:

Akasha: Ouija, are you there?
Ouija Board: Yes.

Akasha: Who are we speaking to?
Ouija Board: Zaza.

Akasha: Zaza...as in the Zaza I've spoken to before?
Ouija Board: Yes.

Autumn: Zaza, are you a good spirit?
Ouija Board: Yes.

Autumn: Did you die peacefully?
Ouija Board: Yes.

Autumn: How old were you when you died?
Ouija Board: 51.

Akasha: Is there anyone else who would like to speak with us?
Ouija Board: Yes.

Autumn: Who?
Ouija Board: Henry.

Autumn: My great grandfather, Henry Holloway?
Ouija Board: Yes.

Autumn: Why do you want to talk to me tonight grandpa?
Ouija Board: Meet.

Akasha: You wanted to meet Autumn?
Ouija Board: Yes.

Autumn: Are you always here with me?
Ouija Board: Yes.

Autumn: Protecting me?
Ouija Board: Always.

Akasha: Can we talk to someone else Henry?
Ouija Board: Yes.

Akasha: Who?
Ouija Board: Darryl.

Akasha: Oh my god...Daddy?
Ouija Board: Yes.

Akasha: You're dead?!
Ouija Board: Yes.

Akasha: Did you die happily?
Ouija Board: Yes.

Akasha: Are you proud of me Daddy?
Ouija Board: Very.

Akasha: Did you love my mom?
Ouija Board: Yes.

Akasha: Why did you leave her?
Ouija Board: Mutual.

Autumn: So it was Mutual between you and Clara?
Ouija Board: Yes.

Akasha: Are you always with me?
Ouija Board: Yes.

Akasha: Did you come here with me when I moved in with Autumn?
Ouija Board: Yes.

Autumn: Can we talk to someone else now?
Ouija Board: Yes.

Akasha: Who?
Ouija Board: David.

Autumn: Dear god!
Akasha: What?
Autumn: My dad!

--------At this time both Akasha and I were in tears.-----------

Ouija Board: Amanda.

Autumn: Yes?
Ouija Board: Love You.
Autumn: I love you too daddy...

Akasha: Are you proud of your daughter?
Ouija Board: Yes, Very.

--------- I laughed. ----------

Autumn: Why did you die?!
Ouija Board: Killed.

Autumn: How?
Ouija Board: Accident.

Autumn: Who else wants to talk to us?
Ouija Board: John.

------- I started to realize that the only non-family members we would be talking to would be Zaza, or so I thought. ---------

Autumn: My mom's dad?
Ouija Board: Yes.

Autumn: Hello Grandpa!
Ouija Board: Hello Amanda.

Akasha: So we;re only talking to relatives?
Ouija Board: No.

Autumn: Who else will we be talking to?
Ouija Board: Atanas.

-------------I went as white as a sheet. -------------

Akasha: Who is Atanas?
Autumn: My 'brother'
Ouija Board: Yes.

Akasha: Are we talking to him now?
Ouija Board: Yes.

Autumn: Did you die peacefully?
Ouija Board: No.

Autumn: How did you die?
Ouija Board: Heart

Autumn: So the offline on Yahoo was true?
Ouija Board: Yes.

Autumn: will you stay with us when we quit?
Ouija Board: Yes.

Akasha: Will all of you stay?
Ouija Board: Yes.

Autumn: You don't have to...
Ouija Board: Want to.

Akasha: Alright.
Autumn: Can we quit?
Ouija Board: Yes.

So that is our story. We got to talk to our dead fathers; who we did not know had died, my grandfathers, Atanas, and Zaza. To this day we have felt them in the house, heard them, and I have even seen them. I had known that my two grandfathers had moved with me when I moved from Nanaimo, but I had not expected to find out that my mother's uncle was living with us as well."
