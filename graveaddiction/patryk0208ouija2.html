Interruption by Satan

February 10, 2008



On February 10, 2008 I received an e-mail from Patryk R. about an interesting ouija board experience:


Earlier today (Saturday, February 9, 2008) I was talking to a spirit named Carrc. Just a short while ago I tried talking to Carrc. This is how the conversation went:

Me: Hello Carrc, are you there?
Carrc: YES

Me: Is it okay if I can ask you some questions about the near future?
Carrc: YES

Me: What will I do tomorrow?
Carrc: Die

At this point I started to get scared, so I sprinkled some salt on the corners of the Ouija board.

Me: Is it okay if I ask you something?
Carrc(?): NO

Me: Why not?
Carrc(?): Rage

Me: Who is this?
Carrc(?): Sata---

I threw the planchette at the wall because I couldn't let it finish.

Me: Is there any other spirit that would like to talk to me?
Board: YES

Me: Who?
Board: Carrc

Me: Why couldn't you get through?
Carrc: Sata---

Me: Please do not say the last letter.
Carrc: YES

Me: Why did he try to stop you?
Carrc(?): F**k

Me: Who is this?!
Carrc(?): Dev---

Again, I threw the planchette at the wall because I cannot let the spirit (Satan) say the last letters. Know this: I used the best precautions; I prayed, sprinkled salt on the board, put a silver coin on the board, and lit candles. Is there anything I did wrong? Please let me know.


If you have any comments for Patryk, please send me an e-mail and I'll forward them on to him.
