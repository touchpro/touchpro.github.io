Annoyed Spirit

December 13, 2008



On December 13, 2008 I received an e-mail from Rachael L. about an interesting ouija board experience:


Kayleigh and I made a ouija board today, and it was extremely eerie since we were talking to a pedophile. Here's how it went...

Us: As there any spirtis here?
Board: yes (almost as if it was annoyed)

Us: Who are we speaking with?
Board: Harry

Us: Are you an evil spirit?
Board: (the glass circled)

Us: OK, how did you die?
Board: Murder

(At this point we began to feel a really strange presence in the room. It didn't feel right)

Us: Why were you murdered, Harry?
Board: Children

Us: Children murdered you?
Board: No I murdered them

(We were both in total shock, and wanted to leave, but we were too intrigued about what he had to say)

Us: Why did you murder them?
Board: sfx

Us: Do you mean sex?
Board: Yes

Us: Do you have a message?
Board: (no answer)

(My friend then felt a grasp on her hair, she began to really freak out.)

Us: Was that you?
Board: Yes sex

Us: Sex? With who?
Board: Both

(We thought he was getting a little too weird, but we thought we would entice him.)

Us: OK, if you're so good, move something.
Board: Yes

(Soon after, one of my friend's Christmas angels fell from the ceiling, and we decided we should end it.)

Us: We're going now, please leave us alone.
Board: no no no no

He then spelled out bitch, then die, so we had to say the Lord's Prayer and command him to leave. I doubt I'll be taking it that far again!!
