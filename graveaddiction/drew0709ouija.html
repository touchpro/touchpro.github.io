Hiding Stuff

July 21, 2009



On July 21, 2009 I received an e-mail from Drew Johns about an interesting ouija board experience:


Me and my sister's friends were playing the Ouija Board in our creepy basement. I wasn't on it, but my sister and friends were.

Sister (Carly)
Sisters friends (Chelsea, Emily and Cassie)
Me (Drew Johns)

Carly: Hello spirit, are you from the sun or the moon?
(The board went to moon)

Chelsea: What's your name?
Board: Joe

Carly: Why are you in hell?
Board: Murder

Emily: Who did you murder?v Board: Mom and Dad

Emily: Why?
Board: They hit me

Cassie: Oh jeez.
Carly: May we contact another spririt?
Board: No

(This is were it gets scariest)

The board told us to put our heads down and make noise for some odd reason.

So we did.

When we put our heads up, one of the candles was gone.

We all screamed and went looking for it.

We found it under the couch cushions.

After that we stopped playing it, and the rest of the day stuff was disappearing. I played it one time, and found out spirits were doing that so we would stop doing the Ouija Board. He/she said that he was getting tired of going on the Ouija Board!!!!!
