Somerset, Ohio

Saint Joseph Catholic Cemetery (Old) is located on OH-383 in Somerset, Ohio. It is located behind Saint Joseph Catholic church. The newer section of the cemetery is located further down the road, but you can see it from the old cemetery.

Saint Joseph Cemetery (Old) is no longer in use. There are many interesting, very old gravestones in the cemetery. While there are still many gravestones still standing, there are also three big stacks of broken gravestones. The cemetery is extremely well taken care of, and for such an old cemetery it's still in great condition!
