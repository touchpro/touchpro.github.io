Scary Much?

April 27, 2009



On April 27, 2009 I received an e-mail from Hannah M. about an interesting ouija board experience:


I have used a ouija board quite a few times, and had many crazy/scary experiences. This is just one that creeped me out the most...I was with my friends Amy, Cait, and Georgina. I joined part way through the session, and this is what happened. This happened 4/26/09, and I am from New Zealand!!!! :)

Hannah: What did I do last night?
Board: Party

Hannah: Whose party was I at?
Board: Jacobs (right)

Then I wanted to leave, so I asked.

Hannah: Can I please take my finger off the glass? (We were using a homemade paper and shot glass board)
Board: no

Hannah: Please?
Board: no

Hannah: Why not?
Board: Dead

Hannah: Will you ever let me take my finger off?
Board: yes

Hannah: Can i take it off yet?
Board: no

Hannah: What do I have to do to make you let me take my finger off?
Board: gun
Board: goodbye.

Scary much?
