Saint Nicholas Ukrainian Catholic Cemetery is located at 8901 West Higgins Road in Chicago, Illinois (Cook County). The coordinates are 41° 59' 11" N, 87° 51' 1" W.

Saint Nicholas Ukrainian Catholic Cemetery is still active. There are many interesting gravestones, and all are in excellent condition. The cemetery grounds are very well maintained.
