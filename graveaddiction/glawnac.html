Wapakoneta, Ohio

Greenlawn Cemetery is located on the south side of Auglaize Street in Wapakoneta, Ohio (Duchouquet Township, Auglaize County). The coordinates are 40° 33' 47.47" N, 84° 13' 9" W.

Greenlawn Cemetery is large, and it is still active. There are several old gravestones, and the majority are in great condition. The oldest are on the north side, and there are some very unique, impressive monuments. The cemetery is very well maintained.
