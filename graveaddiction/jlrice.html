Looker-James-Rice Cemetery is located in the woods off the south side of OH-256 in Pickerington, Ohio (Violet Township, Fairfield County).

Looker-James-Rice Cemetery is very small, and it is no longer active. It is difficult to find, unless you're looking for it. Almost all the gravestones have been damaged, but a few have been repaired. The cemetery grounds are very well maintained.

Many thanks to Cindi Thompson for telling me about this cemetery!
