The Old Burial Ground is located at 87 Hadley Street (MA-47) in South Hadley, Massachusetts (Hampshire County). The coordinates are 42° 15' 30" N, 72° 34' 43" W.

The Old Burial Ground is no longer active, and it is no longer located in its original location. It was moved to Evergreen Cemetery in 1902. There are many old gravestones with interesting carvings, and most are in good condition. Several contain interesting epitaphs. The cemetery is extremely well maintained.
