You Are Bad

August 2, 2006



On August 2, 2006 I received an e-mail from Jessica B. about her interesting ouija board experience:


My name is Jessica and I had an odd happening using the Ouija Board a week ago. Me and my neighbor, not so much friend (quot;Tom"), decided to use the Ouija board. We said a protection prayer that I read on this site. We lit three candles and started playing.

Me: Is there a spirit in this house?
Board: Yes

Me: What is your name?
Board: Zack

Me: What year were you born?
Board: 1860

Me: How old are you, and are you related to me?
Board: 19 and am to your nan (I call my dad's mother Nanny)

Me: If you are related to me, what is my Nanny's Maiden Name?
Board: (SPELLED MY NANNYS MAIDEN NAME) I was amazed

After I asked that question, I felt something grab my arm tight. It hurt so bad, and one all the candles blew out at that moment. I FREAKED OUT but decided to continue and let Tom take over, but I had to ask if it was Zack hurting me...

Me: Is that you, Zack, grabbing my arm?
Board: Yes

Me: Can you let go?
Board: Yes, you welcome

After that the pain went away
So Tom began to speak...

Tom: Is there anybody who wants to talk to me?
Board: Yes

Tom: What would you like to say to me?
Board: You are bad, evil, bad

When the board said that, the door slammed violently. We jumped. So Tom had to walk outside, he was too freaked. Even though I was scared to death, I wanted to know why he was so bad.

Me: Why is Tom bad?
Board: He will hurt you

Me: When
Board: 4 days

Me: How
Board: Be careful love

Me: How will he hurt me?
Board: Goodbye

Well, I was concerned but eventually forgot about it a day later or so. 3 or 4 days later...I went to Tom's garage because he wanted to show me something he made. So I went back there and he asked me to go to a dark corner in the garage. I was going to go, but I saw something move in the corner of my eye - like a ghost - and when I looked over I saw Tom slip a long knife into his jacket. When I saw that I ran to my car and left. Anyway, I wanted to tell this story because I believe this spirit Zack saved my life...if I had not done what the ouija board said, I would have probably been raped or killed by Tom. Since then I haven't spoken to Tom, but every now and then he asks me why I ran and wants me to go back into that garage. Thank you Zack!
