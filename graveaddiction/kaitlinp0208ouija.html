I Watch Over You

February 29, 2008



On February 29, 2008 I received an e-mail from Kaitlin P. about an interesting ouija board experience:


Me (Kaitlin) and Abby talked to her sister! So scary! Both of us are ten.

Me: Spirits, are you with us?
Abby: Melissa Manning, are you there?
Board: Yes. {Abby's Number}

Board: {Abby's Address}
Abby: Oh my god, how do you know all this stuff?
Board: I watch over you.

Me: Where are you?
Board: With you

Abby: In the room?
Board: Yes

Me: Where in the room? The bed?
Board: Yes

Abby: Next to the picture of the dragon?
Board: Yes

We felt the area on the bed. It was breezy and felt like a spirit was with us.

Me: Ok, but we have to go now.
Board: K

Abby: Byee!! I love you!
Board: Goodbye

We felt the spot again. It was warm.

After that, we called Marilyn Monroe.

Me: Hello? We're calling Marilyn Monroe.
Board: Yes

Abby: Is this Marilyn Monroe?
Board: Yes Yes.

Me: I love you so much!! I'm dressing up as you for Halloween this year. Would you like that?
Board: Yes

Abby : I don't know who you are.
Me: Oh my god, you don't know who Marilyn Monroe was?
Abby: No, I think my uncle does though.
Board: F.U. Goodbye
Us: Oh my god, goodbye!
