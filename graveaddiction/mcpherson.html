McPherson Cemetery is located at the intersection of US-20 and OH-101 in Clyde, Ohio (Green Creek Township, Sandusky County). The coordinates are 41° 18' 34.75" N, 82° 58' 5" W.

McPherson Cemetery, also known as Evergreen Cemetery, is large, and it is still active. The oldest gravestones are located on the southwest side, and most are in great condition. The cemetery is very well maintainted.

Many thanks to the staff at the Clyde Public Library for telling me about this cemetery, and for giving me fantastic directions to it.
