What's It Like To Die

November 12, 2005



On November 12, 2005 I received an e-mail from Nick about another interesting ouija board experience he had:


Me and my friends, Matt and Joey, used the oujia board three nights ago.

I asked, "Is anyone there?"

No answer.

I asked again, but there was still no answer. So then I told Matt and Joey that the board is busted. Next thing you know, I heard a weird thumping sound from upstairs...now I'm very scared. I said, "You guys stay here and I'll look up there." I went to the room where the thumping was coming from, and nothing was there! When I came back downstairs, my friends were using the board. They asked me to come over quick and put my hand on the planchette.

Me: What's your name?
Board: Steve

Me: What's it like to die, Steve?
Board: Weird. You can't feel anything for a second and you see a white light and you see people waiting for you up there. It's not so bad, you feel peaceful

Me: Is there such a thing as the underworld???
Board: No, but if you are bad you get reincarnated as an animal or bug or you just become a worldbound spirit for 50 years helping people

Me: What do you do when you die?
Board: Whatever you want, except you can't hurt people from the living realm....you can help though

Me: OK, thank you for your information.
Board: Anytime

My friends and I were happy to hear this, and we want to keep talking to Steve.
