Poplar Fork Cemetery is located on the east side of Poplar Fork Road, north of US-40, in Gratiot, Ohio (Hopewell Township, Licking County). The coordinates are 39° 57' 11.77" N, 82° 13' 26.57" W.

Poplar Fork Cemetery was established in 1823, and it is still active. There are many old gravestones, and although some of the older monuments have been damaged, most are in good condition. There are several gravestones carved by John Strickler. The cemetery is very well maintained.
