Bush Cemetery is located on the south side of OH-145 in Malaga, Ohio (Malaga Township, Monroe County). The coordinates are 39° 51' 30.41" N, 81° 8' 13.21" W.

Bush Cemetery is located behind the church, and was established in 1853. There are many old gravestones, including several with detailed carvings. Most of the gravestones are in good condition, but a few have been damaged. The cemetery grounds are very well maintained.
