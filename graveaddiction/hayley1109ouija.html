Day Before Halloween

November 2, 2009



On November 2, 2009 I received an e-mail from Hayley about an interesting ouija board experience:


It was October 30th, the day before Halloween, when I went to my friend's party. When everyone got there, she pulled out a ouija board. We had always talked about them, but never agreed to plan a date to go buy one or make one. Now there was one right before our eyes.

We turned off all the lights but one, and placed the board on a coffee table. We came in contact with a spirit right away. At first we thought he said his name was Tarzan, but later found it was Tarson. Nothing overly exciting happened. All we knew was Tarson had a brother who was killed by someone he didn't want to share with us. We went home, and by the next day we had a date planned out to try to learn more. But this time only 3 of us, me (Hayley) and my two friends Leah and Olivia. We came in contact with a guy named Jue, so here's our conversation.

Leah: Are you a good spirit?
Board: Yes.

Hayley: Are you going to harm us?
Board: No.

Hayley: Do you by any chance know Tarson?
Board: Yes.

Leah: How?
Board: Brother.

We were all pretty shocked right now!

Hayley: Was Tarson older then you?
Board: Yes.

Olivia:....
Hayley: How many years older was he?
Board: 10.

Olivia: How were you killed?
Board: Fight.

Leah: About what?
Board: Fight (then goes to yes and then no)

Hayley: Does that mean your not sure?
Board: Marriage.

Olivia: Is that what the fight was about?
Board: Yes.

Hayley: Who killed you?
Board: Tarson (really slow now) wife.

Hayley: Okay, that's enough, thank you.
Board: Goodbye.

Okay, now here's the part were we all got a bit spooked, that was nothing ; )

Leah: Are you a good spirit?
Board: Yes (apparently)

Leah: What's your name?
Board: Gus

Leah: Are you a boy?
Board: No.

Hayley: Is that a short form?
Board: Yes, Gustella.

Olivia: Are you in anyway related with Tarson?
Board: Yes, wife.

Hayley: If you don't mind me asking - did you kill Jue?
Board: Yes fight.

Leah: Over marriage?
Board: Yes.

Hayley: Did you mean to kill him?
Board: -goes between yes and no-

Leah: Are you in the room with us?
Board: Yes

Leah: In one of these chairs?
Board: Yes.

Olivia: Which one....
Board: End.

Leah: Do you wanna sit next to me?
Board: Yes.

Leah: Okay, now I have to go for a few minutes. Can Hayley sit here and take over?
Board: Ok.

Hayley: ....
Olivia:....
Board: Yes.

Hayley....
Olivia: Did you say anything?
Board: Yes no yes no -bunch of letters-

Leah: I'M BACK!
Board: Yes.

Leah: Want Hayley to stay holding the pointer?
Board: Yes.

Leah: So what's up?
Board: -points at boxes of apple tarts Leah has brought in-

Hayley: Does she want one?
Board: Yes.

Leah: Okay -places a tart in front of her-
Board: Yes.

Leah: Can we see you while you eat it?
Board: No.

Leah: So were you a human in your past life?
Board: No.

Leah: An animal?
Board: Yes.

Olivia: What kind?
Board: Wakeded.

Leah: You mean a whale?
Board: Yes.

Olivia: -giggles-

Okay so we ask a bunch more questions about like am I going to fail my french test, etc when Leah's brother comes down.

Leah's brother: Oh so you got that stupid thing to work?
Leah: Shh.
Hayley: Nicolas...
Board: -pointer almost goes off the board pointing at him-

Hayley: Do you want to talk to him?
Board: No -goes to no like five times-

Nicolas: WOW YOU STUPID GHOST, YOU HAVE NO CONTROL OVER ME, I CAN TALK TO YOU IF I WANT.
Board: -goes dead still then goes to the top of the board like its trying to escape-

Hayley: Um, say sorry, Nicolas.
-Okay, let's keep in mind he's a 9 year old-
Board: Yes.

-we ended the session-

We later talked to someone else, andwe asked if we had talked to any bad sprits. It said yes, Gus. Why? She's a killer.

Dun dun dun dun.
