The Clark County Home Cemetery is located on the west side of Snider Road, about a ¼ mile north of Lower Valley Pike, outside Donnelsville, Ohio (Bethel Township, Clark County). The coordinates are 39° 54' 6.45" N, 83° 57' 58.91" W.

The Clark County Home Cemetery, also known as the Infirmary Cemetery, is no longer active. It is the final resting place of patients who died at the county home. The gravestones are all dated between 1927-1934, and they are all simple concrete markers with metal letters/numbers. The cemetery is very well maintained.
