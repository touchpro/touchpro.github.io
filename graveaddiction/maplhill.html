Maple Hill Cemetery is located on the east side of Cemetery Road in Stoutsville, Ohio (Clear Creek Township, Fairfield County). The coordinates are 39° 36' 2.76" N, 82° 49' 20.13" W.

Maple Hill Cemetery is sometimes referred to as the Stoutsville Cemetery. It is large, and it is still active. Graves from many smaller cemeteries in the area have been moved here over the years; you can find many of the moved gravestones lined up at the back of the cemetery. There are very few broken gravestones; most are in excellent condition. The cemetery is very well maintained.
