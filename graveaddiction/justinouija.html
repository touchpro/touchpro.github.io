Drake

February 28, 2007



On February 28, 2007 I received an e-mail from Justin about his interesting ouija board experience:


My friends and I bought an Ouija Board over the weekend because we heard so much about them and decided to give it a try. I'm Justin and I was doing the board with my Brother (Brandon), and my friend Steve was scribe. We let Steve be scribe because he didn't really believe anything about Ouija Boards at first.

Conversation 1

Justin: Are you a good spirit?

Board: Bradley

Justin: Creation?
Board: Yes

Justin: Can you tell us this person's name?

Justin: Is Drake at the park now?
Board: Goodbye
Us: Goodbye

That was the end of our first conversation then we came in contact with another spirit, we wanted to find out more about this Drake guy.

The conversation went on and the board was explaining to us that this Drake guy loves perfection and he can affect the world. The board was also explaining how Drake's love for perfection is not good because there will be no diversity, the board said people will be perfect but not happy, and he said people will have fake lives. He gave us his first name but he didn't give us his last because he said if he gave us his last he wouldn't be able to speak to us again which he wants to. We will know its him because he will say something to greet us next time. We haven't done the board since but are hoping to this weekend.
