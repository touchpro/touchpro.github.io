Elmwood Cemetery is located at 1200 Elmwood Avenue in Detroit, Michigan (Wayne County). The coordinates are 42° 20' 57" N, 83° 1' 10" W.

Elmwood Cemetery was established in 1846, and it is still active. It is adjacent to Mount Elliott Cemetery. The cemetery is beautiful, and it is full of interesting gravestones and statues. The grounds are very well maintained.
