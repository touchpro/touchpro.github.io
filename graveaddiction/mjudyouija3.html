Sliding On Floors

October 30, 2006



On October 30, 2006 I received an e-mail from Maria Judy about her interesting ouija board experience:


This is a story that happened to my mom and her sisters when they were young and playing with their ouija board.

During the 1970's sometime, my mom and her sisters were playing with their ouija board in their living room (which was all hardwood floors). Now, I do not know what questions they had been asking, but they had connected with a spirit. I also do not know all the fine details of this particular session. But, somehow they must have made the spirit mad, because the planchette flew off the board and proceeded to chase my Aunt Pam around the living room. The planchette was sliding across the hardwood floors going after my aunt! She finally jumped up on a chair, and the planchette stopped right in front of IT, as if it was waiting for her!! I am not sure how their session ended, but I would be pretty scared to be chased like that!!
