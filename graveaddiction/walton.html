Walton Cemetery is located on the east side of Park Street in Pepperell, Massachusetts (Middlesex County).

Walton Cemetery is large, and it is still active. There are many old gravestones, and most are in excellent condition. The oldest gravestones are located near the front, closest to Park Street. The cemetery is very well maintained.
