Vermont Horror

October 3, 2008



On October 3, 2008 I received an e-mail from Gia about an interesting ouija board experience:


One day my friend Lacey and I were at a hotel in Vermont with our parents. We thought the hotel we kind of freaky so we made a ouija board. There were two pictures of a man and a woman in the hotel, and they were a husband and wife. So we tried to talk to one of them. Here's our story:

Gia: Is there anyone here?
Board: Yes

Lacey: What is your name?
Board: Jane

*that was the name of the woman*

Gia: What is your last name?
Board: Porter

*that was her last name*

We knew we were talking to the girl in the picture.

Lacey: What year did you die?
Board: 1900

Lacey: What year were you born?
Board: 1811

Gia: What was your husband's name?
Board: J

(we went to take a picture of the pictures to see if she was telling the truth)

Gia: Are you still here?
Lacey: Are you still here?
Board: Kind of moved in a circle

Gia: Are you still here?
(waited 10 minutes)
Board: No response

Both: Goodbye
