Bad Stuff

April 15, 2009



On April 15, 2009 I received an e-mail from Dallas about an interesting ouija board experience:


Hello, my name is Dallas. A month ago on a Monday, I was playing the Ouija board with my brother, Devin. We thought that it would be fun, but we ended with an experience we would never forget!

Us: Is anyone there?
Board: Yes

Us: What's your name?
Board: John

Us: How'd you die?
Board: (nothing)

Us: Will we ever die?
Board: Yes

Us: When?
Board: Thursday

Us: What Thursday?
Board: (nothing)

That Thursday, my brother and I were walking to the store, and Devin almost got hit by a car. Weird, right? So, that night we decided to contact John again.

Us: John? Are you there?
Board: Yes

Devin: Did you send a car at me earlier?
Board: No

Devin: Are you evil?
Board: Yes

Devin: Are you in this room?
Board: Yes

Devin: Do you hate me?
Board: Yes

Devin: Do you hate Dallas?
Board: Yes

Devin: Why?
Board: Because I do

Us: Will you haunt us for the rest of our lives?
Board: Yes

Us: Do you want to talk with us anymore?
Board:: No but ill be with you (creepy!)

Us: Goodbye
Board: Goodbye

After that session, strange things have been happening in our house. Flickering lights, opening/closing doors, and sometimes even footsteps. Devin and I have both agreed never to play with the board again, but we are still both highly interested in the Ouija board itself.

For you guys out there who are considering using the Ouija board, don't do it! Trust me, they are BAD stuff!!
