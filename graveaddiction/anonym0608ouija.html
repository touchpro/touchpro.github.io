Heavy Breathing

June 9, 2008



On June 9, 2008 I received an e-mail from an anonymous visitor about an interesting ouija board experience:


One or two years ago (I can't remember) when I was home alone, I drew up a Ouija on a piece of paper with a shot glass for a planchette. I had heard stories about them, and wanted to see if they actually worked. Anyway, before I could even sit down and ask it to spell something out, crows started to flock on the fence and trees around my house and I heard a deep, heavy breathing in my room like that of a beast. This scared the shit out of me so I burnt it in the fire and the sound went away.

That experience really scared me. A few other times I was brave enough to draw one up again (this time with a Bible by my side, even though I wasn't Christian, I wasn't taking my chances), but they didn't work anymore and they have never worked for me since.
