Ghost of the Inn

October 3, 2008



On October 3, 2008 I received an e-mail from Gia about an interesting ouija board experience:


It was the day after we talked to "mom" and since it was raining, we decided to go to the front porch and talk to a ghost. The door in our room was slamming shut all the time, and we heard footsteps all the time, so we wanted to see if we could get ahold of him/her.

Gia: Is anyone here?
Board: Yes

Lacey: Who are you?
Board: Myle

Gia: Is your name Myle?
Board: Yes

Lacey: Where are you?
Board: Inn

Gia: Are you at the inn in Vermont (won't say name of inn)
Board: Yes

Lacey: Are you good or evil?
Board: F-U

*we stopped the question because we know the answer*

Gia: Are you trying to spell *the curse*?
Board: Yes

Lacey: So are you good or evil?
Board: Evil

Gia: Where in the inn?
Board: 26-5

*it stopped at the six for 2 minutes and then went to 5*

Lacey: Do you mean room 26 or 25?
Board: 25

*that was our room*

Gia: The hallway or room?
Board: Hallway

Lacey: Are you the one slamming the door?
Board: Yes

*we now go to the front of the hotel because Lacey thinks the person that owned the hotel's name is Myle, but it was really Marsh*

Gia: Do you mean Marsh, not Myle?
Board: No

Lacey: Do you live in room 25?
Board: No

Gia: Did you die in room 25?
Board: No

Lacey: Then how are you here?
Board: Goodbye
