Union Township Cemetery is located on the north side of Memory Lane, one block east of Main Street and two blocks east of US-40, in Morristown, Ohio (Union Township, Belmont County). The coordinates are 40° 3' 55.93" N, 81° 4' 21.14" W.

Union Township Cemetery is still active. The oldest gravestones are located on both sides; the middle section contains the more recent gravestones. Most are in excellent condition. The cemetery is very well maintained by the township.

Many thanks to Tim Fox, Rob Philabaum, and Marty Myers for visiting this cemetery with me!
