Homemade Ouija Board

December 3, 2006



On December 3, 2006 I received an e-mail from Ryan Johnson about his interesting ouija board experience:


I've been reading all the stuff on your site, but my friends and I couldn't find anywhere to buy a ouija board so we made one. We tried using it in a graveyard and it didn't work, so a few nights later we tried it in my dorm room and it worked. Me (Ryan) and my friends, Becky, Whitney, and Courtney, did it. First on the board was me, Becky, and Whitney. Courtney recorded what happened. Here's what happened...

Us: Is there a spirit here?
Board: (Right away) Yes

Us: What's your name?
Board: Ryan (And that's my name...so I thought one of my friends was moving it, but they weren't)

Us: Are you male or female?
Board: Male

Us: Is there anyone with you?
Board: pr

Us: Could you try again?
Board: dek

Us: We don't understand you...do you want to try spelling it out again?
Board: dej

Us: Ryan, are you feeling playful tonight?
Board: Yes

Us: Is there any reason you're talking to us?
Board: 30

Us: We don't understand, Ryan.
Board: Spelled out B-Y-E instead of going to goodbye

Us: Is there anybody else here?
Board: Ty

Us: Ty, are you a good spirit?
Board: Yes

Us: Is there anything you'd like to tell us?
Board: Stayed at yes

Us: What is it?
Board: no movement.

Us: Do you want to talk to us Ty?
Board: No

Then it didn't move for a while.

Us: Are there any spirits here?
Board: Yes

Us: What's your name?
Board: zez

Us: Is your name Zez?
Board: spelled out B-Y-E again

Then I got off the board and let Becky and Whitney do it while I recorded.

Us: Are there any spirits that want to talk?
Board: Yes

Us: What's your name?
Board: Ryan

Us: Ryan, are you one of our spirit guides?
Board: Yes

Us: Who's spirit guide are you?
Board: 5 -- then it went to the middle of the board

Us: We don't understand you, could you try again?
Board: su

Us: Is there any reason you want to talk to us tonight?
Board: no movement

Us: Are you still with us?
Board: No movement

Us: Is there a reason you're communicating with us?
Board: Yes

Us: Will you tell us the reason?
Board: uy

Us: Are you having trouble spelling, Ryan?
Board: Yes

Us: How many past lives have you had?
Board: 4

Us: Did you know any of us in any of them?
Board: 5

Us: Will you please try again and tell us why you're here?
Board: Yes

Us: Go ahead and tell us.
board: U

Us: Do you mean Whitney?
Board: Yes

Us: What's the reason you wanted to talk to me?
Board: Tap

Us: What do you mean by tap?
Board: u

Then it continued on like this, us asking him questions and him replying with just random letters...I don't know what this means, could anyone tell me?

And then when we used it again, it kept going to the letter y when we asked it questions. It's really confusing us why the ouija board won't talk to us and keeps confusing us.

If you have any advice for Ryan, please e-mail me and I'll send your advice/tips on to him.
