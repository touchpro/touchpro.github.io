Pioneer Cemetery is located on State Street in Westerville, Ohio. It's a rather big cemetery that doesn't quite fit in with the surrounding hotels, restaurants, etc.

It looks like part of this cemetery is still in use. The older part, which is closest to State Street, is seperated from the newer part by a narrow road/driveway. Most of the headstones are in great condition, and the cemetery is very well taken care of. Chrissie and I visited the cemetery a few days after we had a bunch of severe storms so there were a few big tree branches down. It didn't look like they damaged any of the gravestones.
