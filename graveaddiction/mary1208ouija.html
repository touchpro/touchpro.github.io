Witching Hour

December 29, 2008



On December 29, 2008 I received an e-mail from Mary L. about an interesting ouija board experience:


I remember the day it happened - March 23 (my birthday). I got a ouija board for my birthday, and like any other 15 year old would do, I played with it, right?

Well, I started to play with it, and it was 3:00 AM (AKA the Witching Hour). I thought that if I did it at that time, I would surely get to talk to something.

And I did at about 3:15 AM. I finally got a response as I asked questions. I asked the name of this spirit I was talking to, and it replyed "Lusiphur." I believe that is how he spelled it, and last time I heard, it was the devil himself in the flesh. I stopped talking to him, but at night I can hear whispers, noises, screaming, and crying. I can even hear fire crackling, and I smell things burning at night. Ever since this encounter, I wake up with scratches and bite marks. I can't control myself anymore. I cut crosses in my arm to keep him away, but he won't leave. I don't eat that much anymore. I snap at people, and that's not like me. I'm sick and I say things I don't understand, and as it goes on I really can't sit still. I go to church, but everytime I walk close to the building I start to hurt, like I'm burning. I scream in pain. I'm in pain everyday, trying to get this away. Nothing else really happens, just this...THANK GOD!

Every night it happens. I'm fine during the evening, but once it hits 3:00 AM I can't control it. The doctors think I am going to eventually die with all my wounds. All I know is that I WILL NEVER USE A OUIJA BOARD AGAIN! Just don't ever use it and trust the one you are talking to!


If you have any comments or advice for Mary, please send me an e-mail and I will forward it on to her.
