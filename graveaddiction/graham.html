Graham Cemetery is located at the end of Township Road 263, a ½ mile west of County Road 23, outside Alvada, Ohio (Biglick Township, Hancock County). The coordinates are 41° 2' 24.51" N, 83° 26' 48.24" W.

Graham Cemetery is small, and it is no longer active. There are many old gravestones, including several with beautiful willow carvings. Although many of the gravestones have been damaged, the P.A.S.T. group at Findlay High School has repaired several. The grounds are well maintained by the township.

Many thanks to Kate H. for telling me about this cemetery!
