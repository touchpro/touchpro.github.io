Valley Cemetery is located at the intersection of Pine Street and Valley Street in Manchester, New Hampshire (Hillsborough County).

Valley Cemetery was established in 1840. It is very large, and it is still active. There are many old, beautiful gravestones, and most are in good condition. The cemetery is very well maintained. For more information, please visit the official website.
