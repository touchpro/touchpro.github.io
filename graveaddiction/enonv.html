Enon Valley Cemetery is located on the south side of US-224, just east of Township Road 255, outside West Independence, Ohio (Biglick Township, Hancock County). The coordinates are 41° 4' 6.43" N, 83° 29' 27.47" W.

Enon Valley Cemetery has many old gravestones, and most have been damaged. There was an effort to repair the gravestones in late 2010, but unfortunately most were not reset in the proper locations. The cemetery grounds are well maintained.

Many thanks to Kate H. for visiting this cemetery with me!
