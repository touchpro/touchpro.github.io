Amrine Cemetery is located at the end of Township Road 143, east of Raymond Road, outside Marysville, Ohio (Paris Township, Union County). The coordinates are 40° 14' 59.04" N, 83° 23' 35.63" W.

Amrine Cemetery was established in 1817, and it is no longer active. The cemetery was closed for new burials in 1915. There are many old gravestones, and most are in good condition. The township does a great job of maintaining the cemetery grounds.
