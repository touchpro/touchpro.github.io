Sunbury Cemetery is located off US-36, just east of OH-3, in Sunbury, Ohio (Delaware County). The coordinates are 40° 14' 44.82" N, 82° 51' 40.13" W.

Sunbury Cemetery is very large, and it is still active. The oldest sections are located on the southeast side. There are many old gravestones, and almost all of them are in great condition. Very few gravestones have been damaged, and most of the ones that have been broken are now repaired. The city does an excellent job of maintaining the grounds.
