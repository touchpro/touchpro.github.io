Lust Cemetery is located at the intersection of OH-4 and Carey Road outside Chatfield, Ohio (Chatfield Township, Crawford County). The coordinates are 40° 55' 17.57" N, 82° 57' 3.33" W.

Lust Cemetery, also known as the Evangelical Pietist Church Cemetery, is still active. The oldest gravestones are located near the church. Although most are in good condition, a few of the older gravestones have been damaged. The cemetery is very well maintained.
