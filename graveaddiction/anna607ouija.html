Call Me

June 16, 2007



On June 16, 2007 I received an e-mail from Anna about an interesting ouija board experience:


Ellie and I were using the ouija board and the spirit said its name was Kassidy. She was 14.

Us: Is there anything you want to tell us, Kassidy?
Board: Always get cum

Us: *laughing in disbelief* Are you joking or, like, are you serious?
Board: Yes (joking)

Us: Oh yeah, we were going to ask for a number to call. Do you want to give us a number to call?
Board: Yes

Us: Okay, give us the number.
The board gives us a number with I think way too many 2's...

Us: Whose number is this?
Board: Me

Us: This was your number?
Board: Yes

Us: Was this your cell phone number?
Board: No

Us: Was this your home phone number?
Board: Yes

Us: Does your family still live there?
Board: Yes

Us: Does your mom?
Board: No

Us: Does your dad?
Board: Yes

Us: So when we call this number, who should we ask for?
Board: *starts spelling Kassidy*

Ellie and I are really scared of what's going to happen at this point, but we call the number. It doesn't work. It's not a real number.

Us: Was that the right number?
Board: No

Us: Did it have too many 2's?
Board: Yes

Us: Do you want to try again?
Board: Yes *gives us a new number*

Us: Do you want us to put it on speaker?
Board: Yes

We call the new number and a recording says that the number has been disconnected.

Us: Is that all you needed to hear?
Board: Yes

Us: We're sorry. We really would have talked to them for you...
Board: Yeah, thx

I wish it had worked. I think it would have been really cool if someone actually answered and we were able to talk to the father of a 14 year old girl name Kassidy who had died. Would have really made a believer out of me!!
