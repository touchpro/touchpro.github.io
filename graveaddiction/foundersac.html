Founders Cemetery is located at the intersection of Main Street and Second Street in Manchester, Ohio (Manchester Township, Adams County). The coordinates are 38° 41' 19.3" N, 83° 36' 13" W.

Founders Cemetery is also known as the Manchester Presbyterian Church Cemetery and the Second Street Cemetery. It was established in 1791, and was active through 1889. This is an incredible cemetery, full of interesting gravestones. There are many beautiful carvings, and several interesting epitaphs. The cemetery is very well maintained.
