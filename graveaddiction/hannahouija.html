A Call From Mom

September 8, 2005



On September 8, 2005 I received an e-mail from Hannah R. about her creepy ouija board experience:


"A couple weeks ago, very early in the morning, my 2 friends and I were messing around with a ouija board. We had been talking to a spirit for about 15 minutes, when we asked them to give us a sign. We waited for a little bit, but nothing happened. Then we asked the board if the spirit would give us a sign later, and the planchette went to "yes". So about 5 minutes passed when my cell phone started to ring. I answered it...and it was my mother! Keep in mind that it was about 5:45 AM! So I answered and I was all confused and asked why she would be calling me so early, and she asked me, "Are you okay? Did you leave a message on the answering machine? It sounded like you and the girl was saying 'Mom PLEASE pick up the phone! I need you to pick it up, please help me!'" I continued to tell my mother that it wasn't me...

Well, I don't know if this was the spirit's doing, haha, but we all thought it was pretty strange!"
