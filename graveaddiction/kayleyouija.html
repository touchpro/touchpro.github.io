Little Paris

January 31, 2005



On January 31, 2005 I received an e-mail from Kayley about the time she and a friend talked to a little girl named Paris while using the ouija board:


"After we heard about our two friends talking to a guy named Dave (born 136, don't know if it was AD or BC) and Will (died 2004) with their own Ouija Board, me and my friend wanted to try it.

We tried it an hour before we are typing this.

We contacted a girl named Paris.

I wanted to cry.

KK/KD: Is there a spirit present?
Board: Yes

KK/KD: What is your name?
Board: Paris

KK/KD: How old were you when you died?
Board: 5 (thats when i wanted to cry.)

KK/KD: When did you die?
Board: 1992

KK/KD: Is it ok to ask how you died?
Board: No

KK/KD: Do you have any living relatives?
Board: Yes.

KK/KD: Thank you for talking. Goodbye.
Board: Goodbye

So, (i don't know why i wanted to submit this,) if you think you know Paris, please contact me at so_loly_inside_so_busy@hotmail.com. I currently still want to cry. She seemed to sweet."
