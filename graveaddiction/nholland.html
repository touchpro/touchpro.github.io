The New Holland Cemetery is located on the northeast side of Egypt Pike, near the intersection with Bloomingburg-New Holland Road, in New Holland, Ohio (Perry Township, Pickaway County). The coordinates are 39° 33' 34.88" N, 83° 15' 42.15" W.

The New Holland Cemetery is large, and it is still active. Several smaller cemeteries in the area were moved here, including Messmore Cemetery, Aaron Kellar Cemetery, and Porter Cemetery. The gravestones from these cemeteries can be found on the north side. There are many interesting, old gravestones. Most are in good condition, but several of the oldest stones have been damaged over the years. The cemetery is very well maintained.
