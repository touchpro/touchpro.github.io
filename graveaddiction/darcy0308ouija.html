Rescue Me

March 15, 2008



On March 15, 2008 I received an e-mail from Darcy G. about an interesting ouija board experience:


The other night me and my friends, Jed and Jon, brought out our ouija board on January 16, 2008 at 2:32 AM. We contacted a spirit named Nicholas Goodman.

This is how the first conversation went.

Darcy: Are you a boy or girl?
Nicholas: Boy

Darcy: What is your name?
Nicholas: Nicholas Goodman

Darcy: How did you die?
Nicholas: I am not dead. I may be in a coma

Darcy: Where is your body?
Nicolas: Side of road. Hit and Run. Come rescue me.

Jon: How can we rescue you? Where are you?
Nicholas: No wait this will be easier

My cell phone then started ringing. The Caller ID was unknown. I got very frightened so i rejected the call and attempted to end the session when a strong force pulled the planchette towards the letters: ULL B SORR-

Jed quickly ended the session, by flipping the board and putting it away.

The next night I was asleep in bed when my phone started to ring. I thought to myself: Who could be calling at such a stupid time? I checked the time. It was exactly 2:32 AM. I looked at the Caller ID and it was once again, Unknown.
