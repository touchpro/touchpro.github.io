Liar, Liar

May 14, 2005



On May 14, 2005 I received another e-mail from Scott Bodey about a very interesting ouija board experience:


"One of my last contacts on the ouija board was in February 2005. I don't remember who the spirit claimed to be, but it did say it was a good spirit. My fiance and I were talking with it for a while and suprisingly they held good conversations. Choppy sentences but still understandable. This is the conversation we had with the board. Not exact words but the best of my memory.

Board: Be careful

Me: Why?
Board: Hurt

Me: Who?
Board Christi (My fiance's name is Kristi)

Me: Kristi is going to hurt me?
Board Yes

Me: How?
Board: Kids

Me: Kids are going to hurt me?
Board: No, No kids

Me: What do you mean? (This is the part that sticks in my head....)
Board: Bad Eggs

Me: Kristi has bad eggs
Board: Yes

Me: How do you know?
Board: I can see

Kristi was fairly torn up by that so we ended the conversation. It's now May and we have a baby due in December. Kristi wanted nothing more than to have a child, and that spirit knew and tried to mess with our minds. Don't trust them and definitely don't believe them."
