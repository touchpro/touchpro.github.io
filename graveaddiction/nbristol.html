The North Burial Ground is located at 1081 Hope Street (RI-114), at the intersection with Asylum Road, in Bristol, Rhode Island (Bristol County). The coordinates are 41° 4' 18" N, 71° 16' 53" W.

The North Burial Ground is large, and it is still active. There are many interesting gravestones, and several have detailed carvings. Most of the gravestones are in great condition. There is also a beautiful cremation garden that already has stones in place, just waiting for names to be added. The cemetery is very well maintained.

For more information about the North Burial Ground, please visit the Town of Bristol website.
