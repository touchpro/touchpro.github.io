Oak Grove Cemetery is located at the end of Plummer Street, east of US-201, in Gardiner, Maine (Kennebec County). The coordinates are 44° 13' 11" N, 69° 46' 35" W.

Oak Grove Cemetery was established in 1848, and it is still active. There are many old gravestones, including several marble monuments with very detailed carvings. There are also a few white bronze monuments. Although some gravestones have been damaged, most are in great condition. The cemetery is very well maintained.
