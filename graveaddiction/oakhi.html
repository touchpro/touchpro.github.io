Oak Hill Cemetery is located off Grange Hall Road, just west of Fish Lake Road, outside Holly, Michigan (Holly Township, Oakland County). The coordinates are 42° 48' 24" N, 83° 38' 36" W.

Oak Hill Cemetery is still active. There are many old gravestones on the hill; the newer stones seem to be located towards the bottom. Most of the gravestones are in very good shape, but a few have been damaged over the years. The cemetery is very well maintained.
