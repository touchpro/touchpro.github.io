Pleasant Hill Cemetery is located on the north side of US-40 in West Jefferson, Ohio (Jefferson Township, Madison County). The coordinates are 39° 56' 39.4" N, 83° 17' 3.74" W.

Pleasant Hill Cemetery is still active. The oldest gravestones are located on the west side of the cemetery, and most are in great condition. The township does an excellent job of maintaining the grounds.
