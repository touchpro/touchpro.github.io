Harrison Cemetery is located on the south side of Salisbury Road, west of Taylor Homestead Road, outside Salisbury, Illinois (Sangamon County). The coordinates are 39° 53' 34" N, 89° 49' 50" W.

Harrison Cemetery is small, and it is no longer active. It is completely surrounded by a stone wall, and you have to climb up and down a set of stairs to access it. There are several old gravestones, and most are in good condition. The cemetery is very well maintained.
