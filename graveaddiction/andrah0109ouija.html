Ouija Warning

January 29, 2009



On January 29, 2009 I received an e-mail from Andrah about an interesting ouija board experience:


Okay, so this is a story of warning to those who dare to dabble with the ouija board. One night three friends and I decided playing with the ouija board was a good idea. We then contacted another friend, and he arrived after we had already started communicating. He didn't believe in the board, and starting saying it wasn't real and just a whole bunch of negative things towards it. I told him to leave because I didn't need the risk of something happening, so he left. After he had left, the board told us, "show at noon." None of us had any clue what this meant. The next day at noon, the kid who didn't believe in the board and said not so positive things totalled his car. This is just a warning, especially if you are someone who experiences the paranormal; you're stronger than most, and could cause serious issues. Please, please, please think twice before using the board.
