Saint Aloysius Cemetery is located on the grounds of Mount Aloysius Academy, off Tile Plant Road, in New Lexington, Ohio (Pike Township, Perry County). The coordinates are 39° 43' 1.11" N, 82° 13' 49.56" W.

Saint Aloysius Academy was founded by the Sisters of Saint Francis on September 1, 1876. This cemetery is the final resting place for those sisters. The gravestones are old, and all of them are in great condition. The cemetery is very well maintained by the Mount Aloysius Academy.
