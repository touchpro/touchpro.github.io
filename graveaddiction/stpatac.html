Saint Patrick Cemetery is located on the east side of Conner Road, north of Bessemer Road, outside Buchtel, Ohio (York Township, Athens County).

Saint Patrick Cemetery is located next to the Buchtel Cemetery, and it is still active. There are many old gravestones, including several iron cross markers. There are also many homemade markers made out of materials such as concrete, metal, and tile. Most of the monuments are in good condition, but like any old cemetery, some have been damaged. Several of the monuments have Hungarian names and epitaphs. The cemetery is very well maintained.
