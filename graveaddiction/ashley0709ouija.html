Evil Spirit

July 7, 2009



On July 7, 2009 I received an e-mail from Ashley about an interesting ouija board experience:


This happened Thursday of last week. My three friends, Darien, Lauren, Shelby, and I were at a horse show. We were all done showing for the day, and my friend Lauren decided that it would be fun to use the Ouija board she just bought.

Lauren: Is anyone there?
Board: Yes

Me: What is your name?
Board: Ellen

Me: Are you evil?
Board: No

Shelby: How old were you when you died?
Board: 109

Me: What did you die of?
Board: Age

Lauren: Can you tell me what place I will get tomorrow?
Board: yes 3rd

Me: What place will I get tomorrow?
Board: 2nd

Shelby: How about me?
Board: 5th

Darian: Me?
Board: 8th

(All of those placings ended up being true, except Lauren. You will read why later on.)

Me: Do you know any evil spirits?
Board: yes

Shelby: What are their names?
Board: Zeb

Me: Is Zeb here?
Board: Yes
Board: This is zeb

(the planchette was moving very, very fast now)

Lauren: Ellen told us you were evil.
Board: Yes

Me: Are you going to hurt any of us?
Board: No

(Shelby's brother decided to come play, and thought that we were pushing it. And that it was stupid and retarded.)

Shelby: Are you mad at my brother?
Board: yes

Me: What are you going to do to him?
Board: Board hit on head.

Lauren: You are going to hit him on the head with a board?
Board: no lauren is

Lauren: Are you going to possess me?
Board: yes

Lauren: Please don't.
Board: okay

Me: Can we talk to someone else?
Board: yes Goodbye

Shelby: Who is this?
Board: Zane

Me: Are you evil?
Board: yes

Me: Are you going to hurt any of us?
Board: Yes

Me: Who?
Board: you

Me: You as in me?
Board: Yes

(I screamed and ran away)

Shelby: Zane, where are you?
Board: on Ashley (which is my name)

Lauren: Where is she?
Board: By the food stands
Board: tell her not to run I chase her

(right then I came back)

Me: Zane, why are you mad at me?
Board: you are scared

Me: I'm sorry. I will try not to be scared anymore.
Board: yes

(The board was glow in the dark, and Lauren thought it was cool when she scratched her name into it. After she wrote her name with her fingernail on the board a few times, the planchette started to chase her finger when placed in on the board to write her name.)

Shelby: Are you mad at Lauren?
Board: yes

Me: Are you going to hurt her?
Board: I don't want to talk to you

Me: Okay, I will stop talking.
Board: please

Shelby: Are you going to hurt Lauren?
Board: yes

Shelby: How?
Board: Fall off horse

Darien: Is she going to break anything?
Board: leg rib and arm

Me: Goodbye, Zane.
Lauren- Yes, goodbye.
Board- Goodbye

(Lauren never fell off her horse because she refused to ride the next day)

The next day we talked to Zane, Zeb, a spirit named Evil, an alex (who we looked up on Google and was real), and Ellen again.
