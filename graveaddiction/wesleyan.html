The Wesleyan Cemetery is located at 4003 Colerain Avenue in Cincinnati, Ohio (Hamilton County). The coordinates are 39° 9' 35" N, 84° 32' 48" W.

The Wesleyan Cemetery was established in 1843, and it is large. The cemetery is filled with old gravestones. Although many are in good condition, this cemetery wasn't well maintained for many years, and several of the gravestones were damaged. Fortunately, the cemetery is now very well maintained. I was very impressed with all of the detailed carvings on the gravestones; some of the carvings were very unique.

A big thanks to Pam Bohnenstiehl for visiting this cemetery with me!
