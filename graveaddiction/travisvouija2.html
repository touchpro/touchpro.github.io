Flying Planchette

April 5, 2007



On April 5, 2007 I received an e-mail from Travis about his interesting ouija board experience:


I used an ouija board I made, but I burned it.

I asked if anybody was there, and the board moves to yes. I asked who, and it moved to sld. I asked if that was the initials, and it moves to no. I asked what does sld stand for, and it spelled out satin, legion, and devil. I asked it to instantly leave now, and as god as my witness you shall leave! It moved to no, and the planchette started going all over the place...my hand wasn't even on it, so I ran to get my cross. I put it around my neck, grabbed my crucifix and placed it on the ouija board. The planchette went to no, so I tried to move the planchette to goodbye but it wouldn't let me.

The planchette tried to move off the board, but I wouldn't let the damn thing. Then there was no movement, so I put my cross down on the ouija board and this is hard to believe, but it happened...It flew of the damn board, then the planchette tried to spell out hell. I tried to move it to goodbye, but it just wouldn't let me. I kept pushing it away from the letter and numbers, afraid it would spell 666 or hell. Then it stopped. The planchette basically let me move to goodbye. I said goodbye, then the planchette flew of the damn board. Then the board flew at me, and that's the night I burned it.
