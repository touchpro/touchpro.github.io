Our Father

July 9, 2009



On July 9, 2009 I received an e-mail from Brittany about an interesting ouija board experience:


My name is Brittany, and I'm using homemade ouija boards. Here is my story.

Me:Our Father
Which art in heaven
Hallowed be thy name
Thy kingdom come
Thy will be done on earth
As it is in heaven
Give us this day our daily bread
And forgive us our trespasses
As we forgive those who trespass against us
And lead us not into temptation
But deliver us from evil
For thine is the kingdom
And the power
And the glory, forever, Amen

I don't want any bad spirits entering this board and none are allowed to enter. If you are a bad spirit you may not enter, and only good spirits may enter.

Does any entity want to speak with me?
Board: yes

Me: What's your name?
Board: Quinn

Me: May I record this onto my computer?
Quinn: Yes

Me: Then I'm going to leave for a few moments. May I say goodbye?
Quinn: No

Me: Why?
Quinn: scared

Me: You're scared?
Quinn: yes

Me: Don't worry, I'll be right back. I promise. Now goodbye.
Quinn: yes.goodbye

I go to the computer after flipping over the planchette and putting a silver dime on the board for good luck. I type what has happened so far and go to communicate with Quinn more.

Me: Our Father
Which art in heaven
Hallowed be thy name
Thy kingdom come
Thy will be done on earth
As it is in heaven
Give us this day our daily bread
And forgive us our trespasses
As we forgive those who trespass against us
And lead us not into temptation
But deliver us from evil
For thine is the kingdom
And the power
And the glory, forever, Amen

Is Quinn there?
Board: Yes

Me: May I speak with her?
Board: Yes

Me: Is this her?
Board: yes

Me: Quinn, you're female right?
Quinn: Yes

The phone rings. It is Sarah. I do not get it in time.

Mom: Britt, it's Sarah!

Me: I gotta go, I'll be right back. Goodbye.

I move the planchette to goodbye and rush to the phone before calling Sarah back. She doesn't answer. I call again. She still doesn't answer. I turn on Audition to see if Jae (my bf) is on. Audition has to download new patch first. While waiting, I call Christina. Nobody picks up the phone. I plan to try and make up with Jae. We had a fight before this. Computer's being slow. I contact on ouija board again.

Me: Our Father
Which art in heaven
Hallowed be thy name
Thy kingdom come
Thy will be done on earth
As it is in heaven
Give us this day our daily bread
And forgive us our trespasses
As we forgive those who trespass against us
And lead us not into temptation
But deliver us from evil
For thine is the kingdom
And the power
And the glory, forever, Amen

Is Quinn there?
Board: Yes

Me: Is this her?
Board: Yes

Me: I didn't let you go by slipping off my fingers, did I?
Quinn: No.

Me: If I let go, will you come out?
Quinn: No

Me: Hold on.

I shut my door.

Me: May I ask how you died?
Quinn: Gun shot.

Me: You died by a gun shot?
Quinn: Yes

Me: One moment. Can you show me you're here?
Quinn: yes.

Me: You won't hurt, will you?
Quinn: No

Me: Alright. Hold on - don't do anything yet. Okay, you can do something now, but I'm gonna let go.

I begin to wait. One minute passes. I see the light flicker.

Me: Did you make the light flicker?
Quinn: yes

Me: I'm gonna say goodbye now. I'll come talk to you later, is that alright?
Quinn: Yes

I feel a tickling feeling on my arm.

Me: Is that you touching my arm?
Quinn: Yes. Goodbye.

Me: Goodbye.

I end the session.
