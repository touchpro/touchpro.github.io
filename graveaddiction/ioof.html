The Odd Fellows Cemetery is located at the intersection of 8th Street and Cemetery Street in Manchester, Ohio (Manchester Township, Adams County). The coordinates are 38° 41' 40" N, 83° 35' 59.6" W.

The Odd Fellows Cemetery is large, and it is still active. There are many old gravestones, and most are in good condition. A few have been damaged, but this is not uncommon in old cemeteries. Several of the gravestones have interesting carvings and epitaphs. The cemetery is very well maintained.
