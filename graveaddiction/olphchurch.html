The abandoned Our Lady of Perpetual Help Catholic Church is located in the Sedamsville neighborhood in Cincinnati, Ohio.

The Our Lady of Perpetual Help parish was organized in 1878, and the church was dedicated on May 5, 1889. It was used until 1989. Since then, it has been abandoned. Although it has been vandalized, it is still structurally sound.
