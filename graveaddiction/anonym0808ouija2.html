Talking to Boyfriend

August 31, 2008



On August 31, 2008 I received an e-mail from an anonymous website visitor about an interesting ouija board experience:


My friend Bella and I talked on the ouija board last night and met some cool people. This morning we wanted to try it again. So we got it all set up and started talking.

Us: Is anyone there?
Board: Yes

Us: What's your name?
Board: (lots of different letters)

Us: How old are you?
Board: 91

Us: How did you die?
Board: Natural causes

Us: Is there anything you would like to tell us?
Board: Yes

Us: What is it?
Board: Jeremoy

*Jeremy was my friend's boyfriend who was shot one night. She had a long distance relationship with him. She never got to meet him, but the night he was going to leave to see her, he was shot.*

Us: Do you mean Jeremy?
Board: Yes

Us: What about Jeremy?
Board: nz nz nz (we did not know what that meant, but he keep saying it over and over)

Us: May we talk to Jeremy?
Board: No

Us: Does Jeremy want to tell us something?
Board: Yes

Us: May we please talk to Jeremy?
Board: Yes

Us: Is this Jeremy?
Board: Yes

*I told Bella to ask the board something only the two of them would know*

Us: What is Jeremy's middle name?
Board: Curtis

*that is right*

Us: Were you shot?
Board: Yes

Us: Did you want to marry me? (my friend)
Board: Yes

Us: Am I making you sad?
Board: No..

Then he spelled out love you.

Us: Should I move on?
Board: Yes

Us: Will you always be here?
Board: Yes

*we had to get going because we had to go eat with the family*

Us: We have to go. Can we talk to you again?
Board: Yes

Us: Can we talk to you tonight?
Board: Yes

Then we said goodbye and left.

We were both in tears the whole time. As soon as he said Jeremy, we both started crying. It was so sad for the both of us. We are hoping we can talk to him again tonight.

I never really believed all this.

Until now.
