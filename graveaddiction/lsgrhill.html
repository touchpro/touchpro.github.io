Lloyd Sears sent me the following information about the photos pictured above:

"If you ever go back to this cemetery, there are two interesting graves you should check out. The first is in the very back of the cemetery. On the headstone, it states that the man was killed by John Morgan of Morgan's Raiders. The other is located a little closer to the new section. It has no headstone, but the grave itself is covered in concrete. I talked to the caretaker who told me what a family member told him - that the husband had it done that way "to make sure that the b*$%# didn't come back."
