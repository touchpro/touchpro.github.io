Photos taken by Jeremy Lloyd

Jeremy Lloyd from Milton, West Virginia sent me these photos that he took using the night vision feature on his video camera. He's also the first person who told me about the Lakin Industrial Home for Colored Boys!

Jeremy explored the school on February 1 with his friends Jeremy C. and Angie. They thought they heard footsteps and a faint scream on the second floor, but he said that it could have been their imaginations. Also, one part of the building had cold air blowing through it, even though there was no wind outside! Jeremy did capture a few weird things on video. There were several orbs and a sparkly thing. He said that they ruled out some of the orbs as glare, however he said that they're still not sure about a few of them. Also, the sparkly thing may just be dust from Jeremy C. walking in the attic above them.

There is one strange thing in the video that Jeremy and his friends can't explain. They didn't see this when they were actually in the building, but about five minutes into the video there's a part where the camera pans across the hallway a few times. One time when it pans by, the half open door at the end of the hallway opens quickly and then closes back to it's half open position. Jeremy said there was no wind outside, and besides, wind doesn't change direction fast enough to open and close the door in a matter of seconds!
