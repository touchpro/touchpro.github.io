Frysinger Cemetery is located on the north side of OH-117, a ¼ mile west of US-127, outside Rockford, Ohio (Dublin Township, Mercer County). The coordinates are 40° 42' 48.78" N, 84° 35' 1.83" W.

Frysinger Cemetery is also known as Pleasant Grove Cemetery and Frisinger Cemetery. There are many old gravestones, and although some are in very good condition, others have been broken and damaged. The grounds are very well maintained by the township.
