Being Watched

January 25, 2009



On January 25, 2009 I received an e-mail from Savannah about an interesting ouija board experience:


My friends and I got addicted to a Ouija board this summer. We were using it, and we asked to talk to a dead teacher at our school. The board let us talk to her, but then it stopped and went to a guy called ZoZo. He said he is going to kill us when we are forty. Every other time we tried to use the board after that, it would either spell out ZoZo, or just keep saying forty. We stopped using it completely, but I always feel as if I am watched.
