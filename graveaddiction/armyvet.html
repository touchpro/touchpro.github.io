Army Veteran's Photo

submitted by Chris





Chris e-mailed me this photo so he could get my opinion of a few strange things he noticed in this photo. He said that if you look to the right of the soldier's face, you can see a reflection of the clouds. If you take a closer look at the clouds it appears that there is a face. Chris saw the photo of this soldier at a ceremony to give this deceased veteran full military honors. He died at the Battle of the Bulge in Luxembourg during World War II.
