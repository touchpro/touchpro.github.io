Somerford Cemetery is located on the west side of OH-56, just north of I-70 outside Summerford, Ohio (Somerford Township, Madison County).

Somerford Cemetery is a large, very well cared for cemetery. There are quite a few old gravestones, but the cemetery is still active and most of the gravestones are more recent. Several unnamed cemeteries have been moved to this cemetery over time to allow for development/building. It is an interesting cemetery to explore.
