Mount Pleasant Cemetery is located on the south side of OH-138, just west of the intersection with Kinderhook Road, outside Williamsport, Ohio (Deer Creek Township, Pickaway County). The coordinates are 39° 34' 0.62" N, 83° 4' 6.13" W.

Mount Pleasant Cemetery is no longer active. It sits next to the old church, which is now a private residence. There are many old gravestones, but unfortunately, several have been damaged. The cemetery grounds are very well maintained.
