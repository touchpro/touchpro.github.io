Diane G. sent me the following e-mail about Saint Mary's Catholic Cemetery:

St. Mary's Catholic Cemetery is still active, and has beautiful statues throughout. I went out here to find my daughter's father - it took us two trips because we did not realize that there was another section about a block down on the right. It was actually a very somber day for us, she found him within a few minutes.

The two locations are located at the northeast corner of Parkinson Avenue and Kramer Street, with the other section being at the northwest corner of Parkinson Avenue and Reid Avenue.
