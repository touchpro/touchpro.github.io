Godwachu

September 1, 2007



On September 1, 2007 I received an e-mail from Nicole about an interesting ouija board experience:


I have gotten really interested in Ouija boards lately, so I decided to make my own out of a cutting board and some saran wrap. I've used it three times so far, although the first time didn't work so well. The second time I used it with my dad and brother, and we talked to a man who was in one of my dad's ghost stories (which I had thought he had made up until then).

The third time was quite entertaining. It was after everyone else had gone to bed, and my brother and I decided to try one more time. We contacted a spirit who at first said he was a walrus in heaven by the name of Pot who had been killed by a monkey named DK. Then he told us he was joking and said he was actually an angel. He said "godwachu," which we confirmed meant God was watching us (I'm agnostic, so this is interesting). I asked him if he wanted to say anything else, and he told my brother to eat shit, because he didn't like him. After a few more questions, he said he had nothing more to say so we said goodbye.
