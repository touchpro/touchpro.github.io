Vera Cruz Cemetery is located on US-50 in Vera Cruz, Ohio (Perry Township, Brown County). The coordinates are 39° 10' 9.88" N, 83° 58' 1.99" W.

Vera Cruz Cemetery is also known as Holy Ghost Parish Cemetery, and it is still active. The cemetery is very well maintained, and most of the gravestones are in great shape. However, there are several gravestones located near the back (by the woods) that are very overgrown. I'm not sure why these gravestones are so overgrown, since the majority of the cemetery is so very well taken care of.
