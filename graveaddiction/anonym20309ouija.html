Mysterious Spot

March 22, 2009



On March 22, 2009 I received an e-mail from an anonymous website visitor about an interesting ouija board experience:


My stepsister and I were in our basement. She is a little older than me, and she wanted to play. I really didn't want to, so I went upstairs. I shut the light on the way up, and she called me. She yelled my name nervously, and I didn't know why! My board was a glow in the dark one...just keep that in mind. I went to see what the problem was, and I turned the light back on. She told me to shut the light (she is a trickster sometimes, so I was reluctant.) I shut the light, and saw the mysterious spot. The planchette was safely in the box and the board was out. Right there, on the board, was a black shadow mark in the shape of the planchette. We placed the box and it's contents in a plastic bag with salt and threw it out!
