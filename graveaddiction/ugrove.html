Union Grove Cemetery is located at the intersection of Grove Church Road and Lee Wolfe Road outside Gambier, Ohio (Harrison Township, Knox County). The coordinates are 40° 20' 32" N, 82° 21' 29.43" W.

Union Grove Cemetery was established around 1823, and it is still active. It is also known as Grove Cemetery. There are many old marble and sandstone monuments, and several have beautiful carvings. Most of the gravestones are in good condition, but some of the older monuments have been damaged. The cemetery is very well maintained.
