Saint Ambrose Cemetery is located off the west side of Martin Luther King Jr Parkway in Des Moines, Iowa (Des Moines Township, Polk County). The coordinates are 41° 35' 36" N, 93° 38' 48" W.

Saint Ambrose Cemetery is adjacent to the north side of Woodland Cemetery. It was difficult for me to determine which cemetery certain stones were in, so there may be some placed on the wrong page! Most of the gravestones in this cemetery are in great condition, and there is a very nice variety of different monument types to see. The cemetery grounds are very well maintained.

Many thanks to my coworker Nicol for visiting this cemetery with me in April 2010.
