The Fairview Corporation Cemetery is located off Oxford Road, up a long gravel drive just outside the Fairview, Ohio Corporation limits (Oxford Township, Guernsey County). The coordinates are 40° 3' 19.6" N, 81° 14' 23.91" W. You can see the cemetery to the south when driving on I-70.

The old part of the Fairview Corporation Cemetery is no longer an active cemetery; there is a newer section further up the hill that is active. There are many interesting gravestones, and most are still legible and in great condition. The cemetery is very well taken care of. There is a beautiful view of the surrounding hills and fields around the cemetery, and it's a very peaceful place.
