Mount Hope Cemetery is located at 791 Mount Hope Avenue in Rochester, New York (Monroe County). The coordinates are 43° 7' 47" N, 77° 37' 9" W.

Mount Hope Cemetery was established in 1838. It is huge (197 acres), and it is still active. There are many old gravestones and beautiful statues. Most are in excellent condition. The grounds are very well maintained.

For more information about the cemetery, please visit the Friends of Mount Hope Cemetery website.
