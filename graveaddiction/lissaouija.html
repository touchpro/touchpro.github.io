April 25, 2005



On April 25, 2005 I received a very creative e-mail from Lissa with her ouija board story:


"My friends Alicia, Marie, and I were playing with a ouija board. We lit 7 candles. We started playing and contacted a man named Stu. We asked if he was real to knock something over. One candle fell over and the board burned. A tiny flame appeared but we blew it out. There was only a scorch so we kept playing. We asked when Marie would die and the board spelled out "36". We didn't know what this meant, but we stopped playing. Three days later [about 36 hours] Marie's mother found her in bed dead. We still play with the ouija every Saturday but have not yet spoken with Marie. We hope to some Saturday!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!



JUST KIDDING

we made the whole thing up

THE END?"
