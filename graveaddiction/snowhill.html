Snow Hill Country Club is located at 11093 State Route 73 in New Vienna, Ohio (Clinton County).

Snow Hill Country Club was built in the 1820's, as the residence of the Harris family. They operated a tavern in the building for many years, and in 1924 it was turned into a country club and golf course. I visited Snow Hill in October 2008 for one of their "Dinner and a Ghost" events. There have been many reports of paranormal activity there, in both the old and new areas of the facility. There are many unexplained electrical problems, strange sounds, odd smells, and some have even seen apparitions. There were too many people there the night we visited to conduct a true paranormal investigation, but it is a very interesting place to visit.

For more information about Snow Hill Country Club, please visit the official official site.
