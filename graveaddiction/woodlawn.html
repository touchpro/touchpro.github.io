Woodlawn Cemetery is located at 1751 Spencerville Road (OH-117) in Lima, Ohio (Shawnee Township, Allen County). The coordinates are 40° 43' 46.8" N, 84° 7' 56.3" W.

Woodlawn Cemetery is huge (106.04 acres), and it is still active. There are many interesting gravestones, including many with beautiful statues. There are also many interesting mausoleums. The cemetery grounds are extremely well maintained by the cemetery association.
