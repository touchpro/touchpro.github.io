Picture in the Vent

December 14, 2008



On December 14, 2008 I received an e-mail from Stephanie about an interesting ouija board experience:


This is what happened... me (Stephanie), my friend Malinda, and my cousin Ashley were playing with the ouija board. We were talking to Malinda's grandfather, and he said 6 o'clock. Then we asked what he meant by six o'clock. He said somthing good will happen. Malinda asked if he meant 6 AM or PM, and he said AM. So Ashley and I said that we were going to spend the night, and we did.

At 5:57 AM, all three of us woke up. Malinda said she had a dream that there is somthing in the vent in her living room. So we went down to the living room. We looked in the vent, and there was a picture of a man, a lady, and an orb (spirit). After we found it, we got out the ouija board and asked if the spirit was the man or the lady or the orb in the photo. He said the orb. So then we asked who we were speaking with, and he said F Z (initials). Then we asked it what should we do with the photo, and it said that we should burn it at 6 PM this afternoon. So at 6 PM we went outside and burned it!!!!!!

Crazy story!!!
