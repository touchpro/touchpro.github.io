Creepy Experience

July 14, 2009



On July 14, 2009 I received an e-mail from Lexi and Dalton about an interesting ouija board experience:


This is Lexi and Dalton...we have a very creepy ouija board experience that we would like you to share. It happened on July 14, 2009 at my house.

Dalton: Are there any powerful spirits here with us?
Board: yes

Dalton: What is your name?
Board: 666

Lexi: Are you Satan?
Board: yes

Lexi and Dalton: Can you show us that you are here?
Board: just wait

Lexi: When?
Board: 5:00

Lexi: Wait, do you mean five in the morning?
Board: yes

Board: You are all doomed

Lexi: May we leave this game?
Board: yes

We say goodbye.

(Board then spells out die twice)

Dalton: May we please leave this game?
Board: yes

We say goodbye again.

(board then spells die again)

Both Dalton and I: Can we please leave this game?
Board: yes

We say goodbye, then so does the board.

And we wait to see what happens...
