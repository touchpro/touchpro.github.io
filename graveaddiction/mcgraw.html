McGraw Chapel Cemetery is located at the intersection of OH-117 and CR-96 outside Huntsville, Ohio (Richland Township, Logan County). The coordinates are 40° 28' 58.16" N, 83° 48' 47.94" W.

McGraw Chapel Cemetery is no longer active. There are many rows of old gravestones. While many are in good shape, quite a few have been broken/damaged over the years. The cemetery grounds are very well taken care of though.
