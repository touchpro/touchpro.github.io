Fairview Cemetery is located on the east side of Ostrander Road, north of US-36, outside Ostrander, Ohio (Scioto Township, Delaware County). The coordinates are 40° 16' 35.76" N, 83° 12' 52" W.

Fairview Cemetery, also known as Edinburg Cemetery, is still active. The oldest gravestones are located on the south side, and most are in good condition. The cemetery is very well maintained by the township.
