Red Bank Cemetery

Galena, Ohio

Redbank Cemetery is located on the west side of Redbank Road outside Galena, Ohio (Delaware County, Genoa Township). The coordinates are 40° 11' 0.86" N, 82° 52' 17.94" W.

Redbank Cemetery is also known as Copeland Cemetery (although there is a separate listing for another Copeland Cemetery nearby). While there are some more recent burials, the majority of the graves in this cemetery are older. It is very well taken care of, although it is pretty obvious that some of the gravestones have been vandalized.

A big thanks to Dave Boyer for telling me about this cemetery, and for visiting it with me!
