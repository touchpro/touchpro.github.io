Wednesday, May 19, 2004
posted at 9:10:13 PM

So today I turned a quarter of a century old. I really don't feel very different though :)

I did take a half day off work today, but not because it was my birthday. I had my six-month dental checkup down in Lancaster. It's been many, many years since I last heard those dreaded words, so I can't complain too much...I had TWO small cavities. This is the first time I've had to have fillings since my old dentist retired, so I was extremely nervous. See, my old dentist was the greatest - he never had to numb me before doing the fillings. Since I'm needle phobic that was terrific. I knew I wasn't going to be that lucky with the new doctor. I went out to the front desk to schedule a return visit, and the receptionist told me that they actually had a cancellation and they could do the fillings right away. That was pretty cool actually, saved me another 1/2 day off work! But even though that was great, the fillings weren't. He gave me three shots of novocaine on the left side, and decided that he might not have to numb the other side for the second cavity...since I just told you I have needle phobia, you know I didn't protest :) The shot hurt like hell, and I hated the numb feeling. He probably should have numbed the other side too, because the drilling was extremely painful, but I have a pretty high pain tolerance so I was able to get through it without a whimper. So tonight my whole mouth is sore and hurts. I took some pain medicine, I hope it kicks in soon. Definitely wasn't how I planned on spending my birthday, that's for sure!

My parents wanted to take me out to dinner, but since I couldn't feel the left side of my mouth, my bottom lip, or my tongue I decided that might not be such a good idea! I told them we could celebrate my birthday later. I did stay at their house and played with my little brothers (the 3 wiener dogs) for almost an hour.

When I got home I talked with my friend Chrissie on the phone. She really wanted me to meet her and Jos for dinner at Mac's in the Short North. My mouth was still numb, but since the restaurant is so close (within walking distance) I just couldn't refuse. I hadn't seen either of them for ages so it was a lot of fun to catch up. And they were too kind...they bought me a birthday present! Gargoyle bookends, they're really cool :) Chrissie also gave me a cool birthday card with the gift, she always finds the PERFECT cards! Hopefully it won't be another two months before I get to see them again!!!

My coworkers were really nice today too, they took me out for lunch. I tried to buy my own, but they just wouldn't let me. Just one more reason why I say my coworkers are the best!

Anyway, I really should check my e-mail since I haven't checked it since 11AM...but I'm not going too since I feel so awful. Plus the Excedrin PM is kicking in and it's difficult to keep my eyes open!
