Prediction of My Death

September 16, 2009



On September 16, 2009 I received an e-mail from Elektra about an interesting ouija board experience:


My sister-in-law and I played with a Ouija board for the first time last saturday (September 12, 2009). We asked it silly questions at first, and were amazed at how easily the planchette moved across the board. After a little bit, I asked it how old I was going to be when I died, and it went straight to 24. I am currently 24. After asking more questions, I conceded that my husband is going to shoot me on a Monday in between now and November 2. He is currently out of town, and I am not sure when he is going to be back.

Two days later, her boyfriend came over and we played again. I had the same results. Now, I am terrified. I always feel like a man is behind me. I have not seen him, but I know what he looks like in my mind. I almost passed out at work, just a few minutes ago. My body does not feel right, and I am shaky and weak.

Do you hear many stories like this? Please let me know if you have any suggestions. I know that you do not think that the boards are evil, but I am seriously frightened. Thanks.


If anyone has any advice for Elektra, please send me an e-mail and I will forward it on to her.
