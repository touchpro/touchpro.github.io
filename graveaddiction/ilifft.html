The Iliff-Township Cemetery is located on the west side of Flint Ridge Road, a ¼ mile north of OH-93, outside Crooksville, Ohio (Harrison Township, Perry County). The coordinates are 39° 44' 31.61" N, 82° 5' 56.85" W.

The Iliff-Township Cemetery is still active. It is located across the road from the small Iliff Cemetery. There are many old gravestones, and most are in great condition; only a few have been damaged. The cemetery is very well maintained.
