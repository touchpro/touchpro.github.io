Cramer (also spelled Cranmer) Cemetery is located on the west side of US-50, about one mile south of Vera Cruz, Ohio (Perry Township, Brown County). The coordinates are 39° 9' 27.65" N, 83° 59' 14.19" W.

Cramer Cemetery is no longer active. There are many rows of old gravestones, but unfortunately many have been badly broken over the years. Although the majority of the gravestones have been damaged, the cemetery grounds are very well taken care of by the township.
