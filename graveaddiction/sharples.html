Sharples Cemetery is located on the south side of Angola Road, about ¼ mile east of OH-295, outside Swanton, Ohio (Harding Township, Lucas County). The coordinates are 41° 37' 43.83" N, 83° 50' 4.18" W.

Sharples Cemetery is small, but it is still active. The oldest gravestones are located on the west side. Almost all of the gravestones are in great condition; only a few have been damaged. The township does a great job of maintaining the cemetery.

Many thanks to Andy Greenlese for taking me to this cemetery! I really appreciate all the help he has given me with locating cemeteries in Northwestern Ohio.
