Zaz Returns

August 20, 2006



On August 20, 2006 I received an e-mail from Tanya Burnett about her interesting ouija board experience:


One night, I am not sure when or even what year, my sister, Charli (she was about 13), had a sleepover at our house. My sister was addicted to her ouija board, and she persuaded everyone to do a session thing with her. They didn't let me near the board after that, and I couldn't find out why. None of them slept that night either.

Finally, after about a week, my sister told me what happened. She said that she was talking to a spirit named Zaz, and they talked for about ten minutes. Then weird things started happening...a black light fell on my sister's friend, Jenny, and the lights started turning on and off in slow motion. Their fingers were still on the board, and slowly the dial began moving...letters at first, then forming words, then one full sentence: IN THE END THE DEVIL WILL RULE.

After the sleepover the board was thrown away and now my sister is sixteen, and she won't even talk about the board. Wait until I give one to her for her birthday!
