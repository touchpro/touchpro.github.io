Mound Cemetery is located on the east side of Mound Cemetery Road in Piketon, Ohio (Seal Township, Pike County). The coordinates are 39° 3' 8.39" N, 83° 1' 7.28" W.

Mound Cemetery is still active. There are many beautiful gravestones with detailed carvings, and most are in great condition. There are also a few Native American mounds in the cemetery. Two of the mounds have gravestones on them. The cemetery is very well maintained by the township.
