Indian Fork Cemetery

Coldwater, West Virginia

Indian Fork Cemetery is located near the intersection of Indian Fork Road and Cub Run Road outside Coldwater, West Virginia (Doddridge County). The coordinates are 39° 10' 26" N, 80° 38' 16" W.

Indian Fork Cemetery is small, but it appears that it may still be active. Even though it is a small cemetery, there are some very interesting monuments, including two trunk shaped gravestones and one baby in a shell. However, several gravestones have been damaged over the years. The cemetery is very well maintained.
