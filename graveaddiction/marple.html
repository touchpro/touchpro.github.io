Marple Cemetery is located on the east side of Preston Road, about two miles north of Marion Road, outside St. Louisville, Ohio (Newton Township, Licking County). The coordinates are 40° 10' 28.76" N, 82° 27' 2.23" W.

Marple Cemetery is very old, and it is no longer active. There are many rows of old gravestones, and most are in good condition. However, there are many gravestones that have been damaged by weather and vandals over the years. The township does a very good job of maintaining this cemetery.
