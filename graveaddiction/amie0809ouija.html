Talking to her Dead Sister

August 25, 2009



On August 25, 2009 I received an e-mail from Amie about an interesting ouija board experience:


My friend Nelliey and I were trying the ouija board with our friends. Then my friend Nelliey wanted to talk to her sister...we did. She asked the board if it was possible to talk to her sister who committed suicide in 2007.

Just right after we asked, the board went to hello. Then we asked if it was Heather, and it went to yes. We asked her several questions that only she would know the answer to. It was her. We were so happy, but scared at the same time. We asked her if she would hurt us, she said no.

But she was also happy, know how I know? Because she would write hahah several times.
