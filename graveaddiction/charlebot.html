Charleston Bottoms Cemetery is located on the south side of KY-8 (Mary Ingles Highway), at the intersection with Charleston Bottoms Road, outside Dover, Kentucky (Mason County). The coordinates are 38° 41' 29" N, 83° 48' 42" W.

Charleston Bottoms Cemetery is still active. There are many old gravestones, and although most are in good condition, several of the oldest monuments have been damaged. Many of the gravestones have beautiful carvings. The cemetery is very well maintained.
