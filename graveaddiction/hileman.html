Smith Cemetery is located on the south side of OH-81, near the intersection with Schumm Road, outside Schumm, Ohio (Willshire Township, Van Wert County). The coordinates are 40° 44' 31.78" N, 84° 43' 37.42" W.

Smith Cemetery is also known as Hileman Cemetery, Hartzog Cemetery, or Alspach Cemetery. It is no longer active. There are many old gravestones with interesting carvings. Unfortunately, several of the gravestones have been damaged and are lined or stacked up throughout the cemetery. The township does a good job of maintaining the cemetery grounds.
