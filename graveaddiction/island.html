Island Cemetery is located at 30 Warner Street in Newport, Rhode Island (Newport County). The coordinates are 41° 29' 50" N, 71° 18' 53" W.

Island Cemetery is located next to the Common Burying Ground, and it is still active. There are many interesting gravestones, and several have detailed carvings and beautiful statues. Most of the gravestones are in great condition. The cemetery is very well maintained.

Many thanks to Ta Mara C. for visiting this cemetery with me!
