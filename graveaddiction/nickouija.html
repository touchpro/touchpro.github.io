I'm just a kid, but I used the ouija board with my friends Matt and Joey.

Me: Is anyone there?
Board: no movement

Me: Is anyone there?
Board: Yes

Me: Who is this?

Me: Kyl, are you a good spirit?
Board: Maybe

Me: Are you trickster?
Board: Yes

Me: What's it like to die?
Board: Weird

Me: Really? How did you die?
Board: From car crash
