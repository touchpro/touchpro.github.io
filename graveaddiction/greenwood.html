Greenwood Cemetery is located along Canal Street and City Park Avenue in New Orleans, Louisiana (Orleans Parish).

Greenwood Cemetery is a very large, extremely well taken care of cemetery. It was established in 1852 by the Firemen's Charitable and Benevolent Association (the predecessor of the current New Orleans Fire Department). The cemetery is filled with many interesting gravestones, statues, and tombs. It wasn't on any of the tours I saw, but it's well worth visiting!
