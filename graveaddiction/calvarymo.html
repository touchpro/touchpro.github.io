Calvary Cemetery is located at 5239 West Florissant Avenue in St. Louis, Missouri (St. Louis City). The coordinates are 38° 42' 12" N, 90° 14' 17" W.

Calvary Cemetery was established in 1854, making it the second oldest cemetery in the Archdiocese of St. Louis. It is huge, and it is still active. The cemetery is filled with many interesting gravestones, including some with very detailed carvings. There are also several impressive mausoleums. The cemetery grounds are extremely well maintained.
