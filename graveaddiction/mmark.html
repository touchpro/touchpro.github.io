The Maker's Mark Distillery is located at 3350 Burks Spring Road in Loretto, Kentucky (Marion County).

The Maker's Mark Distillery was designated as a National Historic Landmark in 1980. It is the only operating distillery in America with this designation. Visitors are given a free tour of the distillery, including the still house, bottling house, and barrel warehouse. The tour is FANTASTIC, and I highly recommend it if you're ever in the area.

For more information about the Maker's Mark Distillery, please visit the official site.
