Union Methodist Cemetery is located on the north side of Frank Road, just west of Gantz Road, in Columbus, Ohio (Franklin Township, Franklin County). The coordinates are 39° 55' 8.67" N, 83° 3' 2.47" W.

Union Methodist Cemetery is also known as Briggsdale Cemetery or Union Church Cemetery. It was established in 1822, and it is no longer active. It is the site of the old Union Methodist Episcopal Church. Most of the gravestones have been damaged, but it appears that many have been repaired recently. It is a very interesting cemetery to visit.
