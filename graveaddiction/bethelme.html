The Bethel Methodist Episcopal Cemetery is located on the east side of Township Road 58, just south of Township Road 56, outside Sego, Ohio (Madison Township, Perry County). The coordinates are 39° 49' 39.28" N, 82° 10' 43.21" W.

The Bethel Methodist Episcopal Cemetery is small, and it is no longer active. The old church used to be located across the road, but no evidence of it remains today. There are many old gravestones, and most are in great condition. However, like any older cemetery, a few gravestones have been damaged. I had a chance to talk to the person who lives across the road, and he and the other neighbors keep a VERY close watch on it to make sure no further vandalism occurs. The cemetery is very well maintained.
