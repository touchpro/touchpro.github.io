A Shock

April 17, 2007



On April 17, 2007 I received an e-mail from Erebus about an interesting ouija board experience:


Three friends and I were present. Here we go now...

Us: Are there any spirits with us?
Board: Yes

Us: Can you give us your name please?
Board: David Thompson

Us: Hi David, how are you?
Board: I'm fine especially when a fine lady is present.

Us: What is the fine lady's name?
Board: Ann

Ann. You find me attractive then?
Board: Very I wish I was alive then I'd take you somewhere nice

Ann. Where would you take me?
Board: I would take you on a boat to have a romantic dinner at night. We would eat dance and watch the moon shimmering on the river.

Ann. You are a real smooth operator.
Board: Well 1 does try u know

Us: When did you die David, and how?
Board: I came off my motorbike at high speed

Us: Sorry to hear that how old were you?
Board: 23

Us: How did you get in my house? Did you live here?
Board: No I followed Ann

Ann. Do you follow me often?
Board: Yes I like you

Ann. I have a boyfriend.
Board: Yeah ****** is an idiot you can do better (protected Ann's boyfriends name)

Ann. He might be an idiot but he's a great lover.
Board: Lucky Bastard

Ann. Would you like to be my boyfriend?
Board: 1 can dream cant they if I was alive then yes

Us: Is there anything you want to tell us?
Board: I will be looking out for you Ann

Us: Are there any other spirits present?
Board: Yes

Us: Are they good or bad?
Board: dont know

Us: can we talk to them?
Board: Yes

Us: Is there anyone else that would like to talk to us?
Board: yes I'm damien

Us: Hi Damien, how are you?
Board: Dead

Us: Apart from dead, how else are you?
Board: Missing my phyiscal body

Us: Why?
Board: I like women drinking etc

Us: How did you die?
Board: Electrocuted

Us: How?
Board: Drilled through a live cable

Us: Shocking!
Board: How dare u make fun of me

Us: No we were having fun.
Board: My turn to have fun

Us: Ok

Then the candle fell of the table and the wax was spilled on my carpet. I picked up the candle and re-lit it. I then went back to the ouija board and placed my finger on the planchette. The planchette flew off the board. I picked it up and put it on the board, and the table started shaking. I then closed the session by saying goodbye, and by moving the planchette to goodbye.
