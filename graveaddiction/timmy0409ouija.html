Feedback

April 2, 2009



On April 2, 2009 I received an e-mail from Timmy about an interesting ouija board experience:


My name is Timmy, from southern California, and I just thought I would share my story. I personally think its kinda interesting.

A group of my friends and I were over at my friend Brian's house watching a UFC fight, when the topic of the Ouija board came up. Brian said he had one, and I always wanted to try it so we did. We took it upstairs and we weren't getting much feedback, so I went downstairs and asked Brian's mom to try because apparently she's good at it. After a little, it started working. We took turns asking this person questions. This is what we found out about this person...

- His name is Dylan
- He died at the age of six
- His sister killed him with an axe when she was nineteen (freakiest part of the night)
- His parents were with him
- He was on the bed right by us


Now whether or not this was true, I still thought it was pretty cool that a six year old came down to talk to us. He also said that there was no heaven or hell, but God was preventing Brian's grandfather from talking to us.

Another thing that peaked my interest is the fact that this kid knew about the future. He predicted that I was going to make it home 5 minutes late from my curfew (which I did), and that I was going to get in trouble as a result. My dad said I was indeed in trouble, but since this happened last night, I don't know if I really am or not.

Regardless, I completely believe in the Ouija board. It's kinda creepy, but very fun.
