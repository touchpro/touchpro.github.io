Last Experience

June 17, 2008



On June 17, 2008 I received an e-mail from Alissa F. about an interesting ouija board experience:


This was my last experience, and I have not touched a board since. I did not write the conversation down, but remember enough of it.

It is about my ex, who was pretty much a psycho whp would tell me about everything going shitty for him. So I've always thought he would kill himself.

I was at this guy's house one night, who I had been seeing, and we decided to use the board.

Me: Is there anyone who would like to talk to us?
Board: Yes

Me: Could you tell us your name please?
Board: (spells out my ex's name)

Me: **** who?
Board: (spells out ex's full name)

Me: Whatever, this isn't you...ok then, what kind of car do you have?
Board: Skyline (this is right)

Me: What's your mum's address?
Board: (writes out correct address)

Board: O.D O.D O.D (as in over dose)

*by now I was seriously freaked out, and at some point now I was crying*

I asked a few more questions, then went to bed. I woke up in the morning, unable to forget what had happened. So I went back on the board.

Me: Are you there ****?
Board: Yes

Board: I'm sorry
Board: I love you

Me: (crying) You idiot, why did you do that?!!!!!
Board: I'm sorry

Board: I love you like I always did.
(this was something he had also said to me in person when we had had a fight before)

At one point, we had talked about baby names and I remember him telling me a name that he liked. I would not have remembered this name again if I hadn't heard it. The next thing that he said freaked me out.

Board: Call my baby **** (the name he had said before)

I was a mess at this point, so I got the guy I was with to call my ex's phone. He answered and was fine. He didn't know who it was calling or anything.

So I have no idea who was messing with my head or how they knew this stuff. It was not me moving it, because there no way I could have thought of the name otherwise...it's not a common name that you would remember.

If anyone has any idea, please contact me. =)


If you have any advice or information for Alissa, please send me an e-mail and I'll forward it on to her.
