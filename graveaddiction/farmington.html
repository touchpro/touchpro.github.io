Farmington Cemetery is located on the south side of Farmington Cemetery Road, east of North Farmingdale Road, outside Farmingdale, Illinois (Sangamon County). The coordinates are 39° 49' 45" N, 89° 47' 56" W.

Farmington Cemetery was established in 1836, and it is still active. The oldest gravestones are located on the east side, and most are in great condition. The cemetery is very well maintained.
