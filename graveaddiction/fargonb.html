Fargo North Cemetery is located at the intersection of OH-61 and Township Road 216 outside Marengo, Ohio (Bennington Township, Morrow County). The coordinates are 40° 22' 55.82" N, 82° 49' 39.47" W.

Fargo North Cemetery, also known as Butter Cemetery, is still active. The oldest gravestones are located towards the back. While a few gravestones have been damaged, most are in great condition. Many of them have very interesting epitaphs that are still legible. The township does an excellent job of maintaining the cemetery.
