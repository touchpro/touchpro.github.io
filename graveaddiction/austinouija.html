Banging Walls

June 16, 2005



On June 16, 2005 I received an e-mail from Austin Phillips about his eerie ouija board experience:


"I have been playing for a long time now...years...and I've got my fair share of fairly frightening experiences, though this is the most recent. Me and a friend, Rob, as well as my brother's girlfriend Gina were playing in my garage.

Me: Is anyone there?
Board: No response

Me: Please answer
Board: Yes

Rob: Are you evil?
Board: going over moon 10 to 20 times

Gina: Is my grandmother near me?
Board: Yes

Gina: Can I talk to her?
board: No

Rob: Do you follow either of us?
board: Yes

Me: Who?
Board: Rob

Me: Are you male or female?
board: F


At that point the walls begin banging all around us. My brother runs into the garage telling us to stop banging on the walls. At first we concluded that he was playing with us, but then we realized that the bangs were on all sides of us. After a few seconds the banging started again, except this time a massive painting that is about 3 feet by 5 feet in a thick frame fell onto my brother's head and fell on the ground and broke. That concluded our session for the night!"
