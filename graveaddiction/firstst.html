The First Street Cemetery is located at the intersection of First Street (US-250) and Cypress Avenue in Moundsville, West Virginia (Marshall County). The coordinates are 39° 55' 36" N, 80° 44' 8" W.

The First Street Cemetery is no longer active. There are many old gravestones, including several sandstone and marble monuments with detailed carvings. Although some gravestones have been damaged, there are many that are still in good condition. The cemetery grounds are very well maintained.
