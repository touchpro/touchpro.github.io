Smith Cemetery is located on the north side of Boyd Road, about ½ mile west of Converse Chapel Road, outside Plain City, Ohio (Darby Township, Madison County).

Smith Cemetery is now a nature preserve maintained by the Ohio Department of Natural Resources. The ownership of the land was transferred to the ODNR in December 1982. Smith Cemetery is an excellent example of the tall grass prairies that were found throughout the Darby Plains. There are many native grasses and wildflowers growing among the gravestones.
