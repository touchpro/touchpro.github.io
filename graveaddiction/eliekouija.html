On July 24, 2007 I received an e-mail from Elie K. about an interesting ouija board experience:


My name is Elie K. I'm from Lebanon. Well to start, I'll talk about my first try: I was writing sentences and replacing the letters with the one before in the alphabet. So I used the ouija with my cousin and we got an answer after 15 minutes: O D V R. We ended the session, and when I replaced the letter I got NEWS!!! The spirits knew what I was doing before the seance.

Back to the subject. I sat down with my friends (Maya, Reetzy, Nour, and me) and we lit a candle and turned off all the lights. At first I placed my fingers with Maya on the planchette.

Us: Hey, is there anyone here?
Board: no answer

Us: Anyone there?
Board: Hi

Us: Hey, welcome.
Board: Nothing

Us: What's your name?
Board: X PO

(Reetzy replaced me)

Maya and Reetzy: Can you give us your full name please?
Board: Hi

Maya and Reetzy: Can you give us a sign?
Board: No

(I replaced Reetzy)

Us: Can you give us your full name please?
Board: Xavier Pott

Us: What year is now?
Xavier: 2007

Us: How old are you?
Xavier: 93

Us: When was your birth date?
Xavier: 1908

Us: Where are you from?
Xavier: KU

Us: How many spirits are there in this room?
Xavier: 5

Maya: Are you the spirit that always wears a black hat that I used to see? (Maya used to tell me that there's a spirit who keeps showing with a long black hat)
Xavier: No

Us: Do you know him?
Xavier: Yes

Us: Why do we keep seeing him?
Xavier: Jokes

Us: What do you mean?
Xavier: He loves to hear jokes.

Us: Why?
Xavier: Because he loves to hear jokes.

Maya: Do you know my name?
Xavier: No

Me: Do you know my name?
Xavier: Yes

Me: Please spell it.
Xavier: ELIE

Me: Are you my spirit guide?
Xavier: Yes

Maya: Anyone know my name?
Xavier: Maya.

Maya: You said you didn't know it! How do you know it now?
Xavier: Dayz

Us: Do you mean Daysie?
Xavier: Yes

Us: Who is she?
Xavier: I didn't...

Us: Sorry, we have to go now. Thank you for your help, and we wish to talk to you again. Bye!

Well, it was an awesome seance. The planchette was going really slow at the beginning, but in the end it was sooo quick. We had to end it because my brother was in my room and we didn't want him to know what we were doing. IT WAS REAL!!!!
