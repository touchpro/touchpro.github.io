Indian Run Cemetery is located off Dublin Road in Dublin, Ohio. It's behind the Dublin Library in Washington Township (Franklin County).

Indian Run Cemetery was established in 1813, and it has been placed on the National Register of Historic Places. It was restored by the Dublin Historical Society between 1975-1980. Today it is taken care of by the City of Dublin. Although most of the gravestones are broken and propped up or cemented to the cemetery wall, it is very well taken care of. It's definitely an interesting cemetery to visit!
