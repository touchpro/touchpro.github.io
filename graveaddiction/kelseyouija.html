ZAZA

July 22, 2005



On July 22, 2005 I received an e-mail from Kelsey about her creepy ouija board experience:


"My boyfriend and I were going threw a tough time. Well, I had been using the Ouija board and I made a new friend named ZAZA. I met her through the Ouija board, and that night I had a dream that I met a guy and he held me close. Then I called Tom (my boyfriend) and he was like 'Hey Hannah'. My name is Kelsey. I later found out that Tom was cheating on me with a girl named Hannah. I asked the Ouija board if ZAZA sent me the dream and she said yes. The next day I asked the board if I was gonna die, and it said that the board was gonna kill me in 3 days at 6:46 AM. I just found that out. I have 3 days yet to live, it told me to burn it cause it was evil. In 3 days I may be dead... but I doubt it!"
