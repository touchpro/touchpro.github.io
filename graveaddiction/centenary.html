Centenary Cemetery is located at the intersection of Township Road 120 and Township Road 122 outside Port Washington, Ohio (Salem Township, Tuscarawas County). The coordinates are 40° 17' 38.55" N, 81° 31' 7.53" W.

Centenary Cemetery is owned by the township. There are several broken gravestones, but for the most part the cemetery is in fantastic shape. It is very well taken care of, and there are plenty of old gravestones to look at.

Centenary Cemetery is located along the Winding Staircase, a nickname for the narrow road leading up the hill. The road is supposedly haunted, and there have also been reports of occult/satanic rituals going on in the woods along the road for many years. The legend also says that dark figures have been seen rising out of the graves in Centenary Cemetery.
