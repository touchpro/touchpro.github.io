Yoder Cemetery is located on the west side of County Road 1, approximately a ¼ mile north of Township Road 30, outside Valley Hi, Ohio (Monroe Township, Logan County). The coordinates are 40° 17' 19.65" N, 83° 43' 44.39" W.

Yoder Cemetery is small, and it is still active. The oldest gravestones are located towards the back, at the top of the hill. Most are in excellent condition. The township does a terrific job of maintaining the cemetery grounds.
