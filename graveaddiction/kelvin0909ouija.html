First Experience

September 4, 2009



On September 4, 2009 I received an e-mail from Kelvin about an interesting ouija board experience:


Hi, my name is Kelvin, and my friends Dylan and Denyale had our very first Ouija board experience last night. Well, it was the first time for Denyale and I, that is, but Dylan had used it a couple of times way back in the day, according to him. Here is the transcript of my session. Can you tell me what you make of it?

Kelvin: Is there anyone here who wishes to speak with us?
Board: YES

Kelvin: Thank you. What is your name?
[clicking noise is heard in video repeatedly three times]

Kelvin: Can you tell us your name?
Board: YEZAI

Kelvin: Is your name Yezai?
Board: YES

Kelvin: Where are you from?
Board: LARG

Kelvin: Are you from Larg?
Board: (no movement)

Kelvin: How old are you?
Board: 70

Kelvin: Are you 70 years old?
Board: YES

Board: LAKE

Board: YAKIMA

Board: KIN

Kelvin: Has Denyale met you before?

Board: KI

Board: MI [three rapid clicking noises are heard on the video]

Kelvin: Who are we speaking to?
Board: KHI

Kelvin: What does Dylan need to hold interest in?

Kelvin: Is Kim still with us right now?

Kelvin: Is anyone still with us?

Kelvin: Thank you for your presence, we will try again later. Goodbye.
