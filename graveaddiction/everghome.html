Evergreen Home Cemetery is located at 408 33rd Street in Beatrice, Nebraska (Gage County). The coordinates are 40° 16' 4" N, 96° 42' 20" W.

Evergreen Home Cemetery is large, and it is still active. There are many old gravestones, and most are in great condition. My favorite section, of course, was the Beatrice State Developmental Center graves. The oldest ones are small limestone or marble slabs, and the newest are made of granite. There are several that are made of concrete with plastic plates that contain the names and dates of the deceased. The cemetery is very well maintained by the Beatrice Cemetery Association.
