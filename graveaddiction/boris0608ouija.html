Poltergeist

June 4, 2008



On June 4, 2008 I received an e-mail from Boris D. about an interesting ouija board experience:


Let me start my story. I have used the Ouija board several times, but the last time...it was a sort of bad experience for me.

I sat with my friend Marko, and we started the ouija table. I asked, "Are there any spirits to talk with us?" After 5 minutes, one male spirit talked to us.

We asked him when he died, how old he was, and then I asked him...

Me: So, can you tell me why you are at my place?
Board: Poltergast

Me: You mean poltergeists?
Board: Yes

Me: What about poltergeists?
Board: One Poltergeist

Me: So, I have poltergeist in my house?
Board: In sort of way

Me: Is he following me?
Board: Beware

Me: Why, am I supposed to worry?
Board: Yes

Me: How can I get rid of the poltergeist?
Board: Goodbye

Me: Are you still with us?
Board: (no answer)

Me: Are you still with us?
Board: (no answer)

Can you please help me with this?

From that moment I haven't had any poltergeist activity, and that was three months ago. Right now, everything in my house seems to be ok, no poltergeist activity. Can you tell me some answers please, what should I think/do?


If you have any information or advice for Boris, please send me an e-mail and I'll forward it on to him.
