Talk with the Unknown

April 8, 2009



On April 8, 2009 I received an e-mail from Dan S. about an interesting ouija board experience:


This conversation was with a new board that I have just made, my old board is now missing. This conversation took place April 7, 2009 at 10:20 PM - 11:30 PM.

Dan: Are there any spirits that would like to talk to me tonight?
Board: Yes

Dan: What is your name?
Board: Not known

Dan: Are you the spirit I talked with awhile ago?
Board: No

Dan: Was that spirit a guy or a girl?
Board: Not known

Dan: What is your name?
Board: Not known

Dan: Are you a guy or a girl?
Board: A

Dan: Are you a guy?
Board: Not known

Dan: Am I going to die?
Board: Yes

Dan: Am I going to die from this Staph Infection?
Board: No

Dan: How am I going to die?
Board: Yes

Dan: How am I going to die?
Board: Ask later

Dan: When am I going to die?
Board: Winter

Dan: Am I going to die this winter?
Board: Unknown

Dan: Can you give me a date of when I'm going to die?
Board: Never

Dan: Why is that?
Board: Yes

Dan: Can you please tell me your name?
Board: No

(computer turns on and off)

Dan: Are you possessing my computer?
Board: Not clear

Dan: Can you show me that you are here in some way?
Board: Yes

Dan: How?
Board: Not clear

Dan: Can you flicker my lights please?
Board: No

Dan: What are you going to do then?
Board: Not known

Dan: Can you please pick something up in my room?
Board: No

Dan: Are you here to tell me something?
Board: No

Dan: Can I talk to someone else?
Board: No

Dan: Why not?
Board: Future hazy

(fridge opens and shuts)

Dan: Are you getting into my brother's fridge?
Board: No

Dan: Where is your grave located?
Board: Not here

Dan: Where is it then?
Board: Maybe later

Dan: Where did you die?
Board: Not here

Dan: Can you let me talk to anybody else?
Board: No

Dan: Why not?
Board: Not known

Dan: Did you have a job?
Board: Not known

Dan: Are there any other spirits in my house?
Board: No

Dan: Are you in my house?
Board: No

Dan: Where are you?
Board: Far away

Dan: Do any of my friends have spirits in their house?
Board: No

Dan: Do you want to keep talking?
Board: Yes

Dan: What do you want to keep talking about?
Board: Not known

Dan: Can you please let me talk to somebody else?
Board: No

Dan: Are you going to keep being boring?
Board: Not known

Dan: What is going to happen 12/21/12?
Board: Too dark

Dan: What is going to happen 7/31/09?
Board: Too dark

Dan: Is somebody going to die that day?
Board: Maybe later

Dan: Do you want to go?
Board: Yes

Dan: Thanks for coming, Goodbye
Board: Unknown

Dan: Goodbye
Board: Not clear

Dan: Should I use the board I made?
Board: Yes

Dan: What should I use as a planchette?
Board: Future hazy

Dan: Goodbye
Board: Cannot say

Dan: Why not?
Board: Not known

Dan: Goodbye
Board: Goodbye
