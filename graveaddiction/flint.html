Flint Cemetery is located on the west side of Flint Road, about a mile east of US-23, in Worthington, Ohio (Sharon Township, Franklin County). The coordinates are 40° 7' 56.53" N, 83° 0' 32.46" W. It is also known as Gardner Cemetery.

Flint cemetery sits off the road behind a small woods. There are a lot of older headstones at this cemetery, but that's not the most interesting thing I saw at this cemetery. Lots of the graves here are decorated with all sorts of little toys, flowers, etc. It was really fun to ponder why the relatives/friends put those objects on the graves.
