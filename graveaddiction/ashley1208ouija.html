Trickster

December 20, 2008



On December 20, 2008 I received an e-mail from Ashley about an interesting ouija board experience:


I will not give out real names in this story, except for the names of the spirits. My friend, let's call her Casey, came over to my house, lets call me Ashley, and brought her sister's ouija board. This is our story.

Casey: Are you with us?
Board: Yes

Casey: Are we allowed to record what you are talking about with us?
Board: Yes

Casey: You are very polite.
Board: Thank u

Casey: You're welcome.

Ashley: Will you tell us your name?
Board: No

Ashley: Why not?
Board: Gerfid

Ashley: Would you like to try again?
Board: Yes

Ashley: So what is your name?
Board: Stu

Casey: Is your name Stu?
Stu: Yes

Casey: It's nice to meet you, Stu.
Stu: yes

Ashley: Do you live at my house?
Stu: Yes

Casey: Do you know Dug?
Stu: Yes

Ashley: Is he a bitch?
Stu: Yes

Casey: Is this your favorite room in the house? (We were in my room)
Stu: Yes

Ashley: Why?
Stu: HSQ

Ashley: Would you like to try again?
Stu: Yes

Ashley: So why is this room your favorite?
Stu: Dear N

Ashley: At least you spelled a word this time.
Stu: Yes

Ashley: How old are you?
Stu: 4

Ashley: So you're 4?
Stu: Yes

Ashley: Is this still Stu?
Stu: Yes.

Casey: Do you like saying yes?
Stu: Yes

Ashley: May I ask how you died?
Stu: Yes

Ashley: How did you die?
Stu: Raped

Casey: We are very sorry.
Stu: Yes

Ashley: Who raped you?
Stu: Seth

Casey: Was Seth your brother?
Stu: Yes

Casey: Is it easier to say yes than no?
Stu: Yes

Ashley: Is that because you are 4 years old?
Stu: Yes

Casey: What year did you die?
Stu: 143

Ashley: Would you like to try again?
Stu: Yes

Ashley: Are you tricking us?
Stu: Yes

Ashley: Who is this really?
Board: Dug

Ashley: Damn you, Dug.

Casey flips the board over.

That was a very interesting experience!!! I swear this is 100% true! Well, except the names of my friend and I.
