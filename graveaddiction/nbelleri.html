New Belleville Ridge-Dowling Cemetery is located on the east side of Carter Road, close to the intersection with Dowling Road, outside Dunbridge, Ohio (Perrysburg Township, Wood County). The coordinates are 41° 28' 45.31" N, 83° 35' 27.77" W.

New Belleville Ridge-Dowling Cemetery was established in 1888, and it is still active. The oldest gravestones are located towards the front, and just about all are in great condition. There is one Medal of Honor recepient buried here, a Civil War veteran. It's a very interesting cemetery to visit!
