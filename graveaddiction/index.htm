Welcome to Grave Addiction!

This site contains photos I have taken at all of the cemeteries, haunted places, abandoned buildings, and historical parks that I have visited. I'm always exploring new places, so the site is updated with new photos and stories on a regular basis (I attempt to make an update at least once a week). You can see the most recent updates by clicking here.

The photos have been divided into several sections, you can view them by clicking on the links in the navigation bar on the left side of the screen. The site's size can be a little overwhelming, so if you're looking for something specific you can search the site by clicking here.

I should also add that the majority of cemeteries, structures, and parks that I have on the site are from Ohio...that's because I live in Ohio. However, whenever I travel to other states or countries I'll be sure to visit some cemeteries/structures while I'm there and take plenty of photos for the site.

Do you know of any cemeteries or haunted places that I should visit? Have any comments or questions about the site? Need help finding an ancestor's grave? Leave a message in the guestbook, post a message on the message board, or e-mail me. Or if you prefer, you can also reach me by using any of the contact methods listed in the navigation bar on the left (including postal mail). I always enjoy hearing from site visitors and make every effort to reply to each e-mail/letter I receive. If you send letters, photos, or videos to the P.O. Box listed in the navigation bar, please be sure to let me know if it's OK to post the info you sent on the site. Also, if you would like any of the items you send back, be sure to let me know...I have no problems doing that! You might also want to send me an e-mail to let me know that you mailed something, just so I know to expect it and check for it at the post office.
