Wyuka Cemetery is located at 3600 O Street in Lincoln, Nebraska (Lancaster County). The coordinates are 40° 49' 3" N, 96° 39' 54" W.

Wyuka Cemetery was established in 1869, just two years after Nebraska became a state in 1867. The cemetery's name comes from a Lakota Indian word that means "to rest." The cemetery is now over 140 acres, and contains many beautiful gravestones, including several mausoleums and monuments with gorgeous statuary. The cemetery is very well maintained, and visitors can pick up self-guided tour directions in the office.

For more information about Wyuka Cemetery, please visit the official website.
