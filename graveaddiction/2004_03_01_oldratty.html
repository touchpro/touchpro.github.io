Takes a whole lot of the poor little lobster wannabees to fill up a person. A big pot of jambalaya was served too and plenty of beer since that is really good with all that spicy food. Guess out of all the family that went Mommy's mommy was in command of the wildest group that didn't sit long in one place. My ass, recuperate. She has been on the phone setting up appointments for window people to come and give estimates and fence people to come and give estimates. She is determined to get a fence up so when she is out gardening she doesn't have to watch the dumbass dogs too. They think they are big normal dogs instead of the short legged, long bodied mutants that they are. OH WAIT! This is wonderful, seems mommy's daddy got a tee shirt with a long haired dachshund on the front of it, just like Oscar the Rotund. Mommy's mommy says he can wear it around the house but not in public, he will look like a really old silly man. Of course she bought tee shirts with voodoo all over them. I hope to hell she is kidding, she is getting strange as she gets older. Not sure I trust her not to dabble a little with the darker arts...ooooeeeeeeooooooo (scary noise).
