Hazelton Cemetery is located in the woods off the west side of OH-93, north of Old Town Road, outside Shawnee, Ohio (Salt Lick Township, Perry County).

Hazelton Cemetery is no longer active. It is located on private property in the woods, and is difficult to locate. The cemetery is not maintained; the gravestones are overgrown and several have been damaged. There are some neat stones with beautiful carvings though.

Many thanks to Rob D. and Jose L. for visiting this cemetery with me!
