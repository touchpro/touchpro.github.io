Holcomb Cemetery is located on the west side of Township Road 202, just north of the intersection with Royma Road, outside Portersville, Ohio (Bearfield Township, Perry County). The coordinates are 39° 42' 3.51" N, 82° 2' 49.54" W.

Holcomb Cemetery is also known as the Fletcher Chapel Methodist Episcopal Cemetery. There are many old gravestones, and although a few have been damaged, most are in great condition. The cemetery is very well maintained.
