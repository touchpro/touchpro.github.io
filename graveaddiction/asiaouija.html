Self Infliction

June 10, 2007



On June 10, 2007 I received an e-mail from Asia about an interesting ouija board experience:


My friend Kelsey and I were using the Ouija board in the daytime, but my room is dark and all the windows are covered.

Kelsey says a chant to attract the demons - I don't know what it is because I'm new to this.

Kelsey: Are there any demons out there?
Board: Yes

Kelsey: Do you want to hurt us?
Board: No

Kelsey: Are you happy where you are?
Board: No

Kelsey: Is there anything we can do to help?
Board: Yes

Kelsey: What can we do?
Board: KL

Kelsey: Do you mean kill?
Board: Yes

Kelsey: Kill who?
Board: U

Kelsey: Why do you want us to kill ourselves?
Board: Slfinflctn

Kelsey: Do you mean Self Infliction?
Board: Yes

Kelsey: Do you watch us hurt ourselves?
Board: Yes

Kelsey: Do you enjoy watching us hurt ourselves?
Board: Yes

Kelsey: Do you know satan?
Board: Yes

*I feel something gripping my right arm*

Kelsey: Are you grabbing Asia's arm?
Board: Yes

Kelsey: Which arm are you grabbing?
Board: R

Kelsey: Why are you grabbing Asia's arm?
Board: Sex

*Me and Kelsey get freaked out and talk for a second*

Kelsey: What is your favorite number?
*expecting it to say 666*
Board: 69

Board: Goodbye.
