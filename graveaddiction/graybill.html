Graybill Cemetery is located back a grassy lane off the east side of Old Columbus Road, just east of Ety Road, in Lancaster, Ohio (Greenfield Township, Fairfield County). The coordinates are 39° 44' 29.22" N, 82° 37' 53.5" W.

Graybill Cemetery, also known as Bush Cemetery, is very small and it is no longer active. There are many rows of old gravestones, but only a few are still standing. The majority of the gravestones have been damaged, and are scattered throughout the cemetery. The township does a great job of maintaining the cemetery grounds.
