September 17, 2009



On September 17, 2009 I received an e-mail from Sofia about an interesting ouija board experience:


I wanted to tell you a weird and funny story of my first time with the Ouija board.

My first time, it was with me, my dad, and my older brother. We were curious about the Ouija board, so we made one out of paper and cardboard. At about 8-10 o' clock, my dad started showing us how to play. After that, we asked if anyone was there. It said "Yes," so we asked the spirit's name. Here's where it gets kinda weird. The answer was, "Z5Z5Z5Y5J5." We were confused, so we asked him to try again. He said the same thing. So we asked it how it died. This was a long time ago, so I don't quite remember it all, but he either kept saying his name, or said random letters and numbers. We asked if he could speak English, and he said no, so we said goodbye. Nothing bad ever happened, but it was still pretty funny.
