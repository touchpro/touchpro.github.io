Hope Cemetery is located at 175 Maple Avenue (VT-14) in Barre, Vermont (Washington County). The coordinates are 44° 12' 41" N, 72° 30' 5" W.

Hope Cemetery was established in 1895, and it is still active. The cemetery is filled with granite gravestones, made from stone in the town's quarries. Many of the gravestones mark the final resting places of gravestone carvers. There are numerous statues and beautiful carvings.

For more information about Hope Cemetery, including a map, please see the Hope Cemetery Guide.
