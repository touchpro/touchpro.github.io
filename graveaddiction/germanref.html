The German Reformed Cemetery is located at the intersection of OH-95 and Township Road 31 in New Bloomington, Ohio (Big Island Township, Marion County). The coordinates are 40° 35' 7.29" N, 83° 18' 18.29" W.

The German Reformed Cemetery is very small, and it is no longer active. Most of the gravestones are in great condition; very few have been damaged. It is very well maintained by the township.
