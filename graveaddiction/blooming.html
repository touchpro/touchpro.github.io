Blooming Grove Cemetery is located on the west side of OH-13, just south of Noble Road, outside Greenwich, Ohio (Butler Township, Richland County). The coordinates are 40° 58' 23.91" N, 82° 29' 38.41" W.

Blooming Grove Cemetery is very small, but it appears that it may still be active. It is also known as Bethel, Church of God, Mitchell, or Winebrenner Cemetery. The oldest gravestones are located on the east side, and although some are in good condition, many of the older monuments have been damaged. The cemetery grounds are well maintained by the township.
