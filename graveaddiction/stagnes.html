Saint Agnes Cemetery is located at 48 Cemetery Avenue in Menands, New York (Albany County). The coordinates are 42° 42' 9" N, 73° 43' 40" W.

Saint Agnes Cemetery was established in 1867. It is large Catholic Cemetery that is still active. It is adjacent to Albany Rural Cemetery. There are many old gravestones, including many with beautiful statues and crosses. Most of the monuments are in excellent condition. The grounds are extremely well maintained by the Roman Catholic Diocese of Albany.
