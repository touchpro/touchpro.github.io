Greenbriar Cemetery is located at the intersection of Glenmore Road and Willshire-Ohio City Road outside Glenmore, Ohio (Willshire Township, Van Wert County). The coordinates are 40° 46' 17.9" N, 84° 41' 52.58" W.

Greenbriar Cemetery is still active. There are many old gravestones, and although several are in good condition, there are also quite a few that have been damaged. Some of the gravestones are unique; the two I found most interesting were matching marble obelisks that had small slabs of marble covering old portraits. One portrait is almost completely faded, but in the other portrait you can see the image of the woman buried there. The cemetery grounds are well maintained.
