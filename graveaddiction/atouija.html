Quack

November 28, 2005



On November 28, 2005 I received an e-mail from Amanda Taylor about her interesting ouija board experience:



My friend Lynn and I were playing with her glow-in-the-dark board at my house. We knew my house was haunted, because besides the fact that it's over fifty years old and a couple people have died in it, things move themselves around. We started the session at approximately 11:00, and it lasted about two hours. We aren't very experienced with the board, both having used it only a few times.

Me: Is anyone there?
Board: [no movment]

Lynn: We call any spirits dwelling within this house to the board.
Board: [No movement]

We got bored sitting there, staring at the board, and Lynn got a little distracted. There is a duck marionette hanging in my closet, and Lynn was staring at it. Then, she says "Amanda, your chicken is funny looking." I replied,"Lynn, that's a duck."

Right then the board started spelling. It spelt "quack," so we knew we made contact.

Lynn: What's your name?
Board: 666

Lynn: Satan doesn't have time for us, tell us your name.
Board: 666666666666666 [repeatedly until we moved the planchette]

Me: Who are you?
Board: Satan

Me: I don't believe you, tell me your real name.
Board: Dug

Me: Your name is Doug?
Board: Yes

Me: How old are you Doug?
Board: 10

Me: How did you die?
Board: Mum

Me: Did your mom kill you Doug?
Board: Numyourkv
Lynn: It's ok if you don't want to tell us.
Board: Yes

The planchette began making a giant circle around the board and moving inward, like a spiral, and Lynn was staring at the flame (on the candle) and she started humming a really eerie song. After a couple minutes of the planchette moving in a circle and Lynn humming, I began to get annoyed.
Me: Lynn, stop it.
Lynn: [looks up at me smiling and stops humming]

Me: Lynn, what are you doing?
Lynn: [eyes start rolling back in her head and her smile starts fading]

Me: [realizing what is happening] Oh my gosh, LYNN! [I started to go toward her so I could smack her to try to get her to snap out of it, but... I guess whatever it was didn't want trouble.... Soooo.... Lynn's eyes returned to normal and she looked at the board.

Lynn: Oh. It stopped moving.
Me: Lynn, what were you just doing?
Lynn: I was sitting here and watching the board....
Me: What were you doing Lynn??

Lynn looked at me, as though I were the crazy one, and says, "What was I doing?" In an extremely sincere tone of voice.

We touched the planchette again and started speaking with the board.

Me: Doug, did you do that?
Board: Yes

Me: That was not funny Doug!
Board: Yesv
Then I thought that Lynn was just messing with me... So I devised a question she didn't know the answer to.

Me: Doug, what is my password to myspace?
Board: [spelt out my password]

Well, the rest of the night we were just talking with him, and then he let us talk to Jade (The one we were trying to talk with the entire night)
