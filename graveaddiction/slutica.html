South Lawn Cemetery is located on the east side of Reynolds Road, south of US-62, in Utica, Ohio (Washington Township, Licking County). The coordinates are 40° 13' 41.17" N, 82° 27' 20.15" W.

South Lawn Cemetery, sometimes referred to as the Utica South Cemetery, is still active. There are many old marble and sandstone monuments, and several have beautiful carvings. Most of the gravestones are in good condition, but a few of the older monuments have been damaged. The cemetery is very well maintained.
