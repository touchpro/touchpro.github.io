Ewing Cemetery is located on the east side of OH-93, just north of Goat Run-Honey Fork Road, in Ewing, Ohio (Washington Township, Hocking County). The coordinates are 39° 28' 24.09" N, 82° 25' 55.52" W.

Ewing Cemetery is still active, but the majority of gravestones are very old. Just about all of the gravestones are in fantastic condition; there are very few broken ones. Although there were some tall weeds when I visited in October 2005, overall the grounds are very well maintained. In August 2010, I received an e-mail from Jim T. who let me know that the grass had been mowed when he visited in June.
