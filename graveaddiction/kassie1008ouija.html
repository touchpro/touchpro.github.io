Online Ouija Session

October 25, 2008



On October 25, 2008 I received an e-mail from Kassandra Padron about an interesting ouija board experience:


My friend, Eumelia, and I were playing the Ouija board online. I know it sounds pretty weird too play it on a computer, but we were bored. But anyways, we asked many questions and had fun with the answers. Then we started getting curious about the person who was answering our questions. We asked appropriate questions, and everything turned out okay. But I'm ALWAYS scared to ask them about their name or about how they died. Please help me out with that.

Next I asked a question for my friend. She wanted to know who her date to prom would be, and it said "A Stranger." She got kinda freaked out and scared, so then we said goodbye to the spirit. Then got on again about 10 minutes later, and I asked the same question about me. It said the same thing. What do you think that means? Is it bad?

Then we asked a question about someone's death, and it said too dark. We asked it what too dark meant, and it ALMOST spelled out check behind you, but instead it said check back later. Would it have been bad if it DID say "check behind you?" Is it appropriate to ask how I would die? Or any friends or family members?


If you have any advice or comments for Kassandra, please send me an e-mail and I will forward your response on to her.
