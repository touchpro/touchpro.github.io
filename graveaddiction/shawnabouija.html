February 7, 2007



On February 7, 2007 I received an e-mail from Shawna Beeman about an interesting ouija board experience:


From Rachel & Shawna, Ages 12 & 13

We were at our grandparents house in the Thousand Islands, and Rachel had gotten an Ouija Board for Christmas. We went into our grandparent's room to try it out. We met a few spirits.

Day One

Us: Is anyone there?
Board: Yes

Us: Who?
Board: Jack

Us: How did you die?
Board: Tumber

Us: Brain tumor?
Board: Yes

Us: Whose energy are you feeding off of?
Board: Shawna

Rachel: Do you love her?
Board: Yes

Rachel: Will you write it on the bathroom mirror?
Board: Yes

Us: Ok. Bye!
Board: Goodbye

Day Two

Us: Anyone there?
Board: Jack

Us: You didn't write it on the bathroom mirror.
Board: Not enough power

Us: Oh. Will you try again?
Board: No

Rachel: Can we put you in the pendulum that Shawna got for Christmas?
Board: Yes

Us: How?
Board: Chant Ouija 10 times

Us: Ok. We'll put you in later.
Board: Goodbye.

Us: Hello?
Board: I'm Adam

Us: Hi.
Board: I'm going to posses her

Us: Who?
Board: Shawna

Us: Oh my God!
Board: Don't open closet

Us: What happens if we do?

Then the light flickered, our grandmother's crutches fell, and the temperature dropped. And then we left.

Day Three

Us: Adam?
Board: Hi

Us: What did you do to him?
Board: I killed him.

~Later~

Us: Hello
Board: It's Jack

Us: Can we put you in the pendulum now?
Board: yes

And that's where Jack is today. In my pendulum.
