The Old Hadley Cemetery is located on the north side of Cemetery Road in Hadley, Massachusetts (Hampshire County). The coordinates are 42° 20' 36" N, 72° 36' 8" W.

The Old Hadley Cemetery was established in 1661, and it is still active. There are many old gravestones with interesting carvings, and most are in good condition. The oldest gravestones are on the west side. Several contain interesting epitaphs. The cemetery is extremely well maintained.
