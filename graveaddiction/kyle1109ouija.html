Nothing Happened

November 29, 2009



On November 29, 2009 I received an e-mail from Kyle about an interesting ouija board experience:


So, I just bought an ouija board. I have heard many things about them, read many stories, and I was sooo excited when I finally found one. I got it and immediately went back home with my friend to play it. We followed all the instructions. We placed two fingers on the oracle, we were in a dark room, no noise, it was completley on a flat surface. We asked it questions slowly, and precisley, and loud. Questions like who am I, what's my age, is anyone here. Contrary to belief, nothing at all happened. We waited 10 minutes for each question. We went to several rooms. Nothing happened. This was the biggest waste of money, and a waste of happiness. I read all the stories on your website. I personally think they are all fake, not saying that I don't beleive in ghosts; I really do beleive. But this game didn't do anything. I really want you to post this, because it's the truth, and so other people like me don't fall for this. I only bought it because of this website.
