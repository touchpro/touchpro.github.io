Bethel Cemetery is located on the northeast side of Bethel Road, west of OH-93, outside Creola, Ohio (Swan Township, Vinton County). The coordinates are 39° 20' 31.86" N, 82° 28' 0.47" W.

Bethel Cemetery is filled with interesting gravestones. The oldest gravestones are located on the southeast side of the cemetery, and most are in good condition. The cemetery grounds are well maintained by the township.
