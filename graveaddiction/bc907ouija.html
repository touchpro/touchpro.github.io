Our Interesting Experience

September 28, 2007



On September 28, 2007 I received an e-mail from Buddy and Cassie about their interesting ouija board experience:


Buddy and Cassie: Is there anyone there?
Board: Yes

Cassie: When will I lose my virginity?
Board: 14

Buddy: Did you say 15?
Board: No
(I misunderstood it)

Cassie: 14?
Board: Yes

Buddy: How many lives have I lived?
Board: 8

Buddy: What was I in my first life?
Board: Cop

Buddy: What was my name?
Board: Peter Puxz

Cassie: How many lives have I lived?
Board: 13

Cassie: What was I in my first life?
Board: Prince

Cassie: What was my name?
Board: R.S.

Buddy: How many lives has Debbie lived?
Board: 0

Buddy: How many lives has Ryan Wicks lived?
Board: 8

Buddy: What was Ryan Wicks in his first life?
Board: Cop

Buddy: What was Ryan Wicks' name?
Board: F.S

Buddy: What was I in my last life?
Board: DJ

Buddy: What is your name?
Board: NO

Then the board went through the alphabet, and I yelled goodbye. Then the board went to "no," and I just took the planchette off the board.
