The Meeting House Hill Cemetery is located on the west side of Orchard Street outside Brattleboro, Vermont (Windham County). The coordinates are 42° 51' 47" N, 72° 35' 36" W.

The Meeting House Hill Cemetery was established in 1765, and it is still active. There are many interesting gravestones, including many old slate monuments. The cemetery is very well maintained.
