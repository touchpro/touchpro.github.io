Ruff Cemetery is located on the east side of Zwickle Road, just north of Logan Horns Mill Road, outside Logan, Ohio (Marion Township, Hocking County). The coordinates are 39° 37' 1.56" N, 82° 26' 49.23" W.

Ruff Cemetery is very small, and it is no longer active. It is very easy to drive by this cemetery since it sits on a hill and is surrounded by woods. Most of the gravestones have been damaged, but a few are still standing. Although the gravestones are in bad shape, the township does an excellent job of maintaining the cemetery grounds.
