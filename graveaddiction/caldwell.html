Caldwell Cemetery is located on the west side of Old US-35, about a ½ mile north of Higby Bridge Road, in Rittenours, Ohio (Jefferson Township, Ross County). The coordinates are 39° 13' 18.62" N, 82° 51' 1.83" W.

Caldwell Cemetery is still active. There are many old gravestones, including several with detailed carvings. The oldest gravestones are located on the northeast side. Although some of the older monuments have been damaged, most are in good condition. The cemetery grounds are very well maintained.
