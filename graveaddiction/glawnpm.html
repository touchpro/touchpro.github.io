Greenlawn Cemetery is located at 1618 Offnere Street in Portsmouth, Ohio (Scioto County). The coordinates are 38° 44' 42.28" N, 82° 58' 57" W.

Greenlawn Cemetery is very large, and it is still active. There are many old gravestones, and several have interesting carvings and statues. Most are in great condition. There is a large section of Jewish graves too. The cemetery grounds are very well maintained.
