Girl In The Corner, Why Are You Here?

June 10, 2005



On June 10, 2005 I received an e-mail from Briggette Almazan about her interesting experience with the ouija board:


"I was in my room one night after playing the ouija board with my brothers . We had been talking with a girl who says she was killed by her brother, Sam. Her name was Elizebeth and she didn't want to tell me how, but she had been killed where my house is built.

That night I went to bed and I felt soemone in my room. My bed was shaking to the point to where my hands where moving! I sat up and in the corner of my room there stood a girl, which I suppose was Elizebeth but I am not sure. She was looking down and I freaked out! I ran out of my room to my brother's room. I have played the ouija board after that, and strange things occur in my house. Since then, there are shadows at night and the doors close on their own. But the worst thing is that in my house you can hear a baby crying, but there are no babies here."
