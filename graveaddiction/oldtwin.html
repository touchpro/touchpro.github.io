The Old Twin Cemetery is located on the northwest side of US-50 about ½ mile northeast of Bourneville, Ohio (Twin Township, Ross County). The coordinates are 39° 17' 12.69" N, 83° 9' 5.73" W. It is on private property across the street from the Twin Township Cemetery.

The Old Twin Cemetery is a tiny, old cemetery that is no longer active. It is surrounded by fields on private property, which means you should be sure to get permission before visiting. The cemetery is very well taken care of, and there aren't too many broken gravestones.

A HUGE thank you to Mike Hill for telling me about this cemetery! I had driven past it at least two times without noticing it, had it not been for him I may have kept on driving by :)
