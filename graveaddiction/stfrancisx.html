Saint Francis Xavier Catholic Cemetery is located on the west side of US-23, about a ½ mile north of DeBord Road, outside Waverly, Ohio (Huntington Township, Ross County). The coordinates are 39° 13' 46.27" N, 82° 59' 4.4" W.

Saint Francis Xavier Catholic Cemetery is also known as the Old Catholic Cemetery. There are many old gravestones, and although some have been damaged, most are still in good condition. There are several detailed carvings. The cemetery grounds are very well maintained.
