Alexander Union Cemetery is located on the west side of County Road 19, just east of US-50, outside Hebardville, Ohio (Alexander Township, Athens County). The coordinates are 39° 15' 37" N, 82° 9' 51" W.

Alexander Union Cemetery is still active. The old section (where I took these photos) is also known as the Old Cumberland Cemetery. The oldest gravestones are located behind the church. Although some are in good condition, many have been damaged. Several are completely illegible, but a few have new replacement markers in front of the old stones. The cemetery grounds are well maintained.
