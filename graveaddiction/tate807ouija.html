Banana

August 24, 2007



On August 24, 2007 I received an e-mail from Tate about an interesting ouija board experience:


My friend Julia and I decided that we were going to use a Ouija Board, the first time for both of us. I have heard many stories about Ouijas, but wanted to see one in action for myself. So I made one, and Julia and I went into the living room and sat around her coffee table. We were both too afraid to even get near a Ouija board at night.

It took us at least an hour to get an answer from any spirit. When it finally answered, it took forever to move. But here's what happened:

Me: What's your name?
Board: No.

Me: How'd you die?
Board: No.

Me: Who are you?
Board: No.

Me: God damnit, is that your favorite word?!?!?
Board: Yes.

Me: Where do I live?
Board: Buttlerville Rd. (this is true)

Me: What state?
Board: Banana.

Board: Banana.

Board: Banana.

Board: Banana.

Board: Banana.

Board: Banana.

Board: YEE HAW.

Board: Good bye.

And at this point, the board flips over and Julia and I are suddenly gagging from an overpowering aroma of bananas.
