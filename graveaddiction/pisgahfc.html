Pisgah Cemetery is located at the intersection of OH-256 and OH-204 in Pickerington, Ohio (Violet Township, Fairfield County). The coordinates are 39° 55' 43.64" N, 82° 47' 16.89" W.

Pisgah Cemetery is small, and it is no longer active. There are many old gravestones, and most are in good condition. However, like any old cemetery, some of the gravestones have been damaged. The grounds are very well maintained.
