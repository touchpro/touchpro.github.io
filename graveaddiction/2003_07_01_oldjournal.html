And finally, here are my answers to this week's This-or-That questions:

  1. Lemonade or Ice Cold Beer?
    If I had to choose one, I'd have to pick ice cold beer...I'm not a big fan of lemonade. If I could choose something else though, I'd definitely pick iced tea or water!
  2. Swimming pool or beach?
    The beach - at the pool all you can do is swim or lay out in the sun. At the beach you can at least take a walk along the shore.
  3. Long weekends here & there, or a 2-week vacation?
    Long weekends here or there...with a two week vacatation, once it's over that's it. Long weekends here or there seem like more time away from work :)
  4. Destination: Acapulco or Hawaii?
    I haven't been to either, so it's a tie...I'd like to see both!
  5. Destination: Mountains or Beach?
    Definitely the mountains. I'd rather do outdoorsy things than lay around on a beach.
  6. Hotel/motel/B&B or camping?
    I love camping, but all of my friends and family hate it :(
  7. Carefully planned vacation, or play it by ear?
    I kind of like an in between...I like planning things to a certain extent, but leaving some free time to play it by ear.
  8. Sneakers or sandals?
    Sneakers, or closed-toe sandals. I have awful toes, they're really short and if I wear open toes sandals it looks like I only have two or three toes...it looks strange!
  9. Air-conditioning or fans?
    I'm going to have to agree with Luis on this one...definitely air conditioning!!! It is nice to use fans with the air conditioning on though, it helps circulate the cool air.
  10. Concerts in the park or baseball games?
    Concerts in the park, baseball bores me!
