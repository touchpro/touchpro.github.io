Riverside Cemetery is located at the end of Cemetery Road, just off the west side of MA-47 (River Road), in Sunderland, Massachusetts (Franklin County). The coordinates are 42° 27' 40" N, 72° 35' 9" W.

Riverside Cemetery was established in 1714, and it is still active. The oldest gravestones are located on the northwest side, and most are in great condition. There are many old slate gravestones with beautiful, interesting carvings and epitaphs. The cemetery is very well maintained.
