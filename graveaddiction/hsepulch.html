Holy Sepulchre Cemetery is located at 2461 Lake Avenue in Rochester, New York (Monroe County). The coordinates are 43° 12' 49" N, 77° 37' 58" W.

Holy Sepulchre Cemetery was established in 1871. It is huge, and it is still active. There are many old gravestones, as well as a multitude of beautiful statues. Most are in excellent condition. There is also a gorgeous chapel near the center of the cemetery. The grounds are very well maintained.

For more information about the cemetery, please visit the official website.
