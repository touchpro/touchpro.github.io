Well, that day Katy and I saw a cute, fluffy little bunny. We yelled for our mom so she could come over and see it too. Since dad was mowing in that area of the yard mom told us to chase the bunny into the bushes on the side of the house so it'd be safe. Katy ran after it and she succeeded in chasing the cute little bunny into the bushes. She was so proud of herself for saving it. Not even a minute later, we heard a terrible shriek from the bushes, and out came a stray cat that had been hanging around...with the bunny in it's mouth!!! So much for chasing it to safety, huh?! Katy started crying, it was awful. My mom felt bad too, because she was the one who told us to chase it into the bushes.
