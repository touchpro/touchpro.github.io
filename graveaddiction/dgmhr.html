Diane G. sent me the following e-mail about Most Holy Redeemer Cemetery:

Most Holy Redeemer Cemetery is located at 2501 Troy Road in Niskayuna, NY.

The Most Holy Redeemer Cemetery was consecrated in 1925 to serve the needs of Catholic families in Schenectady. The landscape was beautiful, and all the rows were well placed. It is very well maintained.

An actress named Maureen O'Sullivan, original name is Cushing, is also buried here. She played Jane in the Tarzan movies of the 1930's and 40's. She is also Mia Farrow's mother. She died of a heart attack in 1998.

Also, all nine children whom were victims of infanticide murdered by their mother Marybeth Tinning are buried here. Such a horrific and depressing story! There is a book written by Joyce Egginton entitled "From Cradle to Grave."
