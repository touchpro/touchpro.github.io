Cold Air

July 8, 2007



On July 8, 2007 I received an e-mail from Holly about an interesting ouija board experience:


My friend and I were in her bathroom with the lights off and candles glowing. No one was home, and she's had other acquaintances with what seemed to be ghostly activity. We are only 15 and we got really scared, tears shot out of our eyes. Just read.

Michelle: Is anyone here with us?
Board: Yes.

Me: What's your name?
Board: Francis.

Michelle: Are you an evil spirit?
Board: No.

Me: How did you die?
Board: Ains.

Michelle: Did you mean AIDS?
Board: No.

Me: How old are you?
Board: 159

Michelle: Can you make a noise or appear for us?
Board: Yes.

*Cold air rushes through*

Board: Goodbye.

An hour later we waited for the video camera to be ready for us to watch it, and as we heard "make a noise" a scream shot out, and we NEVER heard it before watching the video.

I believe 100%
