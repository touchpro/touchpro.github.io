Lakeside Cemetery is located off Thomas Street in Holly, Michigan (Holly Township, Oakland County). The coordinates are 42° 38' 25" N, 83° 28' 44" W.

Lakeside Cemetery is very large, and it is still active. The oldest gravestones are located towards the front of the cemetery, and near the lake. Most are in great condition. There is a beautiful view of the lake from the cemetery, and the grounds are very well maintained.
