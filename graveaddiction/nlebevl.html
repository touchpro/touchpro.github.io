New Lebanon Evangelical Lutheran Cemetery is located on the west side of Logan Street in Junction City, Ohio (Jackson Township, Perry County). The coordinates are 39° 43' 51" N, 82° 18' 0" W.

New Lebanon Evangelical Lutheran Cemetery is still active. The oldest gravestones are located closest to the church, and most are in good condition. The grounds are very well maintained.
