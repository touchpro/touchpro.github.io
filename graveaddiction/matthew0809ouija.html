The Joker and the Killer

August 19, 2009



On August 19, 2009 I received an e-mail from Matthew M. about an interesting ouija board experience:


One night me, my mother, her boyfriend, and his daughter (named Racheal) were all playing with a homemade ouija board made with just paper letters put in a circle. Racheal and I were sick of all the spirits talking to my mum.

Me: Are there any spirits here looking to talk to us?
Board: crap

Me: What is crap?
Board: pc

Me: Windows PC?
Board: yes

We both laughed.

Me: Apple mac is better than a PC?
Board: yes

Racheal: What is your name?
Board: ger

Me: Gerald?
Gerald: yes

Me: Do you have a message for any of us?
Gerald: no, goodbye

That was the end for Gerald, but when my mum and her boyfriend went to the kitchen, Racheal and did it on our own.

Me: Are there any spirits here with us?
Board: yes

Racheal: Do you have a message for us?
Board: KILL

Me: Are you evil?

The planchette moved extremely quick towards Racheal, which was where the yes was. We both ran to the kitchen laughing, but screaming.
