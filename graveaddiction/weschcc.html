Wesley Chapel Cemetery is located on the west side of Wesley Chapel Road, near the intersection with Sullivan Road, outside Northville, Ohio (Harrison Township, Champaign County). The coordinates are 40° 13' 8.83" N, 83° 49' 4.45" W.

Wesley Chapel Cemetery is still active. The oldest gravestones are located at the back, and most are in good condition. However, several of the older ones have been damaged. The cemetery is very well maintained by the township.
