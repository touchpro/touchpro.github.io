Union Church Cemetery is located off the west side of Buena Vista Road, just west of Opossum Hollow Road, about 2 miles west of Rockbridge, Ohio (Good Hope Township, Hocking County). The coordinates are 39° 34' 38.7" N, 82° 33' 39.69" W.

Union Church Cemetery is located behind the church, and it is still active. The oldest gravestones are located on the east side. Although there are several broken gravestones, most are in excellent shape. The church does a terrific job of maintaining the cemetery.
