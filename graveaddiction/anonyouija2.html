Christmas Present

March 26, 2007



On March 26, 2007 I received an e-mail from an anonymous website visitor about her interesting ouija board experience:


In December of 2006, Christmas eve, My sister and I got an ouija board.

We asked it a question.

My question was "Will I get accepted into the Career Academy for Dental?"

The answer was "Yes."

In February of 2007 [this year] I got accepted and I'll be starting at the career academy for dental the beginning of my junior year.

Then the next day, on Christmas.

We asked it if our grandma was there. It said yes. Then we asked if she was a good spirit or a bad one. It said good.

We asked if she was happy.
She said no.

We asked why.
She said she was mad.

We asked at who or what.
She said Harper [my ex-boyfriend's last name]

I asked it what he did.
She said taste you.

I asked "What's taste you mean?"
She said sex

I asked if she was mad that Josh and I had sex.
She said yes.

I asked "If I stop will you be happy?"
She said yes.

I asked if she was in heaven.
She said she was in hell.

That freaked me and my sister out!

Then, just a couple of days ago, my sister asked who her next boyfriend would be and it said Cam [her current ex-boyfriend].

She asked for how long, and it said 5...not days, weeks, months, or years. Just 5.

Five days later, after my sister and Cam got together, he broke up with her...

I believe in the ouija board, and I think it's great!
