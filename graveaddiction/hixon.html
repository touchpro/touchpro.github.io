Hixon Cemetery is located on the west side of OH-93, north of Benner Road, in Dundas, Ohio (Elk Township, Vinton County). The coordinates are 39° 12' 54.71" N, 82° 28' 55.84" W.

Hixon Cemetery is small, but it appears that it is still active. It sits at the top of a hill, and can be a little difficult to spot while driving south on OH-93. There are several old gravestones, and most are in good condition. Only a few monuments have been damaged. The cemetery is well maintained by the township.
