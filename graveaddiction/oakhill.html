Oak Hill Cemetery is located on the east side of US-42 in London, Ohio (Union Township, Madison County).

Oak Hill Cemetery is a rather large cemetery on a very busy street. It is surrounded by businesses, fast food restaurants, and gas stations...the cemetery just doesn't fit in with it's surroundings! However, the cemetery is extremely well taken care of with most of the gravestones still standing and in great condition. There are many interesting statues, and there's even a unique mausoleum built into a hill. The veteran's memorial in Oak Hill Cemetery is also very nice.
