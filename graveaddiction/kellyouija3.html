Talking with a Spirit Named Danielle

October 14, 2004



On October 14, 2004 I received an e-mail from Kelly Hutchinson from Manchester, Kentucky. She shared a few of her interesting ouija board experiences with me to post on the site. This story is about a discussion she and her friends Ricky, Sperlock, and JJ had with a spirit named Danielle:


Ricky: Any spirits want to talk to us?
Board: no movement

Sperlock: Is anyone present?
Board: no movement

Kelly: Is There anyone that wishes to talk to us?
Board: started to move to yes, but stopped and went back to end

Ricky: Is someone trying to talk to us?
Board: Yes moved very slowly

Kelly: Have we talked to you before?
Board between yes and no

Ricky: Can you spell out your name?
Board: Danielle

Ricky: Danielle? I'v talked to you before.
Board: Yes. Girl.

Kelly: Is there something you wish to say to me?
Board: No. JJH those are my brother's initials

Kelly: You wish to talk to JJH?
Board: Yes. Girl go

Kelly: You wish me to go?
Board: No. Hurt

Ricky: You don't want her to go?
Board: Girl go

Kelly: You want me to get JJH?
Board: Girl go

JJ: Is Danielle here?
Board: Yes a little stronger

Ricky: You're moving faster... Did you not like the girl?
Board: No hurt

JJ: You dont want to hurt?
Board: No. Girl no

Ricky: You don't want to hurt her?
Board: No hurt. Girl no know

JJ: You don't want to hurt her with your secrets because you don't know her and she doesnt know you?
Board: Yes. Kelly later found out that she has spelled some things that she didn't want Kelly to know about her.

JJ: How are you doing?
Board: Slow it started to move slower then

Ricky: Do you want to end? If so go to end and we will try tomorrow if you save more energy
Board: moved to end
