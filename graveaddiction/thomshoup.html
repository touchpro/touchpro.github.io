Thompson-Shoup Cemetery is located on the east side of OH-257, a ½ mile north of Radnor Road, outside Radnor, Ohio (Thompson Township, Delaware County). The coordinates are 40° 23' 27.85" N, 83° 11' 37.82" W.

Thompson-Shoup Cemetery is still active. The oldest gravestones are on the south side, and most are in good condition. However, like any old cemetery, some of the oldest gravestones have been damaged and are weathered. There are also several interesting white bronze monuments. The cemetery is very well maintained.

Many thanks to Rolf R. for taking me to this cemetery!
