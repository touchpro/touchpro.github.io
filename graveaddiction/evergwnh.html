Evergreen Cemetery is located off NH-119 (Richmond Street) in Winchester, New Hampshire (Cheshire County). The coordinates are 42° 46' 15" N, 72° 22' 10" W.

Evergreen Cemetery is large, and it is still active. The oldest gravestones are located at the top of the hill, and although several are damaged, most are in good condition. There are many slate monuments with beautiful carvings. The cemetery is very well maintained.

Many thanks to Ta Mara C. for visiting this cemetery with me.
