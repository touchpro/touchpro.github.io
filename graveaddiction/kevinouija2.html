Eye Stab

October 30, 2005



On October 30, 2005 I received an e-mail from Kevin Davis about his interesting ouija board experience:


"Well, this isn't much of a story, but anyway...it was me and my friend David in my basement around 11 PM or 12 AM.

Question: Are there any spirits present with us?
Answer: Yes

Question: What year did you die?
Answer: 1975

Question: What is your name?
Answer: Crystal

Question: Do you have anything you want to tell us?
Answer: Yes

Question: What?
Answer: Krystal eye stab

Question: You're going to stab her in the eye?
Answer: Yes

Me (Kevin) and David say goodbye and flip the board and planchette over. The spirit's name was Crystal with a "C", and the Krystal with a "K" is my sister."
