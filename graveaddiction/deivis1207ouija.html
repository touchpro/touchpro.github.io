You're Done

December 26, 2007



On November 26, 2007 I received an e-mail from Deivis about an interesting ouija board experience:


Me (Deivis), my friend Jesse, and my cousin Richard one night decided that we were going to play the ouija board we had bought recently. At that time, Richard and I had a Ouija board experience where Jesse did not.

The week before we talked to someone named "Huap." He told us he was an bad spirit, but he was pretty funny and pretty cool (Keep in mind that I have researched Ouija boards throughly and know when a spirit is a dangerous one). Well we got it out and started getting warmed up.

We started asking:

Us: Is there anyone here that would like to speak to us?
Board: Yes.

Us: What's your name?
Board: Kimi (Or Kisi, something like that)

Us: How old are you?
Board: 12

Us: Do you live here?
Board: M-Y-O-U-R-E-D-O-N-E

Us: Huh? Whats that?
Board: Y-O-U-R-E-D-O-N-E

Richard: It said "You are done"
Me: So how am I done?
Board: B-L-O-O-D

(I should've stopped playing here, but I decided that I wanted to check him out)

Me: Whats your name?
Board: J-E-S-S-E

(Here my friend Jesse started getting scared, thinking the board was talking about him)

Me: Are you talking about Jesse or is your name Jesse?
Board: A-B-O-U-T

Me: So you're talking about Jesse?
Board: No

Me: Your name is Jesse?
Board: Yes

Me: So who is done?
Board: R-I-C-H-A-R-D

Me: How?
Board: B-L-O-O-D

Us: We knew that (lol)

(By this time I guessed the spirit was not a demon because of one thing. His name.)

Board: I-A-M-G-O-I-N-G-T-O-K-I-L-L-J-E-S-S-E

(Jesse started panicking and stopped playing...he hasn't played since)

Us (Richard and I): (lol) thats pretty cool
Board: N-O-M-O-M-O-M-O-M-O

Us: Huh?
Board: N-O-M-O-M-O-M-O-M-O

Us: We don't know what that means.
Board: (moving really fast) D-E-I-V-I-S-N-O-M-O-M-O-M-O

Richard: Dude, its that guy we talked to last week.
Me: Is this Huap?
Board: Yes

After this I asked him how he was doing and such...but as usual he puts up gibberish to mess with us lol.

A bit ago we spoke to Jesse again. He's still around, and I found out that him, Huap, and some other spirit that lives in my house like to ride in the car with me. lol Jesse as I guessed is not a bad spirit, nor are any of the 6 that live in my house (as far as I know). Still, even today, I will not play the board alone. Also, before we spoke to Jesse, we kept talking to someone who would constantly slide the planchette through "goodbye." I didn't even ask if there were spirits there - the planchette would just slide through goodbye. If anyone knows a reason why this might happen (other than they didn't want to talk to me) please let me know.


If you have any response to this story, please let me know and I'll send your response on to Deivis.
