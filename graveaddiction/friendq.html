Friends-Fairfield Quaker Cemetery (sometimes referred to as the Old Fairfield Cemetery) is located near the intersection of OH-771 and Fairfield Road, just outside Leesburg, Ohio (Fairfield Township, Highland County). The coordinates are 39° 20' 1.26" N, 83° 32' 58.33" W.

Friends-Fairfield Quaker Cemetery is located around both sides and behind the old Friends Meeting House. The Meeting House is currently being renovated, I'm interested to see what it looks like after it has been completed. The cemetery is huge and no longer active. There are rows upon rows of old gravestones to look at, and most are in terrific shape! There is one walled off section that sits away from the rest of the cemetery that is very old.

A very big thanks to Mike Hill for taking me to this cemetery, and for telling me about the renovations being done to the Meeting House!
