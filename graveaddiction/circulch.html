The Circular Church Graveyard is located at 150 Meeting Street in Charleston, South Carolina (Charleston County).

The Circular Church Graveyard is located by the Circular Congregational Church. It was established in 1681, making it one of the oldest cemeteries in Charleston. The majority of the gravestones are older; I only noticed a few newer stones. The oldest unmarked grave is dated 1695, and the oldest inscribed gravestone is dated 1729. The graveyard is extremely well taken care of by the church.

For more information about the church and the graveyard, please visit their site: Circular Congregational Church.
