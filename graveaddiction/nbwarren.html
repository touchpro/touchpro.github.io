The North Burial Ground is located on the east side of North Main Street (RI-114) in Warren, Rhode Island (Bristol County).

The North Burial Ground is a historical cemetery that is filled with many interesting gravestones. There are several old slate gravestones with detailed carvings, and there are also some interesting marble and granite monuments. Most of the gravestones are in great condition. The cemetery is very well maintained.
