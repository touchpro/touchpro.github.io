Chicken Dance

May 26, 2008



On May 26, 2008 I received an e-mail from an anonymous visitor about an interesting ouija board experience:


Here is my Ouija board story that is true - no word is a lie.

I was seven at time, and I always used Ouija boards with my friend (I'm not using names - read on if you want to know why)

We used it at an abandoned church near where I live. It was around 12:00 at night.

Me: Are there any spirits with us tonight?
Board: Yes (after 2 minutes)

Me: Are you good or bad? If you are good, say something good about god.
Board: (moves to no)

My friend: Why are you bad?
Board: Yes

Me: Well, show yourself then because I'm not scared.
Board: Maybe

Me and my friend: Well, you are a weak spirit and couldn't hurt anyone.
Board: Watch what you say or else

My friend gets up and does a chicken dance.

Me: Ok, stop that friend or I'm putting the board away.
Board: Its to late for your friend (the planchette goes through the alphabet backwards really fast...we did not stop it because we thought it was nothing at the time.)

Board: If I was you, not your friend, I would run now

We suddenly heard a scream.

My friend levitates in the air and starts to bleed from his ears. I get some holy water and throw it on him.

He falls to the ground, gets and picks up a nearby piece of sharp wood and throws it through my arm. Then he starts chanting "here comes the devil."

Me: No! Stop!

I pick up the board, rip it up, then burn and bury it. Next I start to read a charm from a piece of paper, then call forth my spirit guide.

My friend falls on the floor, and I still have the wood in my arm and am still bleeding. I start crying, then my friend wakes up and remembers everything. He says he is sorry, I take the wood out and wrap it with a piece of cloth from my t-shirt, and we run to my friend's house and tell his dad everything. His dad treats my wound and blesses us all (his dad is Christian).

To this day I have never used a ouija since then, so I'm telling you to be very careful when you use one.

I didn't use names just in case we encounter that spirit again, because it is released from the board.
