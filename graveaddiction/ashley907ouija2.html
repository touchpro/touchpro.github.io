Discussion with Death

August 30, 2007



On August 30, 2007 I received an e-mail from Ashley about an interesting ouija board experience:


One night my friend Kelly and I were bored, so we decided to pull out the Ouija board. We talked to a few people, then this guy showed up.

Kelly: Is anyone there?
Board: Yes

Kelly: What is your name?
Board: Will

Kelly: Are you from the sun or the moon?
Board: (Sun)

Kelly: Is there something you would like to tell us?
Board: Yes

Kelly: What is that?
Board: I want sex.

Kelly: You want sex with who?
Board: Both of you

Kelly: Is it possible for you to have sex with us?
Board: Yes

Kelly: How?
Board: You will know when you feel me inside of you

Me: But what if I don't want to have sex with you? I have a boyfriend.
Board: Rape

Kelly: Then you'll rape her?
Board: Yes

Me: You are not from the sun. If you were you would not rape someone.
Board: Goodbye

We tried for 10 minutes to get someone else. Then we got...

Me: Is anyone there?
Board: No

Me: Will, is that you?
Board: No

Me: Who are you?
Board: Death

Me: Like Death himself?
Board: Yes

Me: Oh, how are you doing?
Board: Bad

Me: Why?
Board: My mother screwed up my job

Me: What was your job?
Board: To kill you and Kelly

Me: To kill us? Why would you want to do that?
Board: I don't, it's my job

Me: Oh. Well, do you know why we can't contact anyone?
Board: Rest

Me: We need to rest?
Board: No board

Me: The board needs to rest?
Board: Yes

Me: Why?
Board: dirty

Me: Is it from those crumbs on the table?
Board: No

Me: What is it from?
Board: Will

Me: Will dirtied the board?
Board: Yes

Me: Why?
Board: Because he was the devil

Me: How do we contact people again?
Board: Let the board sit for 24 hours

Me: Ok Death, thank you for your time. Goodbye.
Board: No thank you ladies, Goodbye.
