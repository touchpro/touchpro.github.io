Freaked Out

April 11, 2009



On April 11, 2009 I received an e-mail from Dana about an interesting ouija board experience:


My name is Dana. Me and my friends (Cassy, Faran, and Ashley) were using my ouija board in my basement. Some of the boys where I live were watching (Lealand, Chris, and Tristain)...they were not touching the ouija board at all, so they are not included when it says "everyone." I don't know if some one was moving the planchette, and I don't remember everything that happened.

Me: Is there any one here?
Board: Yes

Cassy: What is your name?
Board: It

Me: Is your name it?
Board: Yes

Faran: Are those your initials?
Board: Yes

Me: Can you tell us your full name?
Board: No

Faran: Are you not allowed to tell us your full name?
Board: Yes

Me: Are you a girl?
Board: Yes

Me: Are you good?
Board: Yes

Me: How old were you when you died?
Board: 16

Ashley: That's pretty young.
Board: Yes

Cassy: What year did you die?
Board: *moves in circles*

Me: What year did you die?
Board: (I don't remember the year she died, sorry)

I asked someone to ask a question.

Cassy: Do you know anyone in this room?
Board: Yes

Now we were gettig a little creeped out.

Me: Do you know me?
Board: No

Faran: Do you know me?
Board: Yes

Faran: How do you know me?
Board: Hospital

I don't remember the full conversation about "it" knowing Faran, but what I do remember is that "it" died while Faran was being born, and "it" saw her as a baby before she died. Now we were completely freaked out.

Cassy: How did you die?
Board: Car

Me: Did you die in a car crash?
Board: Yes

Me: I'm sorry to hear that.
Board: Thank you

Me: What did your parents do when you died?
Board: Eat

Faran: Eat what?
Board: Me

That really freaked us out.

Cassy: Do you like us?
Board: Yes

Everyone: We like you too.
Board: *circles*

Cassy: Do you think I'm hot?
Board: Yes

Cassy: Yay!
Board: lol

Me: What do you think of me?
Board: Nice

Me: Thank you, I think you're nice too.
Board: Yes

The boys who were watching were laughing like idiots.

Ashley: Do you know Lealand?
Board: Yes

Ashley: Is he a retard?
Board: Yes

We all started laughing (Lealand is not really a retard).

Me: Do you have any questions for us?
Board: Yes

Us: Ask away!
Board: What do you taste like

I'm not lying, "it" actually said that. Now we were scared out of our minds.

Everyone: Bad. You don't want to taste us.
Board: Yes

Me: Can we stop talking now?
Board: No

Faran: Please?
Board: No

Me: Will you hurt us if we leave?
Board: Yes

Everyone was so scared that we were shaking, and I almost started crying.

Everyone: Please, can we leave?
Board: Yes

Everyone: Thank you.
Board: Friends

Everyone: Umm...(not really meaning it, and very reluctantly) Yes, we can be friends. Bye.
Board: Goodbye

We turned the board and planchette over, and ran out of my basement and outside. That was in July 2008. I have never used my oujia board since.
