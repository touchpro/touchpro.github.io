Calvary Cemetery is located on the west side of Possum Road, about a ½ mile south of Leffels Lane, outside Springfield, Ohio (Springfield Township, Clark County). The coordinates are 39° 53' 6.24" N, 83° 45' 30.54" W.

Calvary Cemetery is a Catholic cemetery, and it is still active. The oldest gravestones are located on the east side, and most are in very good condition. The grounds are very well maintained.
