Sadness Come

February 3, 2006



On February 3, 2006 I received an e-mail from Anna about her interesting ouija board experience:


"I was having a party with my friends Dani, Max, Mika, and Tiffany. A few days prior to the party I had told them I was getting an ouija board on the first of February, so Max immediatley asked to use the board. The party was at Mika's house.

Max: Hi Spirits! Is anyone there?
Board: no

Max: Why not?
Board: Go

Max: Go where?
Board: Out

Max: But I don't want to go out.
Board: Then sadness come

Max: Sadness from what?
Board: No tell you

Anna: Hello spirit, it's me, Anna. I believe we've met before.
Board: Hello

Anna: What is your name?
Board: Alexandrea

Anna: How did you die, Alexandrea?
Board: Cut

Anna: Cut where?
Board: Myself

Anna: Why did you cut yourself?
Board: Hate world

Anna: How old were you when you died?
Board: 13

Tiffany: Ok, my turn!! Hiya spirit!
Board: Hello

Tiffany: Where did you live before you died?
Board: Rome

Tiffany: Italy?
Board: Yes

My friend Mika didn't believe in the ouija board and claimed we were moving it the whole time. He got into the conversation.

Mika: You guys are moving the board yourselves.
Anna: No we are not!
Mika: Yeah right!
Max: Ask the spirit something.
Mika: Do something to my house then.
Board: *no movement*

Anna: Mika, you idiot!
Max: She could blow up your house! She's a spirit!
Mika: Whatever. It's not like anything happened.

Then we heard a crash from upstairs. As Mika ran upstairs with us following, I knew Mika was in trouble.

Mika's room had been torn apart.

Mika: What happened to my room!?!
Anna: I warned you.
Max: Anna, Mika...come look at this!

As we went over to where Max was, he was pointing to the wall that had the words "THIS IS JUST BEGINNING. I WILL BE BACK" painted in red paint or, as Max claims, in blood. The next day (which is today) was as Alexandrea said. At 3:00 in the morning, Max called me and said that his grandfather who was 46 died that morning. As for Mika, I haven't heard from him and he wasn't at school today so I'm kinda worried."
