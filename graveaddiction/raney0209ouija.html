On February 28, 2009 I received an e-mail from Raney about an interesting ouija board experience:


One day I was my friend Haley's house with my friends: Dallys, Max, and Scoty. We were doing the Ouija board, and we asked the spirit to show itself. All of a sudden, the UNPLUGGED printer started running paper. This startled us very badly, but we still continued to play. Other things that happened that night were flickering lamps and candles. One of the spirits we contacted said that he followed me, and that I would marry a man with the initials MC and he was an 11th grader at Ingelmoore. The next weekend I was at the mall with my friends, and I saw a cute boy. Dallys got his number for me, and as I got to know him, I learned that he matched the description the Ouija board gave me. That freaked me out! Also, the night before I met him I had a dream that a man (I couldn't see who, but I for some reason believed it was the spirit that followed me) scratched me in the face. I woke up the next moring with a huge scratch across my face. All of these things creep me out so much.

That is my story. :]
