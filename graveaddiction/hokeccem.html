Photos taken by Shannon Zroback

Shannon Zroback sent me the following information about the Hoke County Community Cemetery:

"This is a large and unkept cemetery. Some stones are in the bushes and woods. There are a lot of graves that are marked only with funeral home markers. Some are rusty and un-readable. There are also quite a few that are broken and some that have sunken into the ground, and only names are legible. The ones with funeral home markers that are readable may have a first or last name or a date missing."
