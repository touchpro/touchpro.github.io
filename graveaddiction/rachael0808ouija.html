Four Spirits

August 1, 2008



On August 1, 2008 I received an e-mail from Rachael about an interesting ouija board experience:


I am 13, and my dad's friends asked us around for a drink. The ouija board came out, and we played (me, Leah, Ben, and Julie).

We contacted four different spirits: Three were good, one was evil. Our main objective was to contact Leah's mum, who passed last April. We had no success, but we contacted Hazel, Dameion, a spirit with no name, and Mrs. Green. Here's how the conversation went.

Us: Hello, Hazel. Ben misses you. (Hazel is Ben's mum)
Hazel: Hello Ben

Us: Do you have a message to pass on?
Hazel: Frank (Frank is Ben's Dad...he's alive and married again)

Us: What about Frank?
Hazel: Julie, lung cancer

Ben: Which Julie? My Julie!?
Hazel: No, family Julie

Us: Oh, is this Hazel?
Hazel: Yes

Us: Ok, what is your Christian maiden name?
Hazel: c-h-a-d-w-i-c-k (this was correct)

Us: Oh!
Hazel: 1 year Frank

Ben: Dad has one year?
Hazel: Dorothy (this is Ben's dad's new girlfriend)

Us: Do you like Dorothy?
Hazel: Hell, Dorothy

Us: You don't like her?
Hazel: No, Hell

(We lost Hazel, and we began talking to someone named Mrs. Green. Ben used to go to her caravan and stay there when he was a child)

Us: How did you die, Mrs. Green?
Board: Stroke

Us: How old were you?
Board: 70

Us: How are you?
Board: goodbye

We contacted two more spirits. I took my finger off and began to draw Mufasa from the Lion King. While I was doing this, a mischevious spirit came and spelled out Mufasa and then spelled Rachael, Attention (I wasn't paying much attention). The glass went funny so we said goodbye.

We then talked with a boy named Dameion, who was 19. He told us that he was in a car accident and Lucifer burned him and he was in heaven. He wouldn't let me leave at first, but eventually he said goodbye to me.

I would definitely do it again since our experience wasn't a bad one at all. I have many other experiences, but I will e-mail them one day when I have time.
