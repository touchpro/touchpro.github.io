The Milan Cemetery is located at the intersection of Edison Drive and Berlin Street in Milan, Ohio (Milan Township, Erie County). The coordinates are 41° 17' 40.46" N, 82° 35' 47.6" W.

Milan Cemetery is adjacent to Saint Anthony Cemetery, and it is still active. There are many old gravestones, and although several have been damaged, most are in good condition. The cemetery is very well maintained.

The Abbott Tomb in the Milan Cemetery is supposedly haunted by the ghosts of Mr. and Mrs. Abbott. The legend states that the spirits inside the tomb do not like to be disturbed. If someone invades their privacy by knocking on the crypt door, the ghosts will emerge and chase them away.
