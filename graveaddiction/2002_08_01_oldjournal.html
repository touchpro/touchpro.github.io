I reacted quickly and knocked it off my arm, screaming. I HATE spiders more than anything else, they have always terrified me...no matter how tiny they are. There were lots of spiders in that bush, a bunch of eggs must have hatched or something. Well, I decided I wouldn't let anything like that happen again. So I went inside and put on jeans, socks, boots, a long sleeved shirt, and work gloves. I also grabbed my pruning shears and a can of Spider Killer Plus. I throughly sprayed the bush with Spider Killer Plus (which is the best stuff ever made), and then got right to pruning it. I may have gotten a little carried away though. The bush looks really awful now, I hope I didn't prune it back too far. There was this one time years ago that my mom got fed up with these bushes in front of her porch and pruned them too much...within a few weeks they had died, and then they looked even worse. I hope that doesn't happen to me. Well, if it does I'll just have to borrow a saw from someone and cut the whole thing down. We'll see what happens.

The spider fiasco was the most "exciting" thing that happened tonight. I went grocery shopping, which usually isn't one of my favorite things to do, but tonight it wasn't too bad...Kroger's wasn't crowded at all.
