Twins

October 14, 2007



On October 14, 2007 I received an e-mail from Allison B. about another interesting ouija board experience:


Another experience with Rick was last night. We were asking things like how many kids I would have...he said 2. So I asked if it would be twins, and he said "d- well maybe."

I always feel comfortable with Rick, and I know it's because he is younger. He is 17. He died at age 12, but he also sometimes gets more intimate, like puts his replies in our minds - like when he told us how old he was when he died, my friend and I didn't want to ask how but both got the thought of a car accident in our heads at the same time. We asked him if he put these thoughts in our heads, and he said yes.

He also made fun of my friend, saying that she'd be a stripper when she grows up because "boobs are good"...and she has big ones =]

He also told me I would be a forensic scientist (my dream job), and he told me my husband's name would be Adam (which another spirit also told me). He said that my friend and I would go to UCLA together.

Rick always has fun things to say, so I'll probably be back with more stories.
