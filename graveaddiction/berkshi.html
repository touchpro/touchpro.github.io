Berkshire Cemetery is located on the south side of OH-37, about 600 feet east of South Galena Road, outside Sunbury, Ohio (Berkshire Township, Delaware County). The coordinates are 40° 15' 53.44" N, 82° 54' 21.43" W.

Berkshire Cemetery is still active, the oldest section of the cemetery is located on the east side. There are several notable people buried at this cemetery, including Benson Walker Hough, a Major General in the Ohio National Guard and Federal Judge of the Southern District of Ohio, and Lawrence Myers, co-founder of Sunbury. The cemetery is in fantastic shape, most of the broken gravestones have been restored.
