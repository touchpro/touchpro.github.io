Experience With My First Ouija Board

December 29, 2004



On December 29, 2004 I received an e-mail from Blanca Robledo about her first time using an ouija board:


"My ouija board was given to me from one of my close aunts, and since I got it as a Christmas present I decided to play! One night some friends and I decided to have a little get together and just play with my board. So we did. As we sat all together we would ask it questions about our future and many questions would be the same. So we asked it if we how long one of my friends, Brigette, would live and it answered "not long". We were scared so we asked when she would die and it answered December 31...New Year's Eve! The night we were all supposed to go to a party! The spirit said she would end up drinking and before we knew it we were gonna die from a car accident...well luckily I didn't go that night, but my friends never took it serious and before I realized my 3 best friends were gone! Until this day I play with the ouija board, maybe its the feeling you get when you play, the excitement or maybe something or someone attracts us to it? Well, whatever you do as you play the ouija board, just be very careful about what you say and never be rude because you might never know who you may be speaking to!"
