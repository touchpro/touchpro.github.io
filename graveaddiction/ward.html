Ward Cemetery is located on the west side of Township Road 245, just south of OH-309, outside Marseilles, Ohio (Dudley Township, Hardin County). The coordinates are 40° 38' 33.04" N, 83° 26' 20.95" W.

Ward Cemetery, also known as Hastings Cemetery, is no longer active. It is very small, but there are many rows of old gravestones. Most are in great condition. The township does a great job of maintaining the cemetery.
