Scarred for Life

July 1, 2007



On July 1, 2007 I received an e-mail from Steph, Kayla, and Shanna about an interesting ouija board experience:


One time our Aunts, Kelly and Anne, used an ouija board when they were thirteen years old. They asked, "Is anyone with us?" The board said, "Yes." They asked, "What do you look like?" The board said, "You'll see me tonight." They said, "Who will see you tonight?" The board said, "The one behind you." They looked back to see their mother, freaked out, and stopped.

That night their mother was preparing dinner and cutting vegetables and almost cut her finger off!!!!!!!! Later that evening she spotted something peculiar in the mirror. It was a woman who resembled a witch. According to our family, she saw the witch for a while after that. She looked and found the board in Kelly's closet and threw it out. Then a while later, it was there in her closet again, but no one took it out of the trash. Tina, Kayla and Shanna's mother, threw the board in the dumpster really deep and they never saw it again.

This ouija board experience has scarred them and their children for life. We're too afraid to touch it ourselves.
