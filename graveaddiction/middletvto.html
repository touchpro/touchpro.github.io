The Middletown Cemetery is located on the south side of West Street (VT-140) in Middletown Springs, Vermont (Rutland County). The coordinates are 43° 29' 5" N, 73° 7' 10" W.

The Middletown Cemetery is also known as the Old Cemetery. It is no longer active. There are many old gravestones with interesting verses and detailed carvings. The majority of the monuments are marble. Some of the gravestones have been damaged, but most are still in very good condition. The cemetery grounds are well maintained.
