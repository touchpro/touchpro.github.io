Schumm Cemetery is located on the east side of Schumm Road, just north of the intersection with Willshire Eastern Road, in Schumm, Ohio (Willshire Township, Van Wert County). The coordinates are 40° 45' 3.22" N, 84° 43' 37.24" W.

Schumm Cemetery is also known as the Zion Lutheran Cemetery, and it is located across from the church. It is still active. There are many old gravestones, and the oldest are on the north side. Most are in good condition, and several of the damaged monuments have been repaired. The grounds are very well maintained.
