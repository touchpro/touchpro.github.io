Friendly Fred

September 5, 2007



On September 5, 2007 I received an e-mail from Stephanie H. about an interesting ouija board experience:


My mom, my friend, and I talked to this really nice spirit named Fred. This Story is 100% true and I'll remember it forever!

Us: Is there anyone in the room with us?
Board: Yes

Us: May we have your name?
Board: Fred

Us: What is your last name?
Fred: Toschi

Us: Ae you Related to **** Toschi (Our P.E. Teacher!)
Fred: Yes

Us: Cool, what year did you die?
Fred: 1823

Us: How did you die?
Fred: Hung

Us: You were hung? Was it something you didn't do? (At this point I felt really emotional!)
Fred: Yes

Us: Who hung you?
Fred: Crooks

Us: Did you go to Heaven?
Fred: Yes

Us: Did you meet God?
Fred: Yes

Us: Are you happy now?
Fred: Yes

Us: Were did you live?
Fred: Boston

Us: Did you have a wife?
Fred: Yes

Us: May we have her name?
Fred: Maggie

Us: How many kids did you have?
Fred: 1

Us: What was his name?
Fred: Brandon

Us: Ok Fred, is it ok if we talk to you later?
Fred: Yes

Us: Ok Bye
Fred: Good Bye

I hope I can talk to Fred again, he really made me feel happy and comfy! I really like him, and hope he is happy and knows I like him too!
