The Stockbridge Cemetery is located at the intersection of Church Street and Main Street (MA-102) in Stockbridge, Massachusetts (Berkshire County). The coordinates are 42° 17' 8" N, 73° 19' 8" W.

The Stockbridge Cemetery is still active. There are many beautiful monuments with winged soul effigy carvings, as well as many interesting statues. Most of the gravestones are in excellent condition, and the grounds are very well maintained.
