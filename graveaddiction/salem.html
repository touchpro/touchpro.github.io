Salem Church-Slough Cemetery is located at the northwest corner of Salem Church Road and Amanda Northern Road outside Canal Winchester, Ohio in Bloom Township (Fairfield County). It is located behind Salem Church.

Salem Church-Slough Cemetery is a peaceful cemetery...there aren't any houses really close to the cemetery, and it's very quiet. The cemetery isn't in the greatest condition, there are several broken/fallen gravestones. However, the cemetery is very well taken care of other than that. I have a feeling nature isn't responsible for most of the broken gravestones, since it is such an isolated location I'm sure it has been subject to vandalism.

I also recommend checking out the Bloom Township Genealogy page, which contains a lot of great information about Salem Church-Slough Cemetery.
