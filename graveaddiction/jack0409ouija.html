In My House

April 12, 2009



On April 12, 2009 I received an e-mail from Jack L. about an interesting ouija board experience:


I have a couple experiences with the Ouija Board. I got it on the 11th of April. My sister and I talked to a spirit:

Me: Is anyone here?
Board: Yes

Me: What is your name?
Board: Megan

Becca: Boy or girl?
Board: Boy

Me: Really?
Becca: Haha, that's a weird name for a boy.
Board: (scatters all around in a mad fashion)

(I tell Becca to stop upsetting it)

Becca: Sorry.
Board: Its okay

(starts getting mad for some reason, and goes through the alphabet.)

Board: I'm Free

(I flip the board over, and flip it back)

Me: Hello?
Board: Hi. (spells out my name, Jack, then my sister's, Becca, really fast)

Me: You're in my house, right?
Board: Yes

Me: Get out, or I will have my mom call a priest.
Board: Mom

Me: What about her?
Board: Not a believer

Becca: Our mom's not a believer?
Board: Yes

Me: Get out now!
Board: No

Becca: Yes
(the board scatters, and goes to goodbye)

My house is really weird now. Doors open and close. I don't know what to do. I still recommend you buy this to play with it.

JUST BE CAREFUL!!!!!!!!
