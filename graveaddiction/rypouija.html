Stalker

March 5, 2006



On March 5, 2006 I received an e-mail from Ryan Pepi about his interesting ouija board experience:


"This session lasted about 45 minutes, and some freaky things started happening.

Devon: Will I win the lottery?
Board: Yes

Devon: Will Ryan or Kyle win the lottery?
Board: No

Devon: Will Justin win the lottery?
Board: No

Ryan: What are the lottery numbers for mega millions?
Board: 8,3,8,4,9,1

(My name is Ryan. I know I won't win the lottery now...a day after, because my parents will not use those numbers for me, Devon's dad is buying her one)

Ryan: Are you positive?
Board: Yes

Kyle: What is your name?
Board: TJ

Justin: How old were you when you died?
Board: 72

Justin: Thank you.
Board: No Problem

Ryan: Can you move the planchette without our hands on it?
Board: NO

Ryan: Can we talk to someone who can?
Board: No

Kyle: Why not?
Board: EVIL

Ryan: They are evil?
Board: Yes

Ryan: Can we talk to someone else?
Board: Yes

(Next, we met someone equal to Dug. His name is Ritchie)


Ryan: Hello
Board: Hi

Devon: Do you have a message for us?
Board: F***

(We then turned over the board and went to get some soda)

(I came back and flipped the board...at the same time, Justin felt a sharp pain in his neck and scratch marks appeared. They continued throughout the day.)

Justin: Hello?
Board: HI

Devon: What's your name?
Board: Scotty

Justin: Do you have a message for us?
Board: Yes

Devon: What is it?
Board: Sexy

Devon: Do you like me?
Board: HOT

Ryan: Do you like me?
Board: No ugly

Justin: Do you like me?
Board: Yes, sexy

Kyle: Do you think I'm a good person?
Board: No Ugly

Ryan: Okay then
Board: I follow Devon

(The next day my brother asked Devon if Scotty was following her on AIM, right after her computer got messed up and letters went across the screen.)

All of us: Bye
Board: (moved to rest in piece)

Justin: Is anybody there?
Board: NO

Kyle: How did you say no then?
Board: Kiddiong

(At this point we all removed our hands, except for Devon)

Ryan: What was the name of our family dog who just died?
Board: Jacko

Ryan Wow, that's right.
Board: I no

(We then put the board back.)

We were finished for the day."
