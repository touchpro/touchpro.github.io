Center Cemetery is located at the end of Parker Avenue in Northfield, Massachusetts (Franklin County).

Center Cemetery was established in 1685, and it is still active. There are many old gravestones, including many slate monuments with interesting carvings and epitaphs. The cemetery is very well maintained.
