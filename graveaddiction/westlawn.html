West Lawn Cemetery is located at 1919 7th Street NW in Canton, Ohio (Canton Township, Stark County). The coordinates are 40° 48' 26.31" N, 81° 23' 45" W.

West Lawn Cemetery was established in 1859, and it is still active. It located next to the McKinley Monument. Werts Receiving Vault, located in the cemetery, was the temporary resting place of President McKinley, until the nearby monument was completed in 1907. The cemetery is very large, and it is filled with interesting gravestones, statues, and mausoleums. Although it is very well maintained, there are some damaged gravestones.
