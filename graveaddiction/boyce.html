Boyce Cemetery is located on the south side of Taylortown Road, about a ½ mile east of Amoy-Ganges Road, outside Mansfield, Ohio (Franklin Township, Richland County). The coordinates are 40° 51' 27.37" N, 82° 32' 38" W.

Boyce Cemetery is very small. It is also known as the Harmony Baptist Cemetery and the Kuhn's Farm Cemetery. There are several old gravestones, and most are in good condition. There is also a small mausoleum for the Boyce family. The cemetery is very well maintained.
