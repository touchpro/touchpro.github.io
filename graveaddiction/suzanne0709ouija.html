Scariest Night

July 24, 2009



On July 24, 2009 I received an e-mail from Suzanne and Deanna about an interesting ouija board experience:


We played at Suzanne's house, which is next to a cemetery. We started at 11:11 PM.

Us: Is there anyone here?
Board: yes

Us: Are you a kind spirit?
Board: yes

Us: What's your name?
Board: NWL

Us: Are those your initials?
Board: maybe

Us: Did you die here?
Board: maybe

Us: Are you buried in the cemetery?
Board: yes

Us: When did you die?
Board: 1978

Us: Do you have any unfinished business?
Board: yes

Us: Is it revenge?
Board: yes

(we started to hear some noises like banging coming from behind us, outside in the direction of the cemetery)

Us: Are you trying to scare us?
Board: maybe

Us: Will you hurt us?
Board: No

Us: Can you make a sound?
Board: OWETTHUMP

(no sounds)

Us: How many words is that?
Board: 3
(after some unscrambling, we decided it was probally o wet thump)

Us: Is it "O WET THUMP?"
Board: yes

(at this point it was raining and my sky light began to leak, which it never has before, and water was hitting the carpet with a wet thumping noise)

Us: Are you causing that?
Board: yes

Us: Should we continue?
Board: yes

Us: What time should we stop?
Board: no

Us: Can we stop before 1:00 AM?
Board: yes

(the curtains in the room and the candle flames began to blow even though all the windows were closed, and there were no fans or air conditioners on)

Us: Are you doing that?
Board: yes

Us: What time should we stop?
Board: 14

Us: 14?
Board: yes

I happened too look up and notice that it was 12:14 AM. We freaked out, threw the board, and turned on the lights! It was a scary experience, and we hope to continue these experiences and have longer connections with the dead as we love being scared!
