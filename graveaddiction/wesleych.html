Wesley Chapel Cemetery is located on the east side of Rockmill Road, about a ¼ mile south of Marcy Road, outside Lancaster, Ohio (Bloom Township, Fairfield County). The coordinates are 39° 44' 30" N, 82° 43' 54" W.

Wesley Chapel Cemetery was established in 1855, and it is still active. The old Wesley Chapel Methodist Episcopal Church used to stand by the cemetery. There are many old gravestones, and most are in great condition. The oldest are located on the west side. The cemetery is very well maintained by the township.
