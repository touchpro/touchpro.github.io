Mount Zion Cemetery is located on the west side of Cradler-Turner Road outside Marysville, Ohio (Paris Township, Union County). The coordinates are 40° 15' 7.63" N, 83° 25' 6.9" W.

Mount Zion Cemetery was established in 1843, and it is no longer active. The cemetery is very small, and most of the gravestones are located by the back fence. The Mount Zion United Brethren Church used to stand by the cemetery. Today all that remains of the church is a small patch of cement. Although most of the gravestones have been damaged, the grounds are very well maintained by the township.
