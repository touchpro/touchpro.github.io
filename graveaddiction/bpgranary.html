Ben Piver sent me the following information about the Granary Burying Ground:

The Granary Burying Ground is located at Tremont and Park St, next to the Park Street Chruch on the Freedom Trail in Boston. The victims of the Boston Massacre, parents of Benjamin Franklin, Samuel Adams, Peter Faneuil (Faneuil Hall), and Paul Revere are laid to rest here. One interesting thing I noticed was how some of the headstones had sunk into the ground from their own weight over time.
