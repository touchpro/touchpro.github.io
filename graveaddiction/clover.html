Clover Cemetery is located on the west side of Alton-Darby Creek Road in Alton, Ohio (Prairie Township, Franklin County). The coordinates are 39° 57' 18.78" N, 83° 10' 6.1" W.

This cemetery is surrounded by a fence, but there is another large section within the fence that is surrounded by an old iron fence. There is a pile of broken headstones within the iron fence, as well as a bunch of broken footstones that are laid out flat.
