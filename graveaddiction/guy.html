Guy Cemetery is located back a lane off the north side of US-40 (West Cumberland Road) in St. Elmo, Illinois (Fayette County). The coordinates are 39° 1' 15" N, 88° 51' 45" W.

Guy Cemetery is small, but it looks like it may still be active. There are many old gravestones, and although some had been damaged, most are now repaired. There are some very beautiful carvings and interesting monuments. The cemetery is very well maintained.
