Abandoned House

Søllested, Højreby Community, Denmark



Photo taken by Flemming Petersen.



Flemming Petersen sent me the following information about this abandoned house:

This house is located near the village Søllested, Højreby Community. It is probably known to the most people, as it is located right next to a major road that leads to a ferry-connection for Germany. The house is nothing exceptional, however, it has been abandoned for many years as I have never seen it NOT being abandoned. Some people say that it had been a part of an old nursing home, but the story is not confirmed. A typical Danish 1910-1930 architecture.
