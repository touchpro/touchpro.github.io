Stukey Cemetery is located on the west side of Old Logan Road, north of Pump Station Road, outside Sugar Grove, Ohio (Berne Township, Fairfield County).

Stukey Cemetery is still an active cemetery. There are many more recent gravestones towards the front of the cemetery, and there are many rows of old gravestones towards the back. There are at least two Revolutionary War veterans buried at Stukey Cemetery, and many other war veterans as well. The cemetery is very well taken care of...many broken gravestones have been reset, and there are also a few weathered gravestones that have new legible markers in front of them.
