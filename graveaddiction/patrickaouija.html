My Mother's Story

March 12, 2007



On March 12, 2007 I received an e-mail from Patrick A. about his mother's interesting ouija board experience:


This took place when my mother was a kid.

That night she and her sister was playing around the board. My mother asked how many childern she would have, and it said six. Then it was her sister's turn. She asked how many kids she would have, and it said 10. So they looked at one another and started laughting. Then the pointer moved all by itself, and then it just ran off the table.

That night they went to bed, and my mother kept feeling something touching her on her legs. She could also hear footsteps...they sounded like hooves, and then it jumped on the bed and then on her! By that time she screemed and ran to wake her sister up, but her sister was in the closet shaking to death so they ran out of the house.

At daylight they went back in the house and grabbed the board. They closed it and buried it deep...deep as they could get it.
