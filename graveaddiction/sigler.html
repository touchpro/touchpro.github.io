Richmond Dale, Ohio

Sigler Cemetery is located back a lane off the north side of Sigler Road in Richmond Dale, Ohio (Jefferson Township, Ross County). The coordinates are 39° 11' 38.66" N, 82° 46' 33.53" W.

Sigler Cemetery has many old gravestones, including a couple folk grave markers made of concrete. Although many of the gravestones are in good condition, several have been damaged. The grounds are very well maintained.
