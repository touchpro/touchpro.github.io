Strong Presence

January 5, 2008



On January 5, 2008 I received an e-mail from John Prato about an interesting ouija board experience:


I read an article on your website and you said, "Have an open mind, if you believe the board isn't going to work, it probably won't." You're totally wrong about what your said. Now I'm gonna tell you something. I purchased the Ouija board made by Parker Brothers from Toys R Us in September of 2005. I didn't do any research on it, because I didn't believe in the Ouija board. It was about 10:00 PM, and I decided to give it a try. I took the Ouija board out of the box and placed it on my lap. Then I took the planchette and placed it on the board, and then my finger on the planchette. I didn't even use a candle. I started going around in slow circles for about a minute and then I stopped. And then I started saying "Is there anybody out there?" I kept saying it for about 15 or 20 minutes, and then all of a sudden, I felt a strong presence in my room!!!!!! Note: I never felt anything like that in my entire life!!!!! I was so scared that I ran out of my room, and threw the Ouija board in the garbage. I was very shaken, and I didn't get to sleep that night. The strong presence I felt was on the ceiling!!!!!! I didn't even say "prove it or something" for it to come through!!!!!!
