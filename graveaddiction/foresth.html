Forest Hill Cemetery is located at the end of Cemetery Road, just south of East Derry Road, in Derry, New Hampshire (Rockingham County). The coordinates are 42° 53' 26" N, 71° 17' 38" W.

Forest Hill Cemetery is large, and it is still active. There are many old gravestones with interesting carvings, including many slate stones with winged skulls and soul effigies. The cemetery is very well maintained by the town.
