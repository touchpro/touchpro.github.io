Hopetown-Wesley Chapel Cemetery is located on Hopetown Road about three miles north of Chillicothe, Ohio (Springfield Township, Ross County). The coordinates are 39° 22' 38.47" N, 82° 58' 17.26" W.

Hopetown-Wesley Chapel Cemetery isn't very big, but I think it's still active and used for burials. There are several Revolutionary War veterans buried there, and there are many other old gravestones. The grounds are extremely well taken care of, and there are very few broken gravestones...especially considering it's such an old cemetery.

A huge thank you to Mike Hill for telling me about this cemetery, and for visiting it with me!
