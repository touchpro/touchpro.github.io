The Ganges Cemetery is located on both side of Shelby-Ganges Road, just west of Ganges-Five Points Road, in Ganges, Ohio (Bloominggrove Township, Richland County). The coordinates are 40° 54' 15.94" N, 82° 33' 44.9" W.

The Ganges Cemetery is still active. The oldest gravestones are located on the north side of the road. Many of the gravestones contain beautiful, detailed carvings; in fact, one even has a soul effigy carving similar to the ones seen in the New England area. Most of the gravestones are in very good condition; very few have been damaged. The cemetery is very well maintained by the township.
