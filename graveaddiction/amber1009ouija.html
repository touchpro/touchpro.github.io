Talkative Little Girl

October 24, 2009



On October 24, 2009 I received an e-mail from Amber about an interesting ouija board experience:


I've only recently begun experimenting with a Ouija board. This story is from my second experience using one, and took place on Friday night, October 23, 2009. My friends, who I'll call J and C, came over. Around 11:00 PM we began setting up. We lit three white candles, one for each of us, called forth only positive energies, and started. The board we were using had the words "yes," "no," "good," "bad," "goodbye," and the alphabet and numbers 0-9, as well as a cross drawn on it. The following conversation took about an hour.

Question: Are there any spirits here who may speak with us?
Answer: Yes

Question: What is your name?
Answer: D-D

Question: Is your name Deedee/Didi?
Answer: Yes

Question: Are you a good spirit?
Answer: Good

Question: That's great. How old are you?
Answer: 7

Question: What is your birthday?
Answer: A-U-G-U-S-T 3

Question: What year were you born?
Answer: 1971

Question: How did you die, D.D.?
A. Bad

Question: What happened to you?
Answer: R-O-G-A-R

Question: Did a person named Rogar hurt you?
Answer: No

Question: Do you know the name of the person who did?
Answer: N-I-C-E

Question: Is that a last name?
Answer: Yes

Question: As in "Mr. Nice?"
Answer: Yes

Question: Did someone ask you to call him "Mr. Nice?"
Answer: Yes

Question: So who is Rogar?
Answer: Bad

Question: Yes, we know he is bad. Did he know Mr. Nice?
Answer: Yes

Question: Did he introduce you to Mr. Nice?
Answer: Yes

Question: We're very sorry, D.D. Is there anything you'd like us to know?
A. F-O-D-0.

This message of "F-O-D-0" continued for several questions pertaining to any message the spirit would like to give, and when asked what it meant, it would only repeat.

Question: Do you live near here, D.D.?
Answer: Yes

Question: Do you live with any of us?
Answer: No

Question: Did you follow any of us?
Answer: Yes

Question: Did you follow J here?
Answer: Yes

Question: Where did you follow her from?
Answer: K-*-*-*-*

The spirit proceeded to spell out J's boyfriend's name, who I'll call K.

Question: Do you live with K?
Answer: Yes

Question: Do you like living with K?
Answer: Yes

Question: Do you like J and K being together?
Answer: B-E-A-U

Question: ...Did you ever live in Louisiana?
Answer: Yes (*this was a surprise, as the question wasn't asked all too seriously*)

Question: So you moved to Florida?
Answer: Yes

Question: Do you like talking to us?
Answer: Yes

Question: Did you go to school?
Answer: *the planchette moved to a cross we had drawn on the board*

Question: So you went to a Christian school?
Answer: Yes

Question: Did you like to read?
Answer: No

Question: Did you like learning?
Answer: Yes

Question: Do you know your parents?
Answer: No

Question: So were you adopted?
Answer: Yes

Question: Do you have a step-father? (*we asked this thinking it might have been one of the men who had wronged her*)
Answer: No

Question: What about a stepmother?
Answer: Yes

Question: What is your favorite color?
Answer: Y-E-L-L-O-W

Question: What color hair do you have?
Answer: R-E-D

Question: What color eyes do you have?
Answer: G-R-E-E-N

Question: Any last messages you'd like to give us, D.D.?
Answer: S-T-A-Y A-W-A-Y F-R-O-M R-O-G-A-R

Question: We'll keep that in mind, D.D., thank you...
Answer: *the planchette began moving around the board indiscernibly*)

Question: Do you have to leave, D.D.?
Answer: Yes

Question: Are you going back to K's home?
Answer: Yes

Question: Okay, thank you for talking to us. Goodbye.
Answer: Goodbye.
