Washington Cemetery is located off Washington Street in Keene, New Hampshire (Cheshire County).

Washington Cemetery is also known as the Old Prison Street Yard. It is no longer active. There are many old gravestones, including several slate monuments with detailed carvings. Most are in excellent condition. The cemetery is very well maintained by the city.
