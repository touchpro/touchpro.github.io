Fort Meigs Union Cemetery is located along OH-65 in Perrysburg, Ohio (Perrysburg Township, Wood County). The coordinates are 41° 33' 5.68" N, 83° 38' 30.96" W.

Fort Meigs Union Cemetery is right down the road from Fort Meigs. Many War of 1812 veterans who died at the battles that took place at Fort Meigs are buried here. There are many old gravestones, and most are in good condition. The grounds are very well maintained by both the city and the township.
