The Pioneers Repose Cemetery is located on the east side of County Road 8 in Houcktown, Ohio (Jackson Township, Hancock County). The coordinates are 40° 56' 34.07" N, 83° 35' 33.68" W.

The Pioneers Repose Cemetery, also known as the Houcktown Cemetery, was established in 1857. It is still active. The oldest gravestones are located at the back, and most are in great condition. The cemetery is very well maintained.
