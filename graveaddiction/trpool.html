Tracy Rivadeneyra sent me the following information about Pool Cemetery:

From my understanding, all the headstones were found in a stack or pile at another location of the park many years ago. Many years ago, they were relocated to a site that may have been the border of the pioneer family's property. No one knows where the remains of the people are located. Highbanks is a very unique park, there are Indian mounds and other Indian earthworks in the park, so it is very rich in history - not to mention very beautiful with its high shale banks, river, and tributaries.
