Oak Grove Cemetery is located at 334 South Sandusky Street in Delaware, Ohio (Delaware Township, Delaware County). The coordinates are 40° 17' 14" N, 83° 4' 28" W.

Oak Grove Cemetery is very large (80 acres!). It was founded in 1851 and it is still active. The first burial was on June 20, 1851. There are many old, interesting gravestones at the cemetery, including many moved from smaller cemeteries that were destroyed. Saint Mary Cemetery adjoins this cemetery. It is very well maintained by the Oak Grove Cemetery Association.
