Abandoned House

Barrow County, Georgia



Photo taken by Cate S.



Cate S. sent me the following information about her visit to this abandoned house:

This house is located on the same driveway as the Omer Church. My brother was saying he thinks the house could have belonged to the Dillards [the Dillard family - the Dillards gave the Omers the land that the cemetery and Church now stands on], but the Dillards were around in the 1700's and I don't think they would've been building houses like this back then. So, I was thinking it could've possibly been the home of one of the Reverends of the church and his family, or something along those lines.
