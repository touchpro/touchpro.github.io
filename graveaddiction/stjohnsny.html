Saint Johns Cemetery is located at 100 North Brandywine Avenue in Schenectady, New York (Schenectady County). The coordinates are 42° 48' 14" N, 73° 55' 33" W.

Saint Johns Cemetery is adjacent to Vale Cemetery, and it is still active. There are many interesting gravestones, but as you can see from the photos above, the ones I found most intriguing were the homemade markers. They are located closest to Vale Cemetery, and there's a very large section that is almost entirely homemade concrete and metal gravestones. Each one is unique, and it is obvious that much love was put into their construction. The cemetery is very well maintained.
