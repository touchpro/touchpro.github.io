Real Jokester

April 19, 2009



On April 19, 2009 I received an e-mail from Sandy G. about an interesting ouija board experience:


I bought a Ouija board sometime in February - March of 2009. I've played it quite a bit in about a month, with one other person or by myself. I definitely have plenty of stories. I have communicated with at least ten spirits that I know of. Here is one conversation in which the spirit was a real jokester, or maybe just wanted to mess with me a little...

April 2009 Evening: Myself with one other female playing, and another just sitting there.

Board: You are ugly as hell. ha ha ha ha

Me: What makes you think this?
Board: I see you, ha ha ha ha ha ha ha ha ha

Me: I am not ugly.
Board: Rite

Me: What's your name?
Board: Ugly Sandy

Me: No, that's my name.
Board: So your admitting you're ugly...ha ha ha
Board: Bitch boo

Me: I see that you like to joke around, say another joke...
Board: Ni%&$r

Me: Don't call me that, that is not nice.
Board: Ok bitch
Board: Black bitch

Me: Me?
Board: Not you dumb ass
Board: Shadicka

Me: Shadicka, what kind of name is that? (one of the people present but not playing, at the time, her name is Shadira)
Board: Ha ha ha

Me: Why are you saying these things? I am going to go and pray and call God.
Board: I love God

Me: So why are you acting like this?
Board: Because you are bad to everyone

Me: Goodbye
Board: Goodbye

A little later:

Me: Is anyone there?
Board: Shut your shit
Board: F&*%*U bye

Me: F&*%* me bye?
Board: You're dumb

Me: How do you know this?
Board: I watch you...Don't cry ha ha ha ha ha ha
Board: Bitch...ha ha ha ha ha ha ha ha ha ha ha ha

Me: You are a real jokester, aren't you...say another since you're on a roll.
Board: Okay, why are you so dumb (it actually said why is you so dumb) ha ha ha ha

Me: I'm dumb? (in amazement with a smirk)
Board: Indeed
Board: You crazy girl.......

Me: Goodbye
Board: Goodbye

I don't know if this was the same spirit as earlier in the evening, but it sounds like it to me, and they definitely were telling me off.
