Evil Spirit

November 29, 2008



On November 29, 2008 I received an e-mail from Kayla W. about an interesting ouija board experience:


November 27, 2008 my cousin (Rebecca) and I (Kayla) encountered a very evil spirit. We were playing the ouija board tonight, and we saw something. By the way, we are both 12 year old girls.

Me: Is there anyone here who wishes to speak?
Board: Yes

Me: Are you evil?
Board: Yes

Becca: Are you a girl?
Board: Yes

Me: What is your name?
Board: Bedgewalk

That is when things got freaky. Becca said that the Saaturday before we did this, she was at a sleepover and they asked the same questions and got the same name and responses.

Becca: Was that you at the sleepover last week who shattered the lamp?
Board: Yes

Me: Why?
Board: I wanted to kill her

Becca: Who?
Board: Jenna

Me: Why?
Board: She killed me

Becca: How?
Board: Gun

Me: How old were you?
Board: 12

Jenna is 16, by the way.

Becca: Are you the one who has been clawing us?
Board: Yes

Me: Why?
Board: Scared

Becca and me: Well, goodbye...nice talking to you. We feel very sorry for you.
Board: Me too

Board: Be careful of Jenna

Two weeks later Jenna was killed by a gun. We asked Bedgewalk if that was her, and it was.
