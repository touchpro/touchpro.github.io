Boston Cemetery is located on the north side of US-50, behind the Methodist Episcopal Church, in Boston, Ohio (Paint Township, Highland County). The coordinates are 39° 12' 57.06" N, 83° 30' 29.95" W.

You could drive right by Boston Cemetery without seeing it if you weren't looking. It sits pretty far back from the road. But it's definitely worth visiting. There are many old gravestones, and the township does a terrific job of maintaining the cemetery grounds. There are quite a few broken gravestones, but several have been restored.
