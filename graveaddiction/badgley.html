Badgley Cemetery is located off Rodney-Harrisburg Road outside Bidwell, Ohio (Springfield Township, Gallia County).

Badgley Cemetery is an old family cemetery. It is very small, there are only a few people buried there. It is located on private property, so you will have to ask permission before visiting this cemetery. But due to privacy issues I'm not going to specify many details about the exact location of the cemetery...if you really want/need to know where it is, e-mail me and I'll check with the property owner to see if it's OK to give you more specific directions.

A very big thanks to my friend Mark for telling me about this cemetery and getting permission to visit it! I love little, old cemeteries like this one!
