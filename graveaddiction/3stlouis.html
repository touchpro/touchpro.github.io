Saint Louis Cemetery No. 3 is located on Esplanade Avenue in New Orleans, Louisiana (Orleans Parish). The coordinates are 29° 59' 06" N. 90° 05' 12" W.

I visited this cemetery as part of the Gray Line Super City tour. Saint Louis Cemetery No. 3 is the newest of the three Saint Louis Cemeteries. Like the other two, the vast majority of the graves are above ground tombs or vaults in the wall surrounding the cemetery.

Saint Louis Cemetery No. 3 is a little off the beaten tourist path, but it's definitely worth visiting. There are lots of interesting graves and statues to see!
