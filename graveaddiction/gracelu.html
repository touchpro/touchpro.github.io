Grace Lutheran Cemetery is located near the intersection of US-22 and Tent Church Road outside Lancaster, Ohio (Pleasant Township, Fairfield County). The coordinates are 39° 44' 57.17" N, 82° 29' 22.9" W.

Grace Lutheran Cemetery is small, but it is still active. Most of the gravestones are in excellent condition; very few have been damaged. The church does a great job of maintaining the cemetery.
