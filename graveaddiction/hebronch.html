The Hebron Church Cemetery is located on the north side of Crownover Mill Road, 3/4 mile west of Southward-Busick Road, outside Crownover Mill, Ohio (Monroe Township, Pickaway County). The coordinates are 39° 37' 31.66" N, 83° 11' 27.66" W.

The Hebron Church Cemetery was established in 1864. There are many old gravestones, including several with beautiful carvings. Although some gravestones have been broken, most are in good condition. The cemetery grounds are very well maintained by the township.
