Otterbein Cemetery is located at the intersection of County Road 190 and County Road 209 outside Mount Victory, Ohio (Dudley Township, Hardin County). The coordinates are 40° 35' 1.76" N, 83° 30' 16.33" W.

Otterbein Cemetery is located on both sides of the church, and it is still active. There are many old gravestones, and almost all of them are in great condition. The cemetery is very well maintained by the church.
