Walnut Grove Cemetery is located on Cemetery Road, just west of High Street (US-23), in Worthington, Ohio (Sharon Township, Franklin County).

Walnut Grove Cemetery is a large cemetery that was established in 1860. It is still in use today and is taken care of by the City of Worthington and the Sharon Township Trustees. The cemetery is in excellent condition! There are lots of trees and winding paths, it's a very peaceful cemetery to walk through.
