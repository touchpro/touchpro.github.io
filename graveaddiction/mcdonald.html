McDonald Cemetery is located outside West Jefferson, Ohio (Deer Creek Township, Madison County).

McDonald Cemetery, also known as Plymell Cemetery or Upper Glade Cemetery, is no longer active. It is very hidden, and has been abandoned for many years. Most of the gravestones have been damaged, and they have been scattered throughout the cemetery. The grounds are very overgrown.

Many thanks to Ivajean Webb for telling me about this cemetery, and for giving me excellent directions to it!
