Funny Experience

February 23, 2008



On February 23, 2008 I received an e-mail from Kaitlin about an interesting ouija board experience:


Me (Kaitlin), my sister (Shantelle), and Mom (Nicole) were on our Ouija board today. My sister and I had never been on a Ouija board before. I'm 10 and she is 8...It was so funny!

Us: Spirits, are you with us? Friends, are you with us?
Board: Yes

Kaitlin: How old are you?
Board: 7

Mom: Are you a boy?
Board: Yes

Mom: What are your initials?
Board: MJ

Shantelle: How old will I be when I have my first baby?
Board: 45

Shantelle: What is your name?
Board: MJKNOL

Mommy: Do you know how to spell?
Board: No

Kaitlin: You sound like a nice boy. Are you nice?
Board: Maybe.

Us: Ok. We have to go now.
Board: NO. (going in circles furiously and fast)

Us: Goodbye.

It was so much funnier when it actually happened though, AAha!
