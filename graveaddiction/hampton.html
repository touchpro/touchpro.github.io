New Hampton Cemetery is located on the south side of Cemetery Road in West Jefferson, Ohio (Jefferson Township, Madison County). The coordinates are 39° 56' 23.51" N, 83° 16' 9.15" W.

New Hampton Cemetery is all that remains of the old town of New Hampton. The cemetery is still active, but there are many rows of old gravestones. Most of the gravestones are in good condition, but like any old cemetery there are several broken ones. The township does a terrific job of maintaining the grounds.
