Ritchey-Crawford Cemetery is located down a long lane north of County Road 29 outside Crawford, Ohio (Crawford Township, Wyandot County). The coordinates are 40° 55' 18" N, 83° 19' 42" W.

Ritchey-Crawford Cemetery (sometimes spelled Ritchy) is very small. It is very well hidden by a cornfield in the summer/early fall. There are many interesting gravestones, as well as a memorial to Colonel William Crawford (click here to read more about Col. Crawford, and to see photos of the memorial in town). Many gravestones have been damaged over the years, but most have been repaired. The cemetery is very well maintained.
