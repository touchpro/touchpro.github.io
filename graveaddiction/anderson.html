Anderson Cemetery is located on the north side of Bunn Road, west of Taylor Homestead Road, in Richland, Illinois (Sangamon County). The coordinates are 39° 51' 59" N, 89° 50' 17" W.

Anderson Cemetery is small, and it is no longer active. There are many old gravestones, and although some had been damaged, most are now repaired. There are some very beautiful carvings. The cemetery is very well maintained.
