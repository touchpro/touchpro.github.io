We Believed

December 28, 2008



On December 28, 2008 I received an e-mail from Cassi P. about an interesting ouija board experience:


My name is Cassi. My friends Angie, Courtney, and myself decided to use an ouija board. This is our experience.

On December 27, 2008 we made our own board with paper, a pen, and a quarter as the planchette. And it actually worked! I have the glow in the dark board, but it wasn't at my house at the time. So we just made one. And because we believed, it worked. Our session was about 45 minutes long.

Angie: Are there any spirits here?
Board: Yes

Courtney: How old are you?
Board: 18

Cassi: Are you a good spirit?
Board: Yes

Cassi: Are you sure?
Board: Yes

Courtney: Are you a female?
Board: Yes

Courtney: What year did you die in?
Board: 1987

Cassi: Are you comfortable with us?
Board: Yes

Cassi: Would you tell us your name?
Board: No

Cassi: That's okay.
Board: Thank you

Courtney: Did you die in the area we're in?
Board: Yes

Angie: Did you have a good life?
Board: (moved quickly) No

Cassi: How did you die?
Board: Mu

Cassi: It's okay to cry, Courtney!
Board: No

Cassi: Did you get murdered?
Board: No

(about 15 seconds later)

Board: I'm just kidding

Angie: Are you getting more comfortable with us?
Board: Yes. I like you a lot.

Angie: If we play later will you come back?
Board: Yes

All: You sound like a sweet person.
Board: Thank you. Sephus...

Cassi: Are you asking if we're sisters?
Board: No, mine

Angie: You have sisters?
Board: Yes

Angie: How many?
Board: 9

Angie: Were they mean to you?
Board: Yes

Courtney: All nine?
Board: No

Angie: Did they ever hurt you?
Board: No

Cassi: Are any of them the reason you died?
Board: No

Cassi: Will you tell us your name?
Board: No

Cassi: Are you afraid to tell us your name?
Board: Yes

Courtney: Have you been in another board before?
Board: No

Cassi: Is that why you're afraid to tell us your name?
Board: Yes

Angie: That's okay.

Cassi: Would you like to know our names?
Board: Yes

Courtney: I'm Courtney
Angie: I'm Angie
Cassi: And I'm Cassi
Board: I like you guys

Cassi: Can you see us right now?
Board: Yes

Cassi: Will you ever harm me?
Board: No

Angie: Will you protect us?
Board: Yes

Courtney: Are you sitting by us?
Board: Yes

Cassi: By Courtney?
Board: Yes

Cassi: On the sofa?
Board: Yes

Cassi: (Looks straight ahead) Am I looking right at you?
Board: Yes

Courtney: Will you tell us your name now?
Board: Yes, Harpur.

All: That's a very pretty name!
Board: Thank u guys

Cassi: Did you go to heaven?
Board: Yes

Courtney: Is it as good as people say it will be?
Board: Yes

How did you die?
Board: (random letters)

The quarter doesn't move any longer.
