Marlborough Cemetery is located at the intersection of Horseshoe Road and Leonardsburg Road outside Delaware, Ohio (Delaware County, Troy Township). The coordinates are 40° 22' 2.22" N, 83° 1' 49.49" W.

Marlborough Cemetery is a large cemetery that is still in use today. It was moved to this location several years ago due to construction of the Delaware Reservoir. The cemetery is filled with many old, interesting gravestones. There are even a few Revolutionary War veterans buried here. It is extremely well maintained, and it's a very beautiful, peaceful place to visit.
