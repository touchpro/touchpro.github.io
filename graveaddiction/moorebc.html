Moore Cemetery is located back a lane off US-40 outside Morristown, Ohio (Union Township, Belmont County).

Moore Cemetery is small, and it is no longer active. It sits on a hill near the former Mediterranean restaurant. The cemetery is VERY overgrown, and it would be very easy to drive right by if you weren't looking for it. Only a couple gravestones are still standing, and many are missing. It is abandoned; I don't think any maintenance has been done to it for many years.

Many thanks to Tim Fox and Justin Huntsman for visiting this cemetery with me!
