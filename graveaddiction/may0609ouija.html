God Loves You

June 21, 2009



On June 21, 2009 I received an e-mail from May about an interesting ouija board experience:


I have a few stories, but they are all from the same day.

First - my friend and I were at her house. I asked the board a question in my head, so she couldn't fake move the board. I asked in my head, "Who loves me?" It said, "God loves you." This is when I first figured out that I knew the board worked, because there was no way she could answer it.

Second - we were in a friend's room, and we asked if spirits were there. It said, "Yeah, watch out" really fast. We were like, "For who?" and it said me. It was weird because it was moving so fast, like I've never seen before.

Last - we were in my room talking to two nice spirits. They confirmed what I thought - there were ghosts in the house. It scares me, but they are nice to me so it's all good. They made a noise, and I asked if it was them. They said yes, and I just asked to please stop because they were scaring me - its nothing against them, I like them. I'm just scared. And it said, "okay, sorry."

I haven't used it since then, and i don't plan on it...just because I don't want to risk talking to a bad spirit.

Don't use ouija boards - it's not worth the risk. I still have weird feelings all the time.
