The Old Burying Ground is located between MA-122 and Oak Street in Grafton, Massachusetts (Worcester County). The coordinates are 42° 12' 39" N, 71° 41' 30" W.

The Old Burying Ground is also known as the Old Oak Street Burial Ground or the Indian Cemetery. The first burial was in 1731. There are many old gravestones, including many slate monuments with beautiful carvings. Most of the gravestones are in very good condition, but a few have been damaged over the years. The cemetery is very well maintained.
