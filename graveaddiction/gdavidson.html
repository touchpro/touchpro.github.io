Davidson Cemetery

Benton County, Oregon



Photo taken by George I. Davidson.



George I. Davidson sent me the following information about Davidson Cemetery:

Enclosed is a photo of the Davidson Cemetery. It is no longer used for burials; the burials from 1895-1942 are the only 13 recorded burials.

The Cemetery is on the site of an old town named "Peak" that is no longer there, just a forest of trees. It had a post office at one time at the homestead of "Harrison & Virginia Davidson."
