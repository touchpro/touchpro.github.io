The Bridge Street Cemetery is located on the west side of Bridge Street in Northampton, Massachusetts (Hampshire County). The coordinates are 42° 19' 30" N, 72° 37' 38" W.

The Bridge Street Cemetery was established in 1663, and it is still active. There are many old gravestones, including many with interesting carvings and epitaphs. The cemetery is very well maintained.

For more information about the Bridge Street Cemetery, please see the Historic Northampton site.
