Greenwood Cemetery is located at 1526 National Road in Wheeling, West Virginia (Ohio County). The coordinates are 40° 3' 42" N, 80° 40' 45" W.

Greenwood Cemetery is very large, and it's still active. It is very well maintained by the Greenwood Cemetery Association. There are many interesting monuments, and most are in very good condition. I also saw several deer and a toad while I was there!
