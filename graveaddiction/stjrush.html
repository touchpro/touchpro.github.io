Saint Joseph Rushcreek Cemetery is located on the southwest side of Horns Mill Road, about 2 ½ miles from US-33, outside Sugar Grove, Ohio (Berne Township, Fairfield County).

Saint Joseph Rushcreek Cemetery is also known as the Old Catholic-Phillips Cemetery. I thought this was a very interesting cemetery to explore...even though it's small, all the gravestones are old! The cemetery was established in 1853 and the last burial was in 1892. Even though the cemetery hasn't been in use for many, many years, it is still very well taken care of and most of the gravestones are in fantastic shape!
