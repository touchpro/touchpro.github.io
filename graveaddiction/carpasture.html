Bidwell, Ohio

The "Old Car Burial Ground" is a very scary place to visit! This burial ground is located on Mark's parents' farm, and it is where all of his old cars are put "out to pasture". While most people take their old, non-drivable cars to a junk yard, Mark parks them in a field so he can use them for parts or sell parts to other people. He doesn't use or sell many parts from these cars though...there aren't many good parts on them! He is in his thirties and still has almost every car he has ever owned.

I had "met" Old Blue and the Old Gray Buick, but September 7, 2002 was the first time I ever saw the white car and the gold car. Both of those cars are completely dead, they won't run. The white car is in terrible shape...the windshield is broken and the roof is smashed in from an accident his brother had while driving it. It has been sitting there so long that there is even a plant growing through it's floor!!

While we were back at the burial ground Mark decided to see if Old Blue would still run. He took the battery out of the Black Beast and put it in Old Blue. After pumping the gas pedal for several minutes, Old Blue started! Mark decided to take Old Blue for a ride. He put his foot on the brake so he could put the car in drive, but when he pressed down on the brake there was a LOUD popping sound...the brakeline broke! He still drove the car up the hill and back down, but now poor Old Blue doesn't have brakes! Old Blue still smelled like burning oil and gas, and there was lots of black smoke pouring out of the exhaust!

Mark also tried to start the Old Gray Buick, however it wouldn't start. I was amazed at how fast Mark was able to change the batteries in between cars!
