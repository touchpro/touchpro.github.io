Lowell Cemetery is located on Lawrence Street in Lowell, Massachusetts (Middlesex County). The coordinates are 42° 37' 44" N, 71° 17' 35" W.

Lowell Cemetery was established in 1840. It is very large, and it is still active. There are many old gravestones and statues, and most are in excellent condition. The cemetery is very well maintained.
