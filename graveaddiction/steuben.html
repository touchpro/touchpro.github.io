The Steuben Cemetery is located on the south side of OH-162, just east of Peru Center Road, in Steuben, Ohio (Greenfield Township, Huron County). The coordinates are 41° 6' 12.34" N, 82° 41' 0.93" W.

The Steuben Cemetery was established in 1818, and it is still active. There are many old gravestones, and most are in good condition. The oldest gravestones are located on the northeast side. Many contain beautiful, detailed carvings. The cemetery is very well maintained.
