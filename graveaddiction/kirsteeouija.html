Close the Door

June 30, 2007



On June 30, 2007 I received an e-mail from Kirstee about an interesting ouija board experience:


I stumbled on your ouija site and I thought I'd send you the story of what happened to me and three other people when we played the board in an old block of flats.

I was with my friend Missy, her cousin Roxy, and a guy called Brendon.

Us: Is any one there, can someone contact us?
Board: Maybe

Us: What's your name?
Board: Jack, you (it actually just pointed to the letter u, we gave it our names)

Us: How old are you, Jack?
Board: Sixteen

(We told the board how old we all were: 14, 14, 15, 14)

Board: Good, Close the door

Us: Why?
Board: Door

(Missy was just recording the messages, so she got up and closed the door, as she did all the blinds slammed down)

Us: What was that for?
Board: See Inside

(We thought he meant people passing by could look in at us and he didn't like that)

Us: So how did you die?
Board: Bang

Us: With a gun?
Board: tniop knalb (we worked it out - it meant point blank)

Missy: How old were you when it happened? (I don't know why she asked this)
Board: 18................16

(We all got scared then because it had changed it's story)

Us: Goodbye
Board: Hello (the pointer started spinning back and forth)

(Missy and tried to open the door, but we couldn't...it was jammed shut. Then she tried to raise the blinds, but they wouldn't move either)

Board: We're friends right?
Brendon: We need to go now.

Board: Hush, Jack loves you
Brendon: Goodbye.

Board: Don't tell, I didn't tell when you smashed the doll's house

(There was a long pause, in which Missy and I calmed down a bit...we were the cowards of the group)

Board: Poor ole Ray, bang 25

(there was another pause)

Roxy started coughing and scrathing her throat.

Board: Drown her. (Roxy seemed ok again) I did.

At that point the door opened and we ran like hell. We went back to get the board the next week, and found it balanced on top of a door as we came in - in the same way people balance buckets of water they want to tip over. I haven't played since.
