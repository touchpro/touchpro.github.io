Riverside Cemetery is located at 3607 Pearl Road in Cleveland, Ohio (Cuyahoga County). The coordinates are 41° 27' 21.55" N, 81° 41' 46.71" W.

Riverside Cemetery was established in 1876, and it is still active. It is a very large cemetery, and it is filled with beautiful gravestones, statues, and mausoleums. Although a few gravestones have been damaged, most are in very good condition. The cemetery is very well maintained.
