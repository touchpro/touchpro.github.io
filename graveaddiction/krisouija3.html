Contacting Mom

April 28, 2005



On April 28, 2005 I received another e-mail from Kris Trexler in Philadelphia, Pennsylvania about an interesting ouija board experience:


"Ok, I had the Glow-in-the-dark Ouija Board and now I bought a wooden one. We wrote this down...

Kris: Is anyone here tonight?
Board: Yes

Kris: What's your name?
Board: Lin

Kris: Lin? Wait..are you my mom Linda?
Board: I love you so much and I miss you dearly

Kris: Oh my gosh..MOM?
Board: Yes Kris this is Linda

Kris: Why did you leave me?
Board: Cannot live without you

***very creeped out***

Kris: Kim, Are you doing this??
Kim: No, I swear I'm not

Kris: I miss you..Can you show me yourself?
Board: Yes, but I don't wanna scare you

Kris: I'm not scared cause you're my mom
Board: True look outside
(moves to goodbye)

**we look outside***

We can see her face in cloud form and only one star in the sky.

Kim goes to get water.

I heard a soft whisper in my ear, "I will always be with you Kris"

Im not making this up!! I was very happy that I talked to my mom, but I was still crying all night."
