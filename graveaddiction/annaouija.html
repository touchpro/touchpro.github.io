Danger Coming

November 12, 2005



On November 12, 2005 I received an e-mail from Anna about her interesting ouija board experience:


Me: Can I talk to Gerard?
Board: No

Me: Why not?
Board: Tour

Me: He's on tour?
Board: Yes

Me: Who am I talking to?
Board: Dert

Me: Would you like to try that again?
Board: Derik

Me: When did you die?
Board: 1863

Me: How did you die?
Board: Fal

Me: Do you mean fall?
Board: Yes

Me: What did you fall off of?
Board: Desk

Me: You fell off a desk?
Board: Yes

Me: How did you fall off a desk?
Board: Push

Me: Someone pushed you?
Board: Yes

Me: Why did they push you?
Board: No

Me: You don't know?
Board: Yes

Me: Where did you live?
Board: Fred

Me: You lived in Fredrick?
Board: Yes

Me: How old were you when you died?
Board: 12

Me: Are you an evil spirit?
Board: No

Me: Are you a good spirit?
Board: No

Me: Then what are you?
Board: s-a-d

Me: You're sad?
Board: Yes

Me: Why are you sad?
Board: Dead

Me: You're sad because your dead?
Board: Yes

Me: Was your family still living when you died?
Board: Yes

Me: Do you miss them?
Board: Yes

Me: Is there anything else you'd like to say?
Board: Careful

Me: You want me to be careful?
Board: Yes

Me: Careful of what?
Board: Danger

Me: There's danger coming?
Board: Yes

Me: When?
Board: Soon

Me: Goodbye!
