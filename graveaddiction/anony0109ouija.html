Christmas night I was trying out my new board, and it was my first time using one. I was with my two cousins. We were talking to a man named Ben, who knew a lot about me. I asked him if he had been following me, and he said yes. I then started asking Ben stuff about my father's side of the family, and he did not know anything. I asked him if he was related to me, and he said yes. I started asking him about my mom's side of the family, and he was able to answer every question. My mother did not know anyone named Ben in my family, so i am not sure what to think. However, every time I get on the board it is Ben talking...he is kind of a board hog.
