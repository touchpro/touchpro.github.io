The Fitchville Cemetery is located on the east side of US-250 in Fitchville, Ohio (Fitchville Township, Huron County). The coordinates are 41° 5' 54.29" N, 82° 29' 25.22" W.

The Fitchville Cemetery is still active. There are many old gravestones, and the oldest are on the west side. Most of the gravestones are in excellent condition, but several of the older monuments have been damaged. The cemetery is very well maintained by the township.
