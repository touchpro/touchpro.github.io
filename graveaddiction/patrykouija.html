Friendly Ouija Board

August 1, 2007



On August 1, 2007 I received an e-mail from Patryk R. about an interesting ouija board experience:


On August 1, 2007 we had an Ouija board session. Here is how it went:

Me: Do you wish to talk to us?
Board: YES

Me: Who are we talking to? State your name.
Board: SETH

Me: How old were you when you died?
Seth: 64

My friends thought it was too much of a personal question, so we then asked the questions below:

Me: Are you angry at us for asking that question?
Seth: NO

Then we asked the questions below regarding the future:

My Brother: Will I be a rich man?
Seth: YES

Me: Will I be rich when I grow up?
Seth: YES

Me: Who will be richer: Eryk (my brother) or me (Patryk)? Say the first letter.
Seth: E

I wanted to end this session so I asked this:

Me: Did you enjoy talking with us?
Seth: YES

We put the planchette on goodbye, and we ended the session with a prayer. All and all, I had a feeling the spirit was not malevolent.
