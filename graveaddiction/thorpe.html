Thorpe Cemetery is located on the southeast side of Sabina Road in Bloomington, Ohio (Wilson Township, Clinton County). The coordinates are 39° 32' 1.3" N, 83° 41' 51" W.

Thorpe Cemetery is small, and it is no longer active. There are many old gravestones, and although some are in good condition, there are several that have been damaged. The cemetery grounds are very well maintained.
