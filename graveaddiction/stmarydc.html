Saint Mary's Cemetery is located on the east side of OH-121, not far from the intersection with US-127, in Greenville, Ohio (Greenville Township, Darke County). The coordinates are 40° 7' 27.39" N, 84° 36' 26.37" W.

Saint Mary's Cemetery is still active. There are several old gravestones; the oldest are on the southwest side. Most are in good condition, but a few of the older gravestones have been damaged. The grounds are very well maintained.
