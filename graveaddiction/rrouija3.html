Death Will Come

February 7, 2006



On February 7, 2006 I received a second e-mail from Ricardo Rodriguez about another interesting ouija board experience:


"After playing for awhile, I gave it up because I had nothing to ask. I learned everything I could. So I asked my friend if he wanted me to ask my board anything. He said yes. I asked him what he wanted to ask and he told me that he wanted to know how he was going to die. I told him very well, but what happens from here on is not my fault. He acknowledged it and I started.

Me: Is there anyone here that would like to talk to me?
Board: Yes

Me: Is this Mark?
Board: Yes

Me: How will my friend die? ( I am not stating his name)
Board: Car Incident

At that moment I thought it can't be.


Me: When will he die?
Board: 2012 (same year as me)

Me: How will he die?
Board: You

Me: What do you mean me? Will you please clarify?
Board: You crash into him

Me: Do we survive?
Board: No

Me: So we both die?
Board: Yes

Me: What happens if one of us decides to escape death?
Board: Death will still come

Me: When?
Board: 2 days later

Me: What's the day we die?
Board: December 20, 2012

After this I said good bye and left.

This is my story. It's true. When I play the ouija board I play it in the garage, with the lights off, alone in the dark and away from the door. I am not making any of this up. All I can say about this is I hope it doesn't happen this way."
