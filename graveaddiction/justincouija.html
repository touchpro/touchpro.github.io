Freaky Experience

August 6, 2007



On August 6, 2007 I received an e-mail from Justin C. about an interesting ouija board experience:


Hello, my name is Justin. I am 16 years old. I did not believe in the Ouija until last night (August 5), in which some very weird things happened. This involved my friends, Kam and Travis, and myself.

Travis: Are there any spirits present here tonight?
Board: Yes

Travis: What is your name?
Board: Jeff

Justin: Are you doing good, Jeff?
Board: Yes

We all heard rustling in the blinds.

Justin: Was that you, Jeff?
Board: Yes

We then heard three loud pounds on the window.

Kam: Was that Jeff again?
Jeff: Yes

Kam: Why are you messing with us?
Board: Fun

Kam: We don't like it, can you please stop?
Board: Yes

Travis: Kam and I have brought a new friend to meet you, is that okay?
Board: No

Travis: He is really cool, and just wants to check out this Ouija thing, is that okay?
Board: No

Kam: Jeff, please, he is really cool, he is one of our good friends. Is it okay that he's here?
Board: Yes

Justin: What state are you from?
Board: Geor

Justin: Is it Georgia?
Board: Yes

Travis: What city in Gerogia?
Board: Atl

Justin: Atlanta?
Board: Yes

Board: Sad

Kam: You're sad?
Board: Yes

Kam: Why are you sad?
Board: Mom

Travis: What about your mom?
Board: Dead

Justin: Are you happy that she's with you?
Board: No

Justin: Why not?
Board: Bitch

Justin: Why was she a bitch?
Board: Beat

Justin: She beat you?
Board: Yes

Travis: Why did she beat you?
Board: Dru

Justin: Drunk?
Board: gs

Kam: Drugs?
Board: Yes

Kam: Alcohol too?
Board: Yes

Travis: We're sorry Jeff.
Board: Yes

Power flickered on and off in the basement only.

Kam: Was that you Jeff?
Board: Accident

Kam: It was an accident?
Board: Yes

Power then flickered on and off 9 times.

We then stopped and went upstairs.
