The Presbyterian Church Cemetery is located on the west side of Walker Road, about a ½ mile south of OH-762, outside Commercial Point, Ohio (Scioto Township, Pickaway County). The coordinates are 39° 45' 39.58" N, 83° 3' 3.75" W.

The Presbyterian Church Cemetery is full of old gravestones. Many of the gravestones have beautiful carvings, and most are in good condition. However, several of the oldest monuments have been damaged and are found stacked throughout the cemetery. The grounds are very well maintained.
