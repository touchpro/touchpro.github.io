I decided to email you right away so my story happened about five minutes ago!

This was one of the scariest moments in my life!!! I will admit, I started not to believe in the ouija anymore, but the spirits proved me wrong! So now, this is living proof to me and anyone who reads my story and believes me! Thank you again!
