Death

May 14, 2008



On May 14, 2008 I received an e-mail from Nicky about an interesting ouija board experience:


Me, my husband Joe, and my two friends, Christina and Natasha, were on the board just asking if any spirits would like to talk. One came on. I asked it for its name, and it spelled out d-e-a-t-h. I said ok, what do you wanna say? It said, "I'm with Natasha and I want your power." I said, "OK, but you're not getting what I have." He said "yes I will." I said, "You need to go away now and let a good spirit on this board...this is my board, I made it, now go away." He said, "No, I want you." I told the spirit that it couldn't have me, then Joe spells out j-e-s-u-s. The board circles, then Joe spelled out g-o-d and said "I command you to leave now." It circled again, and then said "No, he will not help you now." Then it went down to goodbye.

I was not scared, but that was an experience I will not forget.
