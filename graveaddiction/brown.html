Brown Pet Cemetery is located on the north side of Sawyer Road, just west of Hamilton Road, in Columbus, Ohio (Mifflin Township, Franklin County). It is directly across from Port Columbus International Airport.

Brown Pet Cemetery is the most interesting pet cemetery I have visited yet. The gravestones date back to the 1920's. Some are homemade, while others are as elaborate as gravestones that mark human graves. Many contain sad, beautiful poems and/or porcelain portraits. The cemetery is very well maintained.

Many thanks to Brent Nimmo and Chris Keller for visiting this cemetery with me!
