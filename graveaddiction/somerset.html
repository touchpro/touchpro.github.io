Somerset Cemetery is located on North High Street in Somerset, Ohio (Reading Township, Perry County). The coordinates are 39° 48' 46.47" N, 82° 17' 52.9" W.

Somerset Cemetery is large, and it is still active. There are many old, interesting gravestones, and the majority are in excellent condition. The cemetery is very well maintained by the township.
