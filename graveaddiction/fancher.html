Fancher Cemetery is located on the north side of Fancher Road, approximately 3/4 mile east of Harlem Road, outside New Albany, Ohio (Harlem Township, Delaware County). The coordinates are 40° 7' 58.35" N, 82° 49' 39.64" W.

Fancher Cemetery is located across the road from Maple Grove Cemetery. Both are very well maintained by the township. There are many old gravestones, and the majority are in terrific shape; very few have been damaged/broken over the years.
