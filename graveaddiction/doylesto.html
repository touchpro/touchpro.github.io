Doylestown Cemetery is located at 215 East Court Street in Doylestown, Pennsylvania (Bucks County). The coordinates are 40° 19' 0" N, 75° 7' 35" W.

Doylestown Cemetery is large, and it is still active. There are many interesting gravestones in the cemetery, including the one of W. Atlee Burpee (founder of the Burpee Seed Company). Although a few gravestones have been damaged over the years, the majority are in great condition. The cemetery is very well maintained by the private cemetery association. For more information regarding the Doylestown Cemetery, please visit the official website.
