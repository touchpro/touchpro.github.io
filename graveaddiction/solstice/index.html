Friday, June 25, 2004
posted at 1:07 PM

This is a late blog, but it's been really busy today! Last night was sooo much fun! Beth came over and we decided to find this cemetery we had been unable to find before. So we followed her directions in her cemetery book. We found the general area that it was supposed to be in, but we couldn't see it. We had to walk though a field, which of course I hadn't been prepared for, and had worn sandals. We saw some guy in the distance with his dog, we assumed we were on his land, and decided to ask him if he knew where the cemetery was. I've found that in most situations people can be helpful. He not only told us where it was, but took us there. He had the cutest dog named Max, who helped lead the way. We had to hike up an overgrown path with ticks and mosquitoes, oh my. I only lost my sandal's twice, so it wasn't too bad.

We finally got to the cemetery, and I'll tell you what, it was worth the hike. Beth got some great pictures and put them on her website. I'm horrible at putting links in blogs, so just go to graveaddiction.com. As we walked down the hill a rainstorm started, at least it's Summer, and the rain wasn't too cold.

After that we went to a bar close to Rob's (guy who assisted us) house. He told us some other great places to go to. Then Beth & I went to our favorite Mexican restaurant and stuffed ourselves on Mexican food. I was so tired when I got home, I went straight to bed, and even woke up late for work. Oh well, it was a great night!
