Monday Madness:

1. Describe what you believe is a "good" driver.

A good driver knows to get out of the fast lane if they're not going at least 10 over the speed limit. Also, use turn signals, they're very important.

A bad driver goes 55 on the freeway in the fast lane. Drives me batty. Also bad drivers weave in and out of traffic w/o using turn signals. Bad drivers think they are the only person on the road.

Well, it's another Sunday, almost time to go back to work, YUCK! I wish weekends were longer. I had a pretty good weekend, except for the fact I have a nice cold going on. Not sure where it came from, but now I'm dealing with it.

Well, my date on Friday went pretty well. I could not figure out what the hell to wear. It was that inbetween temp and you're not sure what to wear. I stood in front of the closet for a good 10 minutes. My dog was of no help to me. Sometimes I wish she could talk so I could get a second opinion on outfits. Anyways, I settled on a short black skirt, and a white top. I even got to wear sandals for the 1st time this year.

Anyways, I met him at Easton. We were to meet at the fountain in front of the Cheesecake Factory. It was pouring rain of course. At least I had an umbrella.

So we went to Brio for dinner. We had to wait for an hour & a half. We ended up going to Adobe Gilas for a drink, and looked around in the stores a bit.

Dinner was very tasty, and he insisted on paying, which was very nice (of course I didn't argue). Then we went to Barnes & Noble and looked at some books (pretty exciting Friday night, eh).

Since I had a few drinks at dinner I was pretty talkative. I kept showing him my favorite authors (Laurel K. Hamilton & J.A. Jance). Then I was looking at some tarot card books. He asked if I was into that stuff, I said yes, and he was like, that's cool. Point for him, at least he didn't call me a freak or weirdo, or give me one of those looks that I get from time to time.

So he walked me to my car & then I drove him to his. We sat in the car & talked for a bit. Then he was getting ready to go, and there was that awkward moment of silence. Then I start laughing. Why do I do that? I always laugh at a crutial time. I was like, just give me a hug, I don't know you that well yet. So I got a nice little hug. And that was that, he wants to go out again sometime soon, and I was up for that. No major sparkage, but you never know.

Saturday I hung out with my girlfriends. We ended up buying some wine and having a great gab fest. Sometimes a girl just needs some quality time with her friends. The four of us (Jenny, Amanda, Marian & I) are so close, it's like we're sisters. I think we all trust each other with everything. It's great :)

Now I'm over at my parents doing laundry. I'm going to have to leave soon to meet my Brother for dinner. Cordy is a muddy mess, she's been playing in my parents backyard. I just gave her a bath yesterday. Guess I wasn't planning ahead.

Warning: Spoilers for Dawn of the Dead

Ok, how should I start this? On Friday night I went out to Heath to see Dawn of the Dead. The movie was pretty good, but if you see it, just think of it as your standard zombie movie. Do not, I repeat, do not expect to see a remake of Dawn of the Dead. The only thing the two movies have in common is that they take place partly at a shopping mall. Here's my breakdown of the movie:

The Good:

Good special effects
Plenty of blood & gore
Plot kept me interested

The Bad:

Can you say zombie baby? That part was soooo stupid!!!
Why did they have to put a dog in the movie? Don't worry, it doesn't die.
After they leave the shopping mall, the director decides he wants to change the way he shoots the movie to 28 Days Later.
Zombies that can run???? WHY????
Oh, if you want to use your imagination leave before the credits start. During the credits they show what happens after they leave on the boat. I would rather have it end with them leaving on the boat, and coming to my own conclusions.

I can't think of anything else right now. It was a pretty decent movie. If you like zombie movies, see it. Us zombie movie fans can't be too picky when it comes to new zombie movies.

I ended up going to Heath that night because my friend Deb lives there. She is the one who taught me all about Lucio Fulci, and why he's the best Italian horror movie director (yes, better than Dario).

Anyways we saw the 9:55 showing of the movie. We bought our tickets early. If you ever go to the movie theatre at Indian Mound Mall, bring cash. How weird is it that they don't take cards???? Then we went out to eat at Garfields, which is a restaurant at the mall. I do not recommend it. The food sucked!

Yesterday was the baby shower for my friend Amanda. It went well. Amanda received plenty of new things for her baby. She seemed to have a pretty good time.

Last night I hung out with my Brother & his friends. We went to a pool hall and played pool for a few hours. Since I wasn't driving I had quite a few shots of tequila. Needless to say, I didn't play pool very well. Then we all went to visit my Brother's girlfriend who is a server at a restaurant. Poor girl had to deal with a bunch of drunk people.

Now I'm over at my parents house doing laundry. We went to Bob Evans for dinner. Their idea not mine. Then went to Meijers. I got three rolls of film developed. I'm going to have Beth put a bunch of pictures on her website. There are pictures from Point Pleasant (WV) & the Mothman festival. And a bunch of cool cemeteries.

Well, I better get going. I gotta finish my laundry & go home & clean. My place is a wreak!
