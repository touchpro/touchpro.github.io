Danger Zone

August 15, 2007



On August 15, 2007 I received an e-mail from Chahra about an interesting ouija board experience:


One night, my best friend Kelly and I, along with other friends, played the ouija board. Something happened:

Kelly: Hello, is someone here?
Board: Yes.

Kelly: What is your name?
Board: Curtis.

Kelly: When did you die?
Board: 1999.

Kelly: Is something freaky gonna happen tonight?
Board: Yes.

We all were in shock.

Kelly: What is gonna happen?
Board: Death.

Kelly: Who is gonna die?!
Board: You.

Kelly: Me? Kelly?
Board: Yes.

Kelly: Yeah right!
Me: Kelly believe them!

Kelly: Yeah right!
Board: Believe.

Then we asked some questions and the TV turned on and flipped to a channel were they play a scary movie.

Kelly: Was that you?
Board: Yes.

Kelly: Why did you do that?
Board: No reason.

Kelly: We gotta go, goodbye.
Board: Goodbye.

Then at 11:13 Kelly had a heart attack, of course we dialed 911. The other day she died.
