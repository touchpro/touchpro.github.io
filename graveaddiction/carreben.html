Carr-Ebenezer Methodist Cemetery is located on Carr Road, not far from the intersection with Arch Hill Road, outside of Zanesville, Ohio (Perry Township, Muskingum County). The coordinates are 40° 0' 4.65" N, 81° 49' 40.16" W.

Carr-Ebenezer Cemetery is a very old cemetery. The Carr-Ebenezer Methodist Episcopal Church used to stand to the right of the cemetery. It is no longer being used, the last people buried here were buried in the early 1900's. Several important people who helped form Perry Township are buried in this cemetery.
