Mount Auburn Cemetery is located at 580 Mount Auburn Street in Cambridge, Massachusetts (Middlesex County). The coordinates are 42° 22' 15" N, 71° 8' 43" W.

Mount Auburn Cemetery was established in 1831, and it is recognized as America's first garden cemetery. It is very large, and it is still active. There are many old gravestones and statues. The cemetery is extremely well maintained.
