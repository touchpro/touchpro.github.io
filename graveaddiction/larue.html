The La Rue Cemetery is located at the intersection of OH-37 and La Rue-Decliff Road in La Rue, Ohio (Montgomery Township, Marion County). The coordinates are 40° 35' 57.38" N, 83° 22' 44.42" W.

The La Rue Cemetery was established in 1885, and it is still active. The oldest gravestones are located on the southeast side, and most are in great condition; very few have been damaged. The cemetery is very well maintained.
