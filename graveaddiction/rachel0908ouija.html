Move Something

September 28, 2008



On September 28, 2008 I received an e-mail from Rachael about an interesting ouija board experience:


My friends and I recently used the ouija board again, and it was kind of spooky. Here's how one of the sessions went.

Me: Auntie, are you there?
Board: Yes

Us: What is your name?
Board : Sandra

Us: How did you die?
Board: Ran over

(we were shocked, because that was how she died)

Us: Okay...what is one of your brothers' named?
Board: Gyln

Us: That's right!
Board: I know

Us: Okay Auntie Sandra, where did your mum run away to? (only I knew this)
Board: Wales

Us: That's right!! Can you move something?
Board: Yes

(nothing moves)

Us: Do you have enough energy?
Board: No

Board: Goodbye
Me: Goodbye, I love you.

We then talked to another ghost called Simon. Here's how it went:

Us: Can you spell your name for us?
Board: Simon

Us: How old are you?
Board: Teen

(we stopped him and asked if he was a teenager, and he said yes)

Us: Did you die in this house?
Board: Yes

Us: How?
Board: Murder

Us: I am sorry, do you have enough strength to move something?
Board: Yes

Us: Would you move the curtain for us?
Board: Yes

(shortly after, a small part of the curtain moved)

Us: Was that you?
Board: Yes, goodbye

Us: Thanks, bye!

We also talked to an old close friend who passed away in 2005. I was really shocked when he told us everything that only one of my friends knew, and she didn't have her finger on it! It was amazing, and I really advise that you play it :)
