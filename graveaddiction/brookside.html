Brookside Cemetery is located on South Main Street in South Deerfield, Massachusetts (Franklin County). The coordinates are 42° 28' 15" N, 72° 36' 36" W.

Brookside Cemetery was established in 1835, and it is still active. There are many old gravestones, and most are in great condition. There is also a large receiving vault near the road. The cemetery is very well maintained.
