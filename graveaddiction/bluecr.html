Blues Creek Cemetery is located on the east side of OH-4, about 3.8 miles northeast of US-33, outside Marysville, Ohio (Leesburg Township, Union County). The coordinates are 40° 18' 5.89" N, 83° 20' 12.34" W.

Blues Creek Cemetery is no longer an active cemetery. It is very well taken care of by the Leesburg Township Trustees. Some of the gravestones are a little overgrown, but most are still standing. There are many old, interesting gravestones to look at, but I must admit I was worried the whole time I was there. OH-4 is a rather busy road, and there wasn't a very good place to pull off the road to visit this cemetery! Luckily no one hit the truck :)
