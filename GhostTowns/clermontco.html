Ghost Towns of
Clermont County


Much of Clermont County is bedroom to Cincinnati, but it has a history all its own. Located on the Ohio River, this county has seen generations of rivermen and pirates, bitter winters and catastrophic floods. Many towns along the Ohio didn't make it. Utopia
Franklin Twp.
Nearly forgotten river town where a tribe of spiritualists was washed away by a big flood. Not much remaining today.
