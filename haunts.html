A never-complete compendium of haunted locales in the state of Ohio. A little red ball----on the county page indicates that I have visited the site, and clicking on it will take you to the investigation page. Otherwise, there will just be a description. I have omitted most occupied private residences from the list, since there are thousands of supposedly haunted houses in the state and I think it's only fair to offer places which you stand a chance of being able to visit. Click on a county to get started. And remember to e-mail me if you do visit any of these sites, or if you know of any I don't have on the list.
